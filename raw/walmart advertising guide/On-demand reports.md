## Request an On-demand report

On-demand reports give you the ability to request a report that is downloadable to a CSV or JSON file. To request an On-demand report, navigate to the *Reports* heading and select  **On-demand**.  

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW0000002cmF&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW0000094Bit)

- Select **Request Report.**
- In the pop-up window, select the report type.
- For the History log reports, you will also need to select a log type and provide the associated details to define the report scope.
- **Note:** The below On-demand report types will not contain any data from Sponsored Products campaigns.
	- Sponsored Videos Campaigns Performance report (applicable only to Sponsored Videos campaigns)
		- Sponsored Videos Keywords Performance report (applicable only to Sponsored Videos campaigns)
- Select an Attribution Window: *3 days*, *14 days*, or *30 days.*
- Select how you want the data to be grouped.
	- **Cumulative** summarizes the data over the selected report period.
		- **Daily** provides data by date over the selected report period.
- Select the report period.
	- For the Keyword, Placement and Attributed Purchases and Item Keyword Performance reports, you can only select a maximum report period of 60 days.
		- For the Item Performance report, you can select up to a one year request period if you choose to group results cumulatively. Daily reports are subject to the maximum length of 60 days.
		- Data is available for the last two years.
- Select **Request Report.**

**Note:** For the Campaign Snapshot report, there are no additional options required.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW0000002cmF&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW0000094Bor)

  
Your report has now been requested. It may take up to 30 minutes for your report to be generated. You can refresh the On-demand reports screen to view the current status.  
  
Once the report status has changed from *Pending* to *Generated*, you will be able to download the report by clicking the icon.  
  
**Note:** Reports will expire after 3 days and won't be available for download. If you still need the report, please submit a new report request.

### Keyword Recommendations (manual Sponsored Products campaigns only)

Keyword Recommendations is an On-demand report that recommends keywords that have the potential to **enhance item visibility** and **boost sales** for in-flight **Sponsored Products manual campaigns**.  
  
Use the report to identify new keywords to add to in-flight manual campaigns and optimize performance.  
  
Keyword recommendations are available three days after the campaign launch and refreshed daily.  

### Item Recommendations

Item Recommendations is a **Sponsored Products** tool and report that identifies unadvertised items with the potential to perform well in a Walmart Connect Sponsored Products campaign.  
  
Use the report to identify items to add to new or in-flight campaigns, to help boost sales.  
  
Recommendations are refreshed weekly in Walmart Connect Ad Center on Wednesday afternoons, with data refreshed through the prior day (Tuesday). The report can be filtered by brand, category and department. It's best practice to review your item recommendations on a weekly basis.  
  
[Learn more about item recommendations](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?channel=Sponsored&article=000009961).

### Keyword Performance (keyword bidded manual campaigns only)

This report indicates which keywords your advertised items are being served against. This information can help you optimize keyword bids.

- If the searched keyword is alphabetical or alphanumeric, that means your ad was likely shown on a search page in response to that searched query.
- If the searched keyword is a single number and this number is a valid Walmart Item ID, that means your ad was likely shown on the item page associated with that Item ID. Walmart Connect matches your bidded keyword and match type with one of the anchor item entities, such as item name, item brand or item taxonomy, to determine bid eligibility.
	- To view the associated item page, open Walmart.com/ip/seort/\[INSERT ITEM ID\] in your web browser.
- If the searched keyword is a series of numbers separated by underscores, that means your ad was likely shown on a browse page. Walmart Connect matches your bidded keyword and match type with one of the browse page entities, such as browse page name or browse page taxonomy, to determine bid eligibility.
	- To view the associated browse page, open Walmart.com/browse/\[INSERT BROWSE PAGE PATH STRING\] in your web browser.

  
Inside this report, there are two columns for **Bid** and **Average CPC**.

- Bid is the maximum you will pay for a click when this target triggers your ad.
- Average CPC is the average amount you paid for a click on an ad.

  
**Important:** Keyword and Item Keyword Performance reports only show impression data for keywords with clicks greater than 0. The new reporting condition speeds up report generation and reduces the size of reports by over half.  

### Placement Performance

This report provides key performance metrics for your campaigns by the placement the ads are served in. This information can help you to improve current and future Sponsored Products campaigns. Use bid multipliers to optimize placements based on their performance.

- **Search In-grid** - metrics from ads served on the Search In-grid placement
- **Browse In-grid** - metrics from ads served on the Browse In-grid placement
- **Carousel** - metrics from ads served on any other placement not otherwise listed here
- **Buy Box** - metrics from ads served on the Buy Box placement
- **Homepage** - metrics from ads served on the Home Page placements
- **StockUp** - metrics from ads served on the Stock Up placement

### Item Keyword Performance

This report indicates which keywords your advertised items are being served against, and how well the advertised items are performing for those keywords. The Item Keyword performance report identifies keywords and provides performance data for ads served in Search In-grid placements. This information can help you improve current and future campaigns.

- **Automatic campaigns:** Use this report to identify keywords that showed your ads in Search In-Grid placements and delivered high performance. You can then add these keywords to Manual campaigns.
- **Manual campaigns:** Use the additional information to optimize campaign performance: bid higher on more-valuable keywords and/or remove underperforming items.

  
The Item Keyword Performance report data may not match the data that appears in the Sponsored Search dashboard under Performance summary for Sponsored Products campaigns. This is because the Item Keyword Performance report only covers Search In-grid impressions from Sponsored Products campaigns, while Sponsored Products campaigns may serve ads to customers in other placements (such as Browse In-grid, homepage, carousel or Buy Box). As a result, impressions, clicks, ad spend and attributed sales metrics may be lower in the Item Keyword Performance report than in other reports analyzing the same Sponsored Products campaign(s).

**Important:** Keyword and Item Keyword Performance reports only show impression data for keywords with clicks greater than 0. The new reporting condition speeds up report generation and reduces the size of reports by over half.

### Item Performance

This report provides key performance metrics for the items in all your campaigns, including attributed sales data by fulfillment type: *Shipping* and *Curbside pickup and delivery*. When a SKU ID is available for Marketplace items, the SKU ID will appear in the report, allowing Marketplace sellers to compare item performance by SKU ID. Insights from this report can help you improve the performance of your Sponsored Search campaigns.  
  
Inside this report, there are two columns for **Bid** and **Average CPC**.

- Bid is the maximum you will pay for a click.
- Average CPC is the average amount you paid for a click on an ad.

### Campaign Snapshot

This report allows advertisers to export campaign-structure information in bulk. Advertisers can use this information when migrating campaigns to an API Partner or from one API Partner to another. Structured data will be provided for live, paused, ended and scheduled campaigns.  
**  
Note:** Campaign-structure information with generate in JSON format. Campaign Snapshot reports are limited to one request per day.

### History logs

These reports provide historical log information for an advertising account. There are two main types of History Log reports:  
  
The **Access History** report details the users and API partners who have (or have had) access to your advertising account over the last 90 days.  
  
The **Campaign History** report type details all of the changes/edits that have been made at every level of the campaign hierarchy for all campaigns in your advertising account over the last 90 days. You can pull a Campaign History report at all of the following levels:

- Campaign
- Ad group
- Item
- Keyword
- Bid multiplier
- Placement
- SBA profile

### Search Term Impression Share

The Search Term Impression Share Report provides you with impression share insights at the search term level, you can use these insights to optimize your ad performance. You can see your ad impression share and rank for your Sponsored Products In-grid placement as well as for the top four ad positions.  
   
**Note:** The data in this report focuses on search terms with at least ten impressions per day, prioritizing the most relevant and high-impact terms. 

![image.png](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW0000002cmF&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EM8Y00000DB21V)

  
*Search term impression share* and *top-of-search impression share* includes opportunities for when the advertiser is currently out of budget. It represents the percentage of customer search queries that *could*   have received an ad impression, assuming budget is not a constraint. This calculation is a more comprehensive view of the advertiser's potential reach and reflects all potential instances when an ad may have served, regardless of the advertiser's budget.  

**Note:** This report only includes Search In-grid ads for manual and automatic Sponsored Products campaigns. It does NOT include Sponsored Brands, Sponsored Videos, and Sponsored Products carousels or Buy Box.    

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW0000002cmF&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW0000094C2j)

**Report breakdowns available:**

- **Day** features unique search terms with impression share broken out by day for the last 30 days.
- **Last 30 days** features unique search terms with total impression share for the last 30 days.
- **Week** shows total impression share for unique search terms at a weekly level within a custom date range.
- **Month** shows total impression share for unique search terms at a monthly level within a custom date range.

  
All breakdowns have a maximum limit of 330,000 rows, so for weekly and monthly breakdowns, those rows are divided by the number of weeks or months you select using the date selector. For example, if you are looking back three months, each month can have up to 110,000 rows. The same applies for weekly.  

All report breakdowns also include standard performance and conversion metrics available on other reports, such as average cost-per-click and ROAS.  

Use Search Term Impression Share Report to identify opportunities to improve your campaign performance. Low impression share and rank, but high click-through rate could indicate an increase in ad spend to yield more conversions. High impression share but low Top of Search impression share and click-through rate could indicate that ads are not showing up in the premium placements on Search In-grid.  

Use this information to reallocate budget to competitive keywords or increase spend limits to help maximize exposure and conversions. Remember to request and download the report regularly to monitor for fluctuations in share and ranking. Changes can help identify possible trends and new opportunities.

### Attributed Purchases

This report offers granular insights into purchased products attributed to your Sponsored Search campaigns. This report allows advertisers to identify additional Other items to promote with their Sponsored Search campaigns and understand which of their brand(s)' products are resonating with existing and new-to-brand customers.  

**Key Features**

The report includes click-based attributed metrics for 3-, 14- and 30-day attribution windows.  
  
The data is segmented by campaign and ad group names and IDs, attribution types, advertiser item IDs and names, sales figures, orders, units sold and new-to-brand metrics like sales revenue and percentages.  
  
**Note:** The report includes all attributed Advertised and Other items (halo). It is available only for eligible campaigns with **Orders greater than 0** for the requested period.
