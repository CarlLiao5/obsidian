Welcome! Follow the steps below to sign up for the Walmart Connect Academy and gain access to valuable learning and eCourse content.  
  
To register for access to the Walmart Connect Academy:

1. [Click here](https://connectacademy.walmartlearning.com/student/authentication/register) to register as a new user.
2. Complete all required fields.
3. Click **Register.**

  
**Note:** If you’ve previously registered to the Walmart Connect Academy and forgot your password, visit our [Forgot Password](https://connectacademy.walmartlearning.com/student/authentication/request_password_reset) page to receive a password reset link.
