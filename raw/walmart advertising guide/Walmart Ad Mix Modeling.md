Walmart Ad Mix Modeling can help you better plan and optimize your media spend with Walmart Connect. WAMM is a cross-channel analysis of your ad portfolio that uses a proprietary, statistical approach to measure the incremental impact of Walmart Connect media on omnichannel sales.

## How to access

Navigate to the  icon in the upper right-hand corner on Walmart Connect Ad Center to access all products. Select **Cross-Channel R** **eports**   under *Cross-channel platforms*. 

**![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP4w&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000A4em9)**

Select your report type (Brand WAMM or Brand WAMM Media Planner) under *Walmart Ad Mix Modeling* on the Cross-Channel Reports landing page.

**![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP4w&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000A4emn)**

To learn more, visit the [Cross-Channel Reports](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&article=000012454) guide and the [Access management](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&article=000012444) guide.

## Overview

WAMM reports are available in Walmart Connect Ad Center for eligible advertisers in the Cross-Channel Reports platform. You can understand both media effectiveness and efficiency alongside channel and tactic specific performance to drive budget planning and optimization. You can also compare different budget scenarios and get scenario-based recommendations to help drive incremental sales.

To learn more and request a WAMM report, reach out to your Walmart Connect account team.

Advertiser eligibility\* and reporting granularity vary by WAMM report type:

- **Brand WAMM:**Incremental impact of Walmart Connect on your brand’s sales, across channels and tactics.

## Frequently asked questions

### Will these reports be available automatically in Walmart Connect Ad Center?

No, you must request these reports through your Walmart Connect account team. Please reach out to them to learn more.

### Which campaigns are included in the report? Can I choose campaigns to include?

All the campaigns from accounts within your advertiser organization are included in the report. You cannot include or exclude campaigns. For more information, visit the [Levels of account access](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&article=000009334) guide.

### How can I grant access to more users once my report is available in Walmart Connect Ad Center?

An org admin must grant users access. Only organization standard users will be able to view [Cross-Channel Reports](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&article=000012454).

### What time period is represented in my WAMM report? How often is it refreshed?

There are annual and quarterly views of the report. Annual reporting is at the calendar year level. Your report will not be automatically refreshed, please reach out to your Walmart Connect account team to request a refresh.

### Does Offsite media reporting include social?

Yes, Pinterest, Meta and TikTok are included in reporting where we can map the brand.

### Are Store tactics included in reporting?

No, Store tactics are not included at this time.

### Which sales are included in the report?

All traceable orders from Walmart’s site and app and Store purchases are included in the report.
