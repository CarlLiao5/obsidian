Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM) Data Portal

The Walmart Connect Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM) Data Portal lives within the Cross-Channel Reports platform in Walmart Connect Ad Center. Cross-Channel Reports is the home for reporting products that include multiple channels across all your Walmart Connect media solutions.

## Overview

Marketing Mix Modeling is a marketing effectiveness analysis to understand how your various marketing efforts work together to drive conversions or sales. MMM measures all marketing tactics across all sales channels to help you quantify the sales impacts of all marketing drivers and enable optimizations to improve spend allocation across marketing tactics.

The Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM) Data Portal provides self-serve access to automated and standardized Walmart Connect MMM data across Sponsored Search, Onsite Display and Offsite media in Walmart Connect Ad Center.

## How to access

Navigate to the icon in the upper right-hand corner on Walmart Connect Ad Center to access all products. Select **Cross-Channel Reports** under *Cross-channel platforms*.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP51&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000A4eoe)

Select **MMM Data Portal** under *Data tools* on the Cross-Channel Reports landing page.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP51&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000A4epI)

Visit the [Cross-channel reports guid](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?article=000012454&channel=Display&language=en_US) [e](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?article=000012454&channel=Display&language=en_US) for additional information.

**Important:** Only super admins, org admins and org level users (with Cross-Channel Report access) can access the MMM Data Portal. If you cannot access Cross-Channel Reports, please contact your super admin or org admin. Cross-Channel Reports provide reporting at an organization level and only org level admins can view Cross-Channel Reports by default. Your super admin or org admin can grant additional users access. Please note that only organization level users can be granted access. For more information on how to grant access, visit the [Access management guide](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?channel=Sponsored%20Products&language=en_US&article=000012444).

## Requesting data

What you need to know before you request a report:

- Reports are standardized; dimensions and metrics cannot be customized in the request.
- The portal has a two-day data lag and report requests take up to one business day to process.

There are two types of requests depending on the delivery type — whether you want the data now or scheduled for the future.

**Immediate MMM data request**

Under *Accounts*, select or deselect the accounts you want to include in your report. You will only be able to view accounts that belong to the Walmart Connect Ad Center organization you are logged into.

Select **Request now** to receive a report within one business day.

Using the calendar, select your report start date (this will be the first date included in your report) and your report end date (this will be the last date included in your report).

**Note:** The portal supports data requests for up to two years from today’s date.

Under *Start of the week*, select **Sunday** or **Monday**. Data is set at the weekly level.

Once all fields are filled out, select **Request report**.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP51&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009b6bT)

**Future MMM data request**  
Under *Accounts*, select or deselect the accounts you want to include in your report. You will only be able to view accounts that belong to the Walmart Connect Ad Center organization you are logged into.  

Select **Schedule** to generate a future report.

Using the calendar, select your report start date. The start date of your request will determine the start date of your scheduled report and affect each subsequent report.

Set frequency: weekly, monthly, every three months or every six months. The frequency determines how often your report will be delivered.

Under *Start of the week*, select **Sunday** or **Monday**. Data is set at the weekly level.

Once all fields are filled out, select **Request report**.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP51&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009b6iY)

### Downloading MMM data

A confirmation window will pop up to confirm that the request was submitted.

**Note:** If you do not see this message, request the report again.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP51&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009b8on)

You will receive a confirmation email when the data is ready to download in Walmart Connect Ad Center. Once the download is available, it will remain available for thirty days. Data will be downloadable in a zip file. For requests larger than eight weeks, data will be in multiple CSV files.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP51&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009b6n4)

**Important:** If you do not download your report within thirty days, you will have to request a new report.

For reports requested **now**, the download will be available one business day after you submit your request.

For **scheduled reports**, the download will be available one business day after the reporting period ends.

## Frequently asked questions

### What should I expect if I have multiple organizations in Walmart Connect Ad Center?

For advertisers with multiple organizations, you will have to log into each organization separately to request separate reports.

### What date ranges are available?

The portal supports data requests for up to two years from today’s date. Data is only available at the week granularity; if the start date and/or end date does not align with the week start and/or week end, start and end weeks will have less days. Example:

- Dates inputted into portal: 8/1/2024–8/31/2024
- Start of week inputted into portal: Monday
- First week of data in the output: 8/1/2024–8/4/2024
- Last week of data in the output: 8/26/2024–8/31/2024

### How often is the portal data updated?

Data is updated daily and there is a two-day lag.

**Example**: If you go into the portal on 9/1/2025, data will be updated through 8/30/2025.

### How often can a report be requested?

There is no limit of the number of reports that can be pulled. However, if multiple reports are requested at the same time, there may be delays.

### How can I access the report?

One day after the request is submitted, the CSV file(s) will be available for download in the MMM Data Portal in Walmart Connect Ad Center.

### What can I expect from the reporting output?

The report will have Display and Sponsored Search data in separate CSV files included in a downloadable zip file.

### What channels are available?

Currently, Sponsored Brands, Sponsored Products, Sponsored Videos, Onsite Display and Offsite media is available through this portal. Display channels include both self-serve and Managed Served campaigns; Offsite media includes Meta, TikTok, Pinterest, and Roku (at the national level). Other channels, including in-store are currently not available.

### What is included in ad spend?

All metrics, including ad spend, will reflect what is in Walmart Connect Ad Center and/or Walmart DSP. Offsite Display (The Trade Desk) spend includes media spend and fees (Walmart Connect, The Trade Desk platform, third-party fees).

### Why is the DMA for some data reported as National?

DMA reporting is limited for some historical data from our previous ad server, DFP, due to Google limitations. For these instances, National will be used as the DMA. For media delivered in geos such as U.S. territories, reporting is limited and can only be reported as National. Pinterest, Roku, Meta and TikTok data are only available at the national level (DMA level is currently not available).

### Why are campaigns that ended before my selected date range included in my reporting output?

This is likely due to differences in reporting time zones. Display data is in Eastern time (ET) and Sponsored Search data is in Pacific time (PT).

### I received a download error message, what does this mean?

If your report was too large or the request took too long to process, it will not be downloadable from the portal. Try re-requesting the report. If you continue to receive an error message, reach out to your Walmart Connect team.

### I submitted a report request and a download confirmation box appeared, however, when I returned to the MMM Data Portal after one business day, there was no report available. Where is my report?

This may be due to a limitation of Walmart Connect Ad Center. Please clear your cache and reload the MMM Data Portal. If your download still doesn’t appear, please reach out to your Walmart Connect team.

### Can a scheduled report be cancelled or edited?

No, you cannot cancel or edit a scheduled report.

### I am experiencing an issue with the MMM Data Portal and/or have an issue with data in my report. What should I do?

Please reach out to your Walmart Connect team for further assistance or submit a [Help Center ticket](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/contact?language=en_US) with our support team.
