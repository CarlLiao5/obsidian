In Billing Manager, you can designate tracking numbers, such as Purchase Order numbers to specific Sponsored Search and Onsite Display campaigns. This provides a self-serve experience to populate tracking numbers to invoices at scale.

## When to include a tracking number

You should include a tracking number in Billing Manager if you want to:

- Reference PO numbers printed on Walmart Connect invoices for internal bookkeeping
- Ensure PO numbers are printed on Walmart Connect invoices for agency billing

**Note:** This is an optional field and used for advertiser reference purposes. Including a PO number on an invoice does not constitute acceptance of the legal terms or conditions that may be included in such documents.

## Adding tracking numbers to your Onsite Display campaigns

Walmart suppliers can input the PO number in Billing Manager. Walmart Connect will collect the information and use it to generate accurate, automated invoices.

Within the campaign creation page, ensure **Yes, go to Billing manager** under *Tracking number (optional)* is selected. It should be selected by default. When you select **Save**, you will be redirected to Billing Manager. Select **No** if you don’t need to assign an invoice number.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPJO&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009KIoH)

Select the **pencil icon** in the Actions column to add the tracking number for an associated campaign. A pop-up will appear where you can add the tracking number. The tracking number you added appears in the Tracking Number column.

**Important:** If you need to modify the tracking number after it’s been added, you must file a [Help Center ticket](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/contact?language=en_US) with our support team who can assist you.

**![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPJO&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009XhON)**

**Note:** If you select **Yes** to add a tracking number but do not add the number in Billing Manager, you will receive an email reminding you to add the tracking number to the associated campaigns. If a tracking number is no longer needed, open Billing Manager and deselect the **Tracking number column**. This can only be done if you haven’t added a tracking number already.

## Adding tracking numbers to your Sponsored Search campaigns

If you want to add a tracking number to your Sponsored Search campaign, you must file a [Help Center ticket](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/contact?language=en_US) with our support team. Our support team will help enable this for you.

**Important:** Tracking numbers are not automatically included for Sponsored Search campaigns.

Once it’s been enabled, Sponsored Search campaigns with ad spend incurred will appear in the Billing Manager. To add the tracking number for an associated campaign, select the **pencil icon** in the Actions column. A pop-up will appear where you can add the tracking number. Once you add the tracking number, it will appear in the Tracking Number column.

**Important:** If you need to modify the tracking number after it’s been added, you must file a [Help Center ticket](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/contact?language=en_US) with our support team who can assist you.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPJO&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009XhQE)

**Notes:**

- If you requested this feature but haven’t added a tracking number for your campaign, you will receive an email reminding you to add the tracking number. If a tracking number is no longer needed, open Billing Manager and deselect the **Tracking number column**. This can only be done if you haven't added a tracking number already.
- If you no longer require tracking numbers for your campaigns, you can file a [Help Center ticket](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/contact?language=en_US) with our support team and they will turn off the feature.
