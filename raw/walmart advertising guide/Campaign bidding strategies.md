Bidding strategies are more than just campaign settings; they’re levers for performance. Whether you're looking for manual control, conversion-driven automation, more sales or ROAS efficiency, Walmart Connect offers a bidding solution tailored to your business goals.

The three bidding strategies that Walmart Connect provides are **Dynamic bidding**, **Target ROAS bidding** and **Fixed bidding**. Dynamic bidding helps boost conversion and optimize campaign ad spend. Target ROAS bidding helps maximize sales within a set ROAS target. Fixed bidding allows you to manage a campaign with strict budget limits and predictable spending, but requires you to manually adjust the bid based on campaign performance.

## Why your bidding strategy matters

A bidding strategy dictates how your campaign bids compete in the auction and how budgets are optimized to meet your performance goals. The right bidding approach helps you:

- Improve efficiency in spend
- Align campaign outcomes with business goals
- Balance control and automation

Whether you're scaling campaigns or refining performance, selecting the right strategy is foundational to success.

## Optimized bidding best practices

While each bidding strategy — **Dynamic**, **Target ROAS** and **Fixed** — has its own optimization approach, there are some universal best practices that apply across all bidding strategies. These best practices will help you launch, manage and scale your campaigns more effectively, regardless of the strategy in use.

### New campaigns

- Ensure your items are in stock
- Avoid running identical campaigns for the same collection of products simultaneously
- Set out-of-budget alerts to avoid capping out

**Note:** You cannot create a new campaign using the Target ROAS bidding strategy.

### Existing campaigns

- Make sure the campaign is already seeing some engagement in clicks, impressions and conversions
- Don't duplicate the campaigns; set each with a different bidding strategy

### Optimizing on-going campaigns

- Avoid frequently switching between Fixed, Dynamic and Target ROAS bidding in-flight
- Avoid reducing campaign budget and/or disabling items in-flight
- Increase your campaign budget if your campaign runs out of budget daily
