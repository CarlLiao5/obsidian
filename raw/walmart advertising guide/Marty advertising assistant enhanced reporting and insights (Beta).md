Marty advertising assistant enhanced reporting and insights (Beta)

**DISCLAIMER**

*Marty advertising assistant is currently in beta and only available to select advertisers. You may not see this option in Walmart Connect Ad Center.*

This guide provides an overview of Marty advertising assistant enhanced reporting and insights, available for Sponsored Search in Walmart Connect Ad Center. Core capabilities and best practices are included. Visit the [Marty advertising assistant overview (B](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&article=000012468) [eta)](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&article=000012468) guide for more information.

## Overview

Marty advertising assistant can help address your questions and provide data and insights. You can ask Marty advertising assistant to quickly identify top performing items or keywords and report on key metrics and changes over time.

## Exclusive reports available only through Marty advertising assistant

Marty advertising assistant offers enhanced reporting and insights that provide deeper visibility into your campaign performance and competitive landscape.

### Momentum reports

Gain clarity on how changes in ad spend impact performance metrics.

- **Change Analysis Report:** This report visualizes shifts in two metrics over time, with summarized insights across Sponsored Search metrics like:
	- Ad spend
		- Average CPC, clicks, CTR, impressions
		- New-to-brand metrics
		- Orders, ROAS, sales, unit sold

Available at weekly or monthly granularity.

### Strategic intelligence reports

Benchmark your performance against your industry and uncover growth opportunities.

- **Account Impression Share of Voice Report:** Understand your Sponsored Search impression share by catalog, brand or campaign type.
- **Account Keyword Impression Share of Voice Report:** Understand your keyword-level Sponsored Search impression share.
- **Category Benchmark Opportunity report:** Evaluate your performance against category benchmarks from the previous Walmart fiscal quarter across KPIs like:
	- Average CPC
		- Conversion rate
		- ROAS

**Note:** All reports are limited to the advertiser level and do not include organization-wide data.

## Getting started with Marty advertising assistant (Beta)

Log in to [Walmart Connect Ad Center](https://advertising.walmart.com/). Marty advertising assistant will appear as a new chat window on the right side of the page.

Engage with Marty advertising assistant by typing in your own questions or choosing an available prompt.

Provide feedback using the thumbs up or the thumbs down button.

## Requesting a report

Start by asking Marty advertising assistant to generate your desired report, specifying any necessary filters such as date range, category or brand. Marty advertising assistant will provide a research plan that summarizes the report type and associated parameters. If the report type and associated parameters are correct, respond to approve. Reports can take up to four hours to generate.

### Accessing reports

Once a report is generated, it will be available within the Reports folder in Marty advertising assistant. The Reports folder can be accessed in the upper right corner of the Marty advertising assistant dialogue box by selecting the **folder** icon. Reports expire on the date indicated within the Reports folder. You can download a CSV version of the enhanced report data from the Reports folder.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPPg&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009azpc)

### Number of reports

You can request up to five new enhanced report pulls from Marty advertising assistant per day.

Marty advertising assistant can continue to answer related questions based on the data previously pulled that day.

### Generating a report

Enhanced reports and insights are available exclusively through Marty advertising assistant and cannot be accessed via the standard reporting section of Walmart Connect Ad Center or through Tech Partners.

Once you approve a report request, Marty advertising assistant begins generating the enhanced report and insights. This process may take up to four hours, depending on the complexity and data availability.

While your report is being prepared, you can continue working in Walmart Connect Ad Center, including asking Marty advertising assistant more questions. Marty advertising assistant will notify you as soon as the report is ready through a chat notification and a red dot indicator on the **Reports folder** icon in the upper right corner of the chat window.

### Data validity

Reports will only generate when the minimum Data Governance thresholds are met.

**Example:** A report will not generate if there isn’t enough data in the comparison set for a benchmark report.

### Report date range

Data can be requested for up to the last 13 months.

### Additional report details

**Change Analysis Report**

This report helps you quickly understand shifts in two metrics over time, complete with visualizations and qualitative summarized insights.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPPg&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009azgV)

You can ask Marty advertising assistant to compare change trends across most available Sponsored Search metrics, including:

- Ad spend
- Average CPC
- Clicks
- CTR
- Impressions
- New-to-brand metrics
	- Percentage of units new-to-brand
		- Percentage of orders new-to-brand
		- Percentage of sales new-to-brand
- Orders
- ROAS
- Total attributed sales
- Units sold

Change Analysis helps summarize and visualize existing metrics at a weekly or monthly level.

**Example questions Marty advertising assistant can help with**

- How did \[Metric A\] change over time relative to \[Metric B\]? (will default to a monthly view over the last 13 months).
- How did \[Metric A\] change \[over the last X weeks/months\] \[between X and Y dates\] relative to \[Metric B\] for \[Brand A\]?

**Account Impression Share of Voice Report**

This report helps you understand your account impression share of voice relative to other advertisers in the same category.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPPg&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009azj0)

When you ask about your account impression share of voice, Marty advertising assistant will develop a research plan and once approved, share an Account Impression Share of Voice report.

You can ask about your account impression share of voice at various levels:

- Catalog level
	- Super Department (L1) (e.g., Food)
		- Department (L2) (e.g., Bakery & Bread)
		- Category (L3) (e.g., rolls, bread, buns, etc.)
- Catalog level + Brand level
- Catalog level + Campaign type level

Your results will be color coded; when there is enough data available to comply with Data Governance guidelines, specific values will also be shared.

**Note:** Reports are limited to the advertiser level and do not include an entire organization.

**Example questions Marty advertising assistant can help with**

- What was my \[advertiser\] \[brand\] \[campaign type\] impression share of voice over the last 13 months within the \[food super department\] \[bakery & bread department\] \[bread category\]?

**Account Keyword Impression Share of Voice Report**

This report helps you understand your keyword impression share of voice relative to other advertisers in the same category.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPPg&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009azn7)

When you ask about your keyword impression share of voice, Marty advertising assistant will develop a research plan and once approved, share an Advertiser Keyword Impression Share of Voice report.

You can ask about your keyword impression share of voice at various levels:

- Advertiser level
- Catalog level
	- Super Department (L1) (e.g., Food)
		- Department (L2) (e.g., Bakery & Bread)
		- Category (L3) (e.g., rolls, bread, buns, etc.)
- Brand level

Your results will be color coded; when there is enough data available to comply with Data Governance guidelines, specific values will also be shared.

**Note:** Reports are limited to the advertiser level and do not include an entire organization.

**Example questions Marty advertising assistant can help with**

- What was my \[advertiser\] \[brand\] keyword impression share of voice over the last 13 months within the \[food super department\] \[bakery & bread department\] \[bread category\]?

**Account Category Benchmark Opportunity Report**

This report helps you understand how your campaign performance stacks up against other advertisers within the same catalog level.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPPg&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009azo5)

When you ask about benchmarks, Marty advertising assistant will develop a research plan and once approved, share an Advertiser Category Benchmark Opportunity report.

You can ask about different KPIs, including, but not limited to:

- Average CPC
- Conversion rate
- ROAS

This report calculates 25th to 75th percentile benchmarks based on data from the previous Walmart fiscal quarter, enabling you to assess whether your performance falls below, within or above catalog norms for the selected KPI.

**Note:** Account Category Benchmark Opportunity reports are limited to the previous Walmart fiscal quarter.

Reports are limited to the advertiser level and do not include an entire organization.

## Frequently asked questions

### Why did my ad spend increase, but my ROAS or share of voice decline?

This often happens when market dynamics shift. Increased spend doesn’t guarantee proportional returns because factors like rising CPCs, intensified competition or changes in category demand can offset gains. When competition increases, extra budget may only maintain impressions rather than grow them. Use the Change Analysis report to compare before and after periods, check CPC trends and identify whether spend was allocated to high-performing keywords or placements. Optimization, not just more spend, is key.

### Why is my performance below category benchmarks?

Benchmarks reflect aggregate category norms, not individual strategies. Falling below the 25th percentile may indicate gaps in bidding, item coverage or campaign diversity. Review whether you’re promoting your full catalog, following Suggested Bids and using both automatic and manual campaigns. Marty’s *Account Category Benchmark Opportunity report* highlights where you’re under-indexed and offers actionable steps to close gaps.

### What should I do about my low performance?

Low performance doesn’t always mean something is broken, it could reflect shifts in the market or competitive landscape. Use the Change Analysis report to identify which metrics moved (CPC, CTR, ROAS) and where your ad spend is being allocated. Consider a wide set of optimization levers to improve your sales performance, such as bids, targeting, keywords and item coverage. Ask Marty to help you identify the areas of highest sales opportunity.

### Can I access more share by increasing my ad spend?

Share of voice depends on multiple variables like competition, relevancy, inventory and auction dynamics, not just budget. Increasing ad spend without strategic adjustments may lead to diminishing returns. Use Marty advertising assistant’s optimization prompts to identify where you can best allocate your incremental investment to help grow sales.
