## How to onboard and start advertising with Walmart Connect

- Walmart suppliers can [go here to begin advertising](https://advertising.walmart.com/onboard/supplier) with Walmart Connect.
- Marketplace sellers can [go here to begin advertising](https://seller.walmart.com/signup) with Walmart Connect.

## How to access your advertising account

Log in to the [Walmart Connect Ad Center](https://advertising.walmart.com/signin) using your existing credentials for Seller Center, Supplier Center or Walmart.com (as applicable).

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPRD&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000008vP6j)

- **For new advertisers,** select the advertising account in the dropdown where you’d like to begin running your ads.

- **For existing/returning advertisers,** the advertising account that you last used will pre-populate in the dropdown for your convenience.
	- **Note:** The first time you see this interface (as an existing/returning advertiser), you will need to either search for your desired advertising account or select your desired advertising account from the dropdown.

**Don’t see your account listed?** Try searching for the advertising account using either the name or ID. Type in the first three letters/numbers to start searching.

You will see a gray-out name at the top of your listed accounts. This is your organization name under which all your campaigns are housed. You will not be able to select it, but you can manage organization level permissions. To learn more, visit the [Account access as an admin user](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&article=000012444) and [User roles and permissions](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&article=000009334) guides.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPRD&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000008vQJ0)

Make your media solution selection. Depending on the account you selected, your media solution options will be available in line with that account’s access. Inaccessible media solutions will be grayed out.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPRD&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000008vQHx)

If you are an admin looking to manage user and partner permissions, select any advertising account and media solution combination that you have admin access to.

**Note:** You may not have access to all media solutions if you only have access to one account with one platform (you will not see this selection screen). You will be taken directly to the platform’s home dashboard.

If you are having login issues:

- Check with your advertising admin to ensure that you have been granted the correct access. For information, see [User roles and permissions](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?article=000009334).
- If you are still having login issues, [Contact Support](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/contact?channel=Sponsored%20Products).

## How to request access to Walmart Connect Ad Center

### New Walmart Connect advertisers

### Request a new Walmart Connect Ad Center account

If you are a new advertiser without a Walmart Connect Ad Center account, please refer to these onboarding forms:

- [Walmart Connect supplier onboarding form](https://advertising.walmart.com/onboard/supplier)
	- Before filling out the form, you must have an active [Supplier One](https://supplierone.wal-mart.com/item-mgr/brand-management) account to receive a Supplier ID. This is the first step to onboard.
- [Marketplace seller onboarding form](https://seller.walmart.com/signup)
	- Before filling out the form, you must have an active [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/) account to receive a Seller ID. This is the first step to onboard.

Walmart Connect offers new Marketplace sellers and Walmart suppliers a seamless onboarding experience to the Walmart Connect Ad Center that makes launching campaigns even easier.

**Onboard & new account details for new Marketplace sellers**

**Important:** All new Marketplace sellers are automatically onboarded to our self-serve ad platform, and your Seller Center account admin is automatically assigned as your Walmart Connect Ad Center admin.  

You must have an active [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/) account and receive a Seller ID to get started. Without having an active Seller Center account first, you will not be able to onboard with Walmart Connect.

If you are a new Marketplace seller, you will need to provide the following information:

- Tax ID
- Seller ID or Partner ID
- Name of account admin and email
	- This is the person who will be assigned as the account admin. Admins will have the ability to give additional users access to the account and set/change their access levels

**Onboard & new account details for new Walmart suppliers**

If you are a new Walmart supplier without a Walmart Connect Ad Center account, please follow these steps:

You must have an active account with [Supplier One](https://supplierone.wal-mart.com/item-mgr/brand-management) and receive a Supplier ID to get started. Without having an active Supplier One account first, you will not be able to onboard with Walmart Connect.

- If you have an existing Supplier One account but are having issues logging in, submit a [Help Center ticket](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/contact?language=en_US) with our support team to reset your login. You can also review the [step-by-step instructions here](https://supplierone.helpdocs.io/article/msbpajftru-password-reset-request-retail-link) for resetting your password on Supplier One (Retail Link).
- In Supplier One, you will need to select the associated supplier name to start the onboarding process. In addition, you will need to provide the following information to [set up](https://supplierone.helpdocs.io/article/pytc4hzrc7-supplier-setup-and-maintenance) a new Supplier One account:
	- Organization name
		- Advertiser account name
		- Partner information (if applicable)
		- Billing company name
		- Billing contact name and email address
		- Super admin name and email address

Fill out the [Walmart Connect supplier onboarding form](https://advertising.walmart.com/onboard/supplier).

- **Important:** If your company already has an existing organization with Walmart Connect, you will not be able to create another organization using this form. If a separate organization is needed, submit a [Help Center ticket](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/contact?language=en_US) with our support team.

If you are an admin on your Supplier One account, your organization will be automatically created.

- Supplier One admins are responsible for reviewing, approving and rejecting requests submitted for a new account. They can also review all requests through *Request Management*.

If you are not an admin, a request will automatically be made and all Supplier One admins will be notified to approve or reject the request. You will be informed via email once the request has been approved or rejected.

- If the Supplier One admin doesn’t respond to your request, you can submit a [Help Center ticket](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/contact?language=en_US) with our support team.

#### Receive welcome email

After our team receives all necessary information, we’ll create your *advertiser account*.  
  
Once it’s created, you will receive a welcome email from our team to help you get started with advertising.  

- **Marketplace sellers**: email will be sent to the Seller Center email address you provide
- **Walmart suppliers:** email will be sent to the Walmart.com customer account email you shared

### Existing Walmart Connect advertisers

If you are an existing advertiser, you can run all of your campaigns from a single Walmart Connect Ad Center account.  

If you are having trouble accessing Walmart Connect Ad Center, contact your account admin to request a change of access levels as outlined in [User roles and permissions](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?article=000009334).

We’ve streamlined the onboarding and activation process, providing access-management capabilities within our self-serve Walmart Connect Ad Center platform that allow advertisers to use a single advertising account to:  

- Select or switch between API Partners and agencies
- Work with multiple API Partners and agencies
- Manage API Partner and agency access

Individual users from API Partners can also be granted access privileges to the Walmart Connect Ad Center (in addition to their existing API access). For a complete list of FAQ on streamlined activation, [click here](https://sforce.co/3OmIHsp).  

How to connect to the Walmart Connect Partner network

The Walmart Connect Partner Network provides advertising solutions from third-party partners. If you are unsure where to begin, keep reading to learn how to get started.

### Explore the Partner directory

Head over to the Walmart Connect [Partner page](https://www.walmartconnect.com/partners) to browse a directory of Partners.

### Find the right fit

The directory lets you filter for Partners based on whether you’re a seller, supplier or agency. You can also filter based on need, like campaign management, creative, measurement and retail.

### Connect and discuss

Reach out to potential Partners and discuss your advertising goals. Do not hesitate to interview multiple Partners to find the best fit for your budget and campaign objectives.

### Invite them to your account

Ready to collaborate with your new Partner? Follow [this guide](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?article=000009334) to invite them to your account and get started.

**Remember:** Partners are independent third parties and not affiliated with Walmart Connect. Inclusion in the Walmart Connect Partner Network directory is not an endorsement from Walmart. Offerings and pricing are subject to change at the discretion of the partner.

**Important:** Only Sponsored Search advertising accounts can be consolidated via a single advertising account. Display and Shop Builder accounts cannot be consolidated.

If you have multiple advertising accounts that are active, you can easily consolidate and manage your campaigns via a single advertising account (if you choose to do so) without having to create any new accounts. It is not possible to "merge" advertising accounts. These instructions outline how to move campaign structure from one account to another.  
  
For example, if you have two accounts (Account A and Account B) actively running campaigns, you can duplicate Account A’s campaigns into Account B. Then, pause the campaigns in Account A and run all of our campaigns from Account B going forward.

**Note:** It is not possible to consolidate Marketplace seller and Walmart supplier accounts into a single account at this time.  
  
If you are currently working with an API Partner (or are switching to one) who can ingest campaigns in bulk, leverage the Campaign Snapshot report in On-demand Reports. This allows you to export campaign structure information in bulk. You can use this information when migrating campaigns from Self-serve to an API Partner or from one API Partner to another. Structured data will be provided for live, paused, ended, and scheduled campaigns.

- To download the report, log into your Walmart Connect Ad Center advertising account, and click **On-Demand reports**.
- Select **Campaign Snapshot** in the report type and click **Request Report**.
- Share the Campaign Snapshot report with your API Partner to re-ingest the campaigns into your consolidated advertising account.

**Note:** When you pull the Campaign Snapshot report, the campaign structure information will generate in JSON format. Campaign Snapshot reports are limited to one request per day.  
  
If you need additional help OR if you are a Self-serve advertiser and want to consolidate multiple advertising accounts, please submit a [support request](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/contact) with the following information:

- The Account ID you want to copy campaigns FROM
- The Account ID you want to copy campaigns TO

  
To find your Account ID, log into Walmart Connect Ad Center and use the menu on the left side to select **Reports** then **Advertiser**. You can find your 6-digit Account ID in the URL on this page: [https://advertising.walmart.com/view/report/advertiser/\[ACCOUNT-ID](https://advertising.walmart.com/view/report/advertiser/%5bACCOUNT-ID)\].  

### Best practices and recommendations

- Review AND/OR edit the campaign setup details in the consolidated advertising account before scheduling the campaign
- Only enable the copied campaigns in the consolidated account after confirming the old account’s campaigns are paused to avoid self-competition.

## Locating your vendor ID for onboarding to Walmart Connect

Part of the onboarding process includes providing a vendor ID so your account can be linked to the Walmart Connect Ad Center. A Vendor ID is also known as a *Seller ID*, *Partner ID* or *Supplier Number*, depending on your account type. This article provides instructions for locating this ID in both Seller Center and Supplier One.

### Locating vendor IDs in Seller Center

Advertisers with a Seller Center account can navigate to [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/) and log in using your credentials. Your vendor ID is at the top of the page.

  
![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPRD&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000008vQAr)

Additionally, you can find it on your Company Info if you click the **gear icon** on the top right of the home page.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPRD&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000008vQ9e)

### Locating vendor IDs in Supplier One

Navigate to [Supplier One](https://supplierone.wal-mart.com/) and log in using your Retail Link credentials. From the landing page, click the person icon () labeled *Account* in the top right and select **Supplier profile**.  

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPRD&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000008vQKh)

On the Profile page, your *Supplier number* is listed with your company information.  
![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPRD&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000008vQA8)

## Frequently asked questions

### Why are some media solutions grayed out when I sign in?

If you have multiple accounts, make sure you have correctly selected the account that matches the platform you wish to access.

### Why are the Display and Shop Builder media solutions grayed out when I sign in as a Marketplace seller? Why am I unable to run Sponsored Brands or Sponsored Videos ads as a Marketplace seller?

Display, Shop Builder, Sponsored Brands and Sponsored Videos are only available to Marketplace sellers who are brand owners. [Follow the steps](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?channel=Search%20Brand%20Amplifier&language=en_US&article=000010394) to register as a brand owner so you can take advantage of all advertising and media solutions.

### Where can I find my Partner or Vendor ID?

Find your Partner or Vendor ID by logging into Seller Center or Supplier Center. The ID will be the number below or next your company name.

### How long does the onboarding process take after I submit a request form?

Generally, this process may take up to one week (five business days), depending on your account type and the information provided.

### How can I get help with onboarding?

If you need additional help with onboarding that isn’t covered in this guide, [create a case](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/contact?channel=Sponsored) with Advertiser Help.

### How will I be charged for my ad spend?

By default, for Marketplace sellers, ad spend will be deducted from your net sales on a bi-weekly basis. Marketplace sellers also have the option to add a credit card or a debit card as a primary or alternate payment method. For Walmart suppliers, invoices will be sent on a monthly basis to the billing contact(s) provided during onboarding.
