An item listing is the core of advertising on Walmart, so it's recommend to regularly review and optimize the content on your item listings. A quality item listing can help improve efficiency, increase discoverability and gain repeat customers.

## In this guide you will learn how to:

- Improve efficiency by creating detailed product descriptions and sourcing customer reviews
- Gain product visibility and drive more traffic to your listings
- Maximize returns and gain repeat business with quality content
