The Path to Conversion report helps showcase the customer’s journey from discovery to consideration and purchase along cross-channel media touchpoints to help supercharge media planning. It can help you understand the full funnel customer journey by media exposure on Walmart’s site and app and beyond.

## How to access

Cross-Channel Reports live in Walmart Connect Ad Center. Navigate to the icon in the upper right-hand corner to access all products. Select **Cross-Channel Reports** under *Cross-channel platforms*.

**![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP4r&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000A4ek3)**

Select **Path to Conversion** under *Customer analytics* on the Cross-Channel Reports landing page.

**![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP4r&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000A4ekc)**

To learn more, visit the [Cross-Channel Reports](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&article=000012454) guide and the [Access management](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&article=000012444) guide.

## Overview

The Path to Conversion report provides insights on the path to purchase across Sponsored Search, Onsite Display and Offsite Display and media, including experience touchpoints like add-to-carts and product detail page views. You can see monthly and quarterly reporting on media trends at the brand and category level and the top ten most common media journeys to purchase. The top 10,000 most common paths to purchase are available to download for deeper insights into customer behavior.

**![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP4r&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000A36cI)**

Walmart Connect’s Path to Conversion report can help you understand how media helps influence the customer shopping journey online, validating a full funnel strategy. Reporting can help you understand how different media channels work together to drive sales, while highlighting opportunities to optimize your approach across brands.

### Prerequisites

You must have spent on either Onsite or Offsite Display and have a minimum of 100,000 impressions at the advertiser organization level for a given month to receive reporting for the following month.

### Modules

The report features two modules: **Media trend** and **Media journey**.

- **Media trend:** Features ad exposure metrics that help you understand how Walmart Connect media influenced your customer journey across product detail pages, add-to-carts and purchases, by media solution. See the percent of product detail page views, add-to-carts and purchases exposed to Walmart Connect media. A dynamic chart features exposure trends over time and by media solution.

**![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP4r&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000A36cr)**

- **Media journey:** Features the top ten most common ad-exposed customer paths that led to a purchase, showcasing the customer journey across media touchpoints to conversion. The top 10,000 paths to purchase are available for download in a CSV.

**![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP4r&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000A36cS)**

## Best practices

- Use the **media channel filter** dropdown to see the ad-exposed metrics by media solution.
- Try different combinations of brand(s) and/or category(s) and timeframe (by quarters or month) to see how the trends shift over time.
- Download the CSV with your top 10,000 most common paths to purchase to better understand the customer journey across media solutions.

## Findings from recent studies

Below are key insights from beta advertisers that illustrate the role of different media solutions across the customer journey:

- 48% of the purchases, 41% of add-to-carts and 37% of product detail page views were exposed to Walmart Connect media within a 14-day lookback window for advertisers who qualified for Path to Conversion reporting in a limited beta.
- 97% of the time, an Offsite Display ad is served prior to a Sponsored Search ad when both Sponsored Search and Display media touchpoints are on the online path to purchase for advertisers who qualified for Path to Conversion reporting in a limited beta, helping to build consideration before purchase intent.
- 92% of the time, an Onsite Display ad is served prior to a Sponsored Search ad when both Sponsored Search and Display media touchpoints are on the online path to purchase for advertisers who qualified for Path to Conversion reporting in a limited beta, helping to build consideration before purchase intent.
- 17% of the time, Display ads were the only media touchpoint when they were on the online path to purchase for advertisers who qualified for Path to Conversion reporting in a limited beta.
- 82% of the time, Offsite Display ads were the first or middle media touchpoints when they were on the online path to purchase for advertisers who qualified for Path to Conversion reporting in a limited beta.

*These insights are for illustrative purposes only. Individual campaign and advertiser results will vary.*

*Sources: Walmart first-party data,Jan. 1, 2025–June 30, 2025. Path to Conversion report shows the touchpoints of the online customer journey within a 14-day lookback window across media (Sponsored Search, Onsite Display and Offsite Display and non-media (Product detail page views and add-to-carts) prior to customer’s purchase of advertisers’ brand products from Walmart’s digital properties. To qualify for a Path to Conversion report, advertisers must have both Sponsored Search and Display campaigns active in the prior month.*

## Frequently asked questions

### What is the lookback window?

14 days.

### What channels are included in the report? And which campaigns?

Sponsored Search, Onsite Display and Walmart DSP are included. The campaigns included in the report are listed under **View campaigns** at the top of the page.

### Can I see tactic or format level views in the media journey module?

Yes, but it’s only available for Sponsored Search at this time. Onsite Display and Offsite Display ad interactions are aggregated within a single block. CTV is included but not broken out in the report.

### How frequently is the report updated?

It’s updated monthly. You can expect reporting for a given calendar month about two weeks after the month ends.

### How is brand defined?

Brand mapping is based on the product detail page that was viewed, the item added to cart or the item purchased, depending on the type of conversion (product detail page, add-to-cart, purchase).

- **Display campaigns:** Brand is mapped from the item sets selected for measurement before the campaign begins. Multi-brand Display campaigns are not tied to purchases in the report since the path is tied to all interactions related to the same brand name.
- **Sponsored Search campaigns:** Brand is based on the items a customer clicks.
### Which sales are included in the report?

All traceable orders from Walmart’s site and app are included. Store purchases may be included in the future. 

### How do I access the report?

Navigate to the icon in the upper right-hand corner on Walmart Connect Ad Center to access all products. Select **Cross-Channel Reports** under *Cross-channel platforms*. From there, select **Path to Conversion** and **View report**. Visit the [Cross-Channel Reports](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?article=000012454&language=en_US) guide to learn more.
