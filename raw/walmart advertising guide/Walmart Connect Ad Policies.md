## Overview

Walmart Connect's Advertising Policies are designed to help maintain a positive advertising experience for all involved – our consumers, our advertisers and other publishers through our offsite offerings appear.  
  
To help ensure this positive experience, we've established some guidelines that apply to all advertising placements. However, our Policies are not intended as legal or professional advice. We encourage you to consult with your legal advisor if you have questions about the laws and regulations concerning your ads, as ultimately, it is the advertiser's responsibility to make sure its advertisements adhere to these Policies, as well as any applicable laws, rules, regulations, industry standards and website/platform Policies governing the websites/platforms in which the ads will appear. By placing the advertisements, you, as the advertiser, assume all liability for and arising from the advertisements. Walmart Connect will not be responsible for ensuring legal and regulatory compliance of your ads, though we may request that you submit documentation affirming such compliance. Throughout these Policies, we refer to advertisers alternately as "brands" and "you/your," and to ad placements on Walmart’s owned-and-operated properties as "onsite ads" and to ads that are not on Walmart owned-and-operated properties, e.g., Walmart DSP offsite ad placements, social media platform ad placements, as "offsite ads."  
  
Please note that although these Policies are intended to be a helpful resource, they are not exhaustive. These Policies highlight some key ad Policies that frequently re-occur, and all examples are for illustrative purposes only. Walmart Connect's Ad Policy Team may at any time reject or remove advertisements, revoke approval, suspend campaigns, require the revision of an advertisement or require pre-review prior to launch as a condition of approval in its sole discretion, including in the event that such advertisement (i) is found to be in violation of these Policies, or (ii) Walmart Connect believes that such advertisement may be damaging to its brand or reputation, or otherwise may present legal or regulatory risk. Walmart also reserves the right to suspend all or select ad campaigns in the aftermath of certain catastrophic events, such as natural disasters, mass casualty events or similar national or international incidents, or advertiser/category-specific events that pose reputational risk for Walmart. The duration of any such suspension and the affected ad placements may vary based on the nature and severity of the event. Any such situations will be actively monitored, and ads may be reactivated when Walmart Connect determines it is reasonable to do so.  
  
We also reserve the right to update these Policies at any time without prior notice, so please check this page often to ensure that you are always up to date on our latest Policy requirements.  
  
Use the links below to navigate to each topic addressed in our Policies. If you have questions or need additional information, please contact your Walmart Connect account support team.
