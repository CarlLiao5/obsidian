At Walmart Connect, we are committed to providing advertisers with measurement services that are designed to be valid, reliable and effective. We have received accreditation from the Media Rating Council (MRC) on our clicks and impressions metrics for Sponsored Search.

## Metrics accredited

MRC accreditation covers our clicks, click-through rate (CTR) and impressions metrics for item and video ads on Sponsored Search in desktop, mobile web and mobile in-app environments for Walmart.com and the Walmart US app. We utilize general invalid traffic filtration for these metrics.

**Note:** Item ad is also referred to as display ad by the MRC.

## Finding accredited metrics

## Accessing the Invalid Traffic report

Advertisers can access the Invalid Traffic report for each of their Sponsored Search campaigns in Walmart Connect Ad Center.

Log in to your Walmart Connect Ad Center account and select **Sponsored Search** as your media solution. On the left-hand navigation, scroll down to the *Reports* section and select **Advertiser**.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW0000002aQV&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009JSmD)

Under Report type, select **Campaign performance** and select a campaign. Once you’ve selected a specific campaign, under Report type, select **Invalid Traffic report**.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW0000002aQV&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009JSwm)
