Item recommendations

**DISCLAIMER**

*Campaign recommendations are derived from AI/machine learning models that generate predictions based on patterns in data. Walmart provides recommendations to assist advertisers in optimizing their campaigns but cannot guarantee any future campaign performance results. Review and validate all information provided before making decisions based on these recommendations.*

  
This article provides an overview of the item recommendations tool, such as how it works, accessing and applying item recommendations and best practices.

## About item recommendations

Item recommendations is a **Sponsored Products** tool that identifies unadvertised items with the potential to perform well in a Walmart Connect Sponsored Products campaign.

- Use the tool to identify items and easily add them to new or in-flight automatic campaigns, to help boost sales.
- Advertisers can download item recommendations as an On-demand report.

## How it works

The item recommendations tool scans an advertiser’s full Walmart product catalog for high-quality items that are not live in an in-flight campaign but have the potential to perform well. Items are recommended based on several factors, including, but not limited to, the items’ eligibility to be advertised, if the items are in stock, if the items are high quality and if the items have been previously purchased.  
  
Item recommendations are refreshed weekly on Wednesday afternoons, with data refreshed through the prior day (Tuesday).  

## Access item recommendations

You can access the item recommendations widget on the Sponsored Products Dashboard and Recommendations page. The widget displays a preview of the recommended items, which you can add to a new or in-flight automatic campaign. You can also request the full item recommendations on-demand report.  

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPDa&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AE3ix)

  
**Note:** Marketplace sellers can also see the average listing quality of the recommended items in the widget.  
  
Items that have been added to a new or in-flight campaign will be reflected and removed from the recommendation in the next weekly refresh.  

**Note:** If you do not have any item recommendations available, you will not see the widget on the dashboard.

## Directly apply item recommendations

From the item recommendations widget, you can easily add items to an eligible in-flight automatic campaign, or create a new automatic campaign. Eligible campaigns have automatic targeting, are currently live, and are not out of budget.

To add your recommended items to an in-flight campaign, select **Add items to an existing campaign**.  

![image.png](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPDa&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EM8Y00000DiD3E)

You can then add the items to any eligible in-flight automatic campaign. Campaigns with manual targeting, that are not currently live, or are out of budget cannot be selected.  

To add your recommended items to a new campaign, select **create new campaign**.  

The next step in creating your Sponsored Products campaign is setting a daily budget. The following selections will automatically apply to the new campaign:

- Targeting will be set to *Automatic.*
	- **Note:** This cannot be changed once the campaign goes live.
- Start date will be the date of campaign creation and will run indefinitely.
- No total budget.
	- **Note:** This cannot be changed once the campaign goes live.
- Suggested bids will be applied to recommended items.

  
Using bulk operations, your new campaign will be automatically created within four hours of submission. To check the status of the new campaign creation, go to the Bulk Operations page and view *Your uploads* for more details.

## Request an item recommendations On-demand report

Access the full report by clicking the Request report button in the widget on the dashboard.  

  

You will then be redirected to the **On-demand reports** page to download.

  
**Note:** The lookback window for unadvertised items is 30 days.  

## Best practices

- Check your item recommendations weekly to discover new items that have the potential to perform well in a Sponsored Product campaign.
- Use the item recommendations widget to easily add items to an in-flight automatic campaign or quickly create a new automatic campaign including your recommended items.
