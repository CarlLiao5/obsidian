## Search In-grid

**Provides:**

- Contextual visibility.
- Ads appear in search results and browse pages.

  
**Use to:**

- Promote relevant products.
- Protect your brand against competing products in top spots.
- Increase exposure by appearing in sponsored and organic in-grid results.

  
**Featured ad slots:**

- Ads within top search results may vary in number and position.
- Available for automatic and manual campaigns.

  

**Note:** To create more opportunities for advertisers to help grow their visibility, Walmart Connect is evolving toward a dynamic ad experience, where top Search placements may shift based on shopper context and performance signals. Customers may start to see different Search in-grid layouts. While the dynamic ad experience will become the standard for most customers, experiences may vary as we leverage different signals to surface ads that best align with each shopper’s journey.  

## My Items In-grid

**Provides:**  
Visibility for existing customers at a critical step of their Walmart shopping journey with a high-visibility placement as they review previously purchased items and finalize their cart.  
  
**Use to:**

- Influence customers to add items to their cart they’ve purchased in the past.
- Remind customers to add items they may be running low on since their last purchase with a *Bought x times* message.

  
**Featured ad slot:**

- Appears within a relevant category grid in the Reorder section of a customer’s My Items page.
- Clicking the **Add** button adds the item to the customer’s cart.
- Ads must be for an in-stock item the customer has previously purchased.
- Ads will appear before the organic results.
- Maximum 50% of the category grid will be ads.
- Available for Automatic Campaigns.

## Carousels

**Provides:**  
Increased visibility. Your ad appears in browse, category, curated shelf, bottom of search results, middle and bottom of product details through personalized modules: *Customers also viewed, Customers also considered, Customers also bought*.  
  
**Use to:**

- Promote relevant products.
- Maintain market share for top-selling products.

**Featured slots:**

- Product Carousels appear on Search Results, Browse pages and on Item pages. The maximum Sponsored Products per carousel is 20.
- 1 Sponsored Product Carousel per Search Results page.
- 2 Sponsored Product Carousels per page on item page.
- Ads also appear in Personalization Carousels on the product details page in the following modules: *Customers also viewed, Customers also considered* and *Customers also bought*. Maximum of 8 Sponsored Products per Personalization Carousel.
- More than 1 Personalized Carousel may appear on a page.
- Available for Automatic and Manual campaigns.

## Buy Box

**Provides:**  
Premium placement. High visibility for more traffic, reviews, and sales because it’s located on a product’s detail page.  
  
**Use to:**

- Become eligible for Sponsored Products.
- Get higher click through rates (CTR).
- Increase conversion rates.
- Send customers to your Walmart product or shelf page.

  
**Featured ad slots:**

- The Buy Box is located on the product detail page of a relevant or complementary product.
- Highly competitive placement since multiple sellers can offer the same product.
- One placement per page.
- Available for Automatic and Manual campaigns.

## Stock up

**Provides:**  
Increased visibility. Alert customers to forgotten or undiscovered items with the message, *Missing anything?* appearing above the items.  
  
**Use to:**
- Promote relevant products.
- Maintain market share for top-selling products.
**  
Featured ad slots:**
- Ads appear to customers as they are finalizing their carts.
- You ad serves based on whether the customer has grocery items in their cart of has purchased groceries in the past.
- No more than two ads serve per carousel.
- Stock up is only available for suppliers at this time.
![image example of stock up ad type](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP06&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EM4M0000043Nb0)

**Note:** We want to ensure that our advertisers understand the potential negative impact of clicking on your own ads. Clicking on your ads is considered a wasted click. Clicking on your ads can significantly impact your return on ad spend (ROAS) and compromise the effectiveness of your advertising efforts. Inform your employees on the importance of not searching for and clicking on your ads.
