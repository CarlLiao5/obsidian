Keyword recommendations

**DISCLAIMER**

*Campaign recommendations are derived from AI/machine learning models that generate predictions based on patterns in data. Walmart provides recommendations to assist advertisers in optimizing their campaigns but cannot guarantee any future campaign performance results. Review and validate all information provided before making decisions based on these recommendations.*

  
This guide provides an overview of keyword recommendations, including how they work, where to find them and how to use them.

## Keyword recommendations

Keyword recommendations automatically identify keywords with the potential to enhance item visibility and boost sales for live **Sponsored Products Manual campaigns**, based on the your in-flight campaign performance and organic search data.  
  
You can **save time** and **effort** by easily selecting new keywords that align with your campaign goals and strategies, and adding them to their in-flight campaigns to optimize performance.  
  

## How keyword recommendations work

Walmart’s algorithm analyzes the performance of your active campaigns and Walmart organic search data to surface the top new keyword opportunities that can help boost campaign performance.  
  
Keyword recommendations are available **seven days after the campaign launch** and **refreshed daily**.  

## Accessing keyword recommendations

See a preview of keyword recommendations on the Dashboard and Recommendations page. Directly apply keyword recommendations.

To add all your recommended keywords to active campaigns, select **Apply all**.  All keywords will be added using the platform’s suggested bids.   

**![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPEO&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AE8bX)**

After you apply all recommended keywords, a confirmation will appear.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPEO&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AE8bc)

If you’d like to review keywords before applying them, select **View 2 recommendations**.   

**![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPEO&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AE8cz)**

You can view the recommended keywords and apply them at the campaign or ad group level. 

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPEO&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AE8eq)

**N** **ote:** **Unique keywords** is the count of recommended keywords regardless of match type. For example, if a unique keyword is recommended using exact-, phrase- and broad-match types, that will count as one unique keyword and three total keywords. **Total keywords** is the count of recommend keywords including all match type combinations. For example, if a unique keyword is recommended using exact-, phrase-, and broad-match type that will count as one unique keyword and three total keywords. 

  
Access the full report by selecting **Request report** in the widget on the dashboard.  

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPEO&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AE8fZ)

  
You will be redirected to the [On-demand reports](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?channel=Sponsored%20Products&article=000009366) page to download.

Up to **20 keywords per ad group** are provided as recommendations in the report. Recommended keywords are mapped to **Manual** campaigns and ad groups, with suggested bids for each.  
  
You can use the bulk operations feature to simultaneously apply recommended keywords to multiple ad groups. Learn more about [Bulk operations](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?channel=Sponsored%20Products&article=000010023).  

## Frequently asked questions

### How often are the keyword recommendations refreshed?

The recommendations are refreshed daily and the lookback window is 30 days.

### How are keyword recommendations different from suggestions within ad group?

The suggestions within an ad group will include a list of up to 220 keywords to select from. These keywords are the options our platform provides based on relevancy to the items in your ad group. If there are live ad groups, these suggestions will also include performance-based keyword suggestions that will surface to the top of the list. You can leverage the suggested keywords to expand reach and continue to explore optimal keywords for your campaigns. Keyword recommendation on the other hand will only appear for ad groups that are live and have been running for at least 3 days. These recommendations are generated by analyzing the performance of the advertiser’s live Sponsored Products Automatic and Manual campaigns and organic search data.

### Why can I request Keyword Recommendations report only once?

The report is refreshed, updated and generated once daily. No new keyword recommendations will occur until the next day. Once generated, the report will be accessible via On-demand reports as many times as needed.

### How many keywords from Keyword Recommendation report should I use?

The keyword recommendation report provides up to 20 recommendations per ad group. While you may benefit by adding all the recommendations, we suggest reviewing the report and selecting new keywords that can help improve your campaign performance.

### I do not have any keyword recommendations listed in the widget, why?

If you don't see any keyword recommendations, it could be because your campaigns have not been live and running for more than 3 days, or all available keyword recommendations have already been added. Since keyword recommendations are refreshed daily, please keep checking the tool every day. Also, you may not see the keyword recommendation widget if none of the ad groups are live.

### How are keyword recommendations generated?

Our algorithm generates the recommendations by analyzing the performance of your live Sponsored Products Automatic and Manual campaigns and organic search data and automatically identifies new keyword opportunities that have the potential to enhance item visibility and boost sales.
