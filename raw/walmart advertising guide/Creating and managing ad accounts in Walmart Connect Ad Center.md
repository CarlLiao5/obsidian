This guide explains how account management in Walmart Connect Ad Center is set up, including how to access a consolidated view of your organizations and associated accounts, which helps streamline the account management process through self-serve tools.

### Creating a new account

Only super admins can create new advertiser accounts within their organization. A super admin is an advertiser user with organization-level access (we do not recommend granting this level of access to agencies or partners). Super admins can assign *admin*, *write* or *read* access to users across accounts within their organization. Each organization can have up to five super admins and a maximum of 50+ advertiser accounts.

If you’re not a super admin but need a new account created, you can view the super admins for an organization and contact them directly to request a new account in Walmart Connect Ad Center.

Click the person icon () labeled *Account* at the top right corner and select **Manage profile and account**.  

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPoR&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000008vZHI)

Navigate to *Manage your ad accounts* section on the Ad accounts page and select **Superadmins for this organization**.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPoR&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AHHIW)

You can also view your account admins under the Ad Account Admins column. If there are multiple account admins, you will be able to click the blue text.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPoR&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AHHJF)

A pop-up will appear with the full list of account admins.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPoR&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AHHJe)

You can choose to create a new advertiser account when:

- Onboarding a new brand or vendor under your organization
- Requiring separate billing or IO/PO structures for different business units
- Setting up distinct campaign structures (i.e., per brand or per channel)
- Reorganizing or deactivating old accounts due to brand/vendor changes
- Granting an agency restricted access

Each account can have a unique:

- Catalog mappings (brands, vendors or items)
- Managing entity (supplier, agency or partner)
- Billing entities (supplier or agency)
- Admins and users with designated access levels
- Advertising channels (Display, Sponsored Search, In-store)

### How to create a new account

To create a new advertiser account, as a super admin of an organization:

- Select **\+ Create ad account** on the Ad accounts page.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPoR&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AHHOo)

- Enter an advertiser account name for this new account.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPoR&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AHHQ1)

- Partner information is an *optional* field and the default setting will be to manage this account directly. If the new account will be managed by an API partner or agency, the form will prompt the super admin to submit a support ticket.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPoR&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AHHQG)

- Select the vendors or brands you would like to associate with this specific ad account.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPoR&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AHHQV)

- *Optional**:* Assign an account admin for this new account by providing their email ID. The account admin should be someone who will be managing the account closely and can assign additional users.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPoR&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AHHQu)

- Add the necessary billing information, including a main billing contact for this account.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPoR&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AHHRn)

- - **Note:** You can edit or add more billing info once the account is created. Marketplace sellers can do this in Billing Manager and Walmart suppliers can file a [support ticket](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/contact?language=en_US).
- Select **Create** once all the information has been entered.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPoR&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AHHSg)

Once you create the account, it will appear in your organization’s account list on the Ad accounts page.

**Notes:**

- Accounts automatically inherit organization-level users.
- Non-super admin users can only view the account details of the accounts they have access to.

### How to edit an existing account

Only super admins can update select account details directly from the Ad accounts page.

- Navigate to the *Manage your ad accounts* section on the Ad accounts page and find the account you would like to edit. Select the **three dots** under the *Actions* column and select **Edit Account**.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPoR&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AHHTt)

- Edit each section as needed by selecting **Edit** on the top right corner. Select **Save** to save your changes.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPoR&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AMM0v)

**Important:** You can add new brands or vendors under *Catalog mapping* but cannot remove existing ones. Only our support team can remove brands or vendors from an account. If you need to add any additional brands or vendors that you don't see available or need to remove any brands or vendors, please file a [support ticket](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/contact?language=en_US).
