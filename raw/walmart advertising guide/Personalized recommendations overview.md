Personalized recommendations overview

**DISCLAIMER**

*Campaign recommendations are derived from AI/machine learning models that generate predictions based on patterns in data. Walmart provides recommendations to assist advertisers in optimizing their campaigns but cannot guarantee any future campaign performance results. Review and validate all information provided before making decisions based on these recommendations.*

This guide provides an overview of what personalized recommendations are, what categories they are available for, how they work and how to learn more about them.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPDV&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW0000090xoB)

## About personalized recommendations

Personalized recommendations help you optimize campaign setup and maximize performance. Personalized recommendations are available for budget, bidding strategy, item and keyword management and can be found on the Dashboard and Recommendations page in Walmart Connect Ad Center.

## How personalized recommendations work

Walmart Connect’s platform analyzes your Sponsored Search campaign setup and performance to determine whether an optimization will be recommended to help improve your campaign performance. Not all campaigns will have a recommendation at any given time, since recommendations are only available when your campaign fits certain eligibility criteria. The platform will provide a personalized recommendation when we think it could help you better optimize your campaign performance.
