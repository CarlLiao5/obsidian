## Walmart Connect Amended IAB/AAAA Standard Terms and Conditions

These terms (the "Amended IAB Terms") amend the IAB/AAAA Standard Terms and Conditions for Interactive Advertising For Media Buys One Year or Less Version 3.0 (the "IAB Terms") and, when incorporated within an insertion order (the "IO") or when otherwise incorporated by reference, shall govern all purchases of media for Walmart Connect managed serve campaigns. These Amended IAB Terms, the applicable IO and any Joint Business Plan Agreement currently in place between parties comprise the "Agreement." Capitalized terms not otherwise defined below shall have the respective meanings set forth in the IAB Terms. To the extent any of the terms included in these Amended IAB Terms conflict with the IAB Terms, these terms shall govern. To the extent Advertiser has agreements with Media Company business units outside of Walmart Connect and any terms of those agreements conflict with this Agreement, the terms of this Agreement shall govern with respect to the subject matter of the applicable Insertion Order or Joint Business Plan Agreement. If Advertiser is entering into an IO directly (i.e., without an Agency), then the parties agree that the Amended IAB Terms applicable to that IO shall be amended so that all references to "Agency" shall be to "Advertiser," as applicable. To the extent an Advertiser executes any IOs through its Agency, it is understood that, unless otherwise explicitly agreed in such IO between Media Company and Agency that those Agency terms override these Amended IAB Terms, these Amended IAB Terms shall apply.  
  
Advertiser acknowledges and agrees that the purchase of Deliverables via any self-serve platforms shall be governed by separate terms accepted by Advertiser or its Agency as made available through such platform.  
  
Certain Walmart Connect advertising products have additional terms that apply to them, as set forth in the [Walmart Connect Product Terms](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?article=000010103) (the "Product Terms"), which shall apply to the applicable IO only to the extent those products are listed on this IO.

1. The following shall be added as a new defined term: "AXT Program" is a Media Company program where Media Company targets its first party audience or lookalike audiences (or suppresses targeting to such audiences) in the delivery of Ads offsite (outside of Media Company Properties) on behalf of Advertiser (i.e., through the purchase of advertising inventory from a third-party demand-side platform vendor like the Walmart DSP, or social media or other third party platform vendors, to serve advertising on Network Properties).
2. The following shall be added as a new defined term: "CPD Deliverable" means Deliverables sold on a cost per day basis.
3. The definition of "Media Company Properties" is deleted in full and replaced with the following: "Media Company Properties" are websites, mobile applications, devices, screens (including in-store checkout display screens and/or TV walls), or any other digital platforms, communications online point of presence and/or Walmart Connect Advertising products set forth in the Walmart Connect Product Terms specified on an IO that are owned, operated, or controlled by Media Company or any Media Company Affiliates.
4. The definition of "Policies" is deleted in full and replaced with the following: "Policies" means advertising criteria or specifications made conspicuously available, including content limitations, technical specifications, privacy policies, including Walmart’s Privacy Policy available at [https://corporate.walmart.com/privacy-security/walmart-privacy-policy](https://corporate.walmart.com/privacy-security/walmart-privacy-policy), user experience policies, policies regarding consistency with Media Company's public image, community standards regarding obscenity or indecency (taking into consideration the portion(s) of the Site on which the Ads are to appear), other editorial or advertising policies, all policies applicable to AXT Program platforms and Network Properties, and Advertising Materials due dates, including, without limitation, Walmart Connect's Ad Policy, available at [https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?channel=Display&article=000009813](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?channel=Display&article=000009813), and such other applicable requirements and policies accessible through the Walmart Connect Advertising Help Center at [https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/).
