Open a case with Advertiser Help

  
  
**Note:** Sponsored Search support via cases is for Self-Serve advertisers and Walmart Platform Partners only. If you are a Managed Serve advertiser looking for Sponsored Search support, please reach out to your Walmart Connect Sales representative for assistance.

## Contacting Advertiser Help

If you’re an advertiser experiencing issues with your account or campaign and need assistance, you can request help from our Advertiser Help team through the Advertising Help portal.  

### Creating a new case

1. Click on the [Contact Support](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/contact) link from the menu bar at the top of the Advertising Help portal, or click the **Contact Support** button at the bottom of most pages.

  
  
![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPFv&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000ADcCk)

1. Next, you’ll need to either login as a Marketplace seller, a Walmart supplier (DSV or Owned), Ad Agency/API Ad Partner or continue as a guest.

Learn more about the difference between Marketplace sellers and Walmart suppliers [here](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/faqs?articlenumber=000008396&article=Miscellaneous).

- When logging in as a **Marketplace (3P) Seller**, please enter the same credentials as you would when logging into Seller Center.
- When logging is as a **Supplier (DSV/Owned)**, please enter the same credentials as you would when logging into Supplier Center or Retail Link.
	- If you do not have your Supplier Center credentials and need to get them, [contact Partner Support](https://supplierhelp.walmart.com/s/contact). Choose **Password or Other Access Issue**, and then **Something not listed** to open a case without logging in. Enter your request in the text box and provide your contact information.
		- If you prefer to use your Retail Link email credentials and have trouble using them or need to get them, please contact the help desk at **(479) 273-8888** and clearly state "Supplier Services." You’ll be connected to a support agent that will be able to assist you.
- When logging in as an **Ad Agency/API Ad Partner**, please enter the credentials provided by WMC. If you do not have credentials provided by WMC, submit a ticket as a guest and request login credentials by using the **Help Site login** option.
- Select the **Continue as Guest** option if you do not have login credentials for Seller Center or Supplier Center/Retail Link. Choose the issue you’d like assistance with and then enter your request in the text box and provide your contact information.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPFv&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000ADcEW)

1. After you have logged in, you’ll see a set of categories. Select the one that best describes your issue.
![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPFv&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000ADcFP)
1. After you choose the category, a list of common issues will appear. Choose the combination of Category/Issue that best describes your problem, so it can be routed to the team that can best assist you.

  
![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPFv&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000ADcGS)  

1. Depending on the Category/Issue selected, you may be provided with suggested guides and/or related FAQ. Our guides and FAQ are updated regularly to answer common questions, so you may find your solution here without needing to open a case. If you don't find the solution for your issue, select **Open a case via Email**.

  
![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPFv&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000ADcI9)

1. Before submitting your case to the Advertiser Help team, you’ll need to fill out some information.

**Note:** Some issue types may require additional information in order for our Help team to best assist you.

- **Subject:** The Subject field is pre-filled with the issue you selected. However, feel free to modify it if desired.
- **Advertiser Account Name:** Please enter your account name as shown in Walmart Connect Ad Center. Learn more about how to [find your account name](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&channel=Sponsored&article=000009632).
- **Advertising Demand Channel**
	- **Self-Serve:** Please select this option if you manage your account from Walmart Connect Ad Center.
		- **API:** Please select this option if you manage your account from a Walmart Advertising Partner platform. This option should only be used by our Walmart Advertising Partners.
- **Description field:** Here you’ll be able to enter a detailed description of your issue. Depending on the issue you selected, we will suggest information for you to provide so that we can assist you as quickly as possible. Such as:
	- Campaign name and ID
		- Report type (if applicable)
		- Specific items and/or keywords causing an issue (if applicable)
		- Error message received and steps to reproduce
		- Tags being used (Display only)
		- Audience names and IDs (Display only)

Fill out the required information in the fields provided and select **Open Case**.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPFv&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000ADcK0)

  
After you select **Open Case**, you’ll be given the opportunity to upload files related to your issue. Once you upload your files, or if you do not have any files to upload, select **Go to Case**.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPFv&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AEHtL)

  
After opening your case, you'll see a confirmation at the top of the page and be redirected to the case details page, where you can see your Case Number and check for updates.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPFv&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AEHtV)

  
An Advertiser Help agent will reply to your case via email. Please note that cases are handled in the order that they are received, and the Advertiser Help team strives to resolve your issues as quickly as possible. Please ensure that you choose the most accurate Category and Issues, so that your cases are sent to the correct teams to avoid unnecessary rerouting.  

## My cases

If you have already created a case and want to check the progress, select [My cases](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/activity) from the menu at the top of the Advertising Help portal. You can select the case number to view your original request and to view replies from the Advertiser Help team.  

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPFv&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000AEHtp)
