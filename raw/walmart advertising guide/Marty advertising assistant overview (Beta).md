Marty advertising assistant overview (Beta) ▶️

**DISCLAIMER**

*Marty advertising assistant is currently in beta and only available to select advertisers. You may not see this option in Walmart Connect Ad Center.*

This article provides an overview of Marty advertising assistant for Sponsored Search in Walmart Connect Ad Center, including core capabilities and best practices.

![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jZHSRmsAZTY)

## Overview

Managing an ad campaign can often mean navigating dozens of decisions, from budget allocations and bidding strategies to item selection and optimization. Advertisers often spend valuable time combing through performance reports, toggling between dashboards and searching for answers they need in the moment. **We’re changing that.**

**Marty advertising assistant is powered by AI** and designed to reduce friction and accelerate decision-making for advertisers. In real time, it can:

- Answer campaign-specific questions
- Surface recommendations
- Deliver enhanced reporting and insights
- Launch new campaigns on command

This is delivered through an intuitive chat interface, making campaign management **more conversational, accessible and proactive than ever before.**

Marty advertising assistant is currently available in beta for select advertisers. You may not see this option in Walmart Connect Ad Center.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP2g&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009ag3y)

## Benefits

- **Get started faster than ever:** With Marty advertising assistant, getting things done can be as simple as asking a question. It can assist with campaign setup and troubleshoot questions with quick, clear responses so that you can launch and optimize campaigns faster.
- **Optimize for sales growth:** Marty advertising assistant helps streamline workflows, summarize performance trends and keep your growing set of items well optimized. Every time you log in, you receive timely alerts and recommendations to help increase exposure and grow sales.
- **Know where to start and what to prioritize:** Marty advertising assistant helps you get a quick snapshot of your account and campaign performance and prioritizes recommendations that are likely to have the maximum impact.
- **Generate enhanced reporting and insights:** Marty advertising assistant can provide new metrics and reporting capabilities directly within Walmart Connect Ad Center, including impression share of voice, keyword impression share, category benchmarking and more. These can help you better understand the impact of your Sponsored Search investment and dive deeper into brand and category performance.

## Getting started with Marty advertising assistant

Login to [Walmart Connect Ad Center](https://advertising.walmart.com/). Marty advertising assistant will appear as a new chat window on the right side of the page.

Engage with Marty advertising assistant by typing in your own questions or choosing an available prompt.

Provide feedback using the thumbs up or the thumbs down icon.

## How to engage with Marty advertising assistant

- **Help content****:** Ask Marty advertising assistant to explain things or troubleshoot with you.
- **Alerts and recommendations****:** Ask Marty advertising assistant about how you can improve your campaign settings, including budgets, bids, items and keywords.
- **Create and optimize campaigns****:** Ask Marty advertising assistant to help you create a new Sponsored Products campaign or optimize an existing one.
- **Performance and trends****:** Ask Marty advertising assistant to report on key metrics and how they have changed over time.
- **Enhanced reporting and insights****:** Ask Marty advertising assistant about your impression share of voice, or how your performance stacks up against your industry.

## Help content

Marty advertising assistant is integrated with Advertising Help and can answer your questions about any Sponsored Search help content topic.

You’ll also get helpful source links that direct you straight to the related Advertising Help guides with more details.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP2g&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009ag3Z)

### Example questions Marty advertising assistant can help with

- What is the difference between Sponsored Products and Sponsored Brands?
- What does automatic targeting mean?
- What are the benefits of Dynamic bidding?
- Can I pay for my advertising with credit card?

## Alerts and recommendations

Marty advertising assistant is integrated with our Sponsored Search [personalized recommendations](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&article=000012410) and alerts and can answer your questions and provide recommendations for billing, budgets, bids, items and keywords.

You can also review your recommendation details and apply recommendations in just a couple of clicks via the chat window.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP2g&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009ag7M)

### Available alerts and recommendations

- **Billing:** Receive account spend limit and outstanding invoice alerts.
- **Budget:** Get guidance on the optimal daily and/or total budgets to help ensure you do not miss out on potential sales.
- **Bidding:** View more optimal bidding strategy, target bids and budget settings that could drive higher sales.
- **Items:** Consider recommended high-quality items that are not live in an in-flight campaign but have the potential to perform well.
- **Keywords:** View keywords with the potential to enhance item visibility and boost sales based on your in-flight campaign performance and organic search data

### Example questions Marty advertising assistant can help with

- How many outstanding invoices do I have? (for Walmart Marketplace sellers)
- Which of my campaigns are out-of-budget?
- Do you have any keyword suggestions?

**Note:** Campaign recommendations are derived from AI/ML model predictions. Review and validate all information provided before applying recommendations. Walmart cannot guarantee any future campaign performance results.

## Create and optimize campaigns

Marty advertising assistant can help simplify the Sponsored Products automatic campaign creation and optimization processes via a simplified chat experience.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP2g&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009ag8F)

### Campaign creation

Create new Sponsored Products automatic targeting campaigns directly via Marty advertising assistant. Most advertisers only need to enter a daily budget and Marty advertising assistant can take care of the rest or can take you to the full campaign creation experience. To access all campaign creation options, select **Save and review**. This will take you to the campaign edit page where you can view and/or make any changes.

### Campaign optimization

Ask Marty advertising assistant to help you optimize your Sponsored Products campaigns by making edits directly via chat. You can ask Marty advertising assistant to help you edit:

- Campaign name
- Expanded targeting (including Brand term targeting and Complementary targeting)
- Daily and total budget
- Start and end date
- Bidding strategy

In addition, you can make any other edits on the campaign edit page. To access the campaign edit page, search for your campaign on the All Campaigns page and select the campaign name.

### Example questions Marty advertising assistant can help with

- Can you help me create a new campaign?
- Which of my campaigns are under-spending?
- Update my daily budget for *Example campaign name 1* to $500
- Enable complementary targeting for *Example campaign name 2*

## Performance trends

Marty advertising assistant proactively provides performance snapshots and can respond to multi-dimensional ad hoc performance and reporting questions.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP2g&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009agAG)

### Performance snapshots

If you are actively advertising, Marty advertising assistant will proactively share an overview of your account performance when you log into Walmart Connect Ad Center.

### Ad hoc analyses

You can ask Marty advertising assistant multi-dimensional questions about your advertising performance across all levels of your account (e.g., campaign, item, keyword, etc.).

### Example questions Marty advertising assistant can help with

- What were my top performing campaigns last month?
- Which of my campaigns generated over $100 in sales yesterday?

## Enhanced reporting and insights

Access brand new metrics and insights, with new reports available only through Marty advertising assistant in Walmart Connect Ad Center.

View the [Marty advertising assistant enhanced reporting and insights guide](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&article=000012452) for more information.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP2g&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009agHC)

- **Momentum reports:** Understand the impact of shifts in your ad spend on key metrics
	- Change Analysis Report
- **Strategic intelligence reports:** Understand how your investment stacks up against your industry
	- Account Impression Share of Voice Report
		- Account Keyword Impression Share of Voice Report
		- Category Benchmark Opportunity Reports

### Example questions Marty advertising assistant can help with

- How has my increased CPC bid impacted my ROAS?
- What is my share of voice for my top three categories?
- Which of my keywords are contributing the most to my share of voice?
- How does my ROAS compare to others in my industry for my top category?

## Additional Marty advertising assistant functionality

Marty advertising assistant also supports the following functionality in Walmart Connect Ad Center:

- Use the **thumbs up** or **thumbs down** icon after each chat to provide feedback on your experience.
- Use the **folder** icon to access enhanced reporting and insights once Marty advertising assistant has generated them.
- Use the **two-arrow** icon to move between the standard chat window and full screen.
- Use the **minimize (–)** icon to close the Marty advertising assistant window.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oP2g&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009agJI)

You can share your feedback by using the thumbs up or the thumbs down icon after each chat. If your issue persists, please reach out to your Walmart Connect representative.
