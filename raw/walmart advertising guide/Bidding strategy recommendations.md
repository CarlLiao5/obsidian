Bidding strategy recommendations

**DISCLAIMER**

*Campaign recommendations are derived from AI/machine learning models that generate predictions based on patterns in data. Walmart provides recommendations to assist advertisers in optimizing their campaigns but cannot guarantee any future campaign performance results. Review and validate all information provided before making decisions based on these recommendations.*

*Target ROAS bidding is currently only available to advertisers who have previously used this feature. Access for new advertisers is temporarily paused and will expand to additional advertisers in the coming months.*

This guide provides an overview of what bidding strategy recommendations are, how they work, how to use them and best practices.

## Bidding strategy recommendations

Bidding strategy recommendations identify Sponsored Products campaigns that could perform better with different bidding strategy settings. Bidding strategy recommendations include three types of Target ROAS recommendations as well as Suggested Bids.

## Target ROAS recommendations

### About Target ROAS recommendations

The three types of Target ROAS bidding strategy recommendations are:

1. **Switch to Target ROAS****:** These recommendations identify when a campaign using fixed or dynamic bidding could potentially drive higher sales if switched to Target ROAS.
2. **Optimize your ROAS target****:** These recommendations identify when a campaign using Target ROAS bidding could potentially drive higher sales if the ROAS target is updated and budget settings are updated.
3. **Target ROAS Learning paused alerts****:** These alerts notify you when a Target ROAS campaign cannot successfully exit the learning phase and requires review.
4. **Target ROAS performance alerts:** These alerts highlight when item-level changes may require a target ROAS adjustment to maintain performance.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPGZ&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW0000090xqW)

Learn more about [Target ROAS bidding strategies](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&article=000012446).

### How Target ROAS recommendations work

Our platform evaluates your Sponsored Products automatic campaigns to determine if there are more optimal bidding strategies, ROAS targets and budget settings that could help drive higher sales.

Target ROAS recommendations are refreshed daily, with data refreshed through the prior day. As a reminder, campaigns using Target ROAS bidding go through a learning phase. The learning period lasts for 14 – 30 days. Reducing the campaign budget, increasing the ROAS target or removing items may restart the learning phase.

### How to use Target ROAS recommendations

Target ROAS recommendations can be found in the Sponsored Search Dashboard and Recommendations page widgets, on the Campaign edit page and on the All Campaigns page for eligible campaigns. 

### Widgets

When you have a campaign that is eligible for a Target ROAS recommendation, a widget will appear on the Sponsored Search Dashboard and Recommendations page.

There are separate widgets for each of the three types of Target ROAS recommendations:

If you do not see any bidding strategy recommendation widgets on the Dashboard, that means you do not have any available recommendations.

From the **Switch to Target ROAS** bidding strategy recommendation widget, you can easily switch the recommended campaigns to Target ROAS bidding and automatically apply the ROAS target and budget recommendations that we predict will help drive more optimal sales selecting **Apply all**.

In the widget, you can view a summary of the recommendations, including:

- **Number of campaigns:** the number of campaigns with an eligible recommendation
- **Recommended ROAS target for all campaigns:** the range (min - max) of recommended ROAS targets across all eligible campaigns
- **Combined recommended daily budget increase:** the sum of the recommended daily budget increases across all eligible campaigns
- **Combined potential increase in sales (per week):** the sum of the median potential incremental sales we predict your eligible campaigns can generate if you apply all recommendations following the learning phase

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPGZ&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000008a1GY)

If you would like to review the details of the eligible campaigns and their bidding strategy recommendations, select **View recommendations** and a pop-up will appear.

Within the pop-up, you can see more information for each campaign, including:

- **Current daily budget:** the current daily budget you have set for the campaign
- **Current bidding strategy:** the current bidding strategy you have set for the campaign, either fixed or dynamic
- **Potential sales increase/week:** the range (min–max) of the potential incremental sales we predict your campaign can generate if you apply our recommendation following the learning phase
- **Suggested daily budget:** the recommended daily budget to set for the campaign to help maximize sales at the suggested ROAS target
- **Suggested ROAS target:** the recommended ROAS target to set for the campaign to help maximize sales at the suggested daily budget

You can take several actions:

- Apply all bidding strategy recommendations in bulk by selecting **Apply all.**
- Apply bidding strategy recommendations for each campaign individually by selecting **Apply.**
- Update budgets manually for each campaign in the Current daily budget column.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPGZ&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000008a1M7)

From the **Optimize your ROAS target** bidding strategy recommendation widget, you can easily optimize your campaigns by applying the ROAS target recommendations that we predict will help drive more optimal sales by selecting **Apply all.**

In the widget, you can view a summary of the recommendations, including:

- **Number of campaigns**: The number of campaigns with an eligible recommendation
- **Recommended ROAS target for all campaigns:** The range (min – max) of recommended ROAS targets across all eligible campaigns
- **Combined recommended daily budget increase:** The sum of the recommended daily budget increases across all eligible campaigns
- **Combined potential increase in sales (per week):** The sum of the media potential incremental sales we predict your eligible campaigns can generate if you apply all recommendations following the learning period

If you would like to review the details of the eligible campaigns and their bidding strategy recommendations, select **View recommendations** and a pop-up will appear.

Within the pop-up, you can see more information for each campaign, including:

- **Current daily budget:** the current daily budget you have set for the campaign
- **Current ROAS target:** the current ROAS target you have set for the campaign
- **Potential sales increase/week:** the range (min–max) of the potential incremental sales we predict your campaign can generate if you apply our recommendation following the learning period
- **Suggested daily budget:** the recommended daily budget to set for the campaign to help maximize sales at the suggested ROAS target
- **Suggested ROAS target:** the recommended ROAS target to set for the campaign to help maximize sales

You can take several actions:

- Apply all bidding strategy recommendations in bulk by selecting **Apply all**

- **Number of campaigns:** the number of campaigns with an eligible recommendation

- Switch your bidding strategy to dynamic bidding\* or
- Add additional items to your campaign that have listing quality to restart learning

### Campaign edit page

**Switch to Target ROAS** and **Optimize your ROAS target** bidding strategy recommendations are also available in the Campaign bidding strategy section of the Campaign edit page for eligible campaigns. Select **Apply** to automatically apply the recommendation to your campaign.

You can find the **Switch to Target ROAS** bidding strategy recommendation at the top of the Campaign bidding strategy section of the Campaign edit page for an eligible campaign. Select **Apply** to automatically switch your bidding strategy to Target ROAS, add the recommended ROAS target and update your budget to the recommended value.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPGZ&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000008a1Th)

You may see **Target ROAS performance alerts** in the Campaign bidding strategy section of the Campaign edit page when item-level changes are impacting your campaigns. These alerts appear when a top-selling item status (enabled/disable), Out-of-stock issues, Buy Box win rate changes and Unit price changes occurs. Select **Download File with Affected Items** to see more details. Based on the changes surfaced, consider resolving item issues or updating your ROAS goal to better align campaign performance with current item conditions. 

You can also find the **Optimize your ROAS target** bidding strategy recommendation in the Campaign bidding strategy section of the Campaign edit page for an eligible campaign, under your current ROAS target. Select **Apply** to automatically update your ROAS target and budget to the recommended values.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPGZ&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000008a1Y3)

### Best practices

- Check for new bidding strategy recommendations regularly when optimizing your campaigns.
- Use the bidding strategy recommendations to easily apply recommendations and automatically update your bidding strategy, bidding target and budget settings.
- Use Target ROAS performance alerts to identify item-level changes early, update affected items and consider recommended target ROAS adjustments to support stronger campaign performance.

## Suggested Bids

Suggested Bids are automated bid-price recommendations provided to enhance the campaign management process by providing bid suggestions based on winning bids on similar ads and delivering more relevant impressions.  
  
Suggested Bids help minimize bidding guesswork and can increase the chance of your ad being served. This feature provides more granular automated bid-price recommendations based on winning bids for similar ads. These automated bid-price recommendations are offered at the product type level and at the keyword level for broad-, phrase- and exact-match keywords across all demand channels. With Suggested Bids, you can gain competitive bid insights and compare your current bid to be more successful on our platform.  
  
If you are unsure of what bid to start with for your campaign(s), we recommend using the suggested bid. This will aid you in delivering impressions compared to ads similar to yours.  

### How to get started

The Suggested Bids feature is available to all Sponsored Brands, Sponsored Products and Sponsored Videos campaigns. Use the steps below to locate Suggested Bids in Walmart Connect Ad Center.

### For automatic campaigns

For automatic Sponsored Products campaigns, Suggested Bids appear next to each item in the campaign in the Campaign creation and Campaign edit pages.

![image.png](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oPGZ&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EM8Y000004QPuO)

### For manual campaigns

For manual Sponsored Products campaigns, Suggested Bids appears in the Keywords section of the Campaign creation and Campaign edit pages.  

## Frequently asked questions

### How do advertisers determine their starting bid?

Our bid suggestion feature helps advertisers determine a starting bid for their SKUs and keywords.

### How often are suggested bids updated?

Bid suggestions are updated daily for each campaign.

### What suggested bids are offered for automated campaigns vs. manual campaigns?

We provide product type level suggestions for Automatic campaigns and keyword level suggestions for Manual campaigns.

### Are suggested bids different at placement levels?

No, there is one suggested bid for all placements.

### Does each keyword match type have different bid suggestions?

Yes, this feature offers different bid suggestions for the various match types, ie., broad-, phrase- and exact-match keywords across all demand channels.

### When should advertisers use suggested bids?

You can use suggested bids when you're looking for additional insight, including:

- **A comparison point:** If you already know what your average Cost per Click (CPC), the Suggested Bid can provide a helpful comparison point.
- **Competitive insights:** The suggested bid can give you helpful insight into what the competition is bidding on and provide a good place to start.

### What is the suggested bid for my items?

We suggest starting with the minimum bid amounts. If your item is not performing, we recommend gradually increasing your bids until you see improvement. We also recommend using our Automated Rules tool which allows you to set up automatic notifications, bids and budget actions without being in the platform. Learn more about Automated Rules [here](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?article=000009792).
