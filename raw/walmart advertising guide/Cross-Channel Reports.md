Cross-Channel Reports is a platform in Walmart Connect Ad Center that consolidates performance reporting and tools across your total Walmart Connect investment.

## Overview

The reports featured in Cross-Channel Reports allows you to see how various media solutions help drive sales and helps you make more strategic decisions around your media mix.

**Important:** Cross-Channel Reports represent all the campaigns within your [organization](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&article=000009334). Campaigns or specific advertiser accounts within your organization cannot be added or removed for individual reporting purposes.

## How to access

Navigate to the icon in the upper right-hand corner on Walmart Connect Ad Center to access all products. Select **Cross-Channel Reports** under *Cross-channel platforms*.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oOtP&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000A4egB)

On the landing page, you can select the report type you want to access.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oOtP&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW00000A4eh0)

**Important:**If you cannot access Cross-Channel Reports, please contact your super admin or org admin. Cross-Channel Reports provide reporting at an organization level and only org level admins can view Cross-Channel Reports by default. Your super admin or org admin can grant additional users access. Please note that only organization level users can be granted access. For more information on how to grant access, visit the [Access management](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?channel=Sponsored%20Products&language=en_US&article=000012444) guide.

## Available reports

The Cross-Channel Reports platform features a range of new reports and tools available to Walmart Connect advertisers. The Cross-Channel Reports platform is available to all advertisers, but not all reports will be available. Unavailable reports will be grayed out with *Not eligible* messaging.

**Note:** If you work with an account team at Walmart Connect, reach out to them to learn more about these reports. If you do not work with an account team, more information will be shared as these reports become more widely available.
