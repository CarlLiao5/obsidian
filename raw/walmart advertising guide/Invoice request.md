## How to access invoices

Invoices are issued between the 10th and 25th of every month and cover the previous month’s ad spend.  
  
You can access copies of invoices on Walmart’s accounts-payable portal, [High Radius](https://walmart.highradius.com/). After logging in, follow these steps:

- To view a Sales Invoice PDF copy, navigate to **EIPP**.
- Select either the **OPEN BILLS** (unpaid claims) or **CLOSED BILLS** (paid claims) tab.
- Select **View** under the *Sales Invoice PDF* column to review. This will show the current month's invoices. If you want to view previous invoices, please use the advance search functionality and mention the 10-digit customer number in the *Customer Number* column.

![](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka0KW000000oOu3&feoid=00N6100000Hxa5T&refid=0EMKW000009FigP)

You can search for a specific bill or credit balance subledger movement from these tabs by using the **Advanced Search** option on the top right of the screen. The 6000 or 7000 series must be placed in the Invoice or Document Number fields.

For more information about High Radius and login instructions, refer to the [High Radius EIPP guide.](https://gecrm.my.salesforce.com/sfc/p/61000000ZKTc/a/KW00000035Wu/fipXq0.DxaGUTQDmAng.WljVOu1YlcUWPixRkXt5PZM) If you’re unable to locate an invoice in High Radius, please [create a support ticket](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/contact?channel=Display&language=en_US). In the body of the ticket, please indicate the month the invoice will cover. Please allow one to two business days for a reply.
