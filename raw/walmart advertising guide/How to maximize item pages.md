Today's consumers are research obsessed and seek out helpful product descriptions, images, reviews, and fair pricing. You only have a few seconds to convince a consumer to purchase your product.  
  
If your product detail page does not provide the information the consumer is looking for, they'll move onto the next. This is why your product detail page is the single most important part of your strategy and why you must maximize the strength of your product pages to increase relevancy and sales.  

## Maximize and optimize your product detail page

Your ads will drive customers directly to your product detail page, so you want to make sure customers are confident to buy your products.  
  
**Your product pages should have the following:**

- Detailed product description
- High quality images of the product, including color SKUs or variations and views from different angles
- Ratings and reviews
- Competitive pricing
- Profitable assortment
- Variant products available

**Optimize product pages for relevancy:**

- Regularly optimize product titles, descriptions, images and key features
- Ensure relevant keywords are woven into your product’s description
- Confirm product categorization so that items serve appropriately, making them easy to find organically
- Use Keyword Performance and Item Keyword Reports to identify keywords that are driving conversions and higher click volume and add to item pages
