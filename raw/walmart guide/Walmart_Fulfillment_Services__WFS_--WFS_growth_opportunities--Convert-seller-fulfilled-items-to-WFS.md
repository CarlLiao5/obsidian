Convert seller-fulfilled items to WFS

Overview

When you sell on Walmart Marketplace, you’ll see a few fulfillment options:

- **Seller fulfilled:** Items that you manage, pack and ship to customers.
- **Walmart fulfilled (WFS):** Items that Walmart stores, picks, packs and ships to customers on your behalf.
- **Seller fulfilled (WFS eligible)**: Items that can be fulfilled by either you or Walmart. These items aren't currently fulfilled by Walmart, but will be eligible once there's inventory at a fulfillment center.

## What to expect

You can convert almost any item you currently fulfill on your own to Walmart-fulfilled. Some exceptions apply, though, so make sure to check [WFS product requirements](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/walmart-fulfillment-services-wfs-overview?locale=en-US#product_requirements) and [WFS prohibited products policy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/wfs-prohibited-products-policy).

When you use WFS to fulfill items, you’ll keep the same information from your seller-fulfilled listing. However, we ask for extra information to safely store and handle your items. Here’s some, but not all, information you’ll need to convert an item:

- State restrictions
- Country of origin
- Dimensions and weight of the item, including packaging
- Hazardous material details, such as battery type and [Safety Data Sheet](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-compliance-safety-data-sheets?locale=en-US)

After submitting an item, we’ll review the item. If there are no errors, your item will be automatically published. If your item has hazardous materials, it will be held for up to 3 business days for a [compliance review](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup?locale=en-US).

Pro tip

Not sure which items to convert? Go to [**Success Hub**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/success-hub) and select _Fulfill with WFS_ to view recommended items and potential sales increase.

## Convert a single item

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/items-and-inventory/manage-items) and select the item you want to convert to Walmart-fulfilled. Next, scroll to the end of the row and select the 3 dots. Choose **Fulfill with WFS**.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltbdc7be93f1e7f065/64ff4f14103154000dae68a9/fulfill_with_WFS_dropdown.jpeg?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 2 – Item details and attributes

New sections and fields will appear for your Walmart-fulfilled item, such as [compliance](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup) and [dimensions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-trade-item-configuration). Once all the required fields are complete, select _Submit_. Then [track the status](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20growth%20opportunities/Convert-seller-fulfilled-items-to-WFS#trackstatus) to check for errors and compliance reviews.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt2c788651c773d636/66b25344e80192b9e8ddd251/9923_Gif_1.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

Make sure your item dimensions are accurate. We measure items during receiving, and mismatched dimensions may affect your [WFS fees](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-fees?locale=en-US). We do not provide images of our measurements, but you can download the [Storage report](https://seller.walmart.com/wfsLite/reports) to compare dimensions.

## Convert multiple items

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/items-and-inventory/manage-items) and select all items you want to convert to Walmart-fulfilled. You can also filter by fulfillment type to find all seller-fulfilled items. In the actions bar, select _Download spreadsheet_ and then _Convert to WFS_.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt722f18b9a5a56994/68f7aa9d4f215c5d71734fe6/bulk_select_convert.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 2 – Download template

Go to the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed?feedsFor=downloads) page. From the **Downloads** tab, download the WFS convert template you just created. Fill in the blank fields with required information about your items. For best practices and tips on filling out the template, view [_Item template for WFS._](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-item-spec-sheet-setup)

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt38b8789104ba87bb/66fd7c896c7a3553dfe36ef4/activity_feed_downloads_tab.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

Your items can’t be published if required or conditionally required fields are missing. Changing the data in the first 6 rows, including tab names, may also lead to data errors. Go to [_Troubleshoot WFS item setup errors_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Troubleshooting/troubleshoot-item-setup-errors) to learn more.

### Step 3 – Upload template

Once you’ve filled out the template, navigate to the **Catalog** page and select _Add items_. Select the _Upload in bulk_ link located under **Add thousands of items using a spreadsheet**. Upload the completed template, then [track the status](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20growth%20opportunities/Convert-seller-fulfilled-items-to-WFS#trackstatus) to check for errors and compliance reviews.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt691e8ae41dc08342/68f7aab4c15265cfd3f0455b/Upload_spec_sheet3.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

Our system will immediately check for common errors, such as missing fields, wrong data types and invalid check digits. If there are any, we’ll generate an error report for you to download.  In the report, you’ll see the number of errors and descriptions in columns A and B. The cells that contain errors will also be highlighted in yellow. Correct the errors in the report and reupload it.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt55cec588ef974dc8/65004e67066da0000df61113/9238_Feed_Status_Error.jpeg?branch=us_mplearn)

## Update with GTINs

If you have a lot of items to convert, you can copy-paste GTINs to create a template instead. It’s a quick and easy way to convert items without needing to select items individually.

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/items-and-inventory/manage-items) and select _Update items_. Choose _Update with GTINs_. In the pop-up, select _Convert to WFS_. You can then enter up to 5,000 GTINs to convert. If you paste a list of GTINs, we’ll automatically separate each GTIN for you and check for errors. When you’re done, select _Download file_ to create the template.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltdc1c964e5a6b9f2a/68f7aab26b4c03b06dbcf209/update_with_GTINs.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 2 – Download template

Go to the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed?feedsFor=downloads) page. From the **Downloads** tab, download the WFS convert template you just created. Fill in the blank fields with required information about your items.

### Step 3 – Upload template

Once you’ve filled out the template, navigate to the **Catalog** page and select _Add items_. Select the _Upload in bulk_ link located under **Add thousands of items using a spreadsheet**. Upload the completed template, then [track the status](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20growth%20opportunities/Convert-seller-fulfilled-items-to-WFS#trackstatus) to check for errors and compliance reviews.

## Track status

After you submit an item, track the publishing status from the **Uploads** tab in the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed?feedsFor=uploads). If there are no issues, your item will be published within 24 hours. Look for these key columns in the table:

- **Feed ID:** This is a system-generated ID for the item or uploaded file. Include this feed ID in any Support cases.
- **Errors:** If your item has errors, you’ll see a number in this column. Select the number to view errors. You can then fix errors directly in Seller Center and resubmit the item.
- **Status**: The status shows the progress of your item in the system _. Submitted_ means yousent in items. _Pending_ means our system is still processing items. _Processed_ means they’ve either been published or are waiting for compliance review on the Pending Review page. _Error_ means there’s incorrect information that’s stopping the item from being published.
- **Feed type:** This describes the type of submission.You can filter by _WFS Convert_ to see only items that are being converted.
- **Error report:** If you have several errors, you can download a spreadsheet to view all of them at once.

If your items have hazardous materials, they may be put on hold for up to 3 business days for a [compliance review](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup#ComplianceReview). Go to the [**Pending Review**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/pending-review) page for updates.

## Troubleshoot errors

If your item hasn’t been published yet, there may be errors you need to fix. Make sure to go to these pages:

- Check [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed?feedsFor=uploads) after you submit any item. If there are any issues, select the number in the _Errors_ column to review or download a report from the _Error report_ column. Then make changes and resubmit the items or template.
- Check [**Pending Review**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/pending-review) if your item contains a hazardous material or doesn’t have any errors on the Activity Feed. Select the _Action Needed_ status to fix errors and resubmit.

This table lists the most common errors during WFS item setup and how to fix them. Some will only apply if you’re adding a single item, multiple items or using APIs. For more help with errors, go to [_Troubleshoot WFS item template errors_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Troubleshooting/Troubleshoot-item-feed-errors?locale=en-US).

| Error message | Next steps |
| --- | --- |
| ‘Each’ pack config is required. | Add the width, height, depth and weight of the item, including any packaging. |
| \`Product is or Contains this Battery Type\` is a required attribute. Enter value for the attribute \`Product is or Contains this Battery Type\`. | If your item is or contains a battery, select the type from the dropdown list. Find this information on the battery, its packaging or within a [Safety Data Sheet](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-compliance-safety-data-sheets?locale=en-US). If your item doesn't have a battery, choose "Does Not Contain a Battery."<br>For more guidance, go to [_WFS compliance: Batteries._](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-compliance-batteries-FAQ?locale=en-US) |

| ‘Selling Price’ is a required attribute. Enter value for the attribute ‘Selling Price.’ | Add the price that the customer pays for the item. Do not include commas or currency symbols. |

Please use Seller Center ‘Pending Review’ page for status tracking. | Your item may contain a chemical, aerosol, pesticide or battery. The compliance team will put the item on hold to review, which can take up to 3 business days. Track the status on the [**Pending Review**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/pending-review) page or [Hazmat Items On Hold API](https://developer.walmart.com/us-marketplace/reference/wercsfeedback).<br>Learn more about the compliance review at [_WFS hazardous materials: Item setup_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup). |

- Feed ID

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Can an item be both Walmart-fulfilled and seller-fulfilled?

Not at the same time. When you set up an item as Walmart-fulfilled and send us inventory, we’ll fulfill orders for that item until the inventory you sent us runs out. Then, if our systems see that you still have inventory for that item in your own fulfillment centers, fulfillment will [switch to you.](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Troubleshooting/Troubleshoot-Walmart-fulfilled-orders-switching-to-seller-fulfilled) You’ll then be responsible to fulfill orders for that item.

* * *

If I convert an item to Walmart-fulfilled, can I switch it back to seller-fulfilled?

It can switch back to seller-fulfilled if there’s no WFS inventory stored in our fulfillment centers. You’ll need to completely [remove the item](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20Inventory%20management/WFS-inventory:-Remove-items-from-a-fulfillment-center) from the WFS network, as well as stop restocking inventory. Once inventory levels are at 0, orders will automatically switch to “seller fulfilled (WFS eligible).”

* * *

What does “seller fulfilled (WFS eligible)” mean?

Once you convert an item, you’ll see this fulfillment status on the Catalog page. It means there’s no inventory stored at Walmart fulfillment centers yet, so the item is not currently being fulfilled by WFS. Make sure to [send us inventory](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-inbound-orders:-Send-inventory) to switch the status to “Walmart fulfilled.”

* * *

Why is my item not published?

Your item may have errors that stop it from being published. Make sure to check the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed?feedsFor=uploads) page and fix item errors. It’s also possible your item was flagged as hazardous material. Check the [**Pending Review**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/pending-review%22%20/t%20%22_blank)  page to see if there are any compliance issues.

* * *

Can I change the product ID or SKU for a Walmart-fulfilled item?

If your item is fulfilled by WFS, you cannot change the GTIN, UPC or SKU. They’re unique to the item and cannot be replaced or reused. This helps prevent item mix-ups at fulfillment centers.

* * *

What do I do after my Walmart-fulfilled item is published?

Send inventory to a Walmart fulfillment center so we can start fulfilling orders. You’ll need to [create a shipping plan](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-inbound-orders:-Send-inventory), telling us which items and how you’re sending them. You’ll also be assigned Walmart facilities to ship to.

* * *

I have an item that ships in multiple boxes. How do I convert it to Walmart-fulfilled?

Converting multi-box items is different from standard items. You’ll need to create new GTINs for each sellable set and its associated boxes through the multi-box template. Go to [_WFS multi-box: Set up items_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-multi-box-items) for guidance.
