Performance dashboard: Regional performance

Last updated on Jan 27, 2026

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Order-&-fulfillment-performance:-regional-performance#frequently-asked-questions)

* * *

Overview

You can gain high-level performance data for each U.S. state you deliver to when accessing the  [**Performance**](https://seller.walmart.com/performance/order-and-fulfillment) dashboard. In this guide, you’ll learn how to view the details of your on-time delivery performance and access the [**Regional performance**](https://seller.walmart.com/partner-analytics/performance/fulfillment/kpi/regional-performance?returnPATH=%2Fpartner-analytics%2Fperformance%2Ffulfillment%2Fkpi%2Fcards) page.

Notes:

If you use Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), most performance metrics will be met for you, with the exception of the Negative Feedback Rate.

## How does it work?

To view your **Regional performance** status, navigate to the [**Performance**](https://seller.walmart.com/performance/order-and-fulfillment) dashboard in Seller Center, locate the **Other metrics** section and select _View details_. Your status may be:

- **Good:** Your delivery performance meets the [Seller Performance Standards](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Seller-performance-standards).

- **Monitor:** Your delivery performance needs attention. Take proactive steps for improvement.

- **Urgent:** Your delivery performance is below expected standards. Immediate corrective measures are necessary to avoid account-level consequences.


## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How do I update my Shipping Templates with accurate delivery estimates for different regions?

You can modify your [Shipping Templates](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Shipping%20Templates/Shipping-templates:-manage-settings) in Seller Center at any time.

* * *

Why am I not seeing any data on my map?

If you haven’t set up a [Shipping Template](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Shipping%20Templates/Shipping-templates:-overview) and assigned SKUs to that template, your delivery data won’t appear on the **Regional performance** page.

* * *

What do I do if my deliveries exceed the allotted transit time on Seller Center?

Review your **Carriers** performance or consider upgrading your [shipping speed](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-methods:-expedited-delivery-programs).

* * *

How do I report an error?

If you think that any of your **Regional performance** information is inaccurate, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.
