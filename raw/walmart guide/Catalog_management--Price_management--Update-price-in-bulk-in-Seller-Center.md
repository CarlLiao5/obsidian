Update price in bulk in Seller Center

Reading time: 1 min

Overview

You can update prices in Seller Center either individually or in bulk. This guide shows you how to use the **Price and Promotion** template.

## Update price in bulk

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center. Select the _Update items_ button and choose _Update with file_ from the dropdown menu.

### Step 2 — Download and complete the template

Select the _Update price and set promo price_ link and save the template to your computer. Then open the template and make your updates. If you’re only submitting basic price updates, you only need to fill out the **SKU** and **Selling Price** information.

When you’re done, save the template without changing the file type.

![2512_Update-price-bulk_screenshot.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt57c2ab9e61a8870b/6930aef29914a33272745113/2512_Update-price-bulk_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 3 — Upload the template

Navigate back to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) and select the _Update items_ button and choose _Update with file_. Then use the file browser window to upload the template. When you’re ready, select _Submit_.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

I only need to update pricing for one item. Do I have to download the template?

There’s another method for individual price updates. Check out [_Update price individually in Seller Center_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Update-price-individually-in-Seller-Center) to learn more.

* * *

I need to update promotional prices. How can I do that?

Check out [_Promotional pricing: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Promotions/Promotional-pricing:-Overview) for more information.
