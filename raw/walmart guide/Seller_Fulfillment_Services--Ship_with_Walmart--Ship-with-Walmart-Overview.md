Ship with Walmart: Overview

Last updated on Nov 20, 2025

Reading time: 3 min

* * *

Overview

If you fulfill your Walmart orders yourself, use Ship with Walmart (SWW) to access discounted shipping rates from trusted domestic and international carriers while protecting your account against delivery related issues. In this guide, you'll learn about the carriers integrated with SWW and the various benefits offered by this feature.

Notes:

Ship with Walmart offers discounted shipping rates (versus market rates), performance metrics protection, and automatic protections for U.S. domestic shipments — offering up to $100 for orders lost in transit or after delivery, provided they’re shipped on time. You can also purchase additional protections up to $500 for orders over $100 from the carrier.

## How does it work?

If you fulfill orders for items shipped within the U.S. you can ship packages with USPS, FedEx and UPS and receive a discount on shipping fees. Below are the different settings you can update using the **Ship with Walmart** page:

| Setting | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Default print settings** | Configure default print settings to apply to all future label printing settings for your orders. |
| **Custom packages** | Set default package dimensions and receive an estimated shipping cost based on the package dimensions and weight that you’ve entered for custom packages. You’ll be charged based on the actual dimensions and weight measured by the carrier. You can preview the cost using our [Ship with Walmart shipping calculator](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/ship-with-walmart-shipping-calculator). |
| **Shipping labels** | [Purchase discounted](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Ship%20with%20Walmart/buy-shipping-labels-in-seller-center) shipping labels through Walmart and choose your preferred package type and weight. |
| **Seller protections** | Ship with Walmart provides [seller protections](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/ship-with-walmart-seller-protections-policy) for delivery issues outside your control. For eligible orders reported as lost in transit or after delivery, Walmart will refund the customer (up to $100), handle the carrier claim and keep your performance metrics unaffected. |
| **Signature on delivery** | Require a signature for the package at the time of delivery, for FedEx and UPS shipments only. |
| **Bring your own account** | [Set up your own account](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Ship%20with%20Walmart/ship-with-walmart-bring-your-own-account) through FedEx to buy labels and add your own account rates. |

## Additional guidelines

If you fulfill orders for items shipped from China to the U.S., you can [enroll in Ship with Walmart Cross Border](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/ship-with-walmart/import-fees-support) using Yanwen and SF Express for door-to-door delivery.

Notes:

Shipping labels aren't available for purchase for orders shipping from China to the U.S.

Shipments fulfilled outside the U.S. are subject to customs requirements. Ship with Walmart carriers handle customs clearance on your behalf, and you’ll pay shipping fees only. When creating labels, the estimated shipping costs don’t include duty estimates.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What carrier options are available when using Ship with Walmart services?

USPS, FedEx and UPS are available for Ship with Walmart packages shipped within the U.S. For shipments from China to the U.S., Yanwen and SF Express are available, while FedEx is available for shipments from India to the U.S.

Notes

Currently, shipping labels are not available for purchase for orders shipping from China to the U.S.

* * *

How is shipping carrier service eligibility determined?

Ship with Walmart carrier service eligibility is based on the ability to meet [delivery promises](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-and-fulfillment-policy) to customers. If you ship your order with one of the eligible services, you won’t be responsible for orders delivered outside your delivery promise, as long as you ship the order on time. Carrier service options are influenced by factors such as [lag time](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Fulfillment%20settings/Update-fulfillment-lag-time-in-Seller-Center), [operating schedule](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Fulfillment%20settings/Fulfillment-Center-operating-schedule), inventory location and Walmart optimized promise dates. Keep your shipping settings up to date to ensure accuracy.

* * *

How do I enroll in Ship with Walmart?

Ship with Walmart is a service that’s available to any Walmart Marketplace seller who fulfills their own orders. To start using Ship with Walmart, go to your account in [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home), navigate to **Shipping Profile** and select [_Ship with Walmart_](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/ship-with-walmart). Then, add or choose a fulfillment center and select it as your _Ship from_ address. If you’re shipping from China, enroll from the [**Ship with Walmart Cross Border**](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/ship-with-walmart-cross-border-account-management) tab.

After enrollment, you’ll have the option to choose Ship with Walmart when buying shipping services. Depending on the country you’re sending packages from, different shipping options may be available.

* * *

Can I use my own carrier after enrolling in Ship with Walmart?

You can choose to use either Ship with Walmart or your own carrier for US domestic shipping. However, once enrolled in Ship with Walmart Cross Border for shipping from China, enrolled items must be shipped through Ship with Walmart.

* * *

How can I unenroll from Ship with Walmart Cross Border?

To unenroll, select _Unenroll_ from the [**Ship with Walmart Cross Border**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/ship-with-walmart/import-fees-support) tab in Seller Center. If you unenroll your account, all items enrolled in the program will be unenrolled as well.

* * *

How will I know the cost for sending a package?

When you use Ship with Walmart to buy a shipping label, you’ll receive an estimated shipping cost based on the package dimensions and weight that you’ve entered. When you use Ship with Walmart to ship packages, you’ll be charged based on the estimation and get reconciliation when the actual dimensions and weight measured by the carrier are available.

The final shipping label cost appears in your Statementsreports as Walmart Shipping Label Service Chargefor shipping within the U.S., and Walmart International Shipping Label Service Chargefor shipping from China.

* * *

How do shipping label charges work?

You’re charged for the label when you mark the order as shipped. If you don’t use the label, you’ll be refunded. Depending on the carrier:

- Refunds may take 14–30 days to appear in your account.

- Any adjustments (credit or debit) to label charges typically take two to three weeks.


To learn more about your Ship with Walmart charges, download the [Ship with Walmart Carrier Reconciliation Report](https://seller.walmart.com/analytics/reports). This report includes estimated and final label charges, insurance costs and carrier-scanned dimensions and weights. Once you’ve purchased your shipping label, it may take a few days for the final charges to appear in the report.

* * *

Where can I find a record of my Ship with Walmart shipping labels?

In Seller Center, navigate to [**Payments**](https://seller.walmart.com/payments/statements) and select the _Download_  link. Then, select _Payments new report_. The information is available in the _Shipping Label Charge_  row of the **Transaction Description** column.

* * *

Is API support available for any of the shipping carriers?

API support is available for USPS, FedEx, Yanwen and SF Express. UPS isn't supported.

* * *

Can I access the claims report through API?

Yes, you can access the claims report from the [**Claims**](https://seller.walmart.com/orders/claims) page under the **Orders** dashboard in Seller Center or from the [claims API](https://developer.walmart.com/us-marketplace/docs/shipment-protection-api-overview).
