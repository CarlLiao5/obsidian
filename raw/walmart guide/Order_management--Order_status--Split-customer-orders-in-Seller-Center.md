Split customer orders in Seller Center

Last updated on Nov 6, 2024

Reading time: 1 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Order%20status/Split-customer-orders-in-Seller-Center#overview)

* * *

Overview

Sometimes, all the items in a customer’s order can’t be shipped together due to size or limited stock availability. In these situations, you can choose to split the order and ship items individually instead. Keep reading to find out how.

## How to split customer orders

### Step 1 — Find the order

Navigate to the [**Orders**](https://seller.walmart.com/orders/manage-orders) page and use the **Order #** drop-down menu or the **Purchase order#** column to locate your order. Select the purchase order number link.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt44e3f3cd21cb9a6c/6682d5db242491000eaaca2d/11185_Screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 2 — Split the order

Select _Ship items_. Then, navigate to the drop-down menus and select _Ship_ and the quantity of items you wish to ship. For the remaining items, select _New_.

In the **Shipping details** section, enter the carrier and tracking number and select _Mark as shipped_ to split the order. When you’re ready to ship the rest of the order, simply use the same purchase order number to repeat the process for the remaining items.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt6036d107aec01861/6682d5dece1b48000dba464a/11185_GIF.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Why do I have to repeat this process to ship the other items in the order?

Since each package has its own tracking information, you must record each split order separately. You should only select _Mark as shipped_ for the items that will be included in the corresponding package.
