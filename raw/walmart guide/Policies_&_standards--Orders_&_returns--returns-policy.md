Seller-fulfilled returns policy

Reading time: 6 min

Overview

For a consistent and easy returns experience for our customers, you’re required to meet a minimum set of requirements for returned Marketplace items. For example, all Marketplace sellers must provide a valid U.S. return center address and meet a minimum set of return window requirements. In this guide, you’ll learn more about the policy and exemptions, including which Marketplace items can and can't be returned.

## What is the policy?

### 1\. Return centers

Before accepting returns, you must set up your return center details in Seller Center with a valid U.S. return address. Your return center address can’t be located in Hawaii, Alaska or U.S. territories including American Samoa, Guam, the Northern Mariana Islands, Puerto Rico and the U.S. Virgin Islands. You also can’t use a P.O. box as your return address.

You can set up multiple return centers but at least one return center must be set up at all times. By default, all returns will be sent to the return center that is closest to the customer’s location unless otherwise indicated by your [item-level return rules](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Returns%20&%20refunds/Create-item-level-return-rules).

Pro tip

If you need a return center based in the U.S., you can work with a Walmart-preferred service provider through our [**Returns Service Provider**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/returns) program.

Notes:

The return center display name can’t include Walmart, or any names associated with Walmart.

Walmart reserves the right to audit your return center addresses for data quality and to standardize return addresses to reflect the appropriate carrier formats.As part of this process, Walmart may request proof of address documentation for verification. If the submitted proof can't be verified, Walmart reserves the right to take appropriate action, including suspension or removal of the return center address.

In the event of your termination, Walmart reserves the right to route packages to Walmart's return center for failed delivery or undeliverable packages to your return center.

Walmart reserves the right to charge you a failed delivery fee for items that are rerouted to Walmart’s Return Center in Las Vegas as a result of a failed delivery attempt, due to an incomplete or invalid address provided in Seller Center. If your Walmart Marketplace returns are incorrectly routed to the Walmart Las Vegas return center instead of the return address listed on your account, follow the steps outlined [here](https://gecrm.my.salesforce.com/sfc/p/#61000000ZKTc/a/8Y000000q9jL/ZEhvV8yRqdKkwH2mziKXjXsC7V0Cxd8cCoBF1zvvK6k) for FedEx or USPS.

### 2\. Return shipping carriers and labels

Unless you specify otherwise, [Walmart’s Returns Shipping Service (RSS)](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Orders%20&%20returns/Returns-Shipping-Service-RSS-policy) will be used as the default label printing method for seller-fulfilled return shipping labels. If you choose to use your own carrier (FedEx or UPS) for label printing, you’ll need to add your [carrier account details](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Returns%20&%20refunds/Update-seller-fulfilled-return-label-settings) in Seller Center.

### 3\. Return windows

You must support a minimum return window of 30 days, which begins seven days after an item's ship date, except for certain electronics, major appliances and luxury items. If preferred, you may choose to provide customers with longer return windows. Items that fall into one of [these categories](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltbbe62a7cfa576158/661e883347e697000d552d39/Return-Window-Exceptions.pdf?branch=us_mplearn) can have a shorter return window.  Customer return requests that are initiated within the return window but not yet finalized past the return window, must be honored and the refund given to the customer.

You can find Final Sale product types listed [here](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt0373c1aa84ea6b2d/Non-Returnable-Product-Types.pdf?branch=us_mplearn).

### 4\. Return policy exemptions

In some cases, you're required to request [return policy exemptions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Returns%20&%20refunds/Request-a-return-policy-exemption) to the standard returns process if the returned item falls into one of [these categories](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltc7a3b4dc4ca4be9e/Return-Policy-Exemptions.pdf?branch=us_mplearn). Typically, these items include but are not limited to items shipped by freight, custom or luxury. The customer will contact you directly to initiate the return for items that fall into these categories.

For products that include hazardous materials (hazmat), a return exemption is still required. However, some of these products may follow the above return process and handling, while others may require in‑store return only. In those cases, Walmart associates will properly label and transport the items back to you when it is safe to do so. Items classified as Fully Regulated for ground shipment will automatically default to a return exemption by Walmart.

Walmart will monitor your return policy exemptions and reserves the right to reject exemptions as unreasonable.

### 5\. In-store returns

Walmart will accept eligible returned Marketplace items from customers in Walmart stores on your behalf, in accordance with your return settings. You retain all rights, title and interest for items returned in-store during the minimum return window from the moment the item is accepted at a Walmart store. You’re responsible for all risks of loss to the returned item except when the returned item is in Walmart’s care and control.

Items that meet your return policy exemptions or items that are odd-sized, freight, oversized, heavy, hazmat, luxury items or major appliances cannot be returned in-store.

You’re responsible for processing, shipping, returns, customer service and all associated costs for items that can’t be returned in-store. This includes cases where an item is incorrectly designated as eligible for in-store return because the required return policy exemption was not requested. If a customer attempts an in-store return for an ineligible item, you’re responsible for the resolution, including any refund issued to the customer and any other costs.

Walmart reserves the right to refuse any return that poses risk to our customers, associates or facilities.

### 6\. Keep It & Partial Keep It Rules

You can set up [Keep It or Partial Keep It Rules](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Returns%20&%20refunds/Add-a-Keep-It-Rule) for items that you don’t want returned to you but are refundable.

For both online and in-store returns, the customer will be asked to keep the item. If the customer doesn’t want to keep the item or if Walmart determines the specific customer transaction is ineligible for the Keep It program, Walmart will have sole discretion to handle the disposal of the item in accordance with Walmart’s procedures, including but not limited to sale, refurbishment, liquidation, donation, recycling or destruction. Walmart may keep all proceeds of any inventory that it disposes of or to which title transfers including returned, damaged, or abandoned items.

Notes:

When a customer requests a return, you’re prohibited from negotiating alternative solutions, unless explicitly authorized through Walmart's Partial Keep It Rules or other pre-approved resolutions. Walmart reserves the right to suspend or terminate sellers who don’t adhere to Partial Keep It Rules and try to haggle with customers.

### 7\. Replacement Rules

You can add [Replacement Rules](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Returns%20&%20refunds/add-replacement-rules) for items that you would like to offer a replacement option instead of a refund to the customer.

Replacement rules don’t apply to in-store returns and are available for certain return reasons (e.g., damaged or incorrect item). Once a replacement order is initiated, a new purchase order will be created. You can find and reference this in the **Order** page within Seller Center. You won’t refund the original order, and you won’t receive credit for the replacement order. If the customer declines a replacement, a refund will be issued.

### 8\. Refunds

The refunding process differs depending on the following situations:

- **In-store returns:** Refunds are issued to customers upon receipt of the item in the store.

- **Walmart.com and Walmart Customer Care returns:** Refunds are issued to customers when the returned package is first scanned by the carrier. Walmart Customer Care may issue refunds for the benefit of the customer on your behalf in handling customer care issues, including returns, as set forth in [this policy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Customer%20care/Customer-Care-policy). If Walmart determines that extenuating circumstances exist, Walmart Customer Care may issue a refund for the benefit of the customer on your behalf for returns outside of the return window. In most cases, the refunded items will be returned to you.

- **Recalls:** Recalled items are issued a refund regardless of purchase condition (WIC/SNAP restrictions apply).


### 9\. Return fees

- **Return shipping fee:** You may not apply return shipping charges to customers.

- **Restocking fee:** You may not apply restocking fees to customers except for eligible items set up with return exemptions. For items set up as return policy exemptions, you may apply additional fees up to 20% of the item price, for any items returned to you that aren’t in their original or sellable condition.

- **Duty and import fees:** You shall not charge any duty or import fees to the customer post purchase. If you do not pay these fees and the shipping carrier reaches out to a customer to request payment, Walmart reserves the right to refund the customer, and you will not be able to open a dispute.


## Additional guidelines

If the customer contacts you directly to initiate the return, you must provide a shipping label and a full refund. Offering discounts, alternative replacements, store credit or incentives to avoid a return is not allowed. If additional offers are given to the customer, Walmart reserves the right to refund the customer on your behalf and the transaction will not be eligible for a dispute. Verify your item settings in Seller Center.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How do I verify my return center address?

If you’re prompted to verify your return center, navigate to [**Account Hub**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/account/account-health) and upload documentation that shows proof of your return center address. Walmart accepts the following types of documentation:

- Utility bill

- Rental or lease agreement

- Bank statement


* * *

Can I cancel a customer-initiated return?

No, however customers can request that initiated returns be canceled by contacting Walmart Customer Care.

* * *

What items can’t be returned?

Walmart retains final authority to accept or decline any item that’s eligible for return. Under no circumstance will Walmart accept the return of an ineligible item. Walmart reserves the right to refuse any return that poses risk to our customers, associates or facilities. Review a full list of Final Sale product types [here](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt0373c1aa84ea6b2d/Non-Returnable-Product-Types.pdf?branch=us_mplearn).

* * *

What if a customer returns an item that requires assembly?

Items that require assembly (example: indoor or outdoor furniture, grills, bicycles, etc.) must be fully disassembled and re-packaged prior to returning to a store or approved carrier location. FedEx and USPS reserve the right to decline service for unpackaged assembled items.

* * *

Can I dispute a return?

If eligible, you may dispute charges for a customer return, a Walmart Customer Care refund, a package that couldn’t be delivered and has been returned to the sender or an incorrectly charged Referral Fee.

* * *

How will returned items affect my tax position?

You should consult your tax advisors regarding adjustments to your tax position relating to in-store returns. You can also refer to the Marketplace Retailer Agreement or the Tax collection and remittance policy addendum.
