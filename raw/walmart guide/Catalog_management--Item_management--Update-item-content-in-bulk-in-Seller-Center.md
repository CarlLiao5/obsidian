Update item content in bulk in Seller Center

Last updated on Apr 2, 2026

Reading time: 3 min

* * *

Overview

To update content for multiple items at once, you can set up a Walmart template to make your updates by attribute or GTIN (Global Trade Identification Number). In this guide, we’ll show you how to set up the template and use each method.

Notes:

AI-generated content must be accurate, truthful, rights-compliant, and aligned with Marketplace policies, including our [_Product details policy_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-Detail-Page:-overview).

## Update by attribute

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center. Select the _Update items_ button and choose _Update with file_ from the dropdown menu.

### Step 2 – Download and complete the template

Select the _Change any item attribute_ link and follow the steps to set up your Walmart template. For example, if you want to set up the template on your own, select _No_ and then choose the fulfillment type, product categories and product types you want. When you’re done, select the _Build spreadsheet_ button.

Open the template and enter the SKU, product type and product identifier for each of your items. You can remove the rest of the attributes or update them based on your needs.

![2511_Update-item-content-bulk_screenshot.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt22ae94c25ebdaf3c/692e021a278184d50455d3fa/2511_Update-item-content-bulk_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Pro tip

If you aren't sure which classification to choose, review our [content standards](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Content-standards:-Overview).

### Step 3 – Upload the template

Navigate back to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items), select the _Update items_ button and choose _Update with file_. Then use the file browser window to upload the template. When you’re ready, select _Submit_.

After submitting the template, you can track its status from the [**Activity feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) in Seller Center. Updates may take up to 24 hours. Any data errors that are detected during the updates will require extra processing time.

Notes:

To ensure a high-quality shopping experience, Walmart selects the best content for each item attribute, giving priority to content provided directly by brand owners and their authorized resellers. Because [brand owners and authorized resellers](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Brand%20Portal/brand-portal-overview) have priority, there are times when you won’t be able to decide what content is published on Walmart.com. This means that if you don’t have brand owner privileges, you won’t be able to update some content fields, including blank fields.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt553bee01e860e2a9/693094ee46b6924a595222a8/update-item-by-attribute.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

## Update by GTIN

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/item/list) page in Seller Center. Select the _Update items_ button and choose _Update with GTINs_ from the dropdown menu.

### Step 2 – Provide GTINs

Enter up to 5,000 GTINs into the **GTINs** text field, using a comma to separate each value. Then select _Download file_ and an editable template will be available in the **Downloads** tab on the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) dashboard. Once you’ve downloaded your pre-populated file, you can make updates in the template as needed.

![2511_update-item-by-gtin_gif.gif](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt59fcc64a532c01ff/6931e1004870f3d6d9370ee2/2511_update-item-by-gtin_gif.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

Pro tip

If you copy and paste multiple GTINs from an Excel template into the field, the commas will automatically appear.

Notes:

You can only enter GTINs for 10 product types at a time **.**

### Step 3 – Upload the template

To upload the template, navigate back to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center, then select the _Update items_ button and choose _Update with file_. Then use the file browser window to upload the template. When you’re ready, select _Submit_.

After submitting the template, you can track its status from the [**Activity feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) in Seller Center. Updates may take up to 24 hours. Any data errors that are detected during the updates will require extra processing time.

Notes:

To ensure a high-quality shopping experience, Walmart selects the best content for each item attribute, giving priority to content provided directly by brand owners and their authorized resellers. Because [brand owners and authorized resellers](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Brand%20Portal/brand-portal-overview) have priority, there are times when you won’t be able to decide what content is published on Walmart.com. This means that if you don’t have brand owner privileges, you won’t be able to update some content fields, including blank fields.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How do I find my item’s GTIN?

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) and use the search field to locate your item. Then select the item name to view its GTIN. You can also use the [Item Report](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Reporting/Generate-an-item-report) to find an item’s GTIN.

* * *

I’ve entered the required information, but my template hasn’t downloaded yet. What should I do?

It may take time to process your request, depending on the number of items and categories you’ve submitted.
