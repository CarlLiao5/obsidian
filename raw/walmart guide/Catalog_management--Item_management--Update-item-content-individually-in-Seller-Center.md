Update item content individually in Seller Center

Last updated on Apr 2, 2026

Reading time: 2 min

Overview

If you need to quickly update an item’s content such as an item’s product name, site description and images, you can do so right from your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center. In this guide, we’ll show you how to update content for up to 25 items at once.

Notes:

If you need to update content for more than 25 items at once, [use a Walmart spreadsheet](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Item%20management/Update-item-content-in-bulk-in-Seller-Center).

## Update a single item

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center and select the checkbox next to up to 25 items you want to update at the same time. You can choose to update the item’s details on your own or choose to use Walmart’s GenAI suggestions.

![2512_update-item-content-individually_screenshot-1.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt5feaf655cdb97164/695562a4487fe79265c03c6e/2512_update-item-content-individually_screenshot-1.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 2 – Edit item details or enhance with GenAI

- **Edit item details:** To update seller-fulfilled items manually select the _Edit items_ button. For Walmart-fulfilled items, updates must be completed using a [spreadsheet](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Item%20management/Update-item-content-in-bulk-in-Seller-Center). If you're updating multiple items simultaneously, the default view may not display all attributes. To access additional attributes, choose the _Columns_ button to reveal any hidden options.
- **Enhance with GenAI:** To use Walmart’s GenAI suggestions, select the _Enhance with GenAI_ button. You’ll only receive suggestions for content that you’re eligible to update. The content quality improvements show how much your content quality score will improve based on Walmart’s GenAI suggestions.

Notes:

AI-generated content must be accurate, truthful, rights-compliant, and aligned with Marketplace policies, including our [_Product details policy_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-Detail-Page:-overview).

### Step 3 – Submit

Once you’ve reviewed the content suggestions and completed all required fields, choose _Submit_. You can track your items’ status from the [**Activity feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) in Seller Center. Updates may take up to 24 hours. Any data errors that are detected during the updates will require extra processing time.

Notes:

To ensure a high-quality shopping experience, Walmart selects the best content for each item attribute, giving priority to content provided directly by brand owners and their authorized resellers. Because [brand owners and authorized resellers](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Brand%20Portal/brand-portal-overview) have priority, there are times when you won’t be able to decide what content is published on Walmart.com. This means that if you don’t have brand owner privileges, you won’t be able to update some content fields, including blank fields. If you’re facing issues with updating content, learn how to [troubleshoot content errors](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/troubleshooting-content-errors).

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How do I update content for more than 25 items at once?

You can use a [Walmart spreadsheet](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Item%20management/Update-item-content-in-bulk-in-Seller-Center) to update your items by attribute or by GTIN (Global Trade Identification Number).

* * *

Can I remove the bulleted formatting in the Key Features field?

No. The key features you add are automatically formatted in a bulleted list to help customers easily review the benefits of the product. If you need to remove a key feature, you can delete the row.

* * *

I’m trying to update an item. Why are some of the content fields locked?

To ensure a high-quality shopping experience, Walmart selects the best content for each item attribute, giving priority to content provided directly by brand owners and their authorized resellers. Because [brand owners and authorized resellers](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Brand%20Portal/brand-portal-overview) have priority, there are times when you won’t be able to decide what content is published on Walmart.com. This means that if you don’t have brand owner privileges, you won’t be able to update some content fields, including blank fields.
