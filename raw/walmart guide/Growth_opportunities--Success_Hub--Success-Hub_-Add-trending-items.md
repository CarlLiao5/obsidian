Overview

Success Hub makes suggestions to help you discover and add new and existing trending items to your catalog. Through analyzing several factors such as customer demand, pricing, category and brand, you’re provided a list of items that have the highest potential to help grow your business. In this guide you'll learn how to access a curated list of trending items and add customer favorites in Success Hub.

## Add customer favorites

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to **Success Hub** by selecting [**Growth**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/success-hub) in Seller Center to add item suggestions. In the **Grow your sales by adding Customer Favorites** section, choose the _Add items_ button.

### Step 2 – Setup your item

Next, choose the _Setup Item_ link on the right side of the row. Depending on the type of item you have selected (existing vs. new to Walmart), you’ll be routed to the respective setup screens to complete the item setup process.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Why isn’t the item added to my catalog?

If you don’t finish the item setup process, the item will not be added to your catalog.

* * *

After I add the item to my catalog, what should I do next?

You’ll need to procure the items on your own to start selling them.

* * *

How can I update my inventory?

You can update inventory in [Seller Center](http://seller.walmart.com/), via Walmart APIs or with your preferred Solution Provider. For more information, visit [_How to update seller-fulfilled inventory: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/How-to-update-seller-fulfilled-inventory:-overview).

* * *

How can I reach someone on the Success Hub team?

Email [successhub@walmart.com](mailto:BizMentorSupport@walmart.com).
