Promotional pricing: Set up via the Promotions dashboard

Last updated on Nov 25, 2025

Reading time: 4 min

* * *

Overview

Promotional pricing allows you to offer customers great deals on your items. In this guide, you’ll learn how to use the [**Promotions**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/promotions) page in Seller Center to create and view Active, Scheduled, Draft, Expired or Canceled Promotional Pricing.

## Set up promo pricing

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to the [**Promotions**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/promotions) page under **Pricing i** n Seller Center,where you can create new or manage existing promotions (e.g., active, scheduled, drafts, expired and canceled).

Pro tip

For an easy way to choose the right items and prices for your promotions, use the **Set up suggested promotion** feature to download a spreadsheet of your high-potential items, along with Walmart’s suggested **Promo price** for each one. Select _Set up suggested promotion_ to get started.

![promo-pricing_screenshot-1.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt0bf0d2eed6beb94e/66d8c6d50b0341db5a26bc67/promo-pricing_screenshot-1.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 2 – Create promotion

To create new promotions, select _Create promotion_ and specify:

- Promotion name: The name that will be used for that promotion group.


- Discount Value: This indicates the value for the promotion group (ex., 5% off, $5 off).


- Strikethrough promotion rules:

  - Percent off: This rule applies a percentage off for all items under your promotion group.
  - Amount off: This rule applies a specific amount off for all items under your promotion group.
  - Custom promo price: This rule defines the specific promotion price for each individual item under the promotion group.

- Start date – End date: This is the promotion start through end date for the promotion group.


- Start time – End time: This is the promotion start through end time for the promotion group.


Pro tip

A promotion must be set at least four hours before you want the promo to begin. As a reminder, the start and end times of your promotions are in Universal Time Zone (UTC) and not in your local time zone.

![promo-pricing_screenshot-2.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt9521370d9adedc04/66d8c6fc0b0341702726bc6b/promo-pricing_screenshot-2.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 3 – Add items

Once you’ve created your Promotion, navigate back to the [**Promotions**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/promotions) page. Then, choose your promotion and select _Assign items._ You can add items to your promotion from your catalog or by using a template.

![promo-pricing_screenshot-3.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt108d0429cb51d259/66d8c7120b0341d3e026bc70/promo-pricing_screenshot-3.png?branch=us_mplearn)

#### **From Catalog:**

After choosing your promotion from the [**Promotions**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/promotions) page, select _From catalog_ from the **Assign items** dropdown. Search for an item in your catalog by name or SKU to assign to this promo group. When selecting the **Percentage off** or **Amount off** value, the promo price is auto-filled based on the strikethrough promo group rule selected in Step 2. If you select the **Custom promo price** option, you’ll need to input the promo price.

![promo-pricing_screenshot-4.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltb319af8ba3209d90/66d8c72464760c7d30042123/promo-pricing_screenshot-4.png?branch=us_mplearn)

#### **From a template:**

After choosing your promotion from the [**Promotions**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/promotions) page, select _Upload a spec file_ from the **Assign items** dropdown. Download the template and complete the following information:

Required:

- **SKU**: Fill in the SKU you’d like to assign to the promo group.


- **Promo Price**: Define a promo price for an item only if you select the custom promo price as the strikethrough promo group rule in Step 2.


Optional:

- **Price Placement**: Price Placements only display the promotional price in the customer’s shopping cart during the checkout process when the item is listed for less than the Minimum Advertised Price (MAP).


- **Promo Type**: Promo Types are identified on Walmart.com with a Reduced Price or a Clearance flag and a strikethrough the Was Price or List Price.


![promo-pricing_screenshot-5.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blte458ba4256f791ce/66d8c73764760c1f59042127/promo-pricing_screenshot-5.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Once you select _Assign items_ or upload the spec sheet, you can use the Feed ID to track the progress of your promotions. Once the promotion is available, it’ll be visible in the **Promotions** page either _Active_ or _Scheduled_ status _._

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How can I manage items within my promotions?

Manage your promotion’s assigned items by selecting the specific promotion name and choosing _Manage items_ to view the list of items belonging to that promo group.

- _Active_ status promotions: You can only delete or remove promotions.


- _Scheduled_ or _Draft_ status promotions: You can add or remove promotions.


* * *

How can I delete a promotion?

You can delete a promotion in two ways:

- Select _Delete_ from the drop-down menu under the **Promo Setting Action** column. Then, enter the _Promo Price Start Date_ and _Promo Price End Date_ that match your existing promotion and leave the rest of the columns blank.
- Select _Replace All_ from the drop-down menu under the **Promo Setting Action** column and leave the rest of the columns blank. Keep in mind that _Replace All_ will delete all your existing promotions for the particular SKU.

* * *

Can I change a promotion rule that is in Scheduled stage?

Yes, go to the **Promotions** page and select the promotion name to view the detail page and update your promotion rule and assigned items. You can’t edit your promotion rule within 30 minutes before your promotion starts.

* * *

How long does it take for a promotion to publish on Walmart.com?

Promotion updates can take up to four hours to publish. For this reason, you should upload your Price and promotion template in Seller Center at least 24 hours before you want the promotion to begin. As a reminder, the start and end times of your promotions are in Universal Time Zone (UTC) and not in your local time zone.
