Overview

If you use Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), there’s a greater chance you’ll see a Gross Merchandising Value (GMV) lift when compared to seller-fulfilled items. In this guide, you’ll learn how to take advantage of Success Hub’s Fulfill with Walmart suggestions by converting your items to Walmart-fulfilled.

## Convert items to WFS

### Step 1 – Access fulfill with Walmart suggestions

Navigate to **Success Hub** by selecting [**Growth**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/success-hub) in Seller Center to access Fulfill with Walmart suggestions. Choose the _Fulfill with WFS_ button To access Fulfill with Walmart suggestions. Select the item you want to convert to WFS, then choose _Convert_ to begin the process.

### Step 2 – Find your template

Once you get to the Fulfill with WFS page, select _Go to Activity Feed_ to find the most recently processed template. Then download the template, complete and save it. To find instructions for completing this template, check out [_Full setup template for WFS_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-item-spec-sheet-setup).

### Step 3 – Find your spec file

Upload the completed template to Seller Center.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Do I have to accept all Success Hub suggestions?

No. All insights are suggestive in nature. As with the Marketplace Program, there's no Business Expectation. You have no obligation and make no promises to purchase any minimum amount of merchandise to list or sell on the marketplace other than what you deem adequate. Success Hub suggestions are based on projections, past purchasing history, and representations about quantities to be purchased aren't binding on the Walmart Marketplace and Walmart be liable for any act or expenditure (including but not limited to expenditures for equipment, labor, materials, packaging or capital expenditures) by the seller in reliance on them.

* * *

When do Success Hub suggestions refresh?

Suggestions refresh daily.
