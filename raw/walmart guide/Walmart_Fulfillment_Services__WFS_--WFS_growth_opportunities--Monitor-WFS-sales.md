Monitor WFS sales

Overview

Wondering what to do next to set up your items for success? We have several features and [reports](https://seller.walmart.com/wfsLite/reports) to help you analyze your business and easily make decisions. You can also download the interactive [WFS Best Practices Checklist](https://gecrm.my.salesforce.com/sfc/p/61000000ZKTc/a/8Y0000011M7n/9gokqx4Y9FeSOtuLp4LMD1DIE4aKQwFBq0_Y_7x0AXM) to keep you on track.

## Sales page

The [Sales](https://seller.walmart.com/wfsLite/sales-dashboard) page allows you to see your sales in nearly real time, updating each hour to show you sales through the day, week, or month. Use the graphs to see if you’re winning or losing compared to yesterday, last week, or last month, and dig into why.

The table provides sales metrics for up to 100 of your items and allows you to slice the data to focus on your top or bottom sellers. It also displays items which have had the largest GMV increase or decrease compared to the previous day, week, or month. Download the file to see your sales performance for all your items.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blte012a09591d0fce1/6500f498380e94000a187691/Monitor_Sales_2.png?branch=us_mplearn)

## Orders report

This report allows you to view your data — at an order level with GMV — over a specific period of time, as well as information on units sold, shipped date, and taxes.

To download your orders report, log into Seller Center and go to [Reports](https://seller.walmart.com/wfsLite/reports).
