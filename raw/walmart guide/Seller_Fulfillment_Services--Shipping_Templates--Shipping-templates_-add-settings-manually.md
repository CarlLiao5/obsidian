Shipping Templates: Domestic or international shipping settings

Last updated on Dec 18, 2025

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

In this guide:

Overview

In this guide, you'll learn how to add domestic and international shipping settings using Shipping Templates in Seller Center.

## Add settings

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to the **Shipping Profile** page in Seller Center and select [_Shipping Templates_](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/shipping-templates).

### Step 2 — Create a template

Select the _Create shipping template_  button and choose one of the following:

- **Manual shipping settings:** Create a template for items that will be shipped from the U.S.


- **International shipping settings:** Create a template for items that will be shipped from China, Hong Kong or India. You must use this option if you want to set up international transit times (7, 9 and 13 days).


![2512_Shipping-templates_Domestic-international-shipping-settings_screenshot.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blta53f268ebf0ab345/694574556366387b9e8cbe67/2512_Shipping-templates_Domestic-international-shipping-settings_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 3 — Add shipping rules

Select _Edit template_ name, thencustomize your shipping settings under the **Shipping Methods and Regions** section.For an international template, choose the country of the fulfillment center location.Select the transit time you want to change from the dropdown menu to add new shipping rules for each shipping method and region.

You may update shipping costs under **Rate**. Shipping costs are subject to the [Egregious Shipping Cost rule](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Pricing-rules). When you’re done, select _Save Template._

Notes:

Shipping methods are available based on whether you’ve chosen domestic or international shipping. For example, OneDay shipping is available for shipping within the U.S., but not for shipping from China, Hong Kong or India.

### Step 4 — Assign SKUs to the template

After creating your template, you'll need to [assign SKUs](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Shipping%20Templates/Shipping-templates:-Assign-SKUs) to your template to define shipping rules for each item. If you recently added a seller-managed [fulfillment center](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Fulfillment%20settings/add-a-seller-managed-fulfillment-center-in-seller-center) in Seller Center, you must wait four hours before you can assign SKUs to the fulfillment center. If you don’t wait the required time, your shipping settings will revert to the Default Shipping Template and the desired template you created won’t be activated.

Pro tip

You can confirm if your mapping was successful using the [**Activity feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) dashboard.

Notes:

You can set up TwoDay to designated regions and sub-regions to accommodate any shipping constraints. You’re responsible for verifying if products can be delivered on time, and for listing any constraints such as weight, distance or other factors that may prevent timely shipping.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Why am I unable to edit the shipping cost for my template?

You’re limited to one change per hour for each template. You can make up to 10 edits per day. If you exceed this limit, your Shipping Template will be locked for 24 hours.

* * *

Why am I unable to set up or edit the price of the total order template?

You won’t be able to create or update your Shipping Template if the shipping value exceeds the [Egregious Shipping Cost rule](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Pricing-rules). Adjust your shipping cost under each item price tier and resubmit your template.

* * *

How do I remove SKUs from Value Shipping?

To remove SKUs from Value Shipping, you must create a [Paid Standard Shipping Template](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Shipping%20Templates/Shipping-templates:-overview). After creating the template, you must wait four hours and then add the SKUs to the Paid Standard Shipping Template. This will remove the SKUs from the Value Shipping Template and add them to the Paid Standard Template.

* * *

How do I disable Value Shipping to Alaska and Hawaii from the Default Shipping Template?

To disable Value Shipping to Hawaii and Alaska, select _Edit templates_, then select _Add Shipping Rule_ and deselect _Hawaii and Alaska – Street_.

* * *

Are there other types of Shipping Templates?

Yes. Default, Custom, Paid Standard and Freight. Visit [_Shipping Templates: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping%20Templates/Shipping-templates:-overview) for more information.

* * *

How do I set up a fulfillment center?

You can set up a fulfillment center through Seller Center. For detailed instructions, visit [_Fulfillment methods: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Fulfillment%20settings/Fulfillment-methods:-overview) .

* * *

How do I add inventory?

You can add inventory in many ways. For more information, check out [_How to update seller-fulfilled inventory: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/How-to-update-seller-fulfilled-inventory:-overview) .
