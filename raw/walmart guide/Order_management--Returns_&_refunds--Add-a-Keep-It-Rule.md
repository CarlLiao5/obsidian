Add Keep It Rules

Last updated on Sep 25, 2025

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

Overview

Sometimes it’s more cost-effective to let Walmart.com customers keep an item and process a refund than it is to pay a return shipping fee. With Keep It Rules, you can provide a full or partial refund to the customer and allow them to keep the item. In this guide, you’ll learn how to set up Keep It and Partial Keep It Rules in Seller Center.

## Add a Keep It Rule

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to the [**Returns**](https://seller.walmart.com/orders/returns) page in Seller Center and locate the **Keep It Rules** or **Partial Keep it rules** sections.

Notes:

You can’t apply Partial Keep It Rules to items with return exemptions.

### Step 2 — Add a rule

- **Keep It Rules:** To add a Keep It Rule, select the _Add Keep It Rule_ button and enter your **Department**, **Item price limit** and **Return Order** **limit** information. Select _Save_.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltc479bc464af50181/67a4e5ed66e248febdba6959/2501_9925_Add-keep-it-rule_screenshot-1.png?branch=us_mplearn)

- **Partial Keep It Rules:** To add a Partial Keep It Rule, select the _Add Partial Keep It Rule_ button and enter your **Department**, **Return Reason**, **% Refund Off** and **Max Amount** information. Select _Add Partial Keep It Rule_.


![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt9493c380f0a7ff99/677c069ae7db5cff2cf0328e/2501_9925_Add-keep-it-rule_screenshot-2.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

You can set item-level Keep It Rules for SKUs that aren’t included in a specific Department or don’t meet your Item Price limits by submitting a [Bulk Return Rules](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Returns%20&%20refunds/Create-item-level-return-rules) template. Allow up to four hours for the system to process your submission.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How are Keep It Rules defined?

Each Keep It Rule is defined by the product **Department**, **Item price limit** and **Return Order limit**. The Item price limit is the dollar amount up to which a customer will be able to keep an item. The Return Order limit is the dollar amount of the total order up to which a customer can keep multiple items from the Department.

* * *

How are Partial Keep It Rules defined?

Each Partial Keep It Rule is defined by the product **Department**, **Return Reason**, **% Refund Off** and **Max Amount**. % Refund Off is the dollar amount that the customer will be refunded for an item in a specific department and return reason. Max Amount is the dollar amount up to which a customer will be able to keep for that item.

* * *

What if the customer doesn’t want to keep the item?

For both online and in-store returns, the customer will be asked to keep the item. If the customer doesn’t want to keep the item or if Walmart determines the specific customer transaction is ineligible for the Keep It program, Walmart will have sole discretion to handle the disposal of the item in accordance with Walmart’s procedures, including but not limited to sale, refurbishment, liquidation, donation, recycling or destruction. Walmart may keep all proceeds of any inventory that it disposes of or to which title transfers including returned, damaged, or abandoned items.

* * *

What if the customer returns a Walmart-fulfilled item without requesting a return online?

If a customer returns a Walmart-fulfilled item in a Walmart store without initiating the return online first, Keep It Rules don’t apply. For more information on Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) returns, visit [here](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20basics/wfs-returns-overview).

* * *

Can I offer alternative solutions in place of a return request?

When a customer requests a return, you’re prohibited from negotiating alternative solutions, unless explicitly authorized through Walmart's Partial Keep It Rules or other pre-approved resolutions. If you’d like to offer a customer the option to keep an item at a discounted price, you must set the discount threshold using the designated Partial Keep It Rules before the time of purchase.
