Add multiple items: Full setup

Last updated on Feb 8, 2026

Reading time: 4 min

Overview

This guide shows you how to add items to your catalog that aren’t currently offered on Walmart.com. To add items to your catalog that are already listed on Walmart.com, review [_Add multiple items: Match Walmart’s catalog_.](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup%20methods/Add-items-in-bulk:-Set-up-items-by-match)

Notes:

You’re responsible for the accuracy of the information provided and ensuring that it complies with all laws, regulations and licensing requirements regarding the sale, shipping, transportation and delivery of these items. Failure to accurately report information may lead to item removal, account suspension or termination.

## Full setup

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center and select the _Add items_ button. Select the _Upload in bulk_  link located under **Add thousands of items using a spreadsheet**. On the next page, navigate to the **New items** section and select the _Build spreadsheet_ button.

### Step 2 — Build spreadsheet

Choose your preferred fulfillment type. You can choose one or both options. Then select _Next_. On the **Select product types** page, choose how you want to build your spreadsheet:

- **Search:** Search for the type of item you’re adding, and then select _Add_ to add the product type to your spreadsheet.


- **Browse:** Make your selections from a list of product categories and their product types. To get started, select the _browse product type categories_ link. For more information about product categories, visit [_Content standards: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Content-standards:-Overview).


Pro tip

In the **Added product types** section, use the **Save selection** link to save your selections as a template for future use. You can save up to 10 templates. To access your templates, navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) page, select _Add items_ and select the _Add items in bulk_ link. Then select the **My templates** tab.

When you’re ready, select _Build spreadsheet_ and save the spreadsheet to your computer. Select _Cancel_ to close the file browser window. Then, choose how you want to continue. For example, you can select _Exit to Catalog_, close the window or keep the window open to upload your spreadsheet later.

### Step 3 — Add item attributes

Open the spreadsheet. The information below describes the item attributes and highlighted sections that you must fill out to successfully set up your items.

Notes:

You must complete the **Spec Product Type** column before filling out the rest of the template. Otherwise, you won’t be able to fill out other required sections or view the attributes’ closed list values.

| Column name | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **SKU** | This attribute is required to successfully set up your items. |
| **Spec Product Type** | This information determines the product type attribute options you can choose from in the template. |
| **Product ID** | Product IDs entered here must adhere to the [_Product identifier (GTIN, UPC, ISBN, or EAN) policy_.](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Choose-a-product-identifier) |
| **Country of Origin** | This attribute is mandatory for item creation of Walmart and seller-fulfilled items. You must identify where a product was manufactured or transformed. If not specified, an error will be displayed in the **Activity Feed** in Seller Center. |
| **Required for the item to be visible on Walmart.com** | Fill out this section to ensure your item is visible on Walmart Marketplace. If any of these fields are missing, you’ll receive data errors. |
| **Conditionally Required based on Product Type** | This only appears if you have two or more product types selected when downloading the spreadsheet. The greyed-out cells indicate that the attribute is not relevant and/or not required. The other cells are required. If any of these fields are missing, you’ll receive data errors. |
| **Recommended to improve search and browse on Walmart.com** | The more information you provide here, the better your chances of helping customers find your item on Walmart.com. |
| **Recommended to create a variant experience on Walmart website** | Fill out this section if your products have [variants](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Variant%20management/Product-variant-groups:-Overview). |

Pro tip

You can add new columns for cells marked with a plus sign. Select the entire column of an existing column with your cursor, then right-click and choose _Copy_. Select the columns to the right of the one you copied, then right-click and choose _Insert copied cells_.

### Step 4 — Upload the template

To upload your completed template, navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center and select _Add items_. Select the _Upload in bulk_  link located under **Add thousands of items using a spreadsheet**. You can drag and drop your file onto the browser window under **Upload** or select the _browse_ link to locate the file on your computer.

After submitting your template, you can track its status from the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) in Seller Center. Updates may take up to 24 hours. Any data errors that are detected during the updates will require extra processing time.

Notes:

Failing to provide required attributes or conditionally required information, modifying the data in the first six rows of your template or changing tab names will lead to data errors.  If you’re having trouble with your upload, review [_Troubleshoot item setup errors_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Troubleshooting/troubleshoot-item-setup-errors) _._

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How can I track my item setup?

After submitting your items, you can track their status from the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) in Seller Center. Updates may take up to 24 hours. Any data errors that are detected during the updates will require extra processing time.

* * *

Do items within the Lawn Chemicals and Live Plants categories have any item setup restrictions?

Yes. Items in the Lawn Chemicals and Live Plants categories must comply with local, state and federal laws to be sold on Walmart.com. Before setting up these items in your Marketplace catalog, familiarize yourself with the [_Prohibited Products Policy._](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Prohibited%20products%20&%20brands/Prohibited-products-policy:-overview) If you need to add sales restrictions, contact your account manager for next steps.

* * *

How is setting up a Walmart-fulfilled item different from seller-fulfilled?

When you choose Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), we pick, pack and ship orders for you. During item setup, you’ll need to enter more required information, like country of origin, dimensions, [compliance](https://marketplacelearn.stg.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup) and [trade item hierarchy](https://marketplacelearn.stg.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-trade-item-configuration).

* * *

How are attributes defined?

You can reference the meaning of each attribute name in the **Data Definitions** tab in the template. To learn more about how attributes are defined, review [this guide](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-detail-page:-Item-attribution-&-categorization).

* * *

How are attributes grouped?

Attributes are grouped by their requirement level– required, recommended or optional.

* * *

How can I add more than one image for an item?

Navigate to the **Additional image URL** columns. Make sure your images follow our [image guidelines and requirements](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-detail-page:-Image-guidelines-&-requirements) _._

* * *

How should I format Key Features in the template?

You can simply add key features in plain text directly into the template. After you’ve uploaded your template and your item is published, the bulleting format will automatically be applied on Walmart.com.Avoid using additional text formatting like html, bullets or numbered lists when adding key features to the template.

* * *

Do I need to fill out the Sales Restrictions column?

If the product must or should be prohibited from being sold from certain states and ZIP codes because of legal or commercial reasons, you must fill out these details.

* * *

What are WFS compliance and trade item hierarchy?

When you set up a Walmart-fulfilled item, we require certain item and trade item configurations along with information to determine if your item is potentially hazardous material. To learn more, review [_WFS hazardous materials (hazmat) item setup_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup) and [_WFS trade item configuration_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-trade-item-configuration) _._

* * *

How can I update an item’s country of origin?

You must delete the item in Seller Center, wait 48 hours then set up a new item with the updated country of origin using the same or a different SKU number. The country of origin for an existing item cannot be updated using any spreadsheet.
