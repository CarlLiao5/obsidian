Multichannel Solutions: Set up items

Reading time: 5 min

Overview

In this guide, we’ll show you how to build your catalog and choose fulfillment types for Walmart Multichannel Solutions. Your items must be set up as Walmart-fulfilled, and this gives us the necessary information to store and fulfill them on your behalf.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt896713e327ee90bc/66c3873354be6cf971184740/Multichannel-Onboarding_Infographic.jpg?branch=us_mplearn)

## Add new items

If you don’t currently have items listed on Walmart.com, you’ll need to add them. You can one item or multiple items. Here's how:

### Create a single item

Follow these steps to add 1 item to your catalog:

1. Log into Seller Center and go to [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/items-and-inventory/manage-items).

2. Select **Add Items**.

3. Select the **create item** link under the Search the Walmart catalog heading.

4. Scroll to Fulfillment options and select **Walmart fulfilled**. This means we’ll fulfill orders from any eCommerce site, including Walmart.com.

5. Fill in all required information to finish setting up your item. For Walmart-fulfilled items, you’re required to give [compliance](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup) and [trade item configuration](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-trade-item-configuration).

6. Your item will be published in about 15 minutes. If there are any issues, it can take up to 4 hours. To track progress and check for errors, go to the [**Pending Review**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/pending-review) page.


Pro tip

If you’d like to sell exclusively on off-Walmart channels, scroll to the Optional section and look forThird Party Product Fulfillment Type. Choose **Multichannel Fulfilled** to unpublish the item, so it won’t appear on Walmart.com.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt3ea65c01e8c82301/66c662be7c1fa027bb926cba/MCS_seller_center_-_fulfillment_type.jpeg?branch=us_mplearn)

### Create multiple items

Follow these steps to add multiple items at once:

1. Log into Seller Center and go to [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/items-and-inventory/manage-items).
2. Select **Add Items**.
3. Find the **Upload in bulk** link and select **Create a full setup template**.
4. Choose the product categories that best fit your items. Then choose up to 20 product types per category.
5. Select **Walmart fulfilled** as your fulfillment type. This means we’ll fulfill orders from any eCommerce site, including Walmart.com.
6. Download and fill in the template. For Walmart-fulfilled items, you’re required to give [compliance](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup) and [trade item configuration](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-trade-item-configuration).
7. In the **Third Party Product Fulfillment Type** column, choose **Walmart Fulfilled** from the dropdown.
8. Upload the completed template.

For more detail on the template, go to [_Full setup template for WFS_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-item-spec-sheet-setup). If there are no issues, your item will be published in about 15 minutes. It can take up to 4 hours if there are any issues. To track progress and check for errors, go to the [**Pending Review**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/pending-review) page.

Pro tip

If you’d like to sell exclusively on off-Walmart channels, choose **Multichannel Fulfilled** in the Third Party Product Fulfillment Type column. This will unpublish the item, so it won’t appear on Walmart.com.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blted7b0680258ded6b/66c663e869e673b710f26e20/mcs_bulk_fulfillment_type.png?branch=us_mplearn)

## Convert existing items

If you’re a Marketplace seller with seller-fulfilled items already set up, you just need to [convert to Walmart-fulfilled](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/Convert-seller-fulfilled-items-to-WFS). Make sure to choose **Walmart fulfilled** as the fulfillment type under Third Party Product Fulfillment Type. But if you’d like to sell exclusively on off-Walmart channels, choose **Multichannel fulfilled** instead.

## Edit items

There are a few ways to keep your item up to date, depending on whether you’re editing a single item, multiple items or using APIs. Go to [_How to update content: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/How-to-update-content:-Overview) to learn more.

Notes:

If you want to sell exclusively on off-Walmart channels, only edit items through the [bulk upload method](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Item%20management/Update-item-content-in-bulk-in-Seller-Center). This applies whether you’re editing 1 item or 100. That’s because if you edit a single item within Seller Center, you won’t see the Third Party Product Fulfillment field.

## Send inventory to Walmart

Once your items are published, you’re ready to send and store inventory at a Walmart fulfillment center. Follow the steps in [_Send domestic inventory_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-inbound-orders:-Send-inventory) to give us item quantity, expected delivery date and more.

For our packing and labeling requirements, see the [WFS Routing and Packaging Guide](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt57da3349569cc081/WFS_Routing_Guide.pdf?branch=us_mplearn). You’ll then ship all multichannel inventory to an assigned fulfillment center.

Pro tip

Integrate with our APIs to [automate item setup](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Walmart%20Multichannel%20Solutions/multichannel-api).

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Does my item have to be listed on Walmart.com in addition to other sales channels?

By default, all items are eligible to be fulfilled for orders placed on Walmart.com and other sales channels. However, you can also sell exclusively on off-Walmart channels only. When you set up the item, look for Third Party Product Fulfillment Type and choose **Multichannel Fulfilled**.

* * *

Do I need to set up items on Walmart, even if I don’t plan to sell on Walmart.com?

Yes. The item setup process gives us important details about your items. We need this information to safely store items at our fulfillment centers and fulfill them.

* * *

What happens if I select the submit button and nothing happens?

This means that some required fields are incomplete. You’ll need to fill out all the information before submitting the form.

* * *

What if there’s already an item that matches my product ID in Walmart.com’s existing catalog?

If another item uses the same product ID, you'll get a match. To search for and add a new item based on Walmart.com's existing catalog, check out [_Add multiple items: Express setup_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Add-a-new-item:-Search-the-Walmart-catalog). You’ll still need to create your own SKU for the item, as these are unique to each seller.

* * *

I added a new item. Why can’t I find it when I search Walmart.com?

To be published on Walmart.com, all your items must have all the required attributes, including price and inventory. If this information is missing, your items will remain unpublished and will only be available in draft format until you complete the form. Or if you chose **Multichannel Fulfilled** as the Third Party Product Fulfillment Type, your item won’t be listed on Walmart.com and will only be fulfilled through off-Walmart channels.

* * *

How do you determine which fulfillment center I’m assigned to?

We’ll pick compatible WFS facilities based on your ship-from address. If any items have special handling requirements (like hazardous materials), we may assign a different one.
