Walmart Connect solutions: Onsite Display

Overview

Target customers using self-serve Walmart Onsite Display ads. Create engaging visuals and use Walmart’s first-party data to help you run personalized campaigns. In this guide, you’ll learn about how Walmart Onsite Display ads attract shoppers at every stage of their journey.

Notes:

Walmart Onsite Display is only available to Marketplace brand owners registered with the [Walmart Brand Portal](https://brandportal.walmart.com/).

## How does it work?

Once you create your Walmart Onsite Display [account](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?channel=Display&article=000009773), you can bid on available Display inventory, build your own creative assets and launch your own campaigns, with full budget and pricing control. Campaigns use a Display Auction which operates on eCPM (effective cost per mille), meaning that you pay for every thousand impressions.

Walmart Onsite Display campaigns use dynamic bidding to adjust your bids up and down depending on how your campaign is pacing. Walmart Onsite Display campaigns also use a first-price auction model, so ads are selected based on the highest bid, and the winner pays their winning bid, but never more than their max bid.

You can bid on available Display inventory, build your own creative assets and launch your own campaigns quickly and easily, with full budget and pricing control over every impression within the self-serve Walmart Connect Ad Center.

Pro tip

Review in-flight display performance metrics and track eCPMs across ad groups and tactics. Analyze post-campaign metrics by reviewing reports on campaign performance, new buyers and bid performance.

## Additional guidelines

Creative visuals are built in the **Creative Platform** using [**Creative Builder**](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?channel=Display&article=000009872). If you need help, sign up with the Walmart [Creative Essentials Ad Certification](https://connectacademy.walmartlearning.com/student/users/20052/edit?display_review_profile_callout=true) to learn how to create compelling visuals for your ads.

You can manage your campaign by setting up a base and maximum bid as well as a daily or lifetime budget strategy. Next, choose from several different targeting tactics:

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **Targeting tactic** | **Description** |
| [**Contextual**](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?channel=Display&article=000009847) | Shows your ad on search or item pages in your chosen department, category or subcategory. |
| [**Behavioral**](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?channel=Display&article=000009849) | Shows your ads to customers based on their online and in-store shopping behavior. There are four types of behavioral targeting options:<br>- **Brand**: Recent buyers, Lapsed buyers and Propensity audiences.<br>- **Category**: Recent buyers and In-market audiences.<br>- **Persona**: Segments customers by Lifestyle and Life stage.<br>- **Custom**: Rule-based or predictive options available to help you create custom audiences. |
| [**Keyword**](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?channel=Display&article=000009862) | Shows your ads on search pages for your chosen keywords. |
| [**Run-of-site**](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?channel=Display&article=000009851) | Enables your ad to appear across the Walmart site or app (except the homepage) with no additional targeting applied. |

Learn more about targeting best practices [here](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?channel=Display&article=000009849).

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Am I eligible for Walmart Onsite Display as a Marketplace brand owner?

Walmart Onsite Display is available to Marketplace brand owners as a [self-serve offering](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/faqs?articlenumber=000010032&article=Advertising_Basics_Display&channel=Display).

* * *

What’s the difference between Sponsored Search and Onsite Display?

[Sponsored Search](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Connect/Advertise-with-Walmart-Connect-sponsored-search) reaches customers who are actively searching Walmart’s site with intent to buy through keyword, item and SKU searches. Onsite Display uses precise audience targeting and eye-catching visuals to inspire shopper discovery and consideration across Walmart’s site, app and in our physical stores.

* * *

Is there a minimum spending requirement to use Onsite Display?

There is no minimum spending requirement to use Onsite Display, but we do recommend spending a minimum of $4,500 per campaign each month. Our Display Auction pricing model gives you full control over your campaign budget.

* * *

How do I register my brand on Walmart Marketplace?

If eligible, you can register your brand [here](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Advertising/Walmart%20Connect/hyperlink%20to:%20https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Onboarding/Register-your-brand-on-Walmart-Marketplace).

* * *

How does keyword targeting for Walmart Onsite Display campaigns differ from running Sponsored Products campaigns?

Keyword targeting within a Walmart Onsite Display campaign works similarly to a manual search campaign and operates from a list of target and negative keywords. However, keyword targeting for display campaigns will render a creative ad rather than a sponsored item.
