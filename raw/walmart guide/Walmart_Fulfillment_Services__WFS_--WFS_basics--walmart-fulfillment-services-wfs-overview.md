Overview

Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) allows Marketplace sellers to leverage Walmart’s end-to-end logistics services, from imports to storage to 2-day fulfillment\* and beyond. When a customer places an order on Walmart.com, we’ll pick, pack and ship the item to the customer on the seller’s behalf. WFS also handles all customer support and returns for these orders.

\\* Heavier items may exceed 2 days for delivery due to ground transportation.

## WFS benefits

With one of the world's largest supply chain infrastructures, WFS has the unique ability to provide sellers with the scale, quality, cost and efficiency to grow a successful business on Walmart.com. Benefits include:

- **Exceptional customer experience.** Delight customers with fast delivery, easy returns and quality customer service.


- **Cost-effective storage and fulfillment.** WFS has some of the lowest rates in the industry.\*


- **Full transparency.** Track inventory, orders and shipments with easy-to-read reports.


- **Increased site visibility.** Sellers using WFS see 50% GMV growth, on average, for items with "Fulfilled by Walmart" and "2-day shipping" tags.\*\*


- **Sell on any channel.** Centralize your inventory with WFS and leverage Walmart’s Multichannel Solutions to fulfill orders from other channels.


- **Omnichannel success.** Optimize your returns with our omnichannel [Free & Easy Returns](https://www.walmart.com/cp/free-returns/1231920?search_redirect=true&redirect_query=store%20returns&redirectQuery=store%20returns&__hstc=51647990.5ec6fe6e2db4db7de521b2b9e2064c2f.1570073339720.1571976466257.1580421683034.5&__hssc=51647990.1.1580421683034&__hsfp=3381822675) program.


- **No minimum or maximum inventory.** Send the inventory amount that works best for your business and adjust as you grow!


- **End-to-end supply chain support.** We’re a full-service solution for all your eCommerce needs, from [freight imports](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Walmart%20Cross%20Border%20-%20Imports/walmart-cross-border-imports-overview) to [preferred carriers](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/Walmart-Preferred-Carrier-(WPC)-program).


\*Walmart first-party data, 2/1/24-12/31/24.

\*\*Walmart first-party data, Feb 2024-Jan 2025.

## How does it work?

## ![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt45b34135d6793842/64f8b9bb7cf6d0000d07b16c/WFS_Intro.jpg?branch=test2)

1. **Create a Marketplace seller account in Seller Center.** If you’re already a Marketplace seller, you’ll just need to add your contact details, billing information and returns preferences in the [WFS section of Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/walmart-fulfillment).

2. **Add or convert your items to Walmart-fulfilled listings.** WFS requires a few extra details about your items in order to store and ship them.

3. **Send inventory to your assigned Walmart fulfillment centers**. Create a shipping plan of items you'll send to our fulfillment centers. As you create this, we’ll tell you where to ship your inventory (Pro tip: Save money and time here with [Inventory Transfer Service](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-Inventory-Transfer-Services) and the [Walmart Preferred Carrier Program](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Walmart-Preferred-Carrier-(WPC)-program)).

4. **Track shipments and item receiving.** You can track the status of shipments in Seller Center so you’ll know exactly when your items are received and available to Walmart.com customers. Once your shipment arrives, the receiving process typically takes 2 business days\*.

5. **Grow your business.** We’ve got storage, packaging, delivery, customer support and returns covered, but you’re the boss! Use our robust suite of reporting tools to decide where you want to drive your growth.


\\* Items that require unplanned prep work (e.g., incorrect labels, missing plan), or that arrive during sales events, holidays, peak months (October 1–December 31) or other circumstances may take up to 10 business days to process.

## Program fee structure

WFS rates average 15% less\*, on average, than most competitors, with no signup or monthly subscription fees. Our simple and straightforward rate card includes:

- Fixed monthly storage fee based on item dimensions and weight


- Fulfillment price based on item dimensions and weight


Apparel, hazardous materials, oversized items and items with low retail prices will incur add-on fees, and you may see a higher storage fee for slow-moving inventory during peak season. Visit [_WFS fees_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-fees) to see how fees apply to you, or try out our [Cost Estimator](https://marketplace.walmart.com/walmart-fulfillment-services-pricing/) to calculate the actual fees you’d pay to have an item stored and fulfilled by WFS.

\*Fulfillment cost per item, on average. Walmart first-party data, orders fulfilled 2/1/24-12/31/24.

## Product requirements

Items that meet these requirements are perfect for WFS:

- Up to 500 lb., including packaging
- Maximum dimensions of 120″ x 105″ x 93″, including packaging
- No regulated or perishable items that require temperature control
- Must ship to Walmart fulfillment centers from within the U.S. or [clear customs](https://marketplace.walmart.com/walmart-cross-border/) before arriving at our facilities
- Must be items that are permitted in the WFS program. See [_WFS Prohibited Products_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/wfs-prohibited-products-policy) and [_Marketplace Prohibited Products Policy_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Prohibited%20products%20&%20brands/Prohibited-products-policy:-overview) for details

## Additional WFS programs

Additional programs may be available to WFS sellers. Find information in the links below, and additional fees may apply.

- Inbound full and less than container load transportation from Asia with [Walmart Cross Border - Imports](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/walmart-cross-border-imports-overview).


- Fulfill customer orders from any eCommerce site with [Walmart Multichannel Solutions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-multichannel-solutions).


- Send Walmart-fulfilled items made up of multiple boxes with [Multi-Box](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-multi-box-items).


- Outsource your inventory preparation with [Prep Services](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-prep-services).


- Enjoy reduced shipping rates with the [Walmart Preferred Carrier Program](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/Walmart-Preferred-Carrier-(WPC)-program).


- Simplify distributing inventory across the WFS network with [Inventory Transfer Service](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-Inventory-Transfer-Services).


- Manage items with serial numbers through [Serial Collection](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20programs%20&%20services/WFS-serial-collection).


## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How is the WFS program different from competitors in the industry?

We work hard to maintain and grow a convenient, transparent and easy-to-use eCommerce fulfillment program. Here’s how we stand out:

- **No minimum or maximum inventory requirements.** You send us the inventory and amounts you choose.


- **No long-term storage fees for a year.** Other companies may start charging for long-term storage when inventory has been stored for 6 months, but we wait a year. Plus, you’ll always have clear, accessible insight into which inventory is approaching the one-year mark, so you can adjust your sales strategy to avoid the higher fee.


- **In-store returns.** Customers can return items to any of our nearly more than 45,5000 Walmart stores, in addition to returning by mail.


- **No private label competition.** Walmart has limited private label items that compete directly with sellers, so you’re only in competition with other sellers like you.


* * *

Do I have to be a current Marketplace seller to enroll in WFS?

Yes, you must be a Marketplace seller to join WFS.

* * *

Do you fulfill items not sold on Walmart.com?

Yes! [Walmart Multichannel Solutions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-multichannel-solutions) is a flexible, cost-effective way to fulfill customer orders from all your eCommerce sites. You can use Seller Center, API integrations and other third-party solution providers as a central hub to manage orders, returns and fulfillment across your other business channels.

* * *

Can an item be both Walmart-fulfilled and seller-fulfilled?

Not at the same time. When you set up an item as Walmart-fulfilled and send us inventory, we’ll fulfill orders for that item until the inventory you sent us runs out. Then, if our systems see that you still have inventory for that item in your fulfillment centers, fulfillment will switch to you, and you’ll be responsible to fulfill orders for that item.

* * *

Do I need to move my entire Walmart catalog to WFS to participate in the program?

Not at all! We recommend starting with a minimum of 50 items. We prefer best-sellers, premium brands or items not currently listed on Walmart.com. You can always add more items or restock as needed.

* * *

What types of items are not a good fit for WFS?

Always check the [_WFS Prohibited Products Policy_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/wfs-prohibited-products-policy) to make sure your items are allowed.

Items in highly saturated categories may not be a good fit. We also don’t recommend slow-moving, out-of-season or customizable items. Go to our [product requirements](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/walmart-fulfillment-services-wfs-overview?locale=en-US#productrequirements) for more guidelines and limitations.

* * *

What can I do to quickly improve my sales with WFS?

Based on WFS successes, we suggest:

- Prioritizing items and brands new to Walmart.com


- Pricing items competitively


- Ensuring high-quality item page content


- Providing enough inventory to meet customer demand


- Joining the [Walmart Sponsored Products program](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?language=en_US&article=000008449) to increase item visibility on Walmart.com


* * *

Do WFS items have any special branding on Walmart.com?

WFS items are displayed with a Fulfilled by Walmart tag to indicate that Walmart fulfills these Marketplace orders. These items also get 2-day shipping tags, which increases customer confidence, thereby helping you organically rank better in search results. If the customer is a W+ member, your WFS items will also have a Free shipping tag.

* * *

How does the customer returns process work?

Walmart processes and handles all WFS customer returns. Customers follow [existing Marketplace returns policies](https://corporate.walmart.com/policies#return-policy). However, all return shipping labels are set to Walmart fulfillment centers for inspection and handling. If you’ve set Keep It or Partial Keep It rules for your returns, WFS will try to honor your preferences, although in some cases this is not possible.

Learn about returns in [_WFS returns overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20basics/wfs-returns-overview).

* * *

What type of packaging options do you offer?

All your WFS orders ship in standard, Walmart-branded packaging. If you use Multichannel Solutions, your orders from other channels will be shipped in unbranded packaging. All packaging for shipping orders is included in the fulfillment fee. (Correct packaging and labeling for storage are not).

* * *

Can I send items to WFS that have been prohibited by Marketplace?

WFS is a Walmart Marketplace service, so we don’t circumvent decisions made by the eCommerce Compliance team. If you have questions regarding prohibited items or brand implications, contact Support via the _Help_ button in the Seller Center menu bar.
