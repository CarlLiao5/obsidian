Overview

As a Marketplace seller, you’re responsible for choosing a carrier method for delivery of your items, unless you choose to ship with [Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS)](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/walmart-fulfillment-services-wfs-overview). You should use a parcel carrier to ship items of standard size and weight. In this guide, you’ll learn more about Walmart-approved carriers that you can use for parcel delivery.

## How does it work?

Whichever carrier method you choose, valid tracking information must be provided to Walmart.com customers for more than 99% of your Marketplace orders, per the  [Seller Performance Standards.](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Seller-performance-standards) Walmart’s top preferred carriers are USPS, FedEx and UPS.

## Additional guidelines

### USPS

USPS ships to residential and commercial locations in all 50 states and Puerto Rico.

| Shipping Method | Dimension & Weight Limits | Ship Speed | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Priority Mail Express** | 108 inches in total length & girth; 70 lbs. max. | Overnight delivery or 2 days. | Overnight delivery with a money-back guarantee. |
| **Priority Mail** | Typically 1-3 business days; plan for 1-4 business days during holidays. | Date-specified delivery. |
| **Retail Ground** | 130 inches in total length & girth; 70 lbs. max. | Typically 5-7 business days; plan for 6-8 business days during holidays. | Best used for large packages to a commercial location. |

### FedEx

FedEx ship to residential and commercial locations in all 50 states and Puerto Rico but doesn’t ship to P.O. boxes.

| Shipping Method | Parcel or Freight | Ship Speed | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **FedEx 2Day** | Parcel Accepted dimensions: Up to 150 lbs. each; up to 119" in length, 165" in length plus girth | 2 business days | For information on shipping speed and preparing your package, refer to the [U.S. package shipping services guide](https://www.fedex.com/en-us/service-guide/us-packages.html). |
| **FedEx Express Saver** | 3 business days |
| **FedEx Ground and FedEx Ground Residential** | 1-7 business days depending on package origin and destination zip codes. |

### UPS

UPS ships to residential and commercial locations in all 50 states and Puerto Rico.

| Shipping Method | Parcel or Freight | Ship Speed | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Next Day Air** | Parcel | 1 business day/overnight | Ideal service for shipments that must arrive the next business day such as perishables. |
| **2nd Day Air** | Parcel | 2 business days | An economical option for shipments that do not need overnight service. |
| **Ground** | Either | 3-4 business days | Day-definite delivery in 1 to 5 days. |

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What if my package exceeds my carrier’s parcel shipping dimensions?

If your packages exceed standard parcel dimensions, you may need to use a freight carrier instead. For more details on when to use a freight carrier, visit [_Shipping Methods: Freight shipping carriers_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-methods:-freight-shipping-carriers) _._

* * *

Where can I learn more about carrier costs?

Reach out to your preferred carrier for more information.

* * *

Can I ship an order with a carrier that is not a Walmart-approved carrier?

No. Shipping a Walmart order using a non-approved carrier is not recommended and may result in suppression, suspension or termination because the tracking information cannot be validated by Walmart. To avoid non-compliance, ensure that you ship with a Walmart-approved parcel or freight carrier.
