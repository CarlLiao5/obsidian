How to update price: Overview

Last updated on Apr 3, 2025

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/How-to-Update-Price:-Overview#overview)
- [How does it work?](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/How-to-Update-Price:-Overview#how)
- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/How-to-Update-Price:-Overview#frequently-asked-questions)

* * *

Overview

Walmart’s Every Day Low Price (EDLP) strategy means all shoppers can get a low price on Walmart.com every day, without having to rely on coupons, promotions or special discounts. In this guide, we’ll go over a few ways to update and maintain your prices so you can remain competitive.

## How does it work?

Our [pricing rules](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Pricing-rules) are consistent with the EDLP strategy, which helps create consumer trust and reduce the need to comparison shop. As a Marketplace seller, you're responsible for setting and maintaining your prices. This chart explains the methods for updating prices and respective use cases.

| **Method** | **Use Case** |
| --- | --- |
| **Update Price Individually** | If you need to quickly update a few items, you can update the price right from your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items). For more information, check out [_Update price individually in Seller Center_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Update-price-individually-in-Seller-Center) _._ |
| **Update Price in Bulk** | To update several items at once, use one of the bulk update methods. For more information, check out [_Update price in bulk in Seller Center_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Update-price-in-bulk-in-Seller-Center) _._ |
| **API** | If you are maintaining a large catalog of items and have a technical team to support your operations, consider integrating via API. For more information, visit [_Integration methods: API_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/Integration-methods-API). |
| **Solution Provider** | Walmart Marketplace works with several third-party Solution Providers that can help you manage price updates. For more information, visit [_Choose a solution provider_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/Choose-a-solution-provider) _._ |
| **Repricer** | You can automate your pricing based on rules you set using the Repricer. The Repricer monitors prices on Walmart.com as well as external marketplaces so you don’t have to manually check or update your offers every time market conditions change. For more information, check out [_Repricer: Overview._](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Repricer:-overview) |

Notes:

Walmart will automatically unpublish items that are egregiously priced when compared to prices for the same items found elsewhere on Walmart Marketplace, on a competing website or on an external marketplace. This automation helps protect consumers by unpublishing items that are priced at levels that could potentially be viewed as unfair or abusive, such as price gouging. This calculation includes shipping fees. For more information, visit [_Pricing rules_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Pricing-rules).

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Can I offer promotional pricing to help my item prices be more competitive?

Yes. It’s recommended that you align your strategy with Walmart’s saving events online and in-store to potentially benefit from increased traffic. Visit [_Promotional pricing: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Promotions/Promotional-pricing:-Overview) for more information.

* * *

What else can I do to improve my pricing?

You can enable notifications within Seller Center to be notified of opportunities to improve your pricing. Check out [_Manage notifications in Seller Center_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Account%20settings/Manage-notifications-in-Seller-Center) for more information.

* * *

What is the recommended pricing practice if I want to cover my shipping cost?

You control shipping fees and should evaluate if you need to charge a shipping fee on any item. If you decide to charge a shipping fee, consider whether including the fee in the item price will make your offer egregiously high compared to other prices offered for the same item on Walmart.com or external competing websites. In such cases, your offer may be flagged as a violation of our Pricing Rules and, therefore, unpublished or ineligible for the Buy Box.

You can update the shipping fees by visiting [_Shipping Templates: Manage settings_.](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping%20Templates/Shipping-templates:-manage-settings)

* * *

My offer was unpublished due to the Pricing Rule. How can I republish my offer?

If prices change on Walmart.com or monitored competing websites and the item now adheres to the pricing rules, Walmart will automatically republish the offer, typically within 48 hours. You can also proactively update the price. If your item remains unpublished after you've submitted a more competitive price, continue to adjust the price until your item no longer violates the Pricing Rule.

For any unpublished item, you can also find a **Reference price** on the [**Unpublished Items**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/unpublished-items) dashboard. You can use this reference price to help you update your listing to a more competitive price and get your item republished. Walmart calculates the reference price for each item using multiple external signals, such as match price from competitor websites, historical selling price, Buy Box winner price, average offer price and Walmart e-commerce and store price.

* * *

What do I do if my items remain unpublished?

If more than 48 hours have passed and you believe your offer remains incorrectly unpublished, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support. Once you’ve confirmed the correct price and provided any necessary documentation, Walmart will review your request and may republish your offer if the price is adhering to the [pricing rules](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Pricing-rules). Walmart has pricing policies to protect both sellers and customers, so it's essential to ensure that your prices are accurate and comply with our pricing rules.
