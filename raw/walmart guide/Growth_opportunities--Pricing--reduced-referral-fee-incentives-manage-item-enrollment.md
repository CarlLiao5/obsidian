Reduced Referral Fee incentives: Manage item enrollment

Last updated on Feb 24, 2026

Reading time: 3 min

Overview

When you participate in the [Reduced Referral Fee incentives program](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Promotions/price-incentives-overview), you’ll receive a reduced Referral Fee if you keep the eligible item’s price at or below the suggested target price. In this guide, we’ll show you how to manage your item’s enrollment in the program.

## Manage item enrollment

### Single item management

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to the [**Reduced referral fees**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/incentives?tab=referral_fee) tab on the [**Incentives**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/incentives) page in Seller Center.

### Step 2 — Choose your items

- **Enroll:** To choose individual items to enroll, select the **Ready to** **enroll** tab and select the checkbox next to the items. Then, select the _Enroll_ button under the **Action** column.

- **Auto-enroll:** To auto-enroll items, select the _Activate auto-enroll_ button in the **Reduced Referral Fee** section. Then, choose your payout threshold. Your minimum payout threshold is the lowest payout percentage you’ll accept for Reduced Referral Fee incentives.

- **Unenroll**: To choose individual items to unenroll, select the _Active_ tab and select the checkbox next to the items. Then, select the _Unenroll_ button under the **Action** column.


![2602_Reduced-referral-fee-manage-item-enrollment_screenshot-1.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltf3bdda88f0603f3f/698cb4ccdaee5e56f6bd78ef/2602_Reduced-referral-fee-manage-item-enrollment_screenshot-1.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 3 – Track your performance

Use the [**Performance**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/incentives?tab=performance) tab to track key insights, monitor incentive thresholds and understand how incentives help you maintain competitive pricing without impacting your payout. Learn more about the key insights below:

| Key insights | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **Price Competitiveness Score (PCS)** | A calculation comparing your Walmart Marketplace offers to comparable external prices and factors in your items’ page views. |
| **Enrolled in program** | Number of items receiving incentives. |
| **Incentives received** | Total amount of incentives received over the last 90 days. |
| **Gross merchandise value (GMV)** | Gross merchandise value of enrolled items in the last 90 days. |
| **Traffic** | Total number of item-level page views. |
| **Savings earned** | Total amount saved from incentives in the last 90 days. |
| **Incentive type** | The type of incentive an item is receiving (e.g., Walmart-funded or Reduced Referral Fee). |

### Bulk management

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to the [**Incentives**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/incentives) page in Seller Center under the **Pricing** section. Choose the _Manage_ _reduced referral fees_ link _._

![2602_Reduced-referral-fee-manage-item-enrollment_screenshot-2.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt4b322b0b10ede024/698cb4ea7eeabd6037848653/2602_Reduced-referral-fee-manage-item-enrollment_screenshot-2.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 2 — Choose your items

On the next page, select _Generate file_ to download the most recent list of items and choose the _cloud symbol_ to download the spreadsheet. In the spreadsheet, locate the **Referral Fee Incentives Enrollment** column. Choose _Y_ if you want to enroll the itemor _N_ if you want to unenroll the item.

### Step 3 — Upload the spreadsheet

Once all updates are complete, save your spreadsheet and upload in bulk in the **Drag and drop a file here** section. When the file upload is complete, select _Submit._

Pro tip

To identify Reduced Referral Fee orders in the [**Payments Report**](https://seller.walmart.com/payments/statements/period) **,** filter the **Commission Incentive Program** column for **Pricing Incentives**. For incentive amounts applied to your orders, locate the **Commission Savings** column. To learn how to access your **Payments Report**, visit [_Payment statements and transactions_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Payments/Payment-statements-and-transactions).

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What determines the Target price for incentive suggestions?

Target prices are determined by competitive prices in the market and internal calculations.

* * *

What happens to Referral Fee incentives that I’ve unenrolled from the Active tab?

It’ll appear in the **Ready to enroll** tab.

* * *

How long does it take after enrolling and unenrolling an incentive to view the price adjustment?

It can take as long as four hours to fully process your latest enrollment configurations.

* * *

What happens when a Reduced Referral Fee incentive expires?

When a Reduced Referral Fee incentive reaches its expiration date, the item price will stay at the incentivized price and the standard referral fee will apply.

* * *

What happens if I submit a price update for an item receiving a Reduced Referral Fee before the incentive expiration date?

If your submitted price update is at or below the Target price, you’ll continue receiving the incentive amount until the expiration date. If your submitted price is above the Target price, you’ll stop receiving Reduced Referral Fee incentives.

* * *

What’s the frequency of new incentive suggestions in the Ready to enroll tab?

For eligible items, new price incentives are added weekly on Monday mornings, with other incentive suggestions added mid-week.
