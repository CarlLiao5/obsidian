Add a partner fulfillment center in Seller Center

Last updated on Dec 3, 2024

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Fulfillment%20settings/add-a-partner-fulfillment-center-in-seller-center#frequently-asked-questions)

* * *

Overview

If you’ve chosen to fulfill your Marketplace orders through our third-party fulfillment providers **Deliverr** (also known as Flexport) and **Shipbob**, you’ll need to add the fulfillment center details to your Seller Center account. Keep reading to learn how.

## Add a partner fulfillment center

### Step 1 – Get started

Once you’ve signed up for Deliverr or Shipbob, you can add the fulfillment center details in Seller Center. Log in to Seller Center, select the gear icon in the Seller Center menu bar and navigate to **Shipping Profile**. Select _Seller Fulfillment_.

### Step 2 – Add the fulfillment center

Select the _Add Fulfillment Center_  button and navigate to the **Select Fulfillment Option** dropdown menu. Select _Fulfillment Partner_, choose your provider and select the _Add_ button. The provider will now fulfill the SKUs that you’ve mapped to this fulfillment center. If you have any questions, reach out to them directly.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt31e9fcfc8555f5c1/674f64d1fe6ad54fa88b5207/2412_11593_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

You’re automatically eligible for expedited shipping when you fulfill items through Deliverr. To use Standard or Value shipping for Deliverr-fulfilled items, contact Deliverr and ask them to upload a Shipping Overrides file.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Can I disable Deliverr?

Yes. To do so, log in to Seller Center **,** select your profile photo and choose Account settings **.** Under [**Shipping Profile**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/seller-fulfillment), choose _Seller Fulfillment_. On the **Seller Fulfillment Settings** screen, locate the Deliverr Fulfillment Center. On the far-right side, select the pencil icon. Toggle the button to _Disabled_.

* * *

What are Deliverr’s product eligibility requirements?

Items must weigh less than 75 pounds. Maximum product dimensions: The longest side must be less than 40", the median side less than 30" and the shortest side less than 15". See Deliverr’s [additional product restrictions](https://www.flexport.com/products/d2c-fulfillment/).

* * *

How do I sign up for Deliverr?

If you’re interested in using Deliverr’s services to fulfill your Marketplace orders, you can [sign up](https://deliverr.com/walmart/) on their website. You can preview the cost for each item in your catalog. Follow their directions to confirm your participation, and then you’ll automatically be approved for the Walmart TwoDay delivery program.
