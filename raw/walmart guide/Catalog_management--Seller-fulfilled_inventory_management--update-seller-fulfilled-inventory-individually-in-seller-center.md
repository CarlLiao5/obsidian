Overview

Updating your inventory within Seller Center can be quick and easy. In this guide, you'll learn how to update your inventory for a small group of items using your [**Catalog.**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items)

Notes:

You must have an existing fulfillment center added to your Seller Center account before you can update your inventory. After you’ve added the fulfillment center, wait at least four hours before mapping inventory. If you attempt to update inventory before your facility is fully processed in our system, you'll receive an error.

## Update inventory

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center.

### Step 2 – Locate the item

Locate the item you want to update. You can search for the item by SKU, Item ID, Item Name, GTIN or UPC.

### Step 3 – Update item inventory

Under the _More actions_, select _Update inventory_. An inventory screen will populate, prompting you to enter your fulfillment center and inventory counts. Complete this information and select _Update_ to save and submit.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How can I find my Fulfillment Center ID?

To find your Fulfillment Center ID follow the instructions found within [_Shipping Templates – assign SKUs_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping%20Templates/Shipping-templates:-Assign-SKUs).

* * *

How can I update my fulfillment center information?

To set up multiple fulfillment centers, visit [_Add a seller-managed fulfillment center in Seller Center_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Fulfillment%20settings/add-a-seller-managed-fulfillment-center-in-seller-center).

* * *

How can I check the status of my inventory update?

You can track progress in the [**Activity feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed).

* * *

What do I do if I submitted my item and it didn’t publish?

The [**Activity feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) allows you to check the status of your item as the upload process completes. If there is an error, it will display in the status. Use the error information to correct the issue and resubmit the item.

* * *

I am a LocalFinds seller – do I need to do anything differently to update my inventory?

If you’re a LocalFinds seller, select one of your stores before updating the inventory by selecting the [**All locations**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) button at the top of the page.
