Shipping methods: Freight shipping carriers

Last updated on Mar 31, 2026

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

Overview

As a Marketplace seller, you’re responsible for choosing a carrier method for delivery of your items, unless you choose to ship with [Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS)](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/walmart-fulfillment-services-wfs-overview). In this guide, you’ll learn about Walmart-approved freight carriers and how to ship items by freight if they meet the requirements.

## How does it work?

If an item's weight is greater than 150 pounds, you’re required to ship it by freight.

### Approved freight carriers

Consider using one of the following Walmart-approved carriers for freight and less-than-truckload (LTL) shipments:

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| - ABF Logistics<br>- AIT Worldwide<br>- AM Trucking<br>- AxleHire<br>- CEVA<br>- DHL Freight<br>- Estes<br>- FIRST MILE<br>- GLS<br>- LSO<br>- Metropolitan Warehouse & Delivery<br>- NTW | - PCF<br>- Maersk<br>- PITT OHIO<br>- Roadrunner Freight<br>- R + L Carriers<br>- SAIA<br>- Seko Worldwide<br>- Southeastern Freight Lines<br>- TForce Freight<br>- XPO Logistics<br>- UDS |

Notes:

Valid tracking information must be provided to Walmart.com customers for more than 99% of your Marketplace orders, per Walmart’s [Seller Performance Standards.](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Seller-performance-standards)

## Additional guidelines

If you’ve determined that using a freight carrier is right for you, follow these next steps.

1. Choose your preferred carrier from the list above.

2. Contact the carrier for their pre-assigned PRO number (a seven-to-10-digit number used to track and organize shipments).

3. Provide Bill of Lading (BOL) samples to your warehouse for reference.

4. Prepare the order and packaging as you would for any LTL ground transportation shipment.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How do I ship a standard-sized parcel?

Get details of parcel shipping carrier methods by visiting [_Shipping methods: Parcel shipping carriers_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-methods:-parcel-shipping-carriers).

* * *

Where can I learn more about carrier costs?

Reach out to your preferred carrier for more information.

* * *

Can I ship an order with a carrier that’s not a Walmart-approved carrier?

No. Shipping a Walmart order using a non-approved carrier is not recommended and may result in suppression, suspension or termination because the tracking information cannot be validated by Walmart. To avoid non-compliance, ensure that you ship with a Walmart-approved parcel or freight carrier.
