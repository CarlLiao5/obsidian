Weekend delivery settings

Last updated on Dec 2, 2024

Reading time: 2 min

Overview

If you already work with carriers that deliver on the weekend, or are looking to start, we recommend opting in to Weekend Delivery in your Seller Fulfillment Settings in Seller Center.

## How does it work?

Weekend Delivery refers to the days that your shipping carrier can transit and deliver packages on Saturdays and Sundays. This allows Walmart to accurately calculate the Estimated Delivery Date (EDD) for Marketplace orders.

## Additional guidelines

Navigate to the **Weekend Delivery** heading in your [fulfillment settings](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/shipping-info). Select the checkbox next to Saturday or Sunday to include these days in your EDD calculation. If you're unsure whether your carriers offer weekend delivery, check their Transit/Service map that is available on their respective website.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Will I automatically be opted-in to Weekend Delivery?

No. You must opt-in.

* * *

Will Weekend Delivery impact my Seller Performance metrics?

Opting into Weekend Delivery can help improve the accuracy of your performance metrics by including the weekend in delivery calculations. This is reflected to customers on Walmart.com by showing accurate delivery estimates to help them make informed purchasing decisions. Opting into Weekend Delivery will not negatively impact your On-Time Delivery score.

* * *

I’m unsure if my carrier ships and delivers on the weekend. What should I do?

Check the Transit/Service map that’s available on the carrier’s website.
