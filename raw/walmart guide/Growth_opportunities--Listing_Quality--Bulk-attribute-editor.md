Listing Quality: Bulk Attribute Editor

Overview

The Bulk Attribute Editor helps you improve your Content quality score, which is one of the key components of your overall [Listing Quality](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/listing-quality) score. In this guide, you’ll learn how to use this tool to update multiple item attributes simultaneously, such as the item’s name, description, key features and images.

Notes:

Some attributes can only be updated by the [brand owner or authorized resellers](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/How-to-update-content:-Overview).

## Improve your content quality score

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to the [**Listing Quality**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/listing-quality) dashboard in Seller Center and select _Create spreadsheet_ to begin setting up your bulk edit file.

![2602_Listing-quality_create-spreadsheet_screenshot-1.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltab09dbcdd3ee23d2/69934ce779d8c8905975dbb4/2602_Listing-quality_create-spreadsheet_screenshot-1.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 2 — Generate your spreadsheet

Use the filters to sort items by **Customer Favorites**, **WFS items**, **Attributes with Issues** or **Refine by Item.** Under the **Spec Category** **&** **Product Type** section, select the radio button next to the product type to display all attributes for that product type. After selecting your filters and attributes, select _Create spreadsheet_ to generate your file.

| Filters | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Customer Favorites** | Your most searched and best-selling items. |
| **Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) Items** | Your active WFS items. |
| **Attributes with issues** | Attributes that need to be edited to improve your Listing Quality score. |
| **Refine by item** | Apply additional filters to narrow in your list based on its Listing Quality score, GMV or page views. |

![2602_Listing-quality_create-spreadsheet_screenshot-2.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltbb1ab1cd6313db79/69934ce73833cb630f08a49d/2602_Listing-quality_create-spreadsheet_screenshot-2.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 3 — Download & submit your spreadsheet

When your spreadsheet is ready, select _Go to Activity Feed_ to view it. In the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed), find your file under the **Downloads** tab and select the _download icon_ under the **Action** column. Open the downloaded spreadsheet, fill in or edit the attribute values you want to change and save your updates. Then return to the Listing Quality dashboard and select _Upload spreadsheet_ to submit your updated file.

Your score may not be updated immediately. Newly published offers may take up to a week to reflect within the **Listing Quality** dashboard.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What are the benefits of improving my score?

By improving your score, you’ll increase your chances of achieving [Pro Seller](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Items%20and%20inventory/pro-seller-overview) status.

* * *

Where can I find extra support?

Interested in a classroom style learning session? [Register here](https://feedbackally.walmart.com/survey/UlLgYypvvIahLRl/) for a Marketplace bootcamp where you’ll learn how to create high-quality listings and apply marketing strategies to boost visibility, traffic and growth.
