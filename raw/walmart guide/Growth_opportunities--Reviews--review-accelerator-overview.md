Overview

Review Accelerator offers effective solutions to help you collect more customer reviews for your Marketplace products. In this guide, you’ll learn about the solutions and how to participate.

## How does it work?

From the [**Review Accelerator**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/review-accelerator) page in Seller Center, you can view item eligibility, enroll items in the following programs and monitor your results. We offer the following programs.

| Program | Learn more | Item eligibility criteria |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Post-Purchase Reviews | Encourage customers to leave a review by [offering incentives](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Reviews/Review-Accelerator-Program:-How-to-enroll) after they make a purchase. Choose this option for items that are getting enough orders but not enough reviews. | - Item must have less than 15 reviews<br>- Item must have prior sales history |
| Recognized Reviewer program | Offer free [product samples](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Reviews/recognized-reviewer-program-how-to-enroll-items) to a select group of trusted reviewers. Choose this option to help boost visibility and sales for items that have no or few reviews. | - Item must have less than 10 reviews |

Notes:

Meeting the listed item eligibility criteria does not guarantee program inclusion. Walmart reserves the right to determine item and seller eligibility at its sole discretion.

## Additional guidelines

Participation in Review Accelerator requires that all reviews comply with Federal Trade Commission (FTC) regulations and [Walmart Marketplace guidelines](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Account/Walmart-Marketplace-seller-retailer-policies). You may not directly solicit reviews outside of Walmart’s Review Accelerator programs (e.g., Post-Purchase Reviews and Recognized Reviewer).

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How are reviews from the Review Accelerator programs displayed?

Incentivized reviews are labeled and displayed alongside organic reviews on item pages.

* * *

How can I increase my Marketplace reviews?

You can increase your Marketplace reviews through the Review Accelerator program or [Review Syndication](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Reviews/Marketplace-reviews:-review-syndication).
