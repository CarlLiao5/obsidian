Overview

The **Sales Rewards and Attribution** program lets you earn up to 10% on qualifying sales of your items when you drive traffic to Walmart.com from other sources. You'll have access to detailed performance insights to make data-driven decisions. In this guide, you’ll learn more about the Sales Rewards and Attribution tool and the benefits of the program.

## How does it work?

After accepting the Terms & Conditions in Seller Center, you can generate unique tracking URLs for any of your owned and commission-eligible items through the **Sales Rewards and Attribution** program. You can apply these URLs to your non-Walmart marketing or share with external parties, such as influencers or media partners, to apply to media campaigns.

Notes:

There isn’t an option to build tracking URLs for non-commissionable items or for items you don’t own.

## Additional guidelines

For eligible items purchased within 14 days of a customer selecting your uniquely generated URL, you'll earn 10% commission about 65 days after the customer’s transaction. Exclusions may apply.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How does participating in this program affect my Referral Fees?

Walmart Marketplace will continue to deduct [Referral Fees](https://marketplace.walmart.com/referral-fees/) from each completed purchase for your owned items. This pricing structure exists regardless of whether you participate in the Walmart Sales Rewards and Attribution program. Participating in the rewards program can offset these Referral Fees, thus increasing your net profit.

* * *

Is this commission available for Search Engine Marketing (SEM) and Walmart Connect?

No, the bonus is only available for non-Walmart marketing channels.

* * *

When will I receive commission payout?

Approximately 65 days following the purchase of qualifying products. You’ll be credited with commissions earned on qualifying products that were shipped during that month to your credit card or bank account on file within Seller Center.

* * *

Are any categories excluded from receiving commission payout?

Yes, not all products sold on the site are eligible to earn commission in the program. To learn more about current exclusions, email [walmartsalesrewards@walmart.com](mailto:walmartsalesrewards@walmart.com).

This offer applies as long as the customer reenters Walmart.com directly during that time. We will not pay you if the customer re-entry can be attributed to a Walmart driven campaign, a campaign run by any other affiliate partner, or an external free or organic search. We will not pay commission on any products that are purchased on our Site when a customer has re-entered our Site (other than through a Qualifying Link from your website) after the 14 day period.
