Overview

The Walmart Marketplace Seller Forum is a space to collaborate, ask questions and share best practices. In this guide, you’ll learn how to use the forum effectively to find information, share insights and interact with other sellers.

## How does it work?

You can access the forum  by logging into [Marketplace Learn](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/forum?forcedLogin=true) where you’ll be assigned an anonymous username that will display each time you log in. Once logged in, you can [post new discussions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/seller-forum-post-a-new-discussion), comment and like posts, bookmark helpful discussions and earn badges. If you’re not logged in with an active Seller Center account, you can view discussions; however, you won’t be able to interact or post.

Walmart moderators oversee the forum to help maintain forum guidelines, verify accurate responses and to help you stay up to date on the latest program and policy changes. Before posting, you must search the forum to find out if the topic or thread exists already to avoid creating duplicate posts.

Notes:

You must adhere to the [Seller Forum policy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/seller-forum-policy).

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Can I use one profile to manage multiple seller accounts?

No. Each profile is linked to a single Seller Center account.

* * *

What is the difference between the forum and Seller Support?

Use the forum for general questions and discussions that may benefit other sellers. For account-specific issues, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

Are my posts anonymous?

While your username is anonymous, Walmart can still view account information.

* * *

How long will it take to get a response to my posts?

Response times depend on how quickly other sellers respond to the discussion. If a discussion requires input from a Walmart moderator, you can expect a response within two business days.
