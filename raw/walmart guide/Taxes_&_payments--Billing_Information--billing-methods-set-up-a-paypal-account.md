Billing methods: Set up a PayPal account

Last updated on Sep 28, 2025

Reading time: 4 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Billing%20Information/billing-methods-set-up-a-paypal-account#overview)
- [Set up your account](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Billing%20Information/billing-methods-set-up-a-paypal-account#set)
- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Billing%20Information/billing-methods-set-up-a-paypal-account#frequently-asked-questions)

* * *

Overview

You can set up a PayPal Business account to your Seller Center account as a billing method to pay for charges that exceed your sales revenue in each billing cycle. In this guide, you’ll learn how to add or update your PayPal Business account within Seller Center.

Notes:

The option to use a PayPal Business account as a billing method is by invitation-only and not available to all Marketplace sellers. If you’re interested and haven’t been invited, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

## Set up your account

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to the [**Billing services**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/financial-settings/billing-services) page in Seller Center.

### Step 2 – Add PayPal payment method

To update your billing method, select _Add billing method._ Choose _PayPal Business account_ and select _Continue_ **.** Once you’ve confirmed that you’ve read and accepted all terms and conditions, select the _PayPal_ button.

Notes:

Only PayPal Business accounts are supported for billing. Personal PayPal accounts aren’t accepted.

![2509_billing-methods-paypal.gif](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt01f2317ebc5395ea/68c43708db062bcfeb266aa7/2509_billing-methods-paypal.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 3- Authorize with PayPal

You’ll be securely redirected to the official PayPal login portal, where you must sign in using your PayPal Business account credentials and authorize Walmart to use the account for billing. Choose the appropriate card or bank account for your billing method then select _Agree and Continue_.

Once this step is complete, you’ll be redirected back to Seller Center, and your PayPal Business account will be successfully added as your active billing method.

Notes:

Your PayPal Business account must be active, verified and have a valid payment method such as a linked bank account or credit card. Walmart will only charge your account when your sales revenue is insufficient to cover the total fees due to Walmart or its affiliates at the end of a billing cycle.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How do I change my default billing method?

To update your default billing method, navigate to the **Billing services** page, select _Set as Default_ to manually select your default billing method. There can only be one default billing method listed on your account.

* * *

Can I update my PayPal Business payment method or account?

To update your PayPal account or payment method within an existing account, delete the existing PayPal billing method in Seller Center then add the new account by repeating the process outlined in Step 2 of this guide.

* * *

How often will my account be charged?

The frequency of PayPal charges by Walmart will vary depending on your settlement cycle and your use of various Walmart services. All charges from your PayPal Business account will follow the terms set in the [Marketplace Billing Authorization](https://6064071.fs1.hubspotusercontent-na1.net/hubfs/6064071/Walmart%20Marketplace%20Billing%20Authorization.pdf). Walmart reserves the right to charge your PayPal account on a recurring basis or periodically, outside of your standard billing cycle, as deemed necessary or appropriate in its sole and absolute discretion.

* * *

Do I have to pay a processing fee?

No. Walmart doesn’t charge payment processing fees.

* * *

Will I get notification about the billing charges?

Yes, you’ll receive a communication before the PayPal charge is initiated. You can also find details about your outstanding balance, transactions and account activity by visiting the [**Statements**](https://seller.walmart.com/payments/statements/period) page in Seller Center.

* * *

What happens if my payment fails?

You’ll immediately be notified by email and Seller Center. You’ll also be prompted to update your billing method or retry payment. If unresolved, your account may be subject to temporary restrictions until the issue is resolved.

* * *

I’ve received a connection error. What should I do?

If authentication fails, you’ll be asked to re-enter your PayPal email address or phone number and password to log into your PayPal account. If you continue to experience issues, select the _Help_ button in the Seller Center menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

Can I switch to ACH or credit card later?

Yes. You can switch between any available billing methods at any time through Seller Center. To prevent billing disruption, there may be instances where a newly added billing method won't take effect until the following billing cycle.

* * *

Is the billing method the same as the payout method?

No. The billing method is solely for paying service fees and outstanding dues to Walmart Marketplace. The payout method is for receiving payouts from Walmart Marketplace through payout service providers.
