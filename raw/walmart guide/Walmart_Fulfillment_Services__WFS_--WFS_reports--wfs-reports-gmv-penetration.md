WFS reports: GMV penetration

Reading time: 2 min

Overview

The Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) report shows you what percentage of your total sales come from Walmart-fulfilled items. It gives you greater visibility into WFS sales at a weekly, daily and item level.

## Access this report

1. Log in to Seller Center.
2. Navigate to the [**WFS reports**](https://seller.walmart.com/wfs/reports) page.
3. Under **Growth**, select _Download_ for the **GMV penetration** report.
4. Set your desired time period.
5. Select _Download_ and open the report.

## Dive into details

### How to use this report effectively

- Get a 12-week view of your WFS GMV divided by your total GMV for all Marketplace items.
- Download this 4-tab report to see a breakdown of your overall, daily and item-level GMV penetration percentage on a weekly basis.
- Weekly buckets will be defined Sunday through Saturday.

### Important insights

- Shifting items from seller-fulfilled to WFS-fulfilled, or improving the inventory availability will increase your penetration.
- Shifting items from seller-fulfilled to WFS-fulfilled will increase your GMV based on faster delivery speeds and consistent experience.
- If your GMV penetration is low, you may be missing out on sales. Help maximize your sales by converting more items to Walmart-fulfilled or keeping your Walmart-fulfilled inventory in stock.

## Understand report metrics

|     |     |     |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Tab** | **Column name** | **Description** |
| Overall | Overall GMV Penetration(Avg of 12 weeks) | Over the last 12 weeks, see an average of the WFS GMV divided by your total GMV. |
|  | Week(Sunday-Saturday) | GMV broken down by week with the dates listed as column headers |
| Daily | Week | 7-day period included in the summary |
|  | Average Weekly | Weekly GMV listed by dates |
|  | Days of the week buckets | GMV broken down by day of week as column headers |
| Item Level | GTIN | 14-digit Global Trade Item Number, including check digit, that's unique to the item. If it's less than 14 digits, add zeros at the beginning |
|  | Item Name | Description of the item entered by the Seller on item setup |
|  | Vendor SKU | Vendor Sku Id is a unique item identifier provided during item setup, that represents the item purchased by the customer |
|  | Product ID | Unique item identifier used for internal Walmart systems |
|  | Weekly buckets | GMV broken down by week listed as column headers |
| Glossary | Term and definition | Easily translate the data in the report by the terms you see in the glossary |
