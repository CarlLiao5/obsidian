Walmart-funded incentives: Manage item enrollment

Last updated on Feb 24, 2026

Reading time: 3 min

* * *

Overview

Your participation in the [Walmart-funded incentives program](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Promotions/price-incentives-overview) is by default. If you want to actively participate in the program, but don’t want to include every item in your catalog, you can manage enrollment for each of your items individually. In this guide, we’ll show you how to unenroll and re-enroll your items in Seller Center.

Notes:

Walmart-funded incentives are provided at Walmart’s discretion. Walmart doesn’t guarantee that you’ll receive incentives for enrolled items.

## Manage item enrollment

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to the [**Incentives**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/incentives) page in Seller Center under the **Pricing** section **.**

### Step 2 — Choose your items

Here are few ways you can manage your item enrollment:

- **Unenroll individual items:** To choose a few items to unenroll in Seller Center, navigate to the [**Walmart-funded incentives**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/incentives?tab=walmart_funded) tab. Select the items you want to unenroll, then select the three vertical dots in the **Action** column and choose _Unenroll item_. Next, select the _Unenroll_ button.

Notes:

To re-enroll your items, follow the steps below.

- **Unenroll or re-enroll in bulk:** Choose the **Walmart-funded incentives** tab and select _Manage Walmart-funded incentives_. Select _Generate file_ to download the most recent list of items and choose the _cloud symbol_ to download the template. Open the spreadsheet and navigate to the **Walmart-funded Incentives Enrollment** column. Choose _Opt-in_ or _Opt-out_. Once all updates are complete, save your spreadsheet and upload the template in the **Drag and Drop a file here** section. When the spreadsheet upload is complete, select _Submit._

### Step 3 —Track your performance

Use the [**Performance**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/incentives?tab=performance) tab to track key insights and understand how incentives help you maintain competitive pricing without impacting your payout. Learn more about the key insights below:

| Key insights | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **Price Competitiveness Score (PCS)** | A calculation comparing your Walmart Marketplace offers to comparable external prices and factors in your items’ page views. |
| **Enrolled in program** | Number of items receiving incentives. |
| **Incentives received** | Total amount of incentives received over the last 90 days. |
| **Gross merchandise value (GMV)** | Gross merchandise value of enrolled items in the last 90 days. |
| **Traffic** | Total number of item-level page views. |
| **Savings earned** | Total amount saved from incentives in the last 90 days. |
| **Incentive type** | The type of incentive an item is receiving (e.g., Walmart-funded or Reduced Referral Fee). |

Pro tip

To identify Walmart-funded orders in the [**Payments Report**](https://seller.walmart.com/payments/statements/period), filter the **Total Walmart Funded Savings Program** column for **Pricing Incentives.** For incentive amounts applied to your orders, filter the **Amount Type** columnfor the **Total Walmart Funded Savings Program.**

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Do Walmart-funded incentives expire?

Walmart-funded incentives can expire at Walmart’s discretion and the item price will revert to your last submitted price.

* * *

What happens if items are unenrolled from Walmart-funded incentives?

When items are unenrolled, they’re removed from any current and future Walmart-funded incentives until they are opted-in again.

* * *

Will I receive incentives for all items opted-in to Walmart-funded incentives?

No, Walmart doesn’t guarantee incentives even if the item is enrolled because these incentives are provided at Walmart’s discretion.

* * *

How does a Referral Fee adjustment affect my payment?

Although the price displayed and charged to the customer is lower than the price provided by you, your payment isn’t affected. You’ll receive the same net amount as if the item were sold at the price submitted due to Walmart-funded incentives.

* * *

What happens if I set a promotion for an item?

The item price under the program is calculated from the current offer, including any promotions.

* * *

How can I tell if a customer order contains an item receiving Walmart-funded incentives?

Customer orders will display a Price Incentive within a pop-up window indicating which incentive program.

* * *

How do I ensure that MAPs (Minimum Advertised Prices) aren’t violated?

You should exclude MAP items using the bulk template process. You can add additional SKUs to the list to opt-out as needed.

* * *

How do I turn off Walmart-funded incentives?

To turn off Walmart-funded incentives, follow the steps highlighted in [_Walmart-funded: Program enrollment_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-funded-program-enrollment).

* * *

How do I access my Payments Report?

For more information on how to access your Payments Report, visit [_Payment Statements and transactions_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Payments/Payment-statements-and-transactions) _._
