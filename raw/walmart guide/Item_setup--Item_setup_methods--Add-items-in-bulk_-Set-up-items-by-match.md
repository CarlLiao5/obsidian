Add multiple items: Match Walmart’s catalog

Last updated on Mar 4, 2026

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

Overview

This guide shows you how to add multiple items to your catalog in Seller Center by matching them to products that are currently listed on Walmart.com. If you’re adding new items to your catalog that aren’t currently offered on Walmart.com, review [Add multiple items: Full setup.](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup%20methods/Add-items-in-bulk:-full-setup)

## Match Walmart's catalog

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center and select the _Add items_ button. Select the _Upload in bulk_ link under **Add thousands of items using a spreadsheet**. On the next page, navigate to the **Match items** section and select the _Build spreadsheet_ button. If you sell in multiple markets, choose the markets you want to apply the changes to.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt9f22ce8cc0098a49/6840a8726b876a3f85f52f21/2505_add-items-bulk-setup-by-match_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 2 — Download and complete the template

Choose how you want to fulfill your items and select _Download file_. Then, open the template. Template sections are grouped by their requirement level: **Required**, **Conditional/Required for Specific Conditions** and **Optional**.

- **SKU:** This is required to successfully set up your offer.
- **Required:** This first section must be completely filled out. If any fields are missing, you’ll receive data errors.
- **Conditional/Required for Specific Conditions:** The Main Image URL of the item is required for the following conditions: **Remanufactured**, **Pre-Owned: Like New**, **Pre-Owned: Good**, **Pre-Owned: Fair** or **New with defects**.

- **Optional:**

  - **Inventory:** You can add seller-fulfilled inventory by providing the fulfillment center and quantity.
  - **Additional Image URL:** You can add additional images for an item. Make sure your images follow the [_Product detail page: Image guidelines & requirements_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-detail-page:-Image-guidelines-&-requirements) _._
  - **Sales Restrictions:** If the product must or should be prohibited from being sold in certain states and ZIP Codes because of legal or commercial reasons, you must fill out these details.

Notes:

Failing to provide the required attributes or conditionally required information, modifying the data in the first six rows of your template and more will lead to data errors.Check out [_Troubleshoot item setup errors_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Troubleshooting/troubleshoot-item-setup-errors?locale=en-US) to learn more _._

### Step 3 — Upload the template

To upload your completed template, navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center and select _Add items_. Select the _Upload in bulk_ link located under **Add thousands of items using a spreadsheet**. You can drag and drop your file onto the browser window under **Upload**, or select the _browse_ link to locate the file on your computer.

After submitting your template, you can track its status from the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) in Seller Center. Updates may take up to 24 hours. Any data errors that are detected during the updates will require extra processing time.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What if I can’t find matches for my items in the Walmart.com catalog?

If you didn’t find a match, we recommend using the **Full Setup** template to add new items to your Catalog in bulk. For more information, review [_Add multiple items: Full setup_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Add-items-in-bulk:-full-setup).

* * *

What if I only need to match one item?

You can use the **Express setup** feature. For more information, review [_Add multiple items: Express setup_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Add-a-new-item:-Search-the-Walmart-catalog).

* * *

How do I add a market if I want to sell an item internationally?

To learn how to add more markets to your account, visit [_Global onboarding: Set up an additional market_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/global-onboarding-set-up-an-additional-market) _._

* * *

What if I don’t know my product tax code?

A product tax code must be assigned to each type of shipping fee that you offer in order to assess the correct sales tax. To review and assign your tax codes:

- **U.S. and California sellers:** Navigate to your **Account** page in [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home), select [_Taxes_](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/partner-profile/taxes) and locate the **Shipping Sales Tax Codes** section.
- **Mexico sellers:** Verify your product tax code [here](http://pys.sat.gob.mx/PyS/catPyS.aspx).

* * *

What if I can’t find my product tax code?

You can find a link to the tax code for your items within the tooltips guidance (info icon) of the item setup process.

* * *

What if I don’t know my item’s country of origin?

We recommend that you confirm the product’s country of origin through the manufacturer, matching the product in the Walmart catalog or validating the specific item. If you can’t identify the country of origin, your item won’t be eligible to cross-list.

* * *

How is setting up a Walmart-fulfilled item different from seller-fulfilled?

With seller-fulfilled items, you use your own fulfillment centers to manage, store and ship your products. When you choose Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), we pick, pack and ship orders for you.

During item setup with WFS, you’ll also need to enter more required information, like country of origin, dimensions, [compliance](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup) and [trade item hierarchy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-trade-item-configuration).
