Overview

This guide shows you how to access your payment statements and transactions in Seller Center. Navigate to the [**Payments**](https://seller.walmart.com/partner-analytics/payments/statements/period) dashboard to view a summary of your payment activity per settlement cycle, or the _Transactions_ tab to search transactions for orders created in the past 90 days.

## Statements

You can use the dropdown to select a _Period summary_ or an _Annual summary._ You can view your outstanding balance for an open pay cycle as well as the scheduled settlement date.  Balances appear in this view approximately 24-48 hours after you update an order to _Shipped_.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt6e91fcea08112235/67786b53f343449c30466912/2501_11521_screenshot-1.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

The terms Referral Fee and Commission are used interchangeably in this guide. The Marketplace Retailer Agreement uses the term Referral Fee. The Payments Report uses the term Commission.

## Payments reports

You have the option to download a **Payments Report**(also called a Reconciliation Report) in the upper right corner of the **Statements** screen by selecting the _Download_ link. The **Payments Report** provides details for all order activity during the payment period, such as shipping charges, commission rates and more.

To export your **Payments Report,** select the _Download_ link at the top right of the screen and choose from the dropdown menu. To see a summarized report, download the _new_ version. To see a more detailed view of the report, download the _legacy_ version.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt8136b6dd1ba02603/67786b53a43ac24db381878f/2501_11521_screenshot-2.png?branch=us_mplearn)

You should review the **Payments Report** each pay cycle to verify that all transactions were processed as expected.  If you use the API, visit the [_Developer Portal Guide_](https://developer.walmart.com/#preloginModal?redirectUri=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.walmart.com%2Faccount%2FgenerateKey%3F_gl%3D1*10d99k0*_gcl_au*NjM3ODE2MTYxLjE3MTgxMTI5ODY.*_ga*MTkxMjA5OTE4NS4xNzE4MDYxOTI3*_ga_1LB22TM2MG*MTcxOTkzMTUyNC4zNy4xLjE3MTk5MzIwODAuMjkuMC4w) for details on how to pull a **Payments Report**.

## Transactions

The [**Transactions**](https://seller.walmart.com/payments/transactions) page allows you to view transactions by searching for a **Purchase order (PO#)** or **Customer Order (CO#)**. You can also use filters to search by transaction type or date.

To review more details about a transaction, select the _ellipsis_ at the end of thetransaction line and then select _View details._ If you need to export transactions for your records, select the _check box_ at the beginning of the line, then select _Download transactions_ in the upper right corner.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blta9f3de6a21285433/66aa7d867e926fde86d6ecb6/11521_GIF.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

Order total Revenue shows the net amount that you earned or will earn on that line item for the order after factoring in all transactions, such as sale, customer refund and disputes.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How frequently will I get paid?

Your payment frequency is determined by your agreement with Walmart. If you have forgotten your payment frequency, you should check either your Walmart Marketplace Retailer Agreement or the dates on your previous **Payments Reports** to view the payment cycle.

* * *

Why does my Payment Summary report say I have a hold on my payments?

New sellers will experience a payment hold that varies based on their business location. Visit [_Payment activities_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Payments/Payment-activities) for more information.

* * *

Why is there a promo code listed on my statement if I’m not offering any promotional discounts?

Customers can receive a promo code from any Walmart led discount program even if you’re not actively offering any promotional discounts. They can apply the promo code to any purchase on Walmart.com. If you’re not running a promotion, the promo code is funded by Walmart. Your payment will not be affected.
