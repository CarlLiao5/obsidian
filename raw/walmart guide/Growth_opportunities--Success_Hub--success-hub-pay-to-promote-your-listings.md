Success Hub: Search Engine Marketing (SEM)

Last updated on Nov 13, 2024

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

Overview

Success Hub suggests a curated set of item recommendations to add to your Search Engine Marketing (SEM) campaigns. In this guide, you’ll learn how to create these campaigns so that you can promote your products within Google search results.

## Reach customers with paid SEM

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to the [**Growth page**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/success-hub) in Seller Center to access **Success Hub** and choose _R_ _each customers with paid SEM_ **.** Then, select _Create campaign._

### Step 2 – Set up your campaign

Next, fill out the **Choose your budget**, **Select dates** and **Name your campaign** fields. You may also opt to receive and customize your campaign notifications.

### Step 3 – Add items and start campaign

Select the items to add to a campaign, then select _Create campaign_. Your campaign will begin and you’ll be charged when shoppers select your ads in Google search results.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How are the item suggestions curated?

Item suggestions are selected on various factors including demand and offer quality.

* * *

How do I pay for the campaigns?

You’re only charged when potential customers select your ads on the Google search results page and land on your product page on Walmart.com. Campaign charges will be billed through the [normal settlement process](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Payments/Payment-processing). If you have a payment method on file and your campaign charges exceed your account balance, we’ll bill the payment method you have on file.

* * *

How does attribution work?

We use a proprietary 14-day lookback model based on customer clicks. If you have further questions, reach out via [email MP-SEM-Assist@walmart.com](mailto:email%E2%80%AFMP-SEM-Assist@walmart.com)  to schedule a session with our SEM team.

* * *

How is SEM different from Sponsored Search advertising?

SEM targets customers who are using external search engines like Google to search for products like yours and directs those customers to your listing on Walmart.com. It enhances your items' discoverability and improves your customer reach through external search engine results. To learn more about Sponsored Search advertising, visit [here](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Onboarding/Before-you-start-selling-on-Walmart-Marketplace).

* * *

How can I reach someone on the Success Hub team?

Email [successhub@walmart.com](mailto:BizMentorSupport@walmart.com).
