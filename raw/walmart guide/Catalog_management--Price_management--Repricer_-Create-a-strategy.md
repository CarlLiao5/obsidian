Repricer: Create a strategy

Overview

When you create a Repricer strategy, the default strategy is set to **Competitive price**.You can choose this strategy or automate your pricing using one of the other strategies. If you’re unsure of which strategy to choose, review [this guide](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Repricer:-overview). In this guide you’ll learn how to set up a new Repricer strategy.

## Create a strategy

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to the **Pricing** section in Seller Center and choose [**Automate pricing**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/automate-pricing).

### Step 2 – Create a strategy

Choose the _Create strategy_ button, then select _Repricer strategy_ from the dropdown menu _._ Agree to the terms and conditions if you’re creating a strategy for the first time. From there, choose your **Price Adjustment**, **Unit** and **Adjustment amount** (if applicable).

Next, [select the strategy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Repricer:-overview) you want to compete with the external price.

Once you configure your strategy, create a **Strategy name** and select _Create strategy._

Pro tip

Review the **Your Strategy** section on the side to view a mock-up of your strategy settings using sample data.

![2507_automate_create.gif](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt481c7c841a3281e5/688a4c8577c1b0acb8413402/2507_automate_create.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 3 – Assign items

After you’ve created your strategy, you’ll need to assign SKUs to it. You can search by **Item ID, Item name** or **SKU**. You may also choose from recommended SKUs or search your catalog. Enter or review the **suggested minimum** or **maximum price** for your items. Once you’ve finished, select _Assign items._

Pro tip

Select _Add items in bulk_ to download and upload a spreadsheet of the SKUs you’d like to assign.

Notes:

You must enter a valid minimum and maximum price for each item. If suggestions are missing, you must resolve the error before assigning an item or you may select _Skip_ this step to assign it later.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

When does the Repricer start to adjust my prices after I first upload my items and select the applicable strategy?

The Repricer will adjust your prices in as little as 15 minutes. In some instances, it may take up to four hours.

* * *

Will the Repricer change my shipping price?

No. the Repricer only adjusts the item price and will never change your shipping price.

* * *

How can I track the status of the items I’ve assigned to my Repricer strategy?

You can [track the status](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Track-item-setup-or-maintenance-activity-in-Seller-Center?locale=en-US) and view any errors found in your template within the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) dashboard.
