Retrieve Form 1099-K

Last updated on Jun 30, 2025

Reading time: 3 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Tax%20information/Retrieve-form-1099-K#overview)
- [Qualifying criteria](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Tax%20information/Retrieve-form-1099-K#qualifying)
- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Tax%20information/Retrieve-form-1099-K#frequently-asked-questions)

* * *

Overview

[Form 1099-K](https://www.irs.gov/businesses/understanding-your-form-1099-k) is a report that includes a summary of the payments you received from Walmart Marketplace orders in the calendar year that just ended. Certain Marketplace sellers who meet the qualifications will need to file a Form 1099-K with the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). Keep reading to find out if you qualify and how to retrieve your form.

## Qualifying criteria

You’ll receive a Form 1099-K if you meet the following conditions in the calendar year that just ended:

- You’re registered with Walmart Marketplace as a W-9 entity
- You shipped more than 200 orders and your gross sales amount was more than $20,000. Filing requirements for some states might differ from federal requirements.
- Your [**tax profile**](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Account%20settings/Update-tax-information-in-Seller-Center) information has been provided in Seller Center.

**What should I do?**

If you meet the criteria, you’ll receive an email from your [payment processor](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Payments/Payment-processing) with instructions on how to access your Form 1099-K. You can view a sample of Form 1099-K [here](http://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/f1099k.pdf). This link displays the form with multiple versions of the 1099-K – copy-A goes to the IRS, copy-B goes to the state, and you’ll receive copy-C.

### I did not receive a Form 1099-K.

Not all sellers meet the minimum requirements for this form.

**What should I do?**

Review the qualifying criteria above. If you don’t meet the criteria, you must self-report your sales figures directly to the IRS. Form 1099-K filings only report sales and do not include any adjustments for credits, cash equivalents, discount amounts, fees or refunded amounts.  If you meet the criteria and haven’t received your Form 1099-K from your payment processor, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

### I need to update my EIN.

While you can change your Employer Identification Number (EIN) at any time, the Tax ID used on your Form 1099-K must be updated prior to or during the year that corresponds with the sales being reported. For example, the Tax ID on your Form 1099-K for 2023 sales must be updated prior to or in 2023. We don’t accept Tax ID updates that are submitted in 2024 for a Form 1099-K filing reporting 2023 sales.

**What should I do?**

If you need to change your Tax ID, you can do so in Seller Center. For more information on how to do this, visit [_Update tax information in Seller Center_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Account%20settings/Update-tax-information-in-Seller-Center) _._

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

My corporate structure changed during the calendar year, can I get a split (or revised) Form 1099-K because my tax ID changed?

If you need a revised Form 1099-K,select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support _._ Provide the following details:

- Legal business name

- Employer Identification Number (EIN)

- Physical address

- Business phone #

- Email address of primary contact


* * *

I sold my business last year. Can I be issued a Form 1099-K for the sales before the transfer of ownership?

To receive a revised Form 1099-K, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support. Provide the following details and the Seller Support team will verify whether you are eligible:

- Legal business name

- EIN


- Physical address

- Business phone #

- Email address of primary contact


* * *

How do I retrieve my Form 1099-K if my payment processor is Payoneer?

You’ll receive an email from Payoneer with instructions on how to access your 1099-K if eligible. Your [tax1099.com](http://tax1099.com/) password will be as follows:

1. Eight characters in length
2. The four characters of your legal business name or first name (all lower case) + last four digits of your EIN.

If you can't log into [Tax1099](https://www.tax1099.com/), contact [Payoneer Support](https://payoneer.custhelp.com/) for assistance.

* * *

How do I retrieve my 1099-K if my payment processor is Hyperwallet?

Follow the process outlined below:

- Log in to your Pay Portal.
- Select _Resources > Tax Documents._
- Locate your Form 1099-K under **Available Year-End Tax Forms**.
- Select _Action > Download._

Walmart does not provide yearly sales reports, those should be managed by individual Marketplace sellers. If you have questions regarding this, consult your tax advisor. Any request to update or change your Tax ID may result in the need to verify your account information.
