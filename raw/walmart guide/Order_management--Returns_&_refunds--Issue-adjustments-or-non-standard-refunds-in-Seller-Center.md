Issue adjustments or non-standard refunds in Seller Center

Last updated on Dec 18, 2024

Reading time: 2 min

Overview

The [Returns Policy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Returns%20&%20refunds/returns-policy) sets consistent standards for customer returns. But sometimes, there are products or issues that fall outside our policy. When you need to issue a partial adjustment or a full refund in these situations, use the [**Adjustments**](https://seller.walmart.com/orders/adjustments) dashboard in Seller Center.

This guide shows you how to issue a refund or adjustment using the [**Adjustments**](https://seller.walmart.com/orders/adjustments) dashboard. See [_Seller Center – Order Management_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Order%20status/Acknowledge-orders-in-Seller-Center) to learn how to issue a refund or adjustment using the [**Orders**](https://seller.walmart.com/orders/manage-orders) dashboard.

Notes:

If you’ve determined a refund falls within the standard [Returns Policy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Returns%20&%20refunds/returns-policy), see [Process a Standard Return in Seller Center](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Returns%20&%20refunds/Issue-a-standard-refund-in-Seller-Center) for more information. Otherwise, keep reading.

## Issue adjustments in Seller Center

### Step 1 — Search for the order

Navigate to the [**Adjustments**](https://seller.walmart.com/orders/adjustments) dashboard in Seller Center and search for the order by the **Purchase Order Number (PO #)** or **Order Number**.

### Step 2 — Make adjustments

If the order has multiple items, you will need to adjust each item separately. Select _Adjust item_ under the **Action** column to issue the adjustment at the item level.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltac56e94064ac11fa/6761d61c206a96de98ae3100/2412_6431_Screenshot-1.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 3 — Choose quantity

If there are multiple quantities of the item, you can choose how many items you’d like to adjust, or you can adjust them all at once.

### Step 4 — Choose adjustment type

Next, choose the type of adjustment. You can refund the entire amount or a custom amount. If you’re offering a partial refund, choose the custom option and provide the percentage or the dollar amount you want to refund from the total amount the customer paid, including tax and shipping. Once you enter the refund amount, the system automatically adjusts the tax and shipping totals for you.

### Step 5 — Provide a reason

Finally, choose the refund category and return reason. The category you pick determines the return options you can choose from. Include additional comments if necessary. When you're ready to submit, select _Adjust_.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt6391d63e25e8aa66/6761d61bf2285e7d815077e4/2412_6431_Screenshot-2.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

Customers aren’t charged until the entire Purchase Order has shipped or been completed. If a customer has ordered multiple line items and more than one item has shipped, the rest of the items will need to be shipped or canceled in order for you to see the charge.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Can I refund a payment method that was not used for the transaction?

No. Marketplace sellers cannot refund a payment method that was not used for the original order. Walmart customers should contact [Walmart Customer Care](https://www.walmart.com/help).

* * *

Can I process a refund if not all the items have shipped?

No. You’ll only be able to process a refund for orders if all of the order lines are either shipped or completed.

* * *

There's a problem with the return I received from the customer. What should I do?

Depending on the circumstances, you may be eligible to file a dispute. For more information, see [_Disputes Standards_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Orders%20&%20returns/Dispute-standards).
