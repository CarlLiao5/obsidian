WFS prohibited products policy: Live plants

Last updated on May 25, 2025

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20policies%20&%20standards/WFS-prohibited-products-policy-live-plants#overview)
- [What is the policy?](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20policies%20&%20standards/WFS-prohibited-products-policy-live-plants#whatisthepolicy)
- [Policy basics](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20policies%20&%20standards/WFS-prohibited-products-policy-live-plants#policybasics)

* * *

Overview

Each prohibited product category has unique requirements that should be followed, or Walmart will automatically unpublish your items. To read the full policy, please read the [Walmart Fulfillment Services Prohibited Products Policy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/wfs-prohibited-products-policy) and the [Prohibited Products Policy for Marketplace Sellers](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Prohibited%20products%20&%20brands/Prohibited-products-policy:-overview).

Notes:

The contents of this document are subject to change without notice, so please check this page often. Sellers are responsible for ensuring that their items comply with this policy and any future amended or updated versions.

## What is the policy?

Any live plants that require special handling, specialty packing/packaging, and/or are time and temperature sensitive are ineligible for WFS. In addition, live plants that do not comply with local, state, or federal regulation, or that require transit and interstate commerce regulatory permits, licenses, and documentation are also ineligible or prohibited.

Products subject to our WFS Live Plants policy may also be subject to our [Prohibited Products Policy for Marketplace Sellers](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Prohibited%20products%20&%20brands/Prohibited-products-policy:-overview), including [Restricted/Illegal Products](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Prohibited-products-policy:-restricted-and-illegal-products) and [Home Goods](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Prohibited-Products-Policy:-Home-goods).

## Policy basics

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| Prohibited | - Plants that are considered an invasive species including but not limited to those indicated in the [Federal Noxious Weed list](https://www.aphis.usda.gov/plant_health/plant_pest_info/weeds/downloads/weedlist.pdf)<br>- Plants that are protected and/or endangered: [Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species of Wild Fauna and Flora (CITES) and the Endangered Species Act (ESA)](https://www.aphis.usda.gov/aphis/ourfocus/planthealth/import-information/permits/plants-and-plant-products-permits/cites)<br>  <br>- Plants and plant products that are illegal and/or do not comply with US regulations (see Related guides)<br>  <br>- Ineligible for WFS: <br>  <br>  - Live plants (bare root, in soil/grow medium, potted, etc.) <br>  - Live nursery plants and cuttings |
| Allowed with restrictions | - Plant products that are eligible for WFS must be in retail-ready shippable packaging adhering to all applicable regulations, including US labeling laws <br>- The below plant products may be allowed if requisite restrictions/disclosures are adhered to: <br>  - Air plants that are already packed in protective retail packaging <br>  - Unbloomed bulbs that are already packed in protective retail packaging <br>  - Retail packaged seeds for planting and gardening <br>  - Plant/mushroom grow kits and grow medium in retail packaging |
| Allowed | - Fake decorative plants <br>  <br>- Dried moss, lichen, and other dried plants for hobbies and arts and crafts that are otherwise not restricted |
