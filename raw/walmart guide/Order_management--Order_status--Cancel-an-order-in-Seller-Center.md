Cancel an order in Seller Center

Last updated on Oct 6, 2025

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Order%20status/Cancel-an-order-in-Seller-Center#overview)
- [Cancel an order](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Order%20status/Cancel-an-order-in-Seller-Center#cancelanorder)
- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Order%20status/Cancel-an-order-in-Seller-Center#frequently-asked-questions)

* * *

Overview

Canceling customer orders can negatively affect the customer experience, as well as your [Cancel Rate performance metrics](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping-and-fulfillment-policy). Cancellations aren't recommended and can't be reversed. You should verify that there aren't other alternatives before you cancel. However, if you need to cancel, use the information within this guide.

Notes:

All sellers should maintain a Cancel Rate of less than 2%.

## Cancel an order

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to your [**Orders**](https://seller.walmart.com/orders/manage-orders) dashboard and locate the **Purchase order#** that corresponds to the order.

![9213_Screenshot-2.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltf8dca5084ed3b7ef/66b0cf3309bc28621cb2521d/9213_Screenshot-2.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 2 – Initiate the cancellation

To cancel the entire order, navigate to the Purchase order# and choose _Cancel order_.

![9213_Screenshot-1.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt9b423e1b0d4887d8/66b0cf3509bc281fd0b25221/9213_Screenshot-1.png?branch=us_mplearn)

To cancel an unshipped item within a partially shipped order, select the three horizontal dots to the right of the order. Choose _Cancel_ in the order details page that opens.

### Step 3 – Finalize the cancellation

Choose _Cancel reason_ from the dropdown provided.  Once you select the reason from the dropdown, choose _Cancel order._  If you don't wish to cancel the order, choose _Do not cancel order_ to close the action screen.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What’s an out-of-stock cancellation?

If a customer order is canceled due to an item being out of stock, Walmart will update the item's status as unavailable in the respective warehouse and the item will become non-sellable. This is done to prevent further orders and subsequent cancellations. However, if the same item is in stock in any of our other warehouses, it’ll remain available to customers. The item status can be revised back to 'in stock' in the original warehouse by replenishing its inventory there.

* * *

Why is my item listed as out of stock even though the warehouse shows a positive inventory quantity?

If a customer order is canceled due to an out-of-stock reason, that item is marked as out of stock in the respective warehouse. However, you can restore the item's status to 'in stock' by replenishing or updating the inventory for that specific item.

* * *

Can I update a canceled order?

No. Once an order has been updated to Shipped or Canceled, they're considered closed and can't be updated or edited. If an order was canceled and subsequently shipped in error, reimbursement won't be provided.

* * *

Can I cancel an order that has been marked as shipped?

No, once an order is marked as shipped it can't be canceled. If you still have the item, you can follow the instructions outlined in [_Issue adjustments or non-standard refunds in Seller Center_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Returns%20&%20refunds/Issue-adjustments-or-non-standard-refunds-in-Seller-Center) _._

* * *

I've canceled orders due to incorrect pricing on Walmart.com. What should I do?

If you have questions, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support immediately and include the list of canceled orders. Our customer service team may reach out to the impacted customers.

* * *

What do I do if a customer requests that we cancel their order?

You'll be notified within Seller Center. Follow the steps above to proceed with the cancellation.

* * *

What should I do if a customer reaches out to cancel an order and the item is already in the carrier’s possession?

The customer should initiate a return from their Walmart account when they receive the order.

* * *

Do canceled orders have an impact on my seller account?

A high cancellation rate, or misuse of order cancellation, will result in account suspension and can lead to [account termination](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Seller-performance-standards).
