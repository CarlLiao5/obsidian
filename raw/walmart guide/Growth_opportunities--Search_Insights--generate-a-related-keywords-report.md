Search Insights: Generate a related keywords report

Last updated on Dec 10, 2024

Reading time: 2 min

Overview

The [**Related keywords report**](https://seller.walmart.com/analytics/search-insights/related-keywords-reports) helps you discover popular keywords that customers use to search for products on Walmart.com. You can also get competitive insights into items that are successful for a particular query. In this guide, you’ll learn how to generate a report and better understand customer data.

## Generate report

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to [**Search Insights**](https://seller.walmart.com/analytics/search-insights/) under the **Analytics** section in Seller Center. Go to the **Related keywords report** tab.

Notes:

The data in the screenshots arefor instructional purposes only.

### Step 2 – Download the report

After you’ve entered a search query in the Query field, select _Generate report_ to download the Generate Related keywords report.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blted57b5ea16f1b02c/66c8f48dbd5ff86d0f4685da/12178_gif1.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

### Related keyword metrics

Related Keyword Metrics give you an in-depth view of the following:

- **Queries:** These are customer searches, in the last seven days, on Walmart.com that are related to the phrase entered.


- **Frequency Rank:** Walmart’s wide rank for how often customers have searched a particular phrase. It indicates how popular a query is. (Ex. Rank 500 indicates that the phrase is the 500th most popular query on Walmart in the selected time range).

- **Top Clicked Products:** The top five most clicked products after a query phrase has been searched.


Notes:

To have the best possible performance on search, we recommend focusing on implementing [**Listing Quality**](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Items%20and%20inventory/Listing-quality-and-rewards-dashboard?locale=en-US) recommendations and verifying that your assigned product type is correct.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

There’s no data populating for my report. Why?

There might not be a customer search related to the phrase entered in the chosen time range.

* * *

What does the Related Keywords report offer?

The Related Keywords report provides insights into seasonal trends and customer demand signals on Walmart.com. The report also helps you understand your competition better by telling you which items get the most clicks for a particular query.

* * *

Can you use query data to understand which queries you should be ranking for?

Yes, you can use this data to better align your listing to customer needs and find out which queries you should rank for. For example, if query data shows that customers are searching for winter coats during a particular time of year, you can adjust your content and marketing strategies to optimize your item performance.
