Add multiple items: Express setup

Last updated on Mar 4, 2026

Reading time: 3 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup%20methods/Add-a-new-item:-Search-the-Walmart-catalog#overview)
- [Express setup](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup%20methods/Add-a-new-item:-Search-the-Walmart-catalog#express)
- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup%20methods/Add-a-new-item:-Search-the-Walmart-catalog#frequently-asked-questions)

* * *

Overview

This guide shows you how to add up to 50 new items to your **Catalog** in [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/) by using products that are currently listed on Walmart.com. If you're adding a new item to your catalog that's not currently offered on Walmart, review [_Add a single item: Full setup_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Add-a-New-Item:-Create-a-new-item).

## Express setup

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center and select the _Add items_ button.

### Step 2 — Add the items

On the next page, enter a product name, keyword or product identification number (GTIN, UPC, EAN, ISBN) in the **Express setup** search field. Separate each item with a comma. You can search for up to 50 items at once.

Select the search icon and add the items you want. If more than one item appears in the search result, select _More info_ and then choose _View on Walmart.com_ to confirm that you’re choosing an exact match. When you’re done, select the _Next_ button and choose your fulfillment type.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt0a7b21f2d995324f/6840a8721efc4cc0946760cf/2505_add-multiple-items-express-setup_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

If the item is part of a variant group, you’ll see the variant attributes listed. Be sure to review the attributes to find a match for your item. You can’t edit existing item details in this tool. If your variant is not part of an existing listing, review [_Add a single item: Full setup_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Add-a-New-Item:-Create-a-new-item).

### Step 3 — Add offer details

If you sell in multiple markets, choose the markets you want to apply the changes to. Next, add the item details, including the SKU, selling price, shipping weight, condition, country of origin and tax code (if applicable) seller-fulfilled inventory (optional). For Walmart-fulfilled items, you may be asked to enter dimensions, [compliance](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup) and [trade item hierarchy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-trade-item-configuration) if we can’t find reusable information in our system.

Notes:

You don’t need to fill out any other product information that’s already in Walmart’s unified catalog such as the images, brand information, etc.

### Step 4 — Submit

Once you’ve completed all required fields, select the _Submit_ button at the bottom of the page to finish adding the item to your catalog. You can track its status from the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) in Seller Center. Updates may take up to 24 hours. Any data errors that are detected during the updates will require extra processing time.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How do I find an item ID?

You can find the Item ID by viewing the item on Walmart.com and copying the 9-digit number at the end of the product name following the forward-slash (/). For example: [https://www.walmart.com/ip/Beautiful-12-piece-Forged-Kitchen-Knife-Set-in-White-with-Wood-Storage-Block/624750836](https://www.walmart.com/ip/Beautiful-12-piece-Forged-Kitchen-Knife-Set-in-White-with-Wood-Storage-Block/624750836).

* * *

How do I update the item’s content?

You may be able to suggest changes to your item’s content after you’ve finished setting up your item. However, because there may be multiple sellers providing content for the same item, you may not always be able to define which content will be displayed on Walmart.com. Walmart will determine the best available content for items unless it is provided by a brand owner or authorized reseller.

Items that cannot be updated will appear locked during the updating process. Learn more in [_How to update content: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/How-to-update-content:-Overview) _._

* * *

How do I make a change to the item variant that appears in the search result?

You can’t change the item variant from this tool. If your item variant isn’t included in the listing, create a new item using the [_Add a single item: Full setup_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Add-a-New-Item:-Create-a-new-item) process.

* * *

What should I do if the item result doesn’t match the identifier?

Follow the [_Add a single Item: Full setup_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Add-a-New-Item:-Create-a-new-item) process to add a new item.

* * *

I want to sell an item internationally. How do I add a market?

To learn how to add more markets to your account, visit [_Global onboarding: Set up an additional market_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Onboarding/global-onboarding-set-up-an-additional-market).

* * *

What if I don’t know my product tax code?

A product tax code must be assigned to each type of shipping fee that you offer in order to assess the correct sales tax. U.S. sellers can review and assign their tax codes in [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/partner-profile/taxes).

To review and assign your tax codes:

- **U.S. and California sellers:** Navigate to your [**Account**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/account/notification-settings) page in Seller Center, select [_Taxes_](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/partner-profile/taxes) and locate the **Shipping Sales Tax Codes** section.
- **Mexico sellers:** Verify your product tax code [here](http://pys.sat.gob.mx/PyS/catPyS.aspx).

* * *

What if I don’t know my item’s country of origin?

An item must have a country of origin to be listed in an additional market. You can confirm a product’s country of origin through the manufacturer, a matching product in the Walmart catalog or validating the specific item.

* * *

Why was there no match found for my item?

If there is no match for your item, the item may be new to the Walmart catalog, meaning it hasn’t been set up by other sellers. To add a new item to your catalog that’s not currently offered on Walmart.com, review [_Add a single item: Full setup_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Add-a-New-Item:-Create-a-new-item).

* * *

How long will it take for the item to be added to my catalog?

After submitting your item, you can track its status from the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) in Seller Center. Updates may take up to 24 hours. Any data errors that are detected during the updates will require extra processing time.

Walmart-fulfilled items that have [hazardous materials](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup?locale=en-US) require a compliance review, which may take up to three business days.

* * *

How is setting up a Walmart-fulfilled item different from seller-fulfilled?

With seller-fulfilled items, you use your own fulfillment centers to manage, store and ship your products. When you choose Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), we pick, pack and ship orders for you.

During item setup with WFS, you’ll also need to enter more required information, like country of origin, dimensions, [compliance](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup) and [trade item hierarchy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-trade-item-configuration).

* * *

Why do I need to provide more information beyond offer details?

For Walmart-fulfilled items, we require dimensions, [compliance](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup?locale=en-US) and [trade item hierarchy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-trade-item-configuration?locale=en-US). If those details haven’t been provided by other sellers, you’ll need to add them just once for your item.
