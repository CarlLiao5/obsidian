Overview

Success Hub suggestions offer personalized, relevant and actionable opportunities to improve your business. These curated suggestions can help drive growth in relevant areas of business, including seller- and Walmart-fulfilled inventory, price and assortment. In this guide, you’ll learn about the suggestions.

## How does it work?

Suggestions are refreshed daily and can be found in **Success Hub** by selecting [_Growth_](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/success-hub) in Seller Center. Suggestions are removed after you review and implement the changes. Suggestion history is viewable by choosing the _View History_ link.

Pro tip

Prioritize implementing the Success Hub content quality suggestions shown under the **Top items to optimize** section.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Do I have to accept all Success Hub suggestions?

No. All insights are suggestive in nature. As with the Marketplace Program, there's no business expectation. You have no obligation and make no promises to purchase any minimum amount of merchandise to list or sell on the marketplace other than what you deem adequate.

Success Hub suggestions are based on projections, past purchasing history and representations about quantities to be purchased aren't binding on the Walmart Marketplace and Walmart won't be liable for any act or expenditure (including but not limited to expenditures for equipment, labor, materials, packaging or capital expenditures) by the seller in reliance on them.

* * *

What are the benefits of Success Hub?

In addition to the curated suggestions, Success Hub users are eligible for program incentives such as Ad Credits or statement credits. If you become eligible for this program, you'll be notified by email.

* * *

How much does it cost?

The analytics and suggestions provided by Success Hub on Walmart Marketplace are provided to you at no cost.

* * *

Are there any performance guarantees for participating?

No, Walmart does not guarantee performance improvement from the suggestions Success Hub provides that you choose to implement.   The suggestions are informational only and are provided “as is” without warranties of any kind. Results vary and depend on many factors, including your items, pricing, inventory, demand and seasonality, as well as other factors outside Walmart’s control. You’re solely responsible for your listings, compliance and business decisions.

* * *

I don't see much improvement in my business KPIs. Who can I talk to?

Success Hub insights require timely actions. Additionally, several factors affect business performance. If you have questions, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

How can I reach someone on the Success Hub team?

Email: [successhub@walmart.com](emailto:successhub@walmart.com)
