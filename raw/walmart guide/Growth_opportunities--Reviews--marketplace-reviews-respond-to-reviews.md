Marketplace reviews: Respond to reviews

Last updated on Oct 22, 2025

Reading time: 6 min

* * *

Overview

Ratings and reviews help increase offer conversion on high-quality items. When a customer leaves a review, their inquiry is sent to the [**Ratings and Reviews**](https://seller.walmart.com/performance/ratings-and-reviews/item-reviews) page in Seller Center. In this guide, we’ll show you how to access your reviews and respond to customers.

## How to respond to reviews

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to the [**Ratings and Reviews**](https://seller.walmart.com/performance/ratings-and-reviews) page in Seller Center. Select the three dots to the right of the review you want to reply to and choose _Respond publicly._

Notes:

You can encourage your customers to submit more reviews with [Review Accelerator](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Reviews/review-accelerator-overview) _._

### Step 2 — Write a response

Review the **Response Guidelines** and then use the text field to write your response. You can choose the **Custom response** option to write your own response, or choose the other options to view samples to help you get started. Remember that your response will be visible to the public.

Once you submit a response, you can’t edit or delete it. Your customer won’t be able to reply, and if their review is deleted, your response will also be deleted. We recommend that you proofread your response before you submit it. Select _Save draft_ to save your response for later.

### Step 3 — Submit response

When you’re ready, select _Submit public response._ If your response is approved, it will appear with your account name on the item page, under your customer’s review. Your response will appear within 72 hours of approval.

Notes:

Violating the **Response Guidelines** may put your account at risk of being revoked or suspended. In addition to these guidelines, you must abide by the [_Marketplace Seller Code of Conduct_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Marketplace-Seller-Code-of-Conduct) _._

Notes:

You can’t respond to syndicated reviews of any kind.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Can customers edit or delete their reviews?

No.

* * *

Can I email customers to request feedback on items they have purchased?

No. Soliciting feedback, offering a gift card in exchange for a review or including any promotional inserts in packages is strictly prohibited and may result in account suspension or termination.

* * *

Can I send customers a sample item for review?

Yes, you can work with one of Walmart’s third-party approved sampling vendors:

- [Field Agent](https://plumshop.com/p/walmart-ratings-reviews)
- [Wholescale](https://wholescale.com/walmart)
- [The Brand Power Company](https://www.thebrandpowercompany.com/contact-us)
- [PowerReviews](https://www.powerreviews.com/walmart/)
- [Bazaarvoice](https://marketplace.walmart.com/bazaarvoice/)

For more information, review the [_Walmart product review sampling guidelines_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/Walmart-product-review-sampling-guidelines).

* * *

Why are my reviews missing?

When reviews are not displaying on Walmart.com it's usually the result of a product family issue or syndication issue:

- Product families enable the sharing of reviews across items that have variants such as size, color, fabric, etc. Product families must be set up and maintained by the merchandising team. Contact your merchant for assistance if reviews are not shared between variants.

- Syndication facilitates the sharing of reviews from a supplier or brand website to Walmart.com. Items must be set up with an item ID and UPC in both the supplier’s catalog and the Walmart catalog. If your syndicated reviews are not displaying, check your catalog to ensure that these necessary data points are present.


* * *

Why is "--" listed in some of the data columns?

This is a known issue due to data limitations. Additionally, our system currently only links data for reviews and orders from September 2023 onwards. We're working to address this by improving our internal linking capabilities so this issue can't be actioned or corrected through a support ticket. If you have questions, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

How long does it take for a new seller or product review to post on Walmart.com?

New reviews will appear on the listing within five to seven business days. If a review isn't posted within that time, it may have violated one of the review guidelines.

* * *

Can customer reviews help with search engine optimization (SEO) and conversion?

Yes. Reviews may help you boost brand awareness and drive customer confidence, leading to better SEO results and conversions.

* * *

I want to remove a review from my listing. What should I do?

To request the removal of a review, visit [_Request to remove a review_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Troubleshooting/Request-to-remove-a-review) _._

* * *

Can a customer leave a seller review if their order was canceled?

No.

* * *

Can a customer leave a seller review if they returned their items?

Yes.

* * *

How do I view all of the reviews for one item?

Navigate to the [**Ratings and Reviews**](https://seller.walmart.com/performance/ratings-and-reviews/item-reviews) page and locate the item you want. Then navigate to the **Action** column, select the three dots and select _View all item reviews._

* * *

Can I use my response to promote other products to the customer?

No. Using a response to promote other products to the customer is a violation of Walmart’s Review Response Guidelines.

* * *

How can I increase reviews for my items?

You have a range of options to increase seller reviews:

- Enroll eligible items into the Review Accelerator Program to incentivize customers to leave an authentic review. Check out [_Review Accelerator Program: How to enroll_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Reviews/Review-Accelerator-Program:-How-to-enroll) _._
- Import reviews from your external website by syndicating them to Walmart.com. To review a list of approved syndication providers, including Walmart's free syndication service, check out [_Marketplace Reviews: Review syndication_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Reviews/Marketplace-reviews:-review-syndication) _._
- Conduct product sampling by working with a third-party solution provider to seed reviews. Check out  [Walmart product review sampling guidelines](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/Walmart-product-review-sampling-guidelines)  for more information.

* * *

Can I see the reviews that have been rejected?

No. Only reviews that have passed moderation will be made available.

* * *

What customer service phone number and email should I provide to customers?

You may include your contact information from the Customer Service section of Seller Center in **your responses.** To review and update these contacts refer to this [guide](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Account/Business-information-policy).

* * *

Which reviews qualify for responses?

You can respond to your own seller reviews, or you can respond to product reviews for offers that you’ve fulfilled if you’re registered as the brand owner for an item in Walmart’s [Brand Portal.](https://brandportal.walmart.com/)
