Overview

The [WFS overview page](https://seller.walmart.com/wfs/wfs-overview) gives sellers easy access to important WFS-specific account insights and personalized recommendations to grow your business. It serves as a quick snapshot of your sales and inventory performance, suggested areas of improvement, and valuable WFS programs and services.

## How to use the page

Log in to Seller Center and select **WFS** from the main navigation. The WFS overview page will appear for sellers who have sold more than $100 of items fulfilled by Walmart.

![full_page_w_nav_highlighted.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt18b8e89d6ee86fea/68078ac8afe29571849009a1/full_page_w_nav_highlighted.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Review your sales metrics for this week, starting from Sunday at 12:00am PST:

- Dollar amount of WFS item sales
- Number of WFS units sold
- Gross merchandise value (GMV) penetration, which is the percentage of your WFS item sales divided by your combined sales of WFS and seller-fulfilled items

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt7047541032c8f703/68078ac85c86357baa5c63db/sales.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Select any point on the graph to compare this week’s sales to last week, and to the same week last year.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltc4215a345ea2e91b/68078ac8afe295164c9009a5/sales_graph.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Select the toggle for a table view.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt46663987a3036ada/68078ac763972283ad6f6ca3/sales_table.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Browse your personalized recommendations:

- If you have out-of-stock or at-risk items, select **Restock your inventory** to replenish these items and maximize their sales.
- If you have seller-fulfilled items with the potential for increased sales if converted to Walmart-fulfilled, select **Fulfill with WFS** to review and convert these items.
- If you have items in storage that are unpublished on walmart.com, select **Fix unpublished items** to republish these items and begin selling them again.

![recs.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt0340a03e22f44b41/68078ac7afe295252590099d/recs.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Check your inventory health:

- Daily in-stock sales rate, the percentage of your total potential sales of items that are active and have available units
- Stock status, a summary of how many of your items are in stock, at risk, or out of stock
- The age of all stored units in your inventory, broken down by their number of days in storage

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt1acf5c6f7286d68a/68078ac85c863524335c63d7/inventory.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Discover the latest in WFS, where we’ve highlighted some of our most valuable programs and services. Follow each link for more details on how your business could benefit.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt88cdbddb3744257b/68078ac74838e635f370dd16/discover.png?branch=us_mplearn)

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How often is data refreshed?

Each metric on the page has a different data refresh rate.Select an individual metric’s tooltip to learn when its data was last updated.

* * *

How do I change to the table view?

Select the toggle above the graph in the **Sales comparison** section to see the same data in a table view.

* * *

Why am I seeing more or less recommendations?

Recommendations help you grow your WFS business while considering many different factors, such as your historical sales, and item price and demand. The more you sell with WFS, the more accurate your recommendations will become, as our data models are continuously refined. Once you complete a recommended action, it may take some time to see a new one. Check the page each week for updated recommendations, personalized just for you.
