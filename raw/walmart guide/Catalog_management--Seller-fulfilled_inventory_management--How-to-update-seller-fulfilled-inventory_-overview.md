Overview

Keeping your inventory up to date ensures that your item never goes out of stock and remains available for purchase on [Walmart.com](http://walmart.com/). In this guide, we’ll give you an overview of four different ways to update inventory.

## How does it work?

If inventory is running low, you should replenish it immediately and update your inventory counts on Walmart Marketplace.

| **Method** | **Use Case** |
| --- | --- |
| **Single Item Upload Method** | You can update inventory for individual SKUs or small groups of items through your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/manage-items) in Seller Center. |
| **Bulk Update Method** | The Inventory spec is an Excel template that allows you to update inventory information for several items at once in Seller Center. For more information, visit [_Update Inventory in Bulk_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Seller-fulfilled%20inventory%20management/Update-seller-fulfilled-inventory-in-bulk-in-Seller-Center) in Seller Center. |
| **API Integration** | If you have a large catalog of items and a technical team to support your operations, consider automating inventory updates through API. For more information, visit [_Integration Methods: API._](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/Integration-methods-API) |
| **Solution Provider** | Walmart Marketplace works with several third-party Solution Providers that can help you outsource inventory updates. For more information, visit [_Choose a Solution Provider._](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/Choose-a-solution-provider) |

Pro tip

Use this [inventory management checklist](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt11e30e5fb8b197e1/69ab686248a8f14dc62b4b18/2603_Inventory-management-checklist_PDF.pdf?branch=us_mplearn) to make sure your items are always in stock.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How are orders reserved and processed?

When a customer places an order on Walmart.com, it gets added to your **reserved** inventory quantity until you’ve marked it as shipped. Orders will be released to you only after a new customer's payment information has passed a fraud check, which typically takes up to five hours.

* * *

My item is appearing as out of stock on Walmart. Why does it show that I have inventory Reserved and On hand in Seller Center?

Once an item has been purchased by a customer, it is added to your **reserved** inventory quantity until you’ve marked it as shipped. If your **on hand** and **reserved** inventory quantities are the same amount, this means customers have purchased all inventory **on hand** and the item will appear as out of stock on Walmart.com.

To avoid this, we strongly encourage you to provide Walmart with an **on hand** inventory quantity that is at least 20 more than the **reserved** inventory quantity.  For example, if you have 30 shirts in your **reserved** inventory you should provide an **on Hand** inventory quantity of 50.

* * *

How do I zero out my Reserved inventory quantity?

Your **reserved** inventory quantity will be zeroed out once you’ve shipped all purchased orders. After you’ve acknowledged an order, you must choose the Ship item button in Seller Center to notify Walmart that the order has been shipped.

* * *

Why is an item still showing as in stock even though I set the On hand inventory quantity to zero?

If you just submitted your inventory update, it could take up to four hours for the update to reflect on [Walmart.com.](https://www.walmart.com/)
