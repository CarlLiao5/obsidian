Walmart Connect solutions: Brand Shop and Shelf

Overview

Brand Shop is a virtual storefront on Walmart.com and the Walmart app where brand owners can promote their Marketplace business. Shelf is a digital aisle showcasing your items and can be linked from a Brand Shop. In this guide, you’ll learn more about Brand Shop and Shelf.

Notes:

Brand Shop and Shelf is only available to brand owners registered with the Walmart Brand Portal.

## How does it work?

A Brand Shop or Shelf can serve as a landing page for your Walmart Sponsored Brands, Onsite Display campaigns and more. Brand Shops are discoverable on Walmart.com via item pages and through the Brand Shop directory found at the bottom of every Walmart.com page.

When you create a Brand Shop, you’ll receive a custom URL that you can share online or link to your Shelf. You may also include links to different Shelves in your Brand Shop. While a Brand may only have one Brand Shop live at a time, there’s no limit to the amount of Shelves that can be live. If you own multiple brands, you can feature items from your brands on the same Brand Shop or Shelf.

Pro tip

Create Shelves to link from your Brand Shop, and use Shelves to curate different product collections (e.g., newly released items and seasonal collections).

## Additional guidelines

Once your brands are registered, start building your virtual shop with Shop Builder. Use the [Marketplace Seller Onboarding](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?channel=ShopBuilder&language=en_US&article=000010394) guide for step-by-step instructions on registration and check out the [Brand Shop Advertising Help](https://advertisinghelp.walmart.com/s/guides?channel=ShopBuilder&article=000010841) guide for more information on curating your experience. Shop Builder provides reporting including sales, orders, visitors, views, units sold, average sales and units per order, average sales and views per visitor.

Notes:

Reporting specific to Shelf is not currently available. However, if you link a Shelf to your Brand Shop, sales made from a Shelf discovered through Brand Shop will be attributed to the reporting shown in Shop Builder.

In Shop Builder, you’ll have three templates to choose from when creating a Brand Shop. Each template has its own requirements which can be found directly in the platform. However, aside from the required sections, each template allows you to add or remove additional sections as desired.

Once a Brand Shop or Shelf has been approved for launch, the only changes that can be made without requiring re-approval are changes to your items featured on the pages. Any other changes require re-approval by Walmart’s moderation team.

Pro tip

If your page is already live and you need to make changes that require re-approval, duplicate the live page and make your changes in the new page.
