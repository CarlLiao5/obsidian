Overview

The [**Returns insights**](https://seller.walmart.com/orders/returns) dashboard provides historical data about your returns, including the reason the item was returned and the refunded amount. In this guide, you’ll learn more about these insights and how to access them.

## How does it work?

The dashboard includes SKU-level details of all of your returned items along with additional relevant data column fields. To access your insights, select _Returns_ under **Orders i** n Seller Center, then choose the **_View returns insights_** button.

The dashboard also allows you to view the reason for a return or identify defective items. Select the underlined number in the **Returns reasons** column to view why a customer returned the item. Below are additional metrics you can track:

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **Performance metric** | **Definition** |
| **Total returns (orders)** | The total number of orders returned by customers. |
| **Refund rate** | The percentage of orders refunded for reasons the seller is responsible for such as an item damaged or incorrect item received. You must maintain a rate of < 6%. |
| **Refund gross merchandise value (GMV)** | The total GMV refunded. |
| **Returns processing fee** | The total fees paid for processing returns. |
| **Units** | The number of units returned within the date range. |

Notes:

The refund rate that is displayed on the [**Returns insights**](https://seller.walmart.com/orders/returns) dashboard may differ from your overall refund rate that is used to calculate your [Seller Performance](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Seller-performance-standards) metrics.

Pro tip

Search for returned items by name or filter by the total number of returns, return reason or fulfillment method.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How often are my metrics updated?

Data is aggregated weekly and presented on Saturday.

* * *

Why is my refund rate different from my refund rate shown on the Performance dashboard?

Depending on the date range you’ve selected, the refund rate displayed on the **Returns insight** dashboard may differ from your overall refund rate that is used to calculate your [Seller Performance](https://seller.walmart.com/performance/order-and-fulfillment) metrics.

* * *

What happens if I process a Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) return?

If you use WFS, a return process fee may be added.

* * *

How quickly should I process a return?

Returns should be processed within 48 hours.

* * *

What should I do if I want to dispute a return?

If you’re eligible, you may be able to dispute a return. For more info, visit  this [guide](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Troubleshooting/Dispute-a-Walmart-Customer-Care-refund).
