Billing methods: Add a credit card

Last updated on Feb 19, 2025

Reading time: 3 min

* * *

* * *

Overview

You can add a business credit card to your Seller Center account as a billing method to pay for charges that exceed your sales revenue. In this guide, you’ll learn how to add or update your business credit card details within Seller Center.

## Manage billing methods

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to the [**Billing Services**](https://seller.walmart.com/finance-and-tax/billingServices) page in Seller Center.

### Step 2 – Add credit card details

Enter the required information including card number, expiration date, CVV and billing address details. The credit card that you use should be a business credit card that is not used for personal, family or household purposes.

Once you confirm that you’ve read and agreed to the terms and conditions, select _Add credit card_ to add the credit card to your account. Once the card has been successfully added to your account, you can choose it as your default billing method.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt81b46a95b6ed453e/66fadbb43e39c91fb2b0944b/Billing_methods_updated_GIF.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 3 – Update credit card details

If you need to, select the pencil icon to the right of the billing method on the **Billing Services** page. After you make your updates, select the _Save changes_ button. If you need to delete a credit card, select the _trash can icon_ to the right of the billing method and choose _Delete account_ to remove the card from your account.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt6b2b0977613b3f04/676446b4dc525331bda979e9/2412_11878_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

You must always have at least one billing method on file. If you only have one billing method on file, you will not be able to delete it until you add an alternative billing method first.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Why do you need my billing information?

We need at least one billing method set up so that you can seamlessly pay for customer refunds and chargebacks or Marketplace services and programs like Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), Search Engine Marketing (SEM), the Review Accelerator program or Walmart Connect.

Charges that are billed to your credit card will be listed as _Amount charged_ in your **Statement Summary** on the [**Payments**](https://seller.walmart.com/partner-analytics/payments/statements/period) dashboard in Seller Center.

* * *

Do I have to use a credit card as a billing method?

No. You can set up an electronic transfer via ACH if you prefer.

* * *

What credit cards are accepted?

Walmart accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, Japan Credit Bureau (JCB) and Union Pay. The credit card should be a business credit card that is not used for personal, family or household purposes.

* * *

Do I have to pay a processing fee?

No. Walmart does not charge payment processing fees.

* * *

Can I pay service charges with more than one billing method?

No. Walmart will only charge the default billing method listed on your account.

* * *

Will merchant cash advance repayments be billed to my credit card on file?

No.

* * *

How do I change my default billing method?

Select _Set as Default_ to manually select your default billing method. There can only be one default billing method listed on your account.

* * *

I received a credit card authentication error. What should I do?

If authentication fails, you will be asked to re-enter your credit card details. Verify that the card number, CVV, name and billing address are accurate before trying again. If you continue to experience issues, contact your credit care provider for assistance.

* * *

How often will my credit card be charged?

The frequency of credit card charges by Walmart will vary depending on your settlement cycle and your use of various Walmart services. Walmart reserves the right to charge your card periodically, outside of your standard billing cycle, as deemed necessary or appropriate in its sole and absolute discretion.

* * *

What happens if I change my settlement cycle?

If you change from a biweekly settlement cycle to weekly, or vice versa, your billing cycle will automatically update to the same cadence.

* * *

How do I identify billing charges on my credit card statement?

Marketplace related billing charges will be listed on your credit card statement as Walmart services or Walmart Marketplace.
