Overview

[Business.Walmart.com](http://business.walmart.com/) is Walmart’s eCommerce site dedicated to serving business and nonprofit customers. If eligible, you can enroll in the Walmart Business program to expand your reach and sell directly to business and nonprofit customers. In this guide, you’ll learn about the requirements to join the program and how to get started selling on the platform.

Notes:

Participation in the Walmart Business program is by invitation-only and not available to all Marketplace sellers. If you’re interested in selling on Walmart Business, you can apply [here](https://share.hsforms.com/1orll4wTlQFeWF7WNmBCpKg3lz2f) and your request will be reviewed on a first-come, first-served basis. [Check your enrollment status](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/exclusive-programs) before submitting a request to enroll.

## How does it work?

To sell on [Business.Walmart.com](https://business.walmart.com/), you must be an active Walmart Marketplace seller with an account in good standing and a proven track record of excellent customer service and success selling on [Walmart.com](https://walmart.com/). Additionally, you must have products suitable for business customers, including but not limited to, product category fit and ability to ship in case packs or fulfill bulk orders.

The Walmart Business team considers your product offers and the last three months of shipping and customer service performance on Walmart Marketplace to determine your eligibility. If you’re a new seller, we recommend operating on Walmart Marketplace for at least three months before applying to sell on Walmart Business.

If eligible, you may automatically be enrolled or you’ll receive an invitation in your email after the review is complete. If you don’t receive an email 30 days after applying, visit [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/exclusive-programs) to check on the status of your application and enrollment.  There are no additional costs to joining the program, however, standard Walmart Marketplace [Referral Fees](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Onboarding/Referral-fee-schedule-for-contract-categories) apply.

## Additional guidelines

Once approved and enrolled, you’ll have access to these features below:

| Feature | Learn more |
| --- | --- |
| **Price management** | Set up [Business pricing](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-business-set-up-pricing) and [volume-based pricing](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-business-set-up-volume-pricing) that cater <br>to business to business (B2B) customers who often buy products in larger quantities. |
| **Reporting** | Download [reporting](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-business-reporting) to track your sales or view which items in your catalog are eligible for the program. |
| **Program and item participation** | Manage [item participation](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-business-troubleshooting) or learn how to opt out of the program entirely. |

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How can I manage my orders and fulfillment for my Walmart Business items?

Order management and fulfillment for Business.Walmart.com follows the same process as your Walmart.com orders.

* * *

Where can I view my Walmart Business sales?

All Walmart Business sales are included in your total sales as shown on your statements in Seller Center. You can download a [B2B order report](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-business-reporting) to view your sales.

* * *

Can I use Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) for Walmart Business?

Walmart Business automatically lists and shares your inventory, so there’s no need to set up a separate inventory feed for Walmart Business. Both your WFS and seller-fulfilled inventory are automatically included.

* * *

Can I set up Sponsored Product ad campaigns for Walmart Business?

Sponsored Product ads aren't currently available, but there are display opportunities. Contact your Walmart Connect manager about other advertising opportunities to highlight your brand or products on Walmart Business.

* * *

What’s Walmart Business+?

Walmart Business+ is a paid membership for our customers. You can compare the customer benefits between the Walmart Business free account and the paid version [here](https://business.walmart.com/plus).

* * *

How do I stop selling on Walmart Business?

To stop selling on Walmart Business, complete [this request form](https://share.hsforms.com/1652oUk6mRC-MW6gDPj6H8w3lz2f). It could take up to 30 days to complete your opt-out request.

* * *

How do I exclude select offers from being sold on Walmart Business?

When you unpublish an offer from Walmart Marketplace, that offer is automatically unpublished from the Walmart Business site.

For offers that you'd like to continue selling on Walmart Marketplace but exclude from Walmart Business, [email](mailto:B2BMarketplace@wal-mart.com) with “B2B Item Exclusion Request” in the email subject line. In the email body, put your Marketplace seller ID and seller display name and attach the list of item IDs to exclude in an excel spreadsheet attachment. It may take up to 30 days for the request to be completed from the date you submitted the information.

* * *

Who can I contact if I have a question?

If you have questions, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.
