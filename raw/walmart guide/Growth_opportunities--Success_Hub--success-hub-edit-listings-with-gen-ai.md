Success Hub: Edit listings with Gen AI

Overview

Success Hub provides AI-powered content suggestions aimed to optimize your product name, description and key features and to help improve your Listing Quality score. In this guide, you’ll learn how to use the tool.

Notes:

AI-generated content must be accurate, truthful, rights-compliant, and aligned with Marketplace policies, including our [_Product details policy_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-Detail-Page:-overview) _._

## Edit listings

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to **Success Hub** by selecting [**Growth**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/success-hub) in Seller Center to edit listings. In the **Edit Listings with Gen AI** section, choose the _Edit items_ button.

### Step 2 – Select items to update

Select the item that you’d like to update, then choose _Next_. Each item must be edited individually.

### Step 3 – Review and submit

Review the suggested content for each of the item descriptions and select _Use this Suggestion_ to move forward with the suggestion. You can choose to keep the suggestion as is or make additional edits. Once you’re satisfied with your description, select _Submit changes_.

Notes:

You’re under no obligation to use the tool or any of the AI-generated output. When using the tool, be sure to check for accuracy as AI can make mistakes.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

I made updates to my item’s content, but it isn’t appearing on Walmart.com. Why?

Multiple Marketplace sellers can offer the same item on Walmart.com. Walmart will determine the best available content for items unless provided by a brand owner or authorized reseller.

* * *

How is Walmart’s generated content different from content that I can get from existing Large Language Models (LLMs) like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, etc.?

Walmart’s Gen AI tool has been trained using Walmart’s [content standards](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Content-standards:-Overview). The suggested content will address any factors that are negatively impacting your [Listing Quality](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Items%20and%20inventory/Listing-quality-and-rewards-dashboard) score.

* * *

I’ve already submitted my content via Success Hub, but I want to make additional changes. How can I do this?

There are several ways to update your content depending on your business needs. Visit [_How to update content: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/How-to-update-content:-Overview) to learn more.

* * *

How are the item suggestions curated?

We select items based on whether we’re able to provide improved content through generative AI. We prioritize the items that we select based on the potential impact that the improved content can have.

* * *

How can I reach someone on the Success Hub team?

Contact us by [email](mailto:successhub@walmart.com).
