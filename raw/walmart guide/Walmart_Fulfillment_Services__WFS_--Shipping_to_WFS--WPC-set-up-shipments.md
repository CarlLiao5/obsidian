Walmart Preferred Carrier: Set up shipments

Last updated on Jan 13, 2026

Reading time: 4 min

* * *

Overview

When you send inventory to Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), you can use a Walmart preferred carrier to ship and schedule delivery. For full truckload (FTL) and some less than truckload (LTL), we’ll also schedule the [carrier pickup](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WPC-prepare-pickup) for you. In this guide, learn how to select a preferred carrier and get an estimated shipping price.

## Create a shipping plan

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt311a697778dc4a34/6877df3eee343026caaae082/WFS_Preferred-carrier-journey_Infographic.jpg?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 1 – Get started

Log into Seller Center and go to the [**Shipping Plans**](https://seller.walmart.com/wfsLite/sendinventory) page, then select _Create plan_.

### Step 2 – Add items

Search for items in your catalog, or upload a spreadsheet with multiple items at once. Then enter the number of units you're sending and how they're packed.

Pro tip

For more detail on creating shipments, view [_Send domestic inventory_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-inbound-orders:-Send-inventory).

### Step 3 – Choose distribution

Your plan may be split into multiple shipments to help with next-day and weekend fulfillment. If so, you’ll see an option to distribute inventory yourself  or use [Inventory Transfer Service.](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-Inventory-Transfer-Services)

### Step 4 – Select shipping

Choose whether you’re shipping as small parcel, LTL or FTL. Then scroll down to either review or add dimensions and weight for the shipment. Once you’ve finalized packing details, select _Confirm_ to see estimated prices and transit in the cards above. If you have multiple shipments, make sure to do this step in each tab.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt476c6e157f31d086/6967d3e72031d1635a7b6721/WPC_shipping_intelligence.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Pro tip

You can also download receiving labels for master cases. This is helpful if you want to palletize the shipment first and then edit the shipping plan.

For LTL and FTL, you’ll also add a preferred pickup date. We’ll use this date to schedule with the carrier, but the actual pickup date may differ based on operational requirements.

### Step 5 – Add contact and operating hours

Notes:

This step is optional for parcel.

For LTL and FTL, add contact info for your warehouse. We’ll email the bill of lading to this person, and the carrier may also send updates about the pickup.

You’ll also add operating hours for when your warehouse is open, a preferred pickup window and any pickup instructions. Make sure these are accurate so we can schedule a pickup with the carrier.

### Step 6 – Review shipping plan

Confirm the details for each shipment, then select _Create plan_. For FTL, we’ll assign a preferred carrier within 24 hours of your pickup date.

## What to expect next

For LTL and FTL shipments, your preferred pickup date is the earliest date you can expect the carrier to arrive. We'll coordinate with the carrier and email you a confirmed date. For more on the pickup process, go to [_Walmart Preferred Carrier: Prepare for pickup_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WPC-prepare-pickup) _._

## How to void charges

Once you’ve submitted the shipment, you’ll have a limited window to void the charges and edit: **24 hours** for parcel and **1 hour** for LTL and FTL. To void charges, search for the shipping plan or shipment on the [**Shipping Plans**](https://seller.walmart.com/wfsLite/sendinventory) page. Then select _View plan_ and look for the _Void charges_ option in each shipment.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Do I need to know weight and dimensions beforehand? Can I add them later?

We use this information to calculate the shipping price. You can always save the plan as a draft and come back later to enter the weight and dimensions. Note that the price may change if you enter packing details and later edit them.

* * *

Why can’t I enter a preferred pickup date for LTL or FTL?

Our carriers have certain windows when they can pick up a shipment. For LTL, your preferred pickup date must be a business day within the next 14 days. For FTL, the date must be 3 to 17 days from today.

* * *

Can I choose the preferred carrier?

For parcel, you can choose between FedEx and UPS. For LTL and FTL, we’ll assign a carrier based on the lowest cost available. We work closely with all our carrier partners to ensure reliability, speed and cost-effectiveness.

* * *

Can I choose the fulfillment center I ship to?

No, you must send inventory to your assigned facility in Seller Center. We’ll pick compatible WFS facilities based on your ship-from address. If any of your items within a shipment has special handling requirements, such as hazardous materials, we may assign a different facility that can safely store them.
