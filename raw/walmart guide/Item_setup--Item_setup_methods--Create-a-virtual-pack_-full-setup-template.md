Add multiple items: Virtual packs

Last updated on Nov 6, 2025

Reading time: 6 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup%20methods/Create-a-virtual-pack:-full-setup-template#overview)
- [Create a virtual pack](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup%20methods/Create-a-virtual-pack:-full-setup-template#create)
- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup%20methods/Create-a-virtual-pack:-full-setup-template#frequently-asked-questions)

* * *

Overview

A virtual pack is a way to sell different combinations of a single product, such as paper towels sold in a two-pack or three-pack. This item setup method is helpful when a manufacturer doesn't provide pre-packaged options. In this guide, you'll learn how to create a virtual pack in Seller Center.

Notes:

Participation in virtual packs is by invitation-only and not available to all Marketplace sellers.

## Create a virtual pack

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Centerand select the _Add items_ button _._ Select the _Upload in bulk_ linkunder **Add thousands of items using a spreadsheet**. On the next page, select the _Download spreadsheet_ button under **Virtual packs**.

Notes:

You can only create a virtual pack for an item that’s already in your catalog.

Pro tip

To encourage customers to buy more of the item, you can offer the pack at a discounted price. Offering a discount is optional.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt6d2d5800e43a9041/690a28cc5c8513098766205e/2510_create-virtual-pack_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 2 — Complete the template

Enter as much information as possible. Make sure to fill out the required fields, or you’ll receive an error during the upload.

**Required**

- **Component GTIN**: The 14-digit Global Trade Item Number for the primary item.

- **Virtual Pack Quantity**: The number of items you want in the pack.

- **Initial Discount Rate:** You must enter a value in this field, such as _0_ for 0% or _5_ for 5%. Offering a discount is optional.

**Recommended**

- **Main image URL:** The web address for an image of the pack. An image can help give customers context for their purchase.

- **Key Features:** Any information you want to highlight about the product. The text you enter here will appear as a bulleted list in Walmart.com search results and on the item’s product pages. If this field is left blank, Walmart will use the item’s product description.


Notes:

The **Recommended** fields are optional. You only need to complete these fields if you have extra information to add. For example, if you want to use an online image of the pack instead of uploading one of your own, enter the image's web address (URL) in the **Main image URL** field.

**Optional**

- **Site Start Date and Site End Date:** The dates that you want the pack to be live on Walmart.com. If these fields are left blank, Walmart will use the primary item’s start and end date.


### Step 3 — Upload the template

To upload your completed template, navigate to your **Catalog** in Seller Center and select _Add items_. Select the _Upload in bulk_ link located under **Add thousands of items using a spreadsheet**. You can drag and drop your file onto the browser window under **Upload,** or select the _browse_ link to locate the file on your computer.

Notes:

After submitting your items, you can track their status from the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) in Seller Center. Updates may take up to 24 hours. Any data errors that are detected during the updates will require extra processing time.

### Step 4 — Manage your virtual pack

Once you’ve created the pack, you can manage it from your **Catalog.** Navigate to your **Catalog** in Seller Center and locate the pack's primary item (virtual packs aren't searchable). Then, hover your cursor over the item and select the _Variant item_ button to edit the item's pack and bundle deals.

Notes:

To make a single component unavailable for purchase, open the item in your **Catalog** and navigate to the **Site Experience** section. Then select the **Bundle Component Transactable** menu and choose _No_ to remove the component's availability from Walmart.com. Only the virtual packs will be available to buy.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Is there another way to set up a virtual pack in Seller Center?

Yes. You can also set up a virtual pack from the [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) page in Seller Center. Navigate to the **Catalog** page, locate the item you want and select the item’s checkbox. Then navigate to the **Actions** column, select the three dots and choose _Create virtual pack_.

* * *

How is buying a virtual pack different from buying two units of the same item?

You should offer customers a discount when choosing the virtual pack option, whereas purchasing two units would be twice the price of the single item. The virtual pack appears on the same page as variants, so you don’t need to create variant groups.

* * *

What's the difference between a virtual pack and a virtual bundle?

A virtual pack is a way to sell a product in various combinations of amounts, such as a white T-shirt that’s sold in a pack of three or a pair of shorts that’s sold in a pack of four different colors. Because the items are the same, each item has the same product ID.

A virtual bundle is a way to sell a group of related items within a single Walmart.com listing, such as a computer keyboard that’s sold with a mouse and mouse pad. Because the items are different, each item has its own product ID. Virtual bundles currently aren’t available on Walmart.com.

* * *

Which items can’t be sold in a virtual pack?

Only new items can be sold in a virtual pack. Ineligible items include Preowned and Refurbished items, beds, gift cards and certificates, and home gyms. For more information, download the [complete list of ineligible items](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltc28b481a58288c3c/Walmart_Virtual_Packs-Ineligible_Product_Types.xlsx?branch=us_mplearn).

* * *

How can I find performance metrics for my virtual packs?

Navigate to the [**Reports**](https://seller.walmart.com/analytics/reports) page in Seller Center, locate the **Report activity** section and  select the **Report type** menu. Choose _Virtual Pack Report_ and select the download icon to download a copy of the report.

* * *

How do I manage inventory for the virtual packs I’ve created?

Inventory is managed at the individual item level, not at the pack level. When inventory is added or removed in Seller Center, it happens in individual units, not in packs. To learn more, visit [_Update seller-fulfilled inventory in bulk in Seller Center_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Seller-fulfilled%20inventory%20management/Update-seller-fulfilled-inventory-in-bulk-in-Seller-Center)  or [_Manage WFS inventory_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20Inventory%20management/manage-wfs-inventory).

* * *

How do I update the price of a virtual pack?

Navigate to the [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) page in Seller Center and locate the item you want to edit. In the **Actions** column, select the three dots and choose _Manage virtual pack_. On the next page, locate the item you want, select the three dots again and choose _Edit virtual pack_.

* * *

Can virtual packs be returned?

Yes. Just as with individual items sold on Walmart.com, customers can return a virtual pack if its contents are damaged or not as described. Customers can also return a single item from a virtual pack if it’s damaged or not as described.

* * *

How will I be charged for a virtual pack?

The Referral Fee for virtual packs is the same as the Referral Fee for the individual item included in the packs. In some product categories, the Referral Fee changes as the price increases. For virtual packs, the Referral Fee is determined by the average unit price, not the bundle price.

If a referral discount is applicable at the unit level, it will apply to the virtual pack as well.  If you use [Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS)](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/walmart-fulfillment-services-wfs-overview), review the WFS FAQs for information about fulfillment and storage fees.

* * *

How do virtual packs affect my shipping?

The same shipping method applies to a virtual pack as to the item it contains. You don’t need to set up additional Shipping Templates for virtual packs.

If you use Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), there’s no change to the shipping process. Once you create a virtual pack, you should create a shipping plan and ship items to the assigned Walmart fulfillment center.

* * *

Am I required to ship virtual pack items together in one box?

No, but it’s strongly recommended.

* * *

How do virtual packs affect fulfillment?

The same fulfillment method applies to a virtual pack as to the item it contains. For example, if the item is fulfilled by WFS, the pack will be too. You’ll need to convert the individual item to a Walmart-fulfilled item if you wish to sell the virtual pack via WFS. A virtual pack can’t be fulfilled by both the seller and WFS.

* * *

What’s the fee structure for virtual packs fulfilled by Walmart?

Fulfillment fees are charged per unit, even when items are sold in multi-packs. For example, a 2-pack listing is treated as two individual units, and Walmart Fulfillment Services applies a separate fee to each unit sold.

However, bundling more units into a virtual pack can reduce your total fulfillment fees. These savings only apply to items with a shipping weight of 20 pounds or less. If the total price of your virtual pack is over $10, you won’t be charged the usual [low-retail fee](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20basics/WFS-fees) of $1.

Keep in mind that the [standard storage fee](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20basics/WFS-fees#Storage_fees) applies to each unit in your virtual pack. For more information and examples of fees, visit [_WFS fees for virtual packs_](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt6432025da1f86c73/VirtualPacks_WFSFeeAddendum.pdf?branch=us_mplearn).
