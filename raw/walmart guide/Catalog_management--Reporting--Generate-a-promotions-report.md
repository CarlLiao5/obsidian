Generate a promotions report

Last updated on Dec 27, 2024

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

* * *

Overview

When you generate a **Promotions report** in Seller Center, you can browse all active and future promotional pricing that you’ve set up.

## Generate a promotions report

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center and select the _Download view_ button. Choose _go to the Reports page_ to navigate to the **Reports** dashboard.

### Step 2 — Generate the report

Navigate to the **On request reports** section and select _Promotions_ r _eport_ from the dropdown menu. Choose _Promotions report_ and select _Request report_.

### Step 3 — Download the report

You can track the progress of your requested report and download the report once it’s ready in the **Report activity** section.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What is the Comparison Price?

The [Comparison Price](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/Comparison-Price-rules) is the price of an item before an applied promotion. This price will only appear in the report when a promotion is active.

* * *

Can I change the Comparison Price?

No.

* * *

How do I modify a promotional offer that is currently live?

If you’d like to edit a live promotional offer, visit [_Promotional pricing: Overview._](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Promotions/Promotional-pricing:-Overview)
