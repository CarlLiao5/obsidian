Walmart+ badge program: Overview

Last updated on Nov 18, 2025

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

Overview

The Walmart+ badge is a special designation that can be added to a seller-fulfilled item listing on Walmart.com. The badge shows customers that the item offers fast, reliable shipping. In this guide, you’ll learn how the Walmart+ badge works and how it can help grow your business.

## How does it work?

The Walmart+ badge program allows you to offer free shipping benefits to Walmart+ members. You’re eligible to participate in the program if you’ve met the [Seller Performance Standards](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Seller-performance-standards) for the last 30 days, and if your items meet the listing criteria. To verify your eligibility, visit the [**Walmart+ Seller Fulfilled**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/walmart-plus) page in Seller Center.

Notes:

When an item is set to ship in three calendar days or less, Walmart applies free shipping to the item and sets its previous shipping price to zero.

Once you've confirmed that you're eligible, navigate to the **Enroll items in Walmart+** section of the page to [enroll your item](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-badge-enroll-your-items). You can enroll and unenroll items at any time. Walmart will review your item enrollment requests and determine eligibility based on shipping speed. Your item must ship in three calendar days or less.

Once an item is enrolled, the Walmart+ badge and the free shipping badge ( **Free with W+**) will appear under the item listing. The badges indicate that the free shipping offer is exclusively for Walmart+ members.

Below is an illustrative example of how the Walmart+ badge will appear:

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltac444547c3325562/69d03dc938cb6d9dfe5ee2e1/2604_Wplus-badging-SFS-offers.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Pro tip

To increase your item’s chances of enrollment, set up the item to ship in three calendar days or less. You can ship with [Walmart's OneDay or TwoDay delivery programs](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-methods:-expedited-delivery-programs), or you can use [**Simplified Shipping Settings**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/simplified-shipping) and choose an expedited carrier service. Then set the item’s lag time configuration to 0.

Notes:

Not all items in your catalog may meet Walmart’s criteria, and enrollment doesn’t guarantee the Walmart+ badge. Because Walmart continuously monitors seller and item performance, the Walmart+ badge may or may not display on a listing as performance metrics change.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Can I unenroll an item?

Yes. If you’re eligible to participate in the Walmart+ badge program, you can [enroll or unenroll items](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-badge-enroll-your-items) at any time.

* * *

I enrolled an item in the Walmart+ badge program. Why isn’t the Walmart+ badge displaying?

First, check the [**Walmart+ Seller Fulfilled**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/walmart-plus) page in Seller Center to verify that you’re eligible for the program. Then confirm that your item is enrolled. If your item is enrolled and you meet all the [Seller Performance Standards](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Seller-performance-standards), it’s likely that the shipping speed for your item exceeds the three calendar days or less requirement. The Walmart+ badge should display when the shipping speed requirement is met.

* * *

Does Walmart cover the shipping cost?

No. Walmart only covers shipping fees for listings that display the Walmart+ badge. Your regular shipping fee configurations apply to all non-Walmart+ orders.
