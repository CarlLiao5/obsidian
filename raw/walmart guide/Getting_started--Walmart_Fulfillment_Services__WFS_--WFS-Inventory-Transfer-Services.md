WFS Inventory Transfer Service: Overview

Last updated on Jan 22, 2026

Reading time: 8 min

Overview

Inventory Transfer Service (ITS) moves your inventory to multiple fulfillment centers across the country. You send all your sortable\* inventory to 1 building, called a **transfer station**, and we take care of distributing it nationwide for a fee. When you use ITS, you can:

- Have inventory available across the nation


- Improve your next-day and weekend delivery


- Save on inbound shipping costs by sending 1 shipment for sortable items


- Streamline prep and tracking by consolidating shipments


## Eligibility

Currently, ITS is available only to a select group of WFS sellers based on inventory type. If you’d like to get access to ITS, please contact your account manager or Support.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt5362f80039617402/68432cd3b045d3d967547bf6/Inventory_Transfer_Service_graphic.png?branch=us_mplearn)

## Fees

The following table breaks down the per-unit fees for ITS. When you create a shipping plan, you’ll also see the estimated total cost.

Here’s how to calculate shipping weight:

- For units that weigh less than 1 lb, use the unit weight. Then round up to the nearest pound.
- If your unit weight is equal to or over 1 lb, use the greater of unit weight or dimensional weight.\* Then round up to the nearest pound.

\*Dimensional weight = (length x width x height in inches) / 139

| Shipping weight | **ITS fee per unit** |
| --- | --- |
| 1 lb or under | $0.25 |
| 1+ to 2 lb | $0.35 |
| 2+ lb | $0.35 + $0.10 for each lb> 2 lb |

**_Find more details about_** [**_ITS fees for Walmart Cross Border: Imports_**](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-Inventory-Transfer-Services#imports) **_in the section below._**

## How does it work?

When you create a shipping plan, you’ll see 2 options:

- If you use ITS, you’ll send inventory to a single transfer station.
- If you choose to [distribute inventory yourself](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20policies%20&%20standards/wfs-shipping-plans-distribute-inventory-yourself), you’ll send it to your assigned fulfillment centers. This may mean splitting your inventory and packing multiple shipments. Remember, you are required to ship your inventory to all fulfillment centers assigned to you. This lets us offer speedy delivery to shoppers across the U.S and improves your next-day and weekend delivery coverage.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blte9486b5cf0a1f958/68432604b045d3d51c547bb6/ITS_selection.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Pro tip

Some shipping plans may not have ITS because your items aren’t sortable\* or eligible.

When you choose ITS, you’ll be assigned a single transfer station to send inventory to. From there, we’ll split the inventory into multiple shipments and distribute it to fulfillment centers across the country. We choose the final destinations based on the item’s level on hand, on order and geography-based demand.

After arriving at the transfer station, some of your inventory may be available to sell within 4 business days. We'll also make sure enough inventory is distributed to the first fulfillment center, so your items stay in stock while we move the rest across the network.

* * *

### Track inventory transfers

You can check when shipments are checked in at a transfer station, on the way to their final destinations and when receiving started. Here’s how to track the progress:

1. Log into Seller Center and go to the [**Shipping Plans**](https://seller.walmart.com/wfs/shipments) page.

2. Search for the plan ID or shipment ID. You can also filter the status by _Transfer in progress_.

3. Select _View plan_, then go to the _Track items_ tab.

4. In the _Received units_ column, you can track how many units are received at their final destinations. Select the number to open a pop-up with the estimated delivery and receiving progress for each destination.


After arriving at the transfer station, some of your inventory may be available to sell within 4 business days. You can check the status of available inventory on the [**Inventory**](https://seller.walmart.com/wfsLite/manage-inventory) page.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt39adb9c7464c08fb/687932f6052683c5f8cffe11/ITS_leg2_tracking.png?branch=us_mplearn)

## ITS for Walmart Cross Border: Imports

From the time items are received at the transfer station until they reach their final destinations, ITS works the same for both Walmart Cross Border: Imports and domestic shipments. In the following sections, we’ll explain key differences for [Walmart Cross Border: Imports](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-cross-border-imports-overview) shipments.

ITS will apply to all Walmart Imports shipments of sortable\* (standard size) inventory using any ocean transport mode (FCL and LCL), all origins and all destinations.

### ITS fees now in effect

As of October 1, 2025, Walmart Cross Border: Imports shipments will be subject to ITS fees. You’ll see an “Inventory transfer service” line on the estimated price calculator and final booking confirmation page when booking shipments of sortable inventory. ITS will only be charged for shipments booked on or after October 1, 2025 and will not affect any previously booked shipments.

### ITS fee maximums for Walmart Cross Border: Imports (as of February 1, 2026)

Limited ITS fees are just one of the [benefits](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-cross-border-imports-overview#benefits) of using Walmart Imports to ship your inventory to WFS. ITS fees are calculated per unit based on weight using the [ITS fee table](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-Inventory-Transfer-Services#fees), until your shipment reaches the Walmart Imports ITS fee maximum. In Seller Center, you'll see your "maximum charge savings" when estimating the price for your shipment.

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **Container/Shipment size** | **Walmart Cross Border: Imports ITS fee maximum** |
| 20’ Dry | $1,700 |
| 40’ Dry | $3,400 |
| 40’ High | $3,800 |
| 45’ High | $4,300 |
| LCL shipments | $55/cbm |

_View_ [_weight and volume limits_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart-Cross-Border-Imports-Estimate-pricing-and-book-a-shipment#Volume-and-weight-limits) _for different container sizes and shipment modes._

After items have been received at their final fulfillment centers, you can see Imports ITS fees in the [Settlement Report](https://seller.walmart.com/wfsLite/reports). Charges for each shipment are listed as “InventoryTransferFee”in the Transaction Type column. These are charged under different line items than Walmart Logistics charges.

### Estimating ITS fees during booking

When booking Walmart Imports shipments of sortable\* (standard size inventory), you’ll see an “Inventory transfer service” line on the _Estimated price calculator_. The price will show as “TBD” until you add items in the _Items to send_ tab. On that page, select _Update price_ after adding items, and you’ll see your new estimated price at the top of the page. The final ITS fee estimate will show in the **Estimated price** section of the _Review shipment_ page before you confirm your booking.

ITS fees may change after booking if booking information is inaccurate. For example, ITS fees will be added for any additional units received, and will not be charged for any non-received units (e.g. any missing units at time of receiving). For [LCL shipments](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-cross-border-imports-less-than-container-load-lcl-shipments), sometimes the cargo measurements at the origin warehouse (CFS) differ from what was provided at the time of booking. In those cases, adjusted ITS fees will be calculated into the new estimated price you accept at that time.

Notes:

\*Sortable items are less than 30 lb (13.6kg) with maximum dimensions of 25" x 20" x 14" (63.5 cm x 58.4 cm x 35.5 cm).

## Manage receiving delays

Before you contact Support, follow these steps:

- [Track the status](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-Inventory-Transfer-Services#tracktransfers) of each transfer, including the receiving progress. Associates inspect and put away each unit by hand, so it may take time to complete receiving.


- Check the Shipping Plans page for [receiving issues](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/troubleshoot-wfs-shipments-with-receiving-issues). Damage, incorrect prep and other issues may delay receiving.


- If you use [your own carrier](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-inbound-orders-Choose-a-shipment-method?locale=en-US), be as accurate as possible with the estimated ship date. This helps us plan labor and inventory capacity.


  - Also add the tracking number as soon as you get it. This helps us schedule deliveries more efficiently.

- Wait at least 10 days from the time of delivery for receiving to finish.


If you believe your inventory is missing, you can [open a dispute](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Troubleshooting/Troubleshoot-WFS-receiving-issues:-open-a-dispute-claim) after 10 days.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What will this mean for my inbound lead times into WFS?

After arriving at the transfer station, it may take 4 business days for your inventory to be available to sell. You can check the status on the [**Inventory**](https://seller.walmart.com/wfsLite/manage-inventory) page in Seller Center. We'll make sure enough inventory is distributed to the first fulfillment center, so your items stay in stock while we move the rest across the network.

* * *

How can I opt out of Inventory Transfer Service?

You can choose to use Inventory Transfer Service or [self-distribute](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20policies%20&%20standards/wfs-shipping-plans-distribute-inventory-yourself) your inventory every time you create a new shipping plan in Seller Center. If you self-distribute your inventory, you'll send your sortable items to multiple fulfillment centers. This may mean splitting your shipping plan or inventory across multiple shipments. If you're an API seller, you'll need to update your [Create Inbound Shipment API](https://developer.walmart.com/us-marketplace/reference/createshipment) to choose in each shipping plan.

* * *

What determines whether an item is moved from a transfer station to fulfillment centers?

We look at an item's level on hand, on order and geography-based demand to make sure the inventory at the site matches the demand.

* * *

How will you handle damaged items?

If the item is damaged on the way to or at the transfer station, we’ll follow your [return rules](https://seller.walmart.com/seller-profile/return-policy) in Seller Center to ship the item back to you or dispose of it. But if the item is damaged on the way to or at the final fulfillment centers, it’s considered _Walmart at fault_. We’ll credit you the original sales price and dispose of the inventory.

* * *

What's the difference between MTR and ITS transfers?

A [Material Transfer Request (MTR)](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20Inventory%20management/wfs-inventory-movements-mtr) happens occasionally when inventory is already stored at a fulfillment center. We may move your stored inventory to another fulfillment center for operational or business purposes. This type of inventory move is free and made at Walmart’s discretion. If this happens, you'll see a new shipping plan that starts with “MTR” on the Shipping Plans page.

In contrast, ITS is a choice you make when you send inventory to a fulfillment center. It’s a cost-effective, efficient way to distribute inventory across our network by sending just 1 WFS shipment.
