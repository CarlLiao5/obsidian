Overview

It’s imperative for sellers to understand and share our commitment to a fair, open, and honest Marketplace culture. All Walmart Marketplace sellers are responsible for being aware of and compliant with all policies, rules, and guidelines. Failure to adhere may result in account suspension or termination from the Marketplace program.

Policies are divided into two sections: seller agreement policies and additional rules and guidelines. You can review complete policies below. Consider bookmarking this page to keep it handy.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

I signed an agreement when joining Walmart Marketplace; where can I view it?

The agreement that sellers must accept when joining Walmart Marketplace is the Comprehensive Walmart Marketplace Program Retailer Agreement (the “Retailer Agreement”). Admin users can view it in Seller Center by selecting the gear icon to access **Account Settings**, then navigating to the **Agreements** page under **Administrator Options**.

* * *

Is this the full list of policies that sellers are required to adhere to?

Sellers are required to adhere to all terms of the Retailer Agreement and all Marketplaces policies, rules, and guidelines. While this is a comprehensive list of the main seller policies, there may be other rules or policies outlined in Seller Help guides. Consider bookmarking this page to keep it handy. Policies are regularly updated.
