Overview

_Big and bulky_ means items that are larger and heavier than usual. Here are just some examples of big and bulky items:

- Home appliances (e.g., big screen TVs, refrigerators)
- Sporting and recreation equipment (e.g., kayaks, bikes, home gyms, pool tables)
- Outdoor items (e.g., pools, trampolines, patio furniture)
- Home goods (e.g., furniture, desks, chairs)


If you‚Äôve used Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) before, the item setup, packaging and shipping processes are all the same. Send big and bulky items to WFS facilities through freight shipping, and we‚Äôll take care of delivering them to customers in a reliable, timely manner.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltd3f6a4f1cd685c13/664faf79b5316f000dc71056/standard_vs_big_and_bulky.png?branch=us_mplearn)

## How does it work?

Your item is considered big and bulky if it meets¬Ý**at least one** of these criteria:

- Unit weight is between 150 lb and up to 500 lb

- Longest side is between 108" and up to 120", including packaging

- Longest side + girth\* is greater than 165", including packaging

\*Girth = 2 x (width + height)


In addition, your big and bulky item must be able to fit through a trailer door. Make sure it follows these limits:

- Median side is less than 105", including packaging

- Shortest side is less than 93", including packaging


Imagine Bentonville Sporting Goods sells kayaks. Each one weighs 36 lb. and is 120" x 28" x 14". To determine if it‚Äôs eligible for the big and bulky tier:

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **Unit weight** | 36 lb. |
| **Longest side** | 120" |
| **Longest side + girth** | 120" + 2\*(28" + 14") = 204" |

This kayak is a big and bulky item because its longest side and longest side + girth meet the criteria. Its dimensions are also within a trailer‚Äôs limits.

## Fees

The simplest way to preview fulfillment and storage fees is through the¬Ý[WFS Cost Estimator](https://marketplace.walmart.com/walmart-fulfillment-services-pricing/). Just tell us the item dimensions and weight, and we‚Äôll automatically calculate estimated fees. Big and bulky fees are based only on unit weight. We do not charge for packaging or pallet weight.

For a complete breakdown of fees, including return processing, go to [_WFS fees_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-fees).

## Shipping speed and delivery

Big and bulky items are shipped using ground transportation and may require more than 2 days for delivery. The shipping speed will depend on your assigned fulfillment center and the customer‚Äôs location. On Walmart.com, items will have a 3+day shipping tag but customers will see a more accurate estimate in the listing‚Äôs details.

The carrier will deliver to the first dry, secure area of a customer‚Äôs home. This could be outside the front door, on a porch or inside a lobby.

## Customer returns

Big and bulky items¬Ý_cannot_ be returned in stores. Instead, customers can return big and bulky items by scheduling a pickup. When a customer starts a return, they'll get instructions on how to do so, such as placing the item outside their home‚Äôs door. They do not need to repackage the item. The carrier will take care of it and transport items directly from a customer‚Äôs home to a WFS return center.

We‚Äôll then follow the¬Ý[WFS returns process](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20basics/wfs-returns-overview)¬Ýto assess the returned item. If it‚Äôs in a sellable condition, we‚Äôll put the item back in inventory. However, customers may return big and bulky items already assembled or with damaged packaging. If the item is unsellable, we‚Äôll ship it back to you or dispose of it. Note that for any returns identified as¬Ý_Walmart at fault_, Walmart will keep the inventory regardless of your¬Ý[return rules](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/returns).

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What fulfillment centers will I ship to?

Our non-sort facilities are distributed throughout the US. You‚Äôll be assigned a WFS facility once you create a shipping plan. We determine the ship-to location based on geographic sales demand and available capacity.

* * *

Can I send an item that has multiple boxes?

Yes, you can send¬Ý[multi-box items](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-multi-box-items)¬Ýto WFS fulfillment centers. You‚Äôll need set up the item in your catalog as a sellable set made up of multiple boxes (e.g., trampoline shipped separately as a safety net, jump mat and legs).

* * *

Are big and bulky items part of Walmart+ assortment?

Yes, big and bulky items are included in Walmart+. Walmart+ members will get free shipping on those items.

* * *

Can I have big and bulky items returned to me?

Yes, you can have your big and bulky items shipped back to you rather than being restocked or disposed of. Make sure to set your [return rules](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/returns) in Seller Center. Removal fees will apply.

* * *

Can I apply discounts to big and bulky item fulfillment?

Promotional discounts are not eligible for big and bulky items.
