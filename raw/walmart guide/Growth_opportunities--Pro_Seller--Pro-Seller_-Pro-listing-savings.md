Pro Seller: Pro Listing Savings

Last updated on Feb 1, 2026

Reading time: 2 min

Overview

The Pro Listing Savings program is designed to help sellers who are in the process of earning or have earned [**Pro Seller**](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Items%20and%20inventory/pro-seller-overview) status. The program is intended to help sellers grow, improve product visibility and earn up to a 10% Referral Fee discount on qualified Pro Listing offers. In this guide, you’ll learn how to improve your chances of qualifying your offers for Pro Listing Savings.

## How does it work?

To view your eligible Pro Listing offers, navigate to the [**Pro Seller**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/seller-tiering) dashboard and select the _Pro Listings_ tab. Your Pro Listing Savings from the last 90-days are displayed at the top of the list.

You can also view how many eligible offers are in your catalog and the number of listings without competitive pricing or fast & free shipping.

In order for items to be eligible for Pro Listing Savings, you must first have an active **Advanced Seller** or **Pro Seller** tier status. Next, your listings will need to meet the following offer requirements:

- **Your offer must have competitive** **pricing** that meets or beats market


- **Your offer must have fast & free shipping** in two days or less


Once these requirements are met, future orders on those Pro Listings will earn you a 5% discount on referral fees as an Advanced Seller or a 10% discount on referral fees as a Pro Seller.

![11467_Pro_Listing_Savings_GIF1.gif](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltca48df8f306350b3/6706b34a02bc608d05dae413/11467_Pro_Listing_Savings_GIF1.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

Referral Fee discounts from other programs can’t be combined with the Pro Listing Savings discount. Your offers may not receive a Pro Listing Savings discount if you’re already receiving a higher discount from another program or if the offer is enrolled in Walmart-funded incentives. Walmart reserves sole discretion in deciding what Referral Fee discount to apply to your offer.

## Additional guidelines

To help increase your chances of qualifying for Pro Listing Savings, follow best practices below.

- **Competitive price:** Enable the [Repricer](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Repricer:-overview) and assign items to a Competitive price or External price strategy based on rules you set to help keep your prices competitive .


- **External price match**: Submit an [external price match](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Troubleshooting/Submit-an-external-price-match) to help customers gauge price competitiveness and define item eligibility in some Marketplace pricing programs. Offers that are missing price match will not be eligible to meet the competitive price requirement.


- **Fast & free shipping:** Enroll in [TwoDay delivery,](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-methods:-expedited-delivery-programs) [Simplified Shipping Settings](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Simplified%20Shipping%20Settings/simplified-shipping-settings-overview), or fulfill orders with [Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS)](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/walmart-fulfillment-services-wfs-overview) to ensure quick and timely delivery to customers. Expand the range of zip codes eligible for TwoDay shipping to reach a larger customer base.


Notes:

You must have the Amazon Standard Identification Number (ASIN) of a comparable item to submit an external price match **.** You can regularly verify ASIN information by generating an [Item Report](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Reporting/Generate-an-item-report) in Seller Center.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How can I view offers that can be price-adjusted?

Navigate to the [**Pro Seller**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/seller-tiering/overview) dashboard and select the **Pro Listings** tab. Under _Listings missing competitive price_, select _View items_.

* * *

How can I view listings that don’t show an item’s external price comparison?

To view listings that don’t show a price match, navigate to _Filters_ in the [**Pro Listings**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/seller-tiering/pro-listing) dashboard and select _Missing price match_ under **Has competitive price.**

* * *

How long does the Referral Fee discount on my Pro Listings last?

Qualified Pro Listing offers will receive up to 90 days of Referral Fee discounts on eligible orders.  Walmart reserves sole discretion in deciding what the length of the Referral Fee discount is and what discount to apply to your offer.

* * *

Why am I not receiving any savings on my Pro Listing orders?

You may not receive savings on an order for multiple reasons:

- Your active tier status on the order date wasn't Advanced Seller or Pro Seller.


- Your offer has received Pro Listing Savings for 90 days and won't receive additional savings—check the **Pro Listing days** column on the [**Pro Listings**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/seller-tiering/pro-listing) dashboard in Seller Center.


- Your offer’s price wasn't competitive at the time of the order. This may occur because of pricing volatility.


- The difference between the **Ordered** date and **Deliver by** date on the order exceeds two-days.
