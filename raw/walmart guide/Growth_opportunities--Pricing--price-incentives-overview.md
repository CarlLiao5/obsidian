Price Incentives: Overview

Overview

The Price Incentives program helps you improve your sales by helping to ensure that customers are offered competitive prices for Marketplace items. The program consists of two different types of incentive adjustments: **Walmart-funded** and **Reduced Referral Fees**. In this guide, you’ll learn about each incentive type.

## How does it work?

### Walmart-funded

Walmart will lower the item price on Walmart.com to make it more competitive, while providing you with a payout equal to the price-adjusted item sold at its original price. Your price is then adjusted to help keep it market competitive, and the incentive will be auto applied to your orders.

Refer to the illustration below as an example:

| --- | --- |
| **Program enrollment** | Eligible items are automatically subsidized unless opted out. For items not opted out, you may view reduced prices for eligible items at Walmart's discretion. You can opt-out of price incentives for specific items and/or turn off Walmart-funded incentives if you don't want to participate in the program at all. For more information, review the [terms and conditions](https://6064071.fs1.hubspotusercontent-na1.net/hubfs/6064071/Walmart%20Funded%20Incentives%20Program%20T&C.pdf) or visit [Walmart-funded incentives: Program enrollment](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Price%20Incentives/walmart-funded-program-enrollment). |
| **Item enrollment** | Although you can't directly enroll items in the Walmart-funded program, Walmart selects eligible items for enrollment at its discretion. If you don't want to participate in the program, you may unenroll specific items through the [**Incentives**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/incentives) page or disable your entire catalog from consideration for the Walmart-funded incentives program. For more information, visit [_Walmart-funded incentives: Manage item enrollment._](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Price%20Incentives/walmart-funded-incentives-manage-item-enrollment) |

Notes:

The incentive amount may vary per sale and is subject to a maximum cap beyond which the item price is restored back to your last submitted price and no incentive is applied. The incentive amount applied on an order is reflected in your Payment report.

### Reduced Referral Fee

Walmart will provide a Reduced Referral Fee that represents the promotional Referral Fee offered during the incentive enrollment period. Your item price will automatically be adjusted to the incentive price listed in the **Ready to enroll** tab on the [**Incentives**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/incentives) page. You can always update the price for items enrolled in Reduced Referral Fee incentives at any time, but you’ll only earn the stated Reduced Referral Fee if you keep the enrolled item price within the incentive price threshold suggested for the duration of the incentive period.

The price threshold refers to the max price that you should maintain to receive referral incentives. If the price is increased above the incentive price threshold or unenrolled, the Reduced Referral Fee won’t be applied, and the standard Referral Fee will be applied.  Refer to the illustration below as an example:

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltb3c39f3cee02b3e0/69cffda7a21ea57524bad384/2603_price-incentives_reduced-referral-fee.png?branch=us_mplearn)

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **Item enrollment** | You must choose to enroll recommendations for each eligible item. You’ll have full control and visibility into your enrolled items and can enroll items individually or activate the auto-enroll feature to automatically enroll items with an estimated payout at or above the minimum payout threshold you set for each incentive period.  Your minimum payout threshold is the lowest payout percentage you’ll accept for Reduced Referral Fee incentives.<br>Each incentive recommendation comes with a specific expiration date after which the standard Referral Fee will be applied to the item.  For more information, visit [_Reduced Referral Fee incentives: Manage item enrollment._](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Price%20Incentives/reduced-referral-fee-incentives-manage-item-enrollment) |

Notes:

Incentives will discontinue if you price items above this threshold.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What happened to the Commission Adjustment Program (CAP)?

CAP has been renamed to Walmart-funded incentives.

* * *

How can I get notified when new Reduced Referral Fee incentives are available for enrollment?

Reduced Referral Fee incentive recommendations refresh on a weekly basis. You can set the frequency of your notifications by navigating to _Settings_ and selecting _Account._ From there, choose _New price incentives_ under **Pricing**. You can also adjust the notifications for **Expiring suggestions** and **New suggestions**.

* * *

How can I view the performance of my enrolled items?

The **Performance** tab gives you a consolidated view of how your price incentives are performing. You can track key metrics such as gross merchandise value **(** **GMV** **)**, **Traffic**, **Price C** **ompetitiveness** **Score (PCS)** and more across Walmart-funded and Reduced Referral Fee incentives. Use this view to identify optimization opportunities and make informed pricing decisions based on how incentives are driving results.

* * *

Does turning off Walmart-funded incentives also opt me out of Referral Fee incentives?

No, turning off Walmart-funded incentives will only disable that incentive type. Reduced Referral Fee incentives will still be available to be enrolled.

* * *

Does Reduced Referral Fee auto-enrollment change my prices or payouts?

No. When you auto- ~~~~ enroll items into the Reduced Referral Fee incentive adjustment, it will only enroll items that meet your selected payout threshold. You’ll have full control over pricing and can update your preferences or enrollment status at any time.

* * *

Can I enroll any item from my catalog in Walmart-funded incentives?

No, you can’t choose which items receive Walmart-funded incentives since they’re provided at Walmart’s discretion. Items that have been opted out won’t receive an incentive.

* * *

Can I enroll any items in my catalog into Reduced Referral Fee incentives?

No, you can only choose from the eligible items in the **Ready to enroll** tab on the **Incentives** page.You can update the price for items enrolled in Reduced Referral Fee incentives at any time, but you will only earn the stated Reduced Referral Fee if you keep the enrolled item prices at or below the suggested incentive price threshold.

* * *

Can I enroll and unenroll into price incentives using APIs?

Yes, Walmart APIs are available to get a list of incentives suggestions, enroll or unenroll into referral fee incentives and opt out from Walmart-funded incentives. Refer to the [Price management section](https://developer.walmart.com/) for guidance on using incentives APIs.

* * *

If I apply promotions or discounts to an item that is currently receiving any active incentive types, will the item be automatically opted out or unenrolled from receiving any incentives?

As long as the promotional price is at or below the incentive price threshold, you will continue receiving incentives.
