Overview

You can use our self-serve [**Media Library**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/media-library) dashboard to incorporate [rich media](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-detail-page:-rich-media) into your product listings. Adding rich media such as videos and 360-spin images can help improve search results, increase order conversions and reduce returns. In this guide, learn how to add media and manage rich media.

Notes:

AI-generated content must be accurate, truthful, rights-compliant, and aligned with Marketplace policies, including our [_Product details policy_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-Detail-Page:-overview).

## Add rich media

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to Seller Center. Under **Catalog**, select [_Media Library_](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/media-library) to add media.

### Step 2 — Add videos

Choose the _Add Media_ button and select _Videos_. Then, upload your file in the _Drag and drop a file_ _or browse_ section. The video will take less than 60 seconds to process. Once you've added the video, fill out the required fields. Then, select _Submit_.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltfcc166842e893f56/67ae0839f2e75c6a055df6c7/Media-library-video_GIF.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

The file must be 100 MB or less and the title can’t be longer than 200 characters.

### Step 3 — Add 360-Spin images

Select _Add media_ and choose _360-spin images_. Then, add or drag and drop up to 24 images (JPG, PNG), not exceeding 1MB. Add a descriptive title under **Title**. You can choose to bulk upload your images to up to 50 GTINs at once. When you're ready, select _Submit_.

![Media-library-360-spin_GIF.gif](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt59235a31b1a23d44/67ae082ad0dfbc2a8bc54e7c/Media-library-360-spin_GIF.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

Pro tip

Make sure your images meet our [guidelines and requirements](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-detail-page:-Image-guidelines-&-requirements) before uploading.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How do I manage rich media in the Media Library?

You can manage rich media content in the Media Library in different ways. For instance, you can add new media, track upload processes in real-time and delete rich media assets directly in the **Media Library** portal. Additionally, you can edit videos and use the search (or magnifying tool) to browse by title or GTIN.

* * *

How long does it take for media assets to get published?

Once a media asset is added and submitted, it’ll get published in the **Catalog** within four hours.

* * *

Can I add a video to multiple GTINs at a time?

Yes, you can add a video to multiple GTINs (up to 50) at a time.

* * *

What if my video or 360-spin image has been disabled?

If you have questions, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.
