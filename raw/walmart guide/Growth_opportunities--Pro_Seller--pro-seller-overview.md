Overview

The Pro Seller program rewards sellers meeting performance metrics with benefits to stand out from competitors and opportunities to save. Once you meet the eligibility criteria, you’ll earn a Rising Seller, Advanced Seller or Pro Seller status automatically. In this guide, you’ll learn about the criteria and rewards.

## How does it work?

When you meet all the criteria on a refresh cycle, you’ll earn the highest status qualified and receive the benefits offered at that tier. Pro Seller metrics and downloadable reports data shown in the dashboard are calculated daily. To qualify, you must meet the following standards:

| Metric | Rising Seller | Advanced Seller | Pro Seller | Definition |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **On-time delivery rate** | Greater than or equal to 90% | Greater than or equal to 92.5% | Greater than or equal to 95% | The rate that orders were delivered by the [Expected Delivery Date (EDD)](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping-and-fulfillment-policy) in the last 90 days. |
| **Cancellation rate** | Less than or equal to 2.5% | Less than or equal to 2.0% | Less than or equal to 1.5% | The rate that orders were canceled by the seller after receiving the order from Walmart in the last 90 days. |
| **Seller response rate** | Greater than or equal to 90% | Greater than or equal to 92.5% | Greater than or equal to 95% | The rate of [customer inquiries](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Customer%20care/Customer-Care-policy) responded to within 48 hours of receipt in the last 30 days. |
| **Shipping speed score** | Greater than or equal to 5% | Greater than or equal to 25% | Greater than or equal to 50% | The percentage of your published catalog offering free ThreeDay or less delivery. |
| **Content quality score** | Greater than or equal to 65% | Greater than or equal to 70% | Greater than or equal to 75% | The average [content quality](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Items%20and%20inventory/Listing-quality-and-rewards-dashboard) score of your published catalog. |
| **Price competitiveness score** | Greater than or equal to 65% | Greater than or equal to 70% | Greater than or equal to 75% | The price competitiveness of your products compared to similar products on the market in the last 30 days. |
| **Orders received** | More than 100 orders | More than 100 orders | More than 100 orders | The number of completed orders in a rolling 90-day timeframe. |
| **Active days** | At least 90 days active | At least 90 days active | At least 90 days active | The number of days you’ve been active after completing onboarding or had your account reinstated. |
| **Marketplace policies** | In compliance with all Marketplace policies | In compliance with all Marketplace policies | In compliance with all Marketplace policies | Visit [_Marketplace Seller Code of Conduct_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Marketplace-Seller-Code-of-Conduct) for details. |

Notes:

Program requirements are subject to change without notice. You may not be eligible for a badge if you receive warnings of policy violations from Walmart. Respond to the warning and fix the issue in a timely manner. Walmart reserves the right to include or exclude sellers into the Pro Seller program at any time.

### Pro Seller tiers

When you become a Pro Seller, you'll receive the Pro Seller Badge next to your business name on each of your Walmart.com listings. There are several tiers to the Pro Seller program, and each one has its own benefits.

The Pro Seller status refreshes on the fifth and 20th of every month. Once you’ve met all eligibility criteria, you’ll receive the highest tier you qualify for on the next refresh cycle. You may lose your status and the associated benefits if you fall below criteria for two consecutive refresh cycles.  To learn more about each tier, review the following chart:

| Status | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Pro Seller** | When you become a Pro Seller, you'll unlock:  <br>- The **Pro Seller Badge** next to your business name on each of your Walmart.com listings<br>  <br>- A $50 monthly credit to apply towards [Search Engine Marketing (SEM)](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Advertising/Search%20Engine%20Marketing%20(SEM)/Search-Engine-Marketing-(SEM):-program-overview)<br>- A 10% Referral Fee discount on [Pro Listings](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Product%20listings/Pro-Seller:-Pro-listing-savings)<br>  <br>- Special [Walmart Marketplace Capital](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Financing%20options/Walmart-Marketplace-Capital:-Program-overview) terms, if eligible  <br>  <br>- [Post-Purchase Reviews](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Reviews/Review-Accelerator-Program:-How-to-enroll) credit<br>  <br>- [Ship with Walmart](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Ship%20with%20Walmart/Cross-border%20services/Ship-with-Walmart-Overview) discounts   <br>  <br>- [Faster Payouts](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/get-paid-early-request-a-one-time-transfer)<br>  <br>- Access to [Flash Deals](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Flash-Deals:-Program-overview)<br>- Access to [Extra Savings](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/discounts-overview) |
| **Advanced Seller** | When you achieve Advanced Seller status, you’ll unlock: <br>- A 5% Referral Fee discount on [Pro Listings](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Product%20listings/Pro-Seller:-Pro-listing-savings)<br>  <br>- Access to special terms on merchant cash advances with [Walmart Marketplace Capital](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Financing%20options/Walmart-Marketplace-Capital:-Program-overview), if eligible <br>  <br>- A $50 monthly credit to apply towards [Post-Purchase Reviews](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Reviews/Review-Accelerator-Program:-How-to-enroll)<br>  <br>- [Ship with Walmart](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Ship%20with%20Walmart/Cross-border%20services/Ship-with-Walmart-Overview) discounts  <br>  <br>- [Faster Payouts](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/get-paid-early-request-a-one-time-transfer)<br>  <br>- Invitation to exclusive [Pro Seller Master Series](https://marketplace.walmart.com/pro-seller-master-series-form/) |
| **Rising Seller** | When you achieve Rising Seller status, you’ll unlock: <br>- Up to a 25% discount on [Ship with Walmart](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Ship%20with%20Walmart/Cross-border%20services/Ship-with-Walmart-Overview) for shipping label purchases  <br>  <br>- Up to a 10% increase on your eligible [Faster Payout](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/get-paid-early-request-a-one-time-transfer) balance |

## Additional guidelines

Referral Fee discounts from other programs can’t be combined with the Pro Listing Savings discount. The seller’s offers may not receive a Pro Listing Savings discount if the seller is already receiving a higher discount from another program. Walmart reserves sole discretion in deciding what Referral Fee discount to apply to seller’s offer.

Walmart reserves the right to verify eligibility, recalculate, or reassess the incentive benefits earned by the seller under the Pro Seller program at its sole discretion. Walmart shall not be held liable for any errors or miscalculations that may occur in the process. If Walmart determines that the seller has received Pro Seller benefits that they were not eligible for or did not earn, Walmart may, at its discretion, offset such amounts from the seller's account without prior notice.

Walmart reserves the right to assess Pro Seller eligibility by reviewing the aforementioned metrics, as well as other performance metrics. There may also be other criteria not specified in this document, but which Walmart may use to determine if a seller is ineligible for the program. Walmart reserves the right to modify or alter a seller's Pro Seller status based on their performance, product or seller ratings. This includes the potential exclusion from reaching Pro Seller status, downgrading a seller's current status to a lower tier, or refusing any benefits related to the seller's status tier.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How can I improve my chances of achieving Pro Seller status?

Visit our [Pro Seller best practices resource](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt188660d5c613894b/Pro-Seller-Best-Practices.pdf?branch=us_mplearn) to learn more about how to maintain standards and provide exceptional customer service.

* * *

What are Pro Listing Savings?

If you’ve achieved Advanced Seller or Pro Seller status, your offers may qualify for [**Pro Listing savings**](https://marketplacelearn.stg.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Items%20and%20inventory/Pro-Seller:-Pro-listing-savings). Offers eligible for Pro Listing Savings will receive a five or 10% Referral Fee discount, depending on your status.

* * *

How much of a Ship With Walmart (SWW) discount will I receive?

Offers that have a delivery promise of TwoDay will receive a discount of 25% on purchases of labels from Ship With Walmart. Offers that have a delivery promise of ThreeDay will receive a discount of 10% on purchases of labels through Ship With Walmart.

* * *

Do monthly credits expire?

Post-Purchase Reviews and Search Engine Marketing credits are valid for three months after they’re issued and are issued once per month. Any seller can use the credits they’ve earned while the credits are valid. You’ll receive credits based on your current seller status.

Search Engine Marketing credits are only available to Pro Sellers. To view your promotion credits balance, navigate to the [**Search Engine Marketing**](https://seller.walmart.com/advertising/search-engine-marketing) page in Seller Center.

* * *

Why is my reward status Locked or Not eligible?

The **Your rewards** section will display one of three statuses:

- **Available:** Your current tier has unlocked the reward and you can view and utilize it.


- **Locked:** Your current tier didn’t unlock the reward. The reward will be greyed out and show the tier needed to unlock it.


- **Not eligible:** You’re not eligible for this reward. This may occur if your account does not meet the eligibility requirements of the reward’s program.


* * *

I’ve earned Pro Seller status. Does access to Flash Deals guarantee inclusion in an event?

No. Your Flash Deals submissions must meet the [qualifying criteria](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Flash-Deals:-Program-overview) and are subject to Walmart's discretionary approval process.

* * *

I’ve earned Pro Seller status. Are all my offers eligible for the Extra Savings discount program?

No. While Pro Seller status may grant access to offer Extra Savings discounts, all offers must meet the [qualifying criteria](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/discounts-overview).

* * *

What is the Pro Seller Master Series?

The Pro Seller Master Series is an exclusive hands-on webinar reserved for Advanced and Pro Sellers. Subject matter experts from various Marketplace programs will cover key topics and new opportunities to drive growth. You can register for future webinars [here](https://marketplace.walmart.com/pro-seller-master-series-form/).
