Overview

As a Marketplace seller, you’re required to provide accurate details about the products you sell. In this guide, you’ll learn about the product detail policy on Walmart.com.

Notes:

Violations of this policy may result in removal of offers or content, and/or suspension or termination of your Marketplace account.

## What is the policy?

Your product’s title, description, key features, images and additional attributes help determine how your items appear in search and browse results on Walmart.com. Content generated by artificial intelligence must be truthful, accurate and not misleading. All claims, images and descriptions must match the actual product delivered, including size, color, quantity, materials/ingredients, features, benefits and limitations. You may only use content to which you have the appropriate rights to, and it must not misappropriate or infringe any third-party intellectual property or other rights.

You’re responsible for reviewing and substantiating AI-generated content before publication and for complying with all Marketplace policies and applicable laws (including advertising, labeling, safety, and IP). AI-generated content that misrepresents a product, makes unsubstantiated or unsafe claims, fabricates reviews or endorsements or otherwise violates Marketplace requirements is prohibited.

The table below lists requirements for accurately filling out the product title, description, key features and imagery for your product detail page.

![2505_product-detail-page-Color.svg](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltf84c6050a00ce680/69cfff797482cf3eef6e461e/2603_product-detail-page_overview.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

Your item content must follow the [Prohibited Products Policy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Prohibited%20products%20&%20brands/Prohibited-products-policy:-overview) and all other Walmart policies. You may refer to our [Content Standards](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Content-standards:-Overview) for additional guidance.

### Product title

| Allowed | Prohibited |
| --- | --- |
| - Writing brief titles not exceeding 150 characters.<br>- Creating clear, descriptive titles without repetitive keywords, multiple brands or multiple product types.<br>- Using proper capitalization.<br>- Including relevant values that describe key attributes (e.g., 5 in, 4 count, 12 oz). | - Writing in all caps or using special characters (e.g., ~, !, \*, $).<br>- Making any promotional claims (e.g., free shipping, Walmart fulfilled, hot sale, top rated, premium quality, best-selling, clearance, Black Friday, savings, low price).<br>- Using Walmart or any other retailer name unless licensed or is part of the product's brand name.<br>- Including irrelevant information (e.g., coming soon, out-of-stock, discontinued).<br>- Incorporating any URLs in any format, including Walmart.com.<br>- Using languages other than English.<br>- Including years (e.g., 2025, 2024) unless it is recommended by the Content standards for your item's product type.<br>- Repeating keywords and phrases multiple times. |
|  |  |

### Description

| Allowed | Prohibited |
| --- | --- |
| - Describing the item’s features and benefits, including the product name, brand and keywords.  <br>- Including related words that customers are likely to search for. <br>- Creating one paragraph with a minimum of 150 words. Word count requirements vary by product category. | - Making claims of competitor exclusivity (e.g., Amazon exclusive or exclusive to Target.com, etc.).<br>- Using Walmart or any other retailer name unless licensed or is part of the product's brand name.<br>- Making any promotional claims (e.g., free shipping, Walmart fulfilled, hot sale, top rated, premium quality, best-selling, clearance, Black Friday, savings, low price). <br>- Making any authenticity claims for any product listed on Walmart.com unless you've obtained Walmart's approval for the claim or if the item is a food product (e.g., authentic Louis Vuitton, Lego assorted minifigures authentic, or 100% authenticated Leather). <br>- Including irrelevant information (e.g., out-of-stock, discontinued). <br>- Using external URLs.<br>- Using emojis or special characters.<br>- Using bullet points or numbered lists. <br>- Using additional text formatting like HTML or numbered lists.<br>- Using languages other than English.<br>- Describing a product different from the one mentioned in product title.<br>- Repeating keywords and phrases excessively. |

### Key features

| Allowed | Prohibited |
| --- | --- |
| - Listing the most important features first, including three to 10 of the most important benefits and features of your item.<br>- Using short phrases to avoid repetitive keywords. | - Using more than 80 characters (spaces included) per key feature.<br>- Making claims of competitor exclusivity (e.g., Amazon exclusive or exclusive to Target.com, ect.).<br>- Using Walmart or any other retailer name unless licensed or is part of the product's brand name.<br>- Making any promotional claims (e.g., free shipping, Walmart fulfilled, hot sale, top rated, premium quality, best-selling, clearance, Black Friday savings, low price).<br>- Including irrelevant information (e.g., out-of-stock, discontinued).<br>- Using external URLs.<br>- Using emojis or special characters.<br>- Using special characters as bullet points.<br>- Using languages other than English.<br>- Describing a product different from the one mentioned in product title.<br>- Repeating keywords and phrases excessively. |

## Additional guidelines

When filling out the product title, description or key features, you may use languages other than English only if it's part of the item or brand name.

In addition to the guidelines above, you must adhere to the following:

| Allowed | Prohibited |
| --- | --- |
| - Ensuring values are relevant to the attribute and correspond with each attribute's unit of measure (e.g., enter numeric values for capacity).<br>- Filling in as many attributes as possible, if accurate information is available. | - Using text where numbers are expected and vice versa (e.g., using purple for weight or 16 oz for color).<br>- Using incorrect values (e.g., using xyz for materials). |

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What happens if my content doesn’t follow all requirements?

Noncompliance may result in products being unpublished.

* * *

What are the guidelines and requirements for imagery?

To comply with Walmart Marketplace’s image requirements and guidelines, visit [_Product detail page: Image guidelines & requirements_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-detail-page:-Image-guidelines-&-requirements).

* * *

How do I update an item’s content?

You may be able to suggest changes to your item’s content after you’ve finished setting up your item. However, because there may be multiple sellers providing content for the same item, you may not always be able to define which content will be displayed on Walmart.com. Walmart will determine the best available content for items unless it’s provided by a brand owner or authorized reseller.

Items that can’t be updated will appear locked during the updating process. Learn more in [_How to update content: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/How-to-update-content:-Overview) _._

* * *

How does discoverability help my item’s performance?

Discoverability helps customers find the items they’re looking for. They can use the filter on the left–hand navigation or select _Shop by category_ on Walmart.com.

If the Discoverability field isn’t filled out or is filled out with a value not listed in the facet or shelving rule, the item is omitted from these results.

* * *

Are there specific guidelines for different product categories?

Yes. Depending on your product, you may have additional requirements or guidelines to follow. For more information, visit [_Content Standards: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Content-standards:-Overview) _._
