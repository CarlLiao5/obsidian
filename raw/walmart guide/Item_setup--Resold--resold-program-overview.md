Resold program: Overview

Last updated on Dec 14, 2025

Reading time: 7 min

Overview

The [Resold program](https://marketplace.walmart.com/resold/) allows you to offer high-quality, refurbished items on Walmart.com. In this guide, you’ll learn more about product eligibility and participation requirements for this program.

Notes:

Participation in the Resold program is by invitation only, and not available to all Marketplace sellers. If you’d like to sell [Pre-Owned](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Resold/resold-program-item-setup) items through the Resold program, visit the [**Exclusives programs**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/exclusive-programs) page in Seller Center to apply.

## How does it work?

To participate in this program, you must be in good standing and consistently meet our [Seller Performance Standards](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Seller-performance-standards) _._ If approved to sell resold items, you’re required to comply with the [Comprehensive Walmart Marketplace Retailer Agreement](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blte9f8c035c2517009/Comprehensive%20Walmart%20Marketplace%20Retailer%20Agreement%20-%207.20.2024.pdf?branch=us_mplearn), [retailer policies](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Account/Walmart-Marketplace-seller-retailer-policies), Seller Performance Standards and Walmart’s [Returns policy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Returns%20&%20refunds/returns-policy).

The Resold program prohibits selling counterfeit items. Violations of this policy can lead to suspension or termination of your account.

## Additional guidelines

All items in any Resold condition must be professionally inspected, tested, cleaned and, if necessary, refurbished to their original condition specified by the manufacturer or an authorized seller or supplier. Eligible products should be in excellent technical condition and include a battery, wall plug or an electronic component.

Resold items are assigned a [condition type](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Item-condition-types) during item setup. Each condition type has specific requirements for eligible product categories that you must adhere to.

| Product type | Product quality requirements |
| --- | --- |
| **All products** | - Restore to its original factory settings and fully unlock for use (if applicable).<br>- Upgrade to the latest firmware supported by the Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) (if applicable).<br>- Include all accessories that come with a newly purchased item.<br>- Comply with the Walmart Marketplace requirements for [packaging](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Packaging-policy) and shipping items.<br>- Include original instruction manuals with the product when legally necessary. In other instances, you should provide instruction manuals or links to the manufacturer's online instructions.<br>- Remove end user data in accordance with the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) [Standard on Media Sanitation](https://www.nist.gov/publications/guidelines-media-sanitization) or other applicable local regulations and standards. <br>- Perform a complete forensic destruction of all Personally Identifiable information (PII) and all other data that originated with or related to an end user contained on or any hardware, software or media component of each product before sale. Examples of end user data include, but are not limited to, name, date of birth, fingerprints or other biometric data, Social Security Number, street address, email address, cloud storage account and credit card information. |
| **Accessories** | - Can be generic but must be compatible.<br>- Consumable accessories (e.g., air and water filters, brushes, strings) must be in new condition. |
| **Batteries** | - Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) shipments containing lithium batteries must be registered with [WERCSmart](https://supplierhelp.walmart.com/s/guide?article=000007868&_gl=1*wsdb4j*_ga*MjE5MzU4NzY4LjE2ODcyOTcyNDk.*_ga_1LB22TM2MG*MTY4NzQ2MjE0NC44LjAuMTY4NzQ2MjE0NC4wLjAuMA..%22%20\t%20%22_self).<br>- Include battery life test information if battery life isn't visible in the device settings.<br>- Include the original battery for restored premium products. |
| **Remote controls** | - Pair with the receiver or include an instruction manual for pairing and troubleshooting. |
| **Screens and displays** | - Screens must have no scratches.<br>- Displays must be free of damage and fully functional like the original item with no dimness. |
| **Wall plugs** | - Must be compatible with the region where the product is sold. |

Notes:

During the item setup process, you can select from a range of eligible product categories. If the category isn’t available during setup, it means the product isn’t eligible.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Are Walmart-fulfilled (WFS) items eligible for the Resold program?

Yes. You’ll use the original GTIN to set up the item, but we’ll also generate a custom fulfillment GTIN to track your Resold item through the Walmart network. The fulfillment GTIN is what’s available on the item label. Visit [_Resold at Walmart: WFS items_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-pre-owned-wfs-items) for more information.

* * *

Are international sellers eligible for the Resold program?

Yes. The program is open to domestic and international sellers.

* * *

If an item does not show the condition during item setup, can I request to add more conditions?

No. Conditions are offered at a product type level dependent on compliance and regulatory laws.

* * *

Are warranties required for Resold program items?

Restored: Premium is the only condition that requires a warranty. Warranties aren’t required for Pre-Owned conditions unless otherwise required by law. However, you must accurately disclose to customers the warranty available (if any), or if the product is sold without a warranty. If Walmart determines it necessary to refund customers due to failure to honor a warranty, you’ll bear responsibility and associated costs when offering a warranty on resold products.

* * *

How are returns processed?

There is no change to the existing Marketplace returns process. Restored: Like New, Restored: Good and Restored: Fair items are required to have an extended return period of 90 days.  For Restored: Premium, the standard 30-day period applies unless the item is otherwise excluded under the 15-day return window for certain electronics.

* * *

How should I price my Resold items?

We recommend that you adhere to top industry standards for pricing Resold items and price the item lower than its new equivalent.

* * *

Why is my Resold item ineligible for the Buy Box?

All Resold items are subject to [Buy Box Ineligibility rules](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Pricing-rules). To ensure a fair and transparent shopping experience for everyone, Walmart may also make Resold ( **Pre-owned**, **Restored** and **Open Box**) items ineligible for the Buy Box if their price exceeds that of a new item on the same product page. Exceptions may apply for certain categories, such as collectibles or some luxury brands.
