Overview

The [Search Insights: Search catalog performance](https://seller.walmart.com/analytics/search-insights) report in Seller Center gives you a detailed understanding of your items’ search performance on Walmart.com. In this guide, you’ll learn how to efficiently use the catalog performance report to understand and improve your items’ search performance.

## Search catalog performance report

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to the [**Search Insights**](https://seller.walmart.com/analytics/search-insights) dashboard in Seller Centerand select the _Download Report_ button to download a full catalog performance report of insights across your catalog _._

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt43410504f1efe635/684b13b0b045d36613552aaa/2506_Search-insights_generate-catalog-performance-report_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 2 – View item performance

Search by the item name, ID or SKU then select the item. After choosing the item’s name, you can review keywords that customers are using to find your item and the recommended keywords that you could add to improve search performance. You’ll also find your item’s sales rank and search funnel rank, which are defined below.

- **Sales rank:** Your sales rank score is the item's current rank within its product type and is determined by the number of customer orders received in the past six months. The item must have six months of listing history and at least one sale within the last six months to be ranked against other competing items.
- **Search funnel rank:** Your search funnel rank score is the item’s rank across search impressions, clicks and added to cart metrics. That number is in comparison to all items in the same product type, only for the given time period. These scores are updated weekly.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt6bbcf99ba3c615a7/682b7b11f9d9c27f4b9aa0bd/2505_Search-insights_generate-catalog-performance-report_screenshot-2.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

A low rank score indicates high performance.  If an item is in the same variant group, the ranking is shared.

### Step 3 – Track your progress

If you implement the keyword recommendations, you can use the Search Performance Impact insights to track the item’s performance 14 days before and after the date the keyword was added to the product title.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt1acc4cb7c45bf71d/684b1cc014d6e570a90c551a/2506_Search-insights_generate-catalog-performance-report_screenshot-3.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

Percentage change values are currently capped at 1000%. Implementing these suggestions doesn’t guarantee improved search results. These recommendations are designed to help create improved listings, which can improve the overall customer experience.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Why am I unable to view some of my items in the Search Insights report?

Items without a performance history on Walmart.com, traffic or sales are omitted from the report.

* * *

I think my item performance is good, but it's showing as low across the Search funnel. Is this correct?

Search funnel performance is based on an item’s performance as compared to competitor items within a subcategory. It's possible to have items that are doing well individually, but still have room for improvement to remain competitive on Walmart.com. We recommend focusing on implementing [**Listing Quality**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/listing-quality) recommendations and verifying that your assigned product type is correct.

* * *

Why does the data change every week?

Items can change ranking throughout the week based on trends and the competitiveness of your offering. We refresh your insights weekly, so you may find some differences in the data. Updates to your items can take time to reflect in search ranking and [**Listing Quality**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/listing-quality) dashboard metrics.

* * *

Why don't some of my items have a ranking?

Sales and search funnel ranks are only available for items in featured categories. If your item hasn’t been listed or had a sale for at least six months or if there’s not enough search data, it may not fall into the featured categories that are eligible for rankings.

* * *

Are there any other ways I can improve my search performance?

Yes, through [Walmart Sponsored Search](https://marketplace.walmart.com/sponsored-search-ads/) from Walmart Connect or through [improving keyword planning](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-detail-page:-Keyword-optimization) and targeting relevant search terms that might be missing from the product title and item description.
