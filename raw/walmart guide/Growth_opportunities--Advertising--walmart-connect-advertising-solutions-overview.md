Overview

As a Walmart Marketplace seller, you can leverage many types of advertising solutions to get your brand and products in front of more customers. In this guide, you’ll learn more about them and how to get started.

## How does it work?

Each type of advertising solution works differently. With [**Walmart Connect**](https://www.walmartconnect.com/) advertising solutions, you’ll gain access to exclusive ad inventory through Sponsored Search, Onsite Display and Brand Shop and Shelf, which allow you to promote your products on Walmart.com and on the Walmart app.

With other solutions, like Search Engine Marketing (SEM) and Sales Rewards and Attribution, you can use the power of Walmart’s brand and data to reach customers shopping on external websites and through search engines like Google.

Each solution has additional guidelines, benefits and requirements. For more information, refer to the table below.

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **Advertising solution** | **Learn more** |
| **Sponsored Search** | Connect with customers actively searching for products like yours on Walmart.com and the Walmart app. To learn more, visit [_Walmart Connect solutions: Sponsored Search_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Connect/Advertise-with-Walmart-Connect-sponsored-search). |
| **Onsite Display** | Target customers with personalized ads at every stage of their shopping journey on Walmart.com and the Walmart app. To learn more, visit [_Walmart Connect solutions: Onsite Display_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-connect-advertising-onsite-display). |
| **Brand Shop and Shelf** | Eligible brand owners can build their own free virtual storefront on Walmart.com and the Walmart app. To learn more, visit [_Walmart Connect solutions: Brand Shop and Shelf_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-connect-solutions-brand-shop-shelf). |
| **Search Engine Marketing (SEM)** | Promote your products through Google Shopping Ads. To learn more, visit [_Search Engine Marketing (SEM): Program overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Advertising/Search%20Engine%20Marketing%20(SEM)/Search-Engine-Marketing-(SEM):-program-overview). |
| **Sales Rewards and Attribution** | Build tracking URLs for your Marketplace products to apply to your non-Walmart marketing campaigns and earn a commission from these sales. To learn more, visit [_Sales Rewards and Attribution: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/sales-rewards-attribution). |

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Can I use all Walmart advertising solutions at the same time?

Yes. If you’re eligible, you can use any combination of solutions together.

* * *

What’s the difference between Seller Center and Walmart Connect Ad Center?

Seller Center is the platform that you use to manage and optimize your Walmart Marketplace account and manage your self-service advertising solutions, such as [**Search Engine Marketing**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/search-engine-marketing/) and [**Sales Rewards and Attribution**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/sales-rewards-and-attribution).

[Walmart Connect Ad Center](https://advertising.walmart.com/) is a platform that you can use to launch and manage Walmart Connect advertising campaigns. You can learn more about Walmart Connect advertising solutions on the [**Advertising**](https://seller.walmart.com/advertising/home) page in Seller Center, such as [**Sponsored Search**](https://seller.walmart.com/advertising/sponsored-search) and [**Brand Shop and Shelf**](https://seller.walmart.com/advertising/display-and-brand-shop). However, to get the most out of your advertising, use Walmart Connect Ad Center.
