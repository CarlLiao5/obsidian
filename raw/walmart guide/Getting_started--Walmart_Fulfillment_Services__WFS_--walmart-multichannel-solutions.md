Overview

Walmart Multichannel Solutions (MCS) is a cost-effective way to scale your operations by fulfilling customer orders from Walmart.com and other sales channels like Amazon, eBay, Shein, Temu and more—simultaneously. Plus, you can manage inventory and returns from a central hub with help from Walmart’s world-class supply chain.

## Why choose Multichannel Solutions?

Multichannel Solutions is ideal if you find yourself juggling customer orders across multiple eCommerce sites. Here are just some of the benefits:

- Fulfill on and beyond Walmart.com by offering customers fast, reliable shipping — wherever they shop.
- Save on costs with competitively priced storage and fulfillment, including order-level discounts.
- Choose between expedited (2 business day) and standard (3–5 business day) shipping.
- Tap into new sales channels with unbranded packaging and returns management.
- Streamline inventory by maintaining 1 pool to fulfill all orders. Any fulfillment center can store multichannel inventory for delivery in all 50 US states and Puerto Rico.
- Move aging and expiring inventory by offering it on multiple sales channels
- Automate customer orders with [API integrations](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Walmart%20Multichannel%20Solutions/multichannel-api) and solution providers.

Notes:

For more details on the program, view the [Walmart Multichannel Solutions (MCS) Service Description](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt9de12b9a74a593a2/69b42ee7e84ada1a269bbe95/Walmart_Multichannel_Solutions_Description_(rev102925).pdf?branch=us_mplearn).

## Supported sales channels

We support any sales channel, as long as the customer orders and returns comply with the terms of service you’ve agreed to on the original eCommerce site. Our shipping speed and packaging should also be appropriate for the sales channel.

You have complete flexibility over which sales channels are fulfilled. By default, items are listed on Walmart.com, but you’re welcome to sell exclusively on off-Walmart channels.

* * *

## Packaging & shipping speeds

Multichannel orders can only be shipped to US addresses within the 50 states and Puerto Rico. We do not ship to P.O. boxes. We ship multichannel orders in unbranded poly bags or brown boxes with plain tape and dunnage. Choose what’s right for your sales channels with  **expedited** ( **2 business day)** or **standard** ( **3–5 business day)** shipping.\*

\\* Only items weighing 31-150lb are only eligible for standard shipping at this time. Shipping speeds are not guaranteed during peak (October 1–December 31), sales events and other circumstances.

* * *

## Eligibility

Before you get started, there are a few criteria:

- You must be using Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) to fulfill customer orders.
- You must have a default billing method on file for WFS fees.
- Items must meet the [product requirements](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-routing-guide-overview) for WFS.
- [Multi-box](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-multi-box-items) and [big and bulky](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-big-and-bulky-items) items are currently not eligible.

You'll be automatically assigned to a fulfillment center, all of which store multichannel inventory for delivery in all 50 US states and Puerto Rico. We do not deliver to P.O. boxes.

Pro tip

Already a WFS seller? Get started with Multichannel Solutions by completing these two steps:

- [Add your sales channels](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/multichannel-setup)
- [Add customer orders](https://seller.walmart.com/wfs/multi-channel-solutions/orders)

## Multichannel fees

Unique to multichannel, we offer an **order-level discount for items with shipping weight under 30 lb**. If a customer buys multiple units of the same item in a single order, there’s a lower fulfillment fee.

**Multichannel storage fees are the same as WFS’ standard storage fees. There are no referral fees for multichannel orders.**

You can preview fulfillment fees based on shipping speed and shipping weight. Go to the [sales channel page](https://seller.walmart.com/wfs/multi-channel-solutions/sales-channels), then select the **Estimate fulfillment fees** link.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt36b09c1e325e70c1/66cfb32d6b0985b654f2d81a/Multichannel_Fee-calculator_GIF.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

Follow these steps to estimate fulfillment fees for yourself:

1. Choose a shipping speed.

2. Determine your item’s shipping weight. If your item weighs less than 1 lb., use the unit weight to determine the fee. If your item weighs over 1 lb., use the greater of unit weight or dimensional weight. Dimensional weight = (length x width x height in inches) / 139

3. To find the final shipping weight, add 0.25 lb. for packaging materials and round up to the nearest whole ounce or pound.

| ≤ 6 oz. | $9.45 | Subtract $2.50 per unit | Subtract $3.00 per unit | Subtract $3.50 per unit |
| Greater than 6 oz. and up to 12 oz. | $9.75 |
| Greater than 12 oz. and up to 1 lb. | $10.05 |
| 2–30 lb. | $11.35 + $0.75 for each lb. over 2 lb. |

| Standard (3–5 business day) shipping \* |
| --- |
| **Shipping weight** | **1 unit** | **2 units** | **3 units** | **4+ units** |
| ≤ 6 oz. | $7.45 | Subtract $2.50 per unit | Subtract $3.00 per unit | Subtract $3.50 per unit |
| Greater than 6 oz. and up to 12 oz. | $7.75 |

\\* Shipping speeds are not guaranteed during peak (October 1–December 31), sales events and other circumstances.

\\*\\* Items over 150 lbs in unit or dimensional weight are not eligible for multichannel at this time.

### Oversize fee

If your item is oversize, an additional fee per unit applies. An item is considered oversize if it meets at least one of the following criteria. (Go to [_WFS fees_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-fees) for more detail.)

- Longest side greater than 48", including packaging

- Median side greater than 30", including packaging

- Longest side + girth greater than 105", including packaging

## How does billing work?

Your account balance is a running total of Walmart.com sales and any fees for Walmart services, including Walmart Multichannel Solutions. View a summary of fees, payments and adjustments on the [**Payments**](https://seller.walmart.com/payments/statements/period) page of Seller Center. For more detail, download the [Settlement Report](https://seller.walmart.com/wfsLite/reports).

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt8de17d3798525dab/66c3873354be6cbdc118473c/mcs_payment_summary.jpeg?branch=us_mplearn)

You must add a default billing method to use Walmart Multichannel Solutions. This covers fulfillment, storage and return fees. At the end of each settlement cycle, we’ll deduct any fees from your earnings and charge the remaining balance to your billing method. If you have any issues adding a billing method, select the _Help_ button in the menu bar to contact Support.

To add a billing method:

1. Log into Seller Center and select the gear icon in the page heading.

2. Choose [**Billing Services**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/financial-settings/billing-services) under Financial Settings.

3. Add a bank account or credit card. Bank accounts will take 2–3 business days to verify. Walmart currently only accepts US-based ACH payments.


## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Do you handle returns?

Yes, we can manage returns for you from any sales channel. We’ll provide a return shipping label for the customer to ship items back to a Walmart facility. We’ll then check the item’s condition and follow your return rules to restock, ship back to you or dispose of it. Go to [_Multichannel Solutions: Create customer returns_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Walmart%20Multichannel%20Solutions/multichannel-returns) to learn more.

* * *

Does my item have to be listed on Walmart.com in addition to other sales channels?

By default, all items are eligible to be fulfilled for orders placed on Walmart.com and other sales channels. However, there’s no requirement, and we’re able to fulfill off-Walmart channels only—like Amazon, eBay, Temu, Shein and other channels.

* * *

Is pricing the same across the US?

Pricing is consistent across the country. We charge one fee for addresses in the US and Puerto Rico, regardless of which fulfillment center items ship from.

* * *

Do I need to add a billing method?

Yes, we require a billing method to charge fulfillment, storage and return fees.

* * *

Why does my Walmart account have a negative balance?

You may not have enough sales on Walmart.com to cover fees for Multichannel Solutions. This is common if you only sell on off-Walmart channels. Since Seller Center doesn’t include payments from other sales channels, your balance may not accurately reflect your overall earnings from using Multichannel Solutions.

* * *

Do you ship internationally?

No, we only ship to US addresses within the 50 states and Puerto Rico.
