Success Hub: Automate pricing with the Repricer

Overview

Success Hub makes suggestions for items in your Catalog that could be priced more competitively using the [Repricer](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Repricer:-overview). Based on the rules you set, the prices are automatically adjusted based on triggers such as the winning Buy Box offers and/or external site prices. The Repricer monitors prices on Walmart.com and on external marketplaces so you don’t have to manually check or update your offers every time market conditions change. Keep reading to learn how to access your current Repricer suggestions.

## Automate your pricing strategy with Repricer

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to the [**Growth**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/success-hub) dashboard and the **Stay competitive with automated pricing** suggestion. Select _Automate prices_.

### Step 2 – Choose a strategy and price

Select a pricing strategy from the **Automatic pricing Strategy** menu, then select the items you want to assign to the strategy. You can use the suggested minimum and maximum prices or adjust them as needed.

Notes:

For a new or existing Repricer strategy, you can select an option to automatically reprice items that are on promotion based on rules you set. If no competitive price is found, Repricer will adjust to your last submitted price.

### Step 3 – Submit

To submit your items, select the _Automate price_ button in the bottom menu bar. You can manage your assigned items from the [**Repricer**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/repricer) dashboard.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Where can I learn more about the Repricer tool and Repricer strategies?

For more information, visit [_Repricer: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Repricer:-overview) _._

* * *

How are the Suggested Minimums and Maximums created?

We use machine learning models that leverage several different factors including past pricing history, category norms as well as other internal and external factors.

* * *

Can I remove any items from my existing strategies using Success Hub?

No, the purpose of the Success Hub is to create quick campaign strategies. To remove your enrolled items, navigate to the [**Repricer**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/repricer) dashboard.

* * *

How do I edit my Repricer strategies once they are running?

You can edit your repricing strategies from the [**Repricer**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/repricer) dashboard.

* * *

How often are suggestions refreshed?

Suggestions are refreshed weekly.

* * *

How can I change the settings for promoted items?

You can edit the settings from the [**Repricer**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/repricer) dashboard.
