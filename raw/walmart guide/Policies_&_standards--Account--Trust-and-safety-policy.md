Trust and Safety policy

Last updated on Dec 10, 2025

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

Overview

Walmart’s Trust and Safety division may restrict the sale of products at its discretion that don’t support our commitments and goals. In this guide, you’ll learn more about the policy.

## What is the policy?

Any products deemed in violation of Walmart’s **regulatory compliance, offensive product, or intellectual property** policies will be unpublished and may experience restricted sales, account suspension or termination of your selling privileges on Walmart.com.

Pro tip

Review the [Prohibited Products Policy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Prohibited%20products%20&%20brands/Prohibited-products-policy:-overview) for a full list of restricted products.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

My item was unpublished due to a regulatory compliance violation. What does this mean?

It means your listing featured an item that is prohibited due to product and food safety regulations.

* * *

My item was unpublished due to an offensive product violation. What does this mean?

It means your listing featured an item that’s offensive in nature.

* * *

My item was unpublished due to an intellectual property violation. What does this mean?

It means your listing featured an item that infringes on intellectual property rights.

* * *

Can I dispute a prohibited item?

Yes. To dispute an item, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center menu bar](https://seller.walmart.com/home) and create a case with Seller Support. Walmart will review your appeal and determine if the item can be reinstated.

* * *

Can I sell a restricted item or product?

To request approval to sell in restricted categories, [submit an appeal](https://seller.walmart.com/home?supporthub=contact-support).
