Overview

Success Hub suggestions offer personalized, relevant and actionable opportunities to improve your business. These curated suggestions help you discover and add new and existing trending items to your catalog. In this guide, you'll learn how to enroll your eligible items in weekly [Flash Deals](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Flash-Deals:-Program-overview).

## Enroll items in Flash Deals

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to **Success Hub** by selecting [**Growth**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/success-hub) in Seller Center. In the **Enroll items in Flash Deals** section, choose the _Enroll items_ button.

### Step 2 – Select items

Next, select the items and variants you want to enroll and select _Submit items_. You can submit as many items as you’d like, but the system will only select the first 20 submitted that are eligible.

### Step 3 – Select promotion price

Promotion prices are pre-populated, but you can adjust them. If you use the suggested promo price, you may get discounted Referral Fees on eligible items. If you change the promo price and it’s too high, those items won’t be approved, and you won’t receive the reduced Referral Fee. Select _Submit items_.

### Step 4 – Enroll items

Next, select _Enroll items_. The items go through an internal review process to ensure eligibility. You can check the status of the items in the [**Deals**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/deals) page.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Why don’t I see my submitted items on the Deals page?

Walmart reviews an item’s promotional price and eligibility before enrolling it in the Flash Deals program. The review process could take several days. The first 20 eligible items that are received and approved will be enrolled in the program. Learn more about [Flash Deals errors](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Troubleshoot-flash-deals-errors).

* * *

How many items can I submit each week?

You may submit as many items as you’d like for consideration. However, the items must meet the eligibility criteria, and only the first 20 items that are received and approved will be enrolled in the program.

* * *

How can I reach someone on the Success Hub team?

Email [successhub@walmart.com](mailto:successhub@walmart.com)
