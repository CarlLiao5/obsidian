Overview

When participating in the Resold or Walmart Collectibles program, you're required to specify your item’s condition and adhere to the item requirements. This guide features a detailed table outlining various product categories, along with their accepted conditions and specific selling requirements.

Notes:

Participation in the [Resold](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Resold/resold-program-overview) and [Walmart Collectibles](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-collectibles-program) program is by invitation only and not available to all Marketplace sellers. If you’d like to sell [Pre-Owned](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Resold/resold-program-item-setup) items through the Resold program, visit the [**Exclusives programs**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/exclusive-programs) page in Seller Center to apply.

## How does it work?

An item’s condition describes its level of wear, prior use (if any), appearance and overall quality. Condition types are attributes used to classify and describe the condition of an item being sold on Walmart Marketplace. Adding this information helps customers better understand the current condition of the item they’re buying.

The following condition types are all associated with invite-only programs except for the **New** condition type. The following descriptions are being provided for reference and may not be available for all item types within the category.

Notes:

Availability of condition types may vary by product, category or program.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Can I create my own condition type if I feel like my item doesn’t fit into a preset condition type?

No. You can only choose from the available conditions listed above. If your item doesn’t meet these guidelines, it shouldn’t be listed on Walmart.com.

* * *

I want to apply for the Resold or Walmart Collectibles program?

Participation in the Resold and Walmart Collectibles program are by invitation only. To participate in either program, you’ll need to choose a Resold item description for your item during item setup. To participate in the Walmart Collectibles program, you’ll need to provide Walmart with an item description, item condition type and proof of your item’s authenticity.

Email [collectibles@walmart.com](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Resold/mailtocollectibles@walmart.com) or contact your account manager to learn more.
