Overview

With the **Sales Rewards and Attribution** program, you can generate referral links in Seller Center and then share them on your social and external media campaigns to get cash back on sales you’ve made through these links. In this guide, you’ll learn how to create a new referral campaign and referral links.

## Generate referral links

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to the [**Sales Rewards and Attribution**](https://seller.walmart.com/advertising/sales-rewards-and-attribution) dashboard in Seller Center.

Notes:

You can only create a campaign for third-party items you’ve listed on Walmart Marketplace.

### Step 2 – Create a campaign

To create a new referral campaign, select the _\+ Create a Campaign_ button. Enter the name and publishing channel for your campaign and select the items you want to enroll. You can also search for your items by item name, SKU and item ID. When you’re ready, select the _Continue_ button.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt2cbcdb03a0ad97f1/683dcba9e2a01b7fb54ddb33/2505_create_referral_campaign_gif1.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

Pro tip

You can add detailed information to your campaign name, such as Meta – Awareness – May 24 – Tshirts. You can also include the External ID.

### Step 3 – Create and add link labels

On the next page, enter the amount of referral links you want to create and select _Continue_. Then, enter a label for each of your links and select _Submit_. The next page displays the details of your new campaign, including your referral links. To shorten your affiliate link, open the campaign then select the chain link icon next to  the **Affiliate link.**

Notes:

You can review and manage your active campaigns anytime from the **Sales Rewards and Attribution** dashboard.

Pro tip

Label your link with information about your advertisement. You can use a keyboard symbol such as a vertical bar (\|) or a hyphen (-) to help structure the details. For example, your label could look like this: Paid \| Social \| Meta \| ABCBrand \| RedShirt \| Version1

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How many referral links can I create for one campaign?

You can create up to 500 referral links within a single campaign.

* * *

Can I use my referral link to drive traffic to my Brand Shop or Storefront?

Yes. If you’re an eligible Marketplace brand owner, you can drive traffic to your [**Brand Shop and Shelf**](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Advertising/Walmart%20Connect/walmart-connect-solutions-brand-shop-shelf) pages.

* * *

Can I create links to a variant of my item? For example, if I’m promoting a sweater that comes in five colors, can I drive traffic to a specific color?

Yes. You can create links to a specific variant such as color because each variant has its own item ID and offer ID. If you need variant-level reporting, you must create a separate link for each one. If you don’t need reporting by variant, you can use a single link. Sales of any variant will still count as conversions and will be eligible for commission.

* * *

Why are some of my items grayed out?

You can only create a campaign for third-party items you’ve listed on Walmart Marketplace. If you have questions, contact [walmartsalesrewards@walmart.com](mailto:walmartsalesrewards@walmart.com).
