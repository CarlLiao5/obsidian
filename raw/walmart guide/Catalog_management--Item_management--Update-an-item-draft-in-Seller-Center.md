Update an item draft in Seller Center

Last updated on Oct 17, 2024

Reading time: 1 min

* * *

Overview

While adding a new item in Seller Center, you may need to pause what you’re doing and complete your work later. Seller Center gives you the option to save an item draft for up to 180 days. This guide shows you how to update and publish an item draft you’ve already saved.

## How to update an item draft

### Step 1 — Access your saved drafts

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) page. Select the _Filters_ button, then _Status_, and choose the _Drafts_ button. Apply the filter to browse only your saved drafts.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blta66f9a63be1b046c/66d898e50b0341b9dc26bb89/9924_Screenshot_(1).png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 2 — Update & submit the item draft

Select the item you want to complete, then select _Edit Item._ Make your changes, then select either _Save as Draft_ to save the item in draft format again or select _Submit._

Notes:

When you create a draft, it’s available for 180 days in Seller Center. After that, it gets deleted. While you can’t manually delete a draft you no longer need, it will automatically delete after the 180-day window.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Can I still make changes to an item after it’s published?

Yes, you can make changes to an item individually once it’s published. Visit [_Update item content individually in Seller Center_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Item%20management/Update-item-content-individually-in-Seller-Center?locale=en-US) to learn more.
