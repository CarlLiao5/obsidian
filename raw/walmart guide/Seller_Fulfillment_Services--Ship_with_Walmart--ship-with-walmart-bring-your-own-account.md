Ship with Walmart: Bring your own account

Last updated on Nov 2, 2025

Reading time: 3 min

* * *

In this guide:

* * *

Overview

With Ship with Walmart: Bring your own account, you can use your own account through FedEx. In this guide, you’ll learn how to set up your own account with the carrier and purchase labels if needed.

Notes:

If you use your own account to buy labels, you will not be eligible for any Walmart discounts, promotions, competitive pricing and benefits.

## Link your own account

### Step 1 – Get started

To link and add your own account in Seller Center, go to the **Settings page** at the top of the dashboard. Then,go to the **Shipping Profile** sectionand select [_Ship with Walmart._](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/ship-with-walmart)

### Step 2 – Enter your account information

Choose _Link new account._ Provide your account information and Ground Shipper Number provided by FedEx.

![2510_SWW-Bring-your-own-account.gif](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt7e78219ba7f2d14b/69038151b5c5261d745efdf7/2510_SWW-Bring-your-own-account.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

If you don’t know your Ground Shipper Number, contact FedEx support.

### Step 3 – Assign a fulfillment center

Locate the fulfillment center you’d like to link out to by selecting the fulfillment centers that have already been added. If your fulfillment center doesn’t appear in the dropdown, choose _Add a new Fulfillment Center._ Once all the information is provided, select _Submit_ to validate and link the account.

All linked accounts will appear as a list on this tab. You can either edit or delete the linked accounts.

Once you’ve followed these steps, you can [buy shipping labels](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Ship%20with%20Walmart/buy-shipping-labels-in-seller-center) using the _Ship with Walmart_ option.

Pro tip

You can only link one fulfillment center to one FedEx account. This is the fulfillment center and address associated with the FedEx account.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Will I get billed for labels purchased from my account by Walmart?

No, you will get billed through your carrier. You can also review shipping costs prior to shipping using the [Ship with Walmart shipping calculator](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/ship-with-walmart-shipping-calculator).

* * *

If I’m using my own FedEx account (Bring your own account), am I eligible for Ship with Walmart seller protections?

No. You need to file a claim directly with the carrier for cases of lost in transit and lost after delivery.

* * *

How many accounts can I link?

You can link as many accounts as possible you have with your carrier. You can only associate one account to one fulfillment center in Seller Center.
