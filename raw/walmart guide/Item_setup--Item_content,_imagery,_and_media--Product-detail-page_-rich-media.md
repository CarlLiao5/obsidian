Overview

Interactive or multimedia content, also known as rich media, enhances the customer experience by dynamically sharing product features. In this guide, you'll learn about the types of rich media you can use on Walmart.com, which hosting services are available and how you can submit rich media.

Notes:

AI-generated content must be accurate, truthful, rights-compliant, and aligned with Marketplace policies, including our [_Product details policy_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-Detail-Page:-overview).

## How does it work?

By adding rich media to your product listings, you can improve search results, increase order conversions and reduce returns. This table lists the different types of rich media that can be used on Walmart Marketplace.

| **Type** | **Description** | **Example** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **360-spin images** | A series of still photos that gives the impression of an object rotating. This feature allows a customer to drag a product image in any direction to view the product from all angles. | ![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt29a223cda2a972f7/69d57c53aebba368f9f96003/2603_Rich-media-product-detail_360-spin.png?branch=us_mplearn) |
| **Video** | An effective video demonstrates key information about the product and can help customers retain more information about your product. | ![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltb431ba69b06d7c61/69d57c863957761d4c83bcae/2603_Rich-media-product-detail_video.png?branch=us_mplearn) |

## Additional guidelines

When you’re ready, you can use the [**Media Library**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/media-library) to upload rich media in Seller Center. Before you submit, review the [submission checklist](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltfc3b34206261f84f/Rich-Media_Submission-checklist.pdf?branch=us_mplearn) and [technical requirements](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt36cadf070214bd20/Rich-Media_Technical-Requirements.pdf?branch=us_mplearn) to ensure eligibility.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What is a .vtt file?

A .vtt file is a text file saved in the Web Video Text Tracks (WebVTT) format. It contains supplementary information about web videos, including subtitles, captions, descriptions, chapters and metadata. A .vtt file doesn't contain any video data. A .vtt file is recommended with your video submission.

* * *

Why can’t I upload GIF imagery for my products?

GIF imagery isn't permitted as it doesn't provide useful information about a product that an informative video would.
