Walmart Preferred Carrier: Overview

Overview

The Walmart Preferred Carrier (WPC) Program is our network of trusted carrier partners with discounted rates. We offer **parcel, less than truckload (LTL) and full truckload (FTL)** when you ship to a Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) facility. When you use a preferred carrier, look forward to:

- **Reduced shipping costs.** Enjoy Walmart-negotiated rates for high-volume shipments and boost your bottom line.
- **Easy carrier management.** There’s no need for you to bid on carriers, coordinate pickup or schedule delivery.
- **Convenient billing.** You’ll get estimated shipping prices when you’re preparing the shipment, and we’ll deduct the costs from your WFS sales.

To add a preferred carrier to your next shipment, view [_Walmart Preferred Carrier: Set up shipments_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WPC-set-up-shipments).

## How does it work?

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt311a697778dc4a34/6877df3eee343026caaae082/WFS_Preferred-carrier-journey_Infographic.jpg?branch=us_mplearn)

When you send inventory, choose the Walmart preferred carrier option and preview the estimated shipping price. Based on the origin, destination and shipment, we’ll assign a carrier at the lowest cost available. For a step-by-step, go to [_Walmart Preferred Carrier: Set up shipments_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WPC-set-up-shipments).

For parcel, you can drop off the shipment at your local FedEx office or arrange pickup. Once the shipment is scanned, FedEx will take care of the rest.

For LTL and FTL, you’ll request a [pickup date](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WPC-prepare-pickup), and we’ll then confirm over email. The carrier will pick up the shipment from your address, schedule a delivery appointment and deliver the shipment to the Walmart facility. You can [track progress](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WPC-track-shipments) from the **Shipping Plans** page in Seller Center.

## Pricing

We estimate the shipping price based on the item weight, dimensions and addresses. Once you’ve accepted the quote, you’ll have a limited window to void the charges and edit the shipment: **24 hours** for parcel and **1 hour** for LTL or FTL. This helps ensure we're locking in the best carrier rate at the time you set up your shipment.

## Shipment requirements

Preferred carriers only apply to shipments that are sent from within the 48 contiguous United States. In addition, check that your shipment meets these requirements.

|  | Parcel | Less than truckload (LTL) | Full truckload (FTL) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Definition** | Master cases that are individually labeled for tracking | - Pallets that are individually labeled for tracking<br>- Shares the truck with other shipments | - Pallets that are individually labeled for tracking <br>- Occupies a whole truck |
| **Requirements** | - Up to 999 cases<br>- Up to 150 lb. per case<br>- Each case is up to 108” in length and 165” in length + girth <br>(Girth = 2x width + 2x height) | - Up to 12 unstacked pallets of 24 stacked pallets<br>- Up to 22,000 lb. total<br>- Up to 1,200 cu. ft. | - Up to 26 unstacked pallets or 52 stacked pallets<br>- Up to 45,000 lb. total |

Pallets must also follow these requirements:

- You must use 48" x 40", 4-way-access, solid wood pallets.
- If a single unit cannot fit on a 48" x 40" pallet without overhanging the edges, use our FTL option.
- For non-stackable pallets, the pallet height must be below 72". For stackable pallets, the height must be below 50".


### Restricted items

Dangerous goods and hazmat items are prohibited on preferred carrier shipments. If you’re shipping these items, you can send shipments using your own carrier. View [_WFS hazardous materials overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/wfs-hazardous-materials-overview) to check what items are considered hazardous and [_WFS prohibited products policy_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/wfs-prohibited-products-policy) for what may be prohibited in the WFS program.

Notes:

For more details on the program, view the [Walmart Preferred Carrier Terms of Service](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt744c7b9659d37931/FTL_Terms_of_service.pdf?branch=test2).

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Can international sellers use Walmart preferred carriers?

Preferred carriers can only pick up from a location within the 48 contiguous United States. You can work with a freight forwarding company to directly import inventory and pass customs.

If you're shipping inventory from Asia, we recommend using [Walmart Cross Border - Imports](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-cross-border-imports-overview) instead. We'll manage the ocean freight shipment from port to door, delivered directly to WFS, with optional customs clearance support from Walmart's designated broker.

* * *

Can I ship products from Hawaii or Alaska?

Preferred carriers only accept shipments that are sent from within the 48 contiguous United States. If you’re shipping from Hawaii or Alaska, you’ll need to use your own carrier.

* * *

I ship my products to Walmart directly from my manufacturer or supplier. Can I still use the Walmart Preferred Carrier Program?

Yes. When you send inventory, choose Walmart preferred carrier and enter the following information:

- Your manufacturer’s or supplier’s ship-from address


- Number of cases or pallets


- Weight and dimensions of each case or pallet


- Contact details for LTL or FTL pickup


After you print the labels and bill of lading, you can send them to your manufacturer or supplier. The assigned carrier will then pick up from the manufacturer or supplier’s address.

* * *

Can I choose which Walmart preferred carrier I’m assigned?

For parcel, you can choose between FedEx and UPS. For LTL and FTL, we’ll assign a carrier based on the lowest cost available. We work closely with all our carrier partners to ensure reliability, speed and cost-effectiveness.
