International variants policy

Last updated on Dec 17, 2025

Reading time: 4 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/international-variants-policy#overview)
- [What is the policy?](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/international-variants-policy#what-is-the-policy)
- [Additional guidelines](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/international-variants-policy#additional-guidelines)
- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/international-variants-policy#frequently-asked-questions)

* * *

Overview

If a Marketplace item has multiple variants based on region and it shares the same [product ID](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Choose-a-product-identifier), the item needs its own listing. This ensures that mismatches in Walmart’s catalog aren’t created. In this guide, you’ll learn about the policy and requirements for listings with international variants.

## What is the policy?

The international version or regional variant must be listed on a separate item page that is specific to that version. Both the product title and description on each item page must contain specific international information about the version. This ensures the customer knows the version they’re ordering.

Only international items that meet all legal requirements for sale in the U.S. may be listed. If an item is intended for a market outside of the U.S., the item cannot be listed under the item listing page for the U.S. product. Items must be compatible for use with U.S. outlets. Items must be FCC compliant, with FCC ID visible on the product itself and/or on packaging.

Pro tip

Consider setting up your items via [Express Setup](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Add-a-new-item:-Search-the-Walmart-catalog) before creating a new international version item to avoid creating duplicates and violating Walmart’s [_Duplicate listings policy_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/duplicate-listings-policy).

Walmart, as determined in its sole discretion, reserves the right to suspend or terminate your Marketplace account or unpublish your Marketplace listings if you fail to meet the guidelines in this policy. Additionally, existing inventory held at a Walmart facility will be returned to you at your expense for any non-compliance of this policy.

## Additional guidelines

Use the table below as a guide to set up items that are compliant with this policy:

| **Attribute** | **Requirement** |
| --- | --- |
| **Title** | The title must show either the international version or where regional or country variants are differentiated, noting the country version or regional variant. E.g., (International Version), (Japan Version), (Dubai Version), etc. |
| **Description (short and long description)** | The short and long description must begin with information noting the item’s country version or regional variant. The description should also include detailed information about any meaningful differences between the U.S. and international variant, and a statement that the item is sold without a U.S. manufacturer warranty.<br>For example:<br>- Short description:<br>  - Item version: Japan<br>  - Warranty: This item is sold without a U.S. manufacturer warranty.<br>- Long description:<br>  - This product is an international version for Japan.<br>  - This item is sold without a U.S. manufacturer warranty. |
| **Imagery** | All listings must include images of the actual international version item. Don’t use domestic version images. Include any images with differences from the domestic version product (packaging, plug, adapter, etc.). |
| **Product identifier (ID)** | The UPC or GTIN for all international variants must be different than the UPC or GTIN for the U.S. version. If the UPC or GTIN provided by the manufacturer is the same, don’t provide your own Product ID. Visit [_Product identifier (GTIN, UPC, ISBN or EAN) policy_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Choose-a-product-identifier) for more information. |

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How can I correct existing international variants listed on domestic items?

If you have international versioned items on domestic listings, these offers must be unpublished, and you must ensure the offer is set up under the correct listing. To correct individual items, search the Walmart catalog for the international version or complete a [full item setup](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Add-items-in-bulk:-full-setup) of the international version.

* * *

Why aren’t international version offers allowed on domestic listings?

International offers listed under domestic listings which share a product ID create mismatches in the Walmart catalog. When a mismatch is created, the customer is unaware that they could receive an international version of an item when they expect a domestic version.

* * *

How do I know when my items have a different version on the same product ID?

Check your product details, including product manual, packaging details and for most electronic items, the chargers and connectors. International versioned items have product indicators that differ from domestic versions.

* * *

How can I avoid creating an international version mismatch?

When you create a new international version item, search the Walmart catalog for the product ID and confirm that the recommended item has the correct international version details. If the version you want to create doesn’t exist in the Walmart catalog, complete a full item setup for the international version by requesting a Walmart-provided custom GTIN through UPC exemption. If you need to make updates to content on an existing international variant to remain compliant with this policy, and you’re unable to make updates, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

What do I do if my listing is unpublished?

Most listing issues can be resolved from the [**Unpublished Items**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/unpublished-items) dashboard. If your item is flagged with a brand gating error, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

What do I do if I have Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) inventory tied to the international listing?

If you’ve combined supply among different versions located in Walmart’s WFS facilities, don’t continue to fulfill orders or inbound comingled inventory. Make sure you unpublish all offers. We can help you relabel existing inventory. Complete an [inventory transfer template](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt0cd7ca164fabcc64/WFS_Inventory_Transfer_Request.xlsx?branch=us_mplearn) and then select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.
