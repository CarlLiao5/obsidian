Retire items in Seller Center

Last updated on Jan 5, 2026

Reading time: 1 min

Overview

If you no longer offer an item or if there’s an issue that requires your item to be removed from Walmart.com, you can retire it in Seller Center. You must have admin access in Seller Center to retire items. In this guide, we’ll show you how to retire an item and keep its data.

## Retire items individually

Notes:

If you use Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), you’ll first need to [remove inventory](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20Inventory%20management/WFS-inventory:-Remove-items-from-a-fulfillment-center) from fulfillment centers. Once the inventory has been shipped back to you or disposed of, you can retire the item.

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center. Search for your item using the SKU, Item ID, Item Name, GTIN or UPC.

### Step 2 – Retire the item

Once you’ve found the item you want to retire, select the _three horizontal dots_ next to the item.Select _Retire item_ and confirm your decision. It may take up to 48 hours to process your request.

Notes:

You can retire items from Walmart.com even if your account is suspended. If you have admin access and **Retire** **item** isn’t appearing, clear your web browser cache and cookies and check again. You can only retire items that are not **Retired**, **Archived**, or **Draft**.

![2511_retire-items-individually.gif](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt5ac74816e2bf4298/69309b04e4b33e75231342ac/2511_retire-items-individually.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

## Retire items in bulk

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center _._

### Step 2 – Download and complete the template

Select the _Update items_ button and choose either _Update with file_ or _Update with GTINs_.Then follow the steps to set up and download your template. If you use the _Update with file_ method, you’ll need to set up your template with information such as your product categories, product types and fulfillment method.

Open the template and navigate to the **Site End Date** column. To retire an item, change its **Site End Date** to a date in the past.

![2512_retire-items-bulk_screenshot.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blte4f9bf904bb28cfd/69309bbf9914a338df7450d2/2512_retire-items-bulk_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 3 – Upload the template

Navigate back to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center and select the _Update items_ button and choose _Update with file_. Then use the file browser window to upload the template. When you’re ready, select _Submit_.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

When I retire items, how long does it take for the changes to take effect?

Retired items are typically reflected on your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) page within minutes. However, if you've retired multiple items, the system may take longer to process your updates.

* * *

How do I find retired items?

You can find retired items in Seller Center by selecting _Filters_ on your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) page.Then,select the _Retired_ checkbox under the **Lifecycle** section.
