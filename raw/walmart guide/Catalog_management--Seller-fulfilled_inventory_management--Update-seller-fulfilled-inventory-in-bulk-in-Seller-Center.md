Update seller-fulfilled inventory in bulk in Seller Center

Overview

If you need to update your inventory in bulk, you can do so using the Inventory template in Seller Center. Keep reading to learn how to complete and submit an Inventory template.

Notes:

You must have an existing fulfillment center added to your Seller Center account before you can update your inventory. After you’ve added the fulfillment center, wait at least four hours before attempting to complete an Inventory template. If you attempt to update inventory before your facility is fully processed in our system, you will receive an error.

## Update inventory

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center. Select the _Update Items_ button and choose _Update with file_ from the dropdown menu.

### Step 2 – Download and complete the Inventory template

Select the _Adjust inventory amount_ link and save the template to your computer. Then open the template and make your updates.

![2511_Update-seller-fulfilled-inventory-bulk-seller-center_gif-1.gif](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt1b4cf083aa8f3a41/6930b1e446b69275675222f2/2511_Update-seller-fulfilled-inventory-bulk-seller-center_gif-1.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 3 – Upload the template

Navigate back to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) and select the _Update items_ button and choose _Update with file._ Then use the file browser window to upload the template. When you’re ready, select _Submit_.

Updates can appear in as quickly as 15 minutes or may take up to four hours.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How can I find my fulfillment center ID?

To find your fulfillment center ID, follow the instructions found within [Shipping Templates: Assign SKUs](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping%20Templates/Shipping-templates:-Assign-SKUs).

* * *

How can I check the status of my inventory update?

You can track progress in the [**Activity feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) dashboard.

* * *

How can I update my fulfillment center information?

For details on setting up multiple fulfillment centers, check out [Add a seller-managed fulfillment center in Seller Center](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Fulfillment%20settings/add-a-seller-managed-fulfillment-center-in-seller-center).

* * *

My item is appearing as out of stock on Walmart. Why does it show that I have inventory Reserved and On hand in Seller Center?

Once an item has been purchased by a customer, it is added to your **reserved** inventory quantity until you’ve marked it as shipped. If your **on hand** and **reserved** inventory quantities are the same amount, this means customers have purchased all inventory **on hand** and the item will appear as out of stock on Walmart.com.

To avoid this, we strongly encourage you to provide Walmart with an **on hand** inventory quantity that is at least 20 more than the **reserved** inventory quantity.  For example, if you have 30 shirts in your **reserved** inventory you should provide an **on hand** inventory quantity of 50.

* * *

How do I zero out my Reserved inventory quantity?

Your **reserved** inventory quantity will be zeroed out once you’ve shipped all purchased orders. After you’ve acknowledged an order, you must select the _Ship item_ button in Seller Center to notify Walmart that the order has been shipped.
