Add a seller-managed fulfillment center in Seller Center

Reading time: 1 min

Overview

If you’ve chosen to fulfill your Marketplace orders through your own fulfillment center, you can add a seller-managed fulfillment center in Seller Center. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the setup process.

## Add a fulfillment center

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to the **Shipping Profile** pagein [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) and select _Seller Fulfillment._

### Step 2 — Add the fulfillment center

Select the _Add Fulfillment Center_ button. In the **Select Fulfillment Option** dropdown menu, choose _Seller Managed Fulfillment Center_. In the **Country** dropdown menu, choose the country that you’ll ship your items from. Provide the required information and select the _Add_ button.

We recommend keeping track of your fulfillment center ID, which you’ll need to assign SKUs to your [Shipping Template](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping%20Templates/Shipping-templates:-Assign-SKUs).

Notes:

If you’re shipping from a country outside the U.S., make sure to enter an international location for your fulfillment center, country of incorporation and business address.

### Step 3 — Add inventory

After you’ve successfully added your fulfillment center in Seller Center, you can add inventory. For more information, visit [_How to update seller-fulfilled inventory: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/How-to-update-seller-fulfilled-inventory:-overview).

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Are Fulfillment Center Code and fulfillment center ID the same thing?

Yes. These terms are used interchangeably.

* * *

How can I locate my fulfillment center ID?

Navigate to the Seller Fulfillmentpage in [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/) and locate your information in the **Fulfillment center name** and **FC ID** columns.

* * *

Can I change my default fulfillment center address?

Yes. If you’re a Seller Center user who has **Admin** or **Read and Write** access, you can edit your default address. To edit the address, navigate to the [**Seller Fulfillment**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/seller-fulfillment) page and select the _three horizontal dots_ to the right of the default fulfillment center name.

You can only update the default fulfillment center’s details. You can’t enable or disable the default fulfillment center.

* * *

Can I create a fulfillment center outside of the U.S.?

Yes. If you’re based in China, India or Hong Kong, you can create a fulfillment center. A verifiable address is required.

* * *

I’m having trouble adding my international fulfillment center. What should I do?

Select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support. You’ll need to provide the following documents to verify your fulfillment center address:

#### Country of incorporation

**China**

Required documents:

- Utility bill (e.g., gas or electricity) with your name and fulfillment center address

**Hong Kong**

Required documents:

- Utility bill (e.g., gas or electricity) with your name and fulfillment center address
- Hong Kong tenancy agreement if the fulfillment center is rented (must include your name and fulfillment center address)

**India**

Required documents:

- Utility bill (e.g., gas or electricity) or bank statement dated within six months
- GST registration with your name and fulfillment center address
- Property ownership document for sole proprietors or a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the property owner for renters
- Lease/sublease agreement that includes your name, fulfillment center and lease duration
