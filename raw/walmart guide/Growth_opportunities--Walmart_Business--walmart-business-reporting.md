Walmart Business: Reporting

Last updated on Aug 26, 2025

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Walmart%20Business/walmart-business-reporting#overview)
- [How does it work?](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Walmart%20Business/walmart-business-reporting#how)

* * *

Overview

Once you’ve enrolled in the [Walmart Business program](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Walmart%20Business/Start-selling-on-Walmart-Business), you can download two types of reports to track your sales and view which items in your catalog are eligible for the program. In this guide, you’ll learn more about the reports available to you.

Notes:

Participation in the Walmart Business program is by invitation-only and not available to all Marketplace sellers. If you’re interested in selling on Walmart Business, you can apply [here](https://share.hsforms.com/1orll4wTlQFeWF7WNmBCpKg3lz2f%22%20/t%20%22_blank) and your request will be reviewed on a first-come, first-served basis. [Check your enrollment status](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/exclusive-programs) before submitting a request to enroll.

## How does it work?

After you’ve become an active seller on Business.Walmart.com, you’ll be able to monitor your B2B transactions by generating the following reports:

- **B2B order report:** This report includes your total sales from your Walmart Business orders. To download this report, navigate to the [**Orders**](https://seller.walmart.com/orders/manage-orders?orderGroups=Unshipped) dashboard and choose the **Shipped** tab. Select the checkbox to choose all orders. Select _Download_ and choose your desired date range. Once the report downloads, you can view sales from your Walmart Business orders by filtering through the **Platform Type** column.

- **B2B item report**: This report lists items that are eligible for the Walmart Business program. To download this report, navigate to the [**Reports**](https://seller.walmart.com/analytics/reports) dashboard in Seller Center and choose _B2B Item report_ from the **On Request Reports** dropdown menu. Once your request is processed, you can download the report under **Report Activity** on that same page.


Notes:

You must be an active Walmart Business seller for 72 hours before you can generate the B2B item report.
