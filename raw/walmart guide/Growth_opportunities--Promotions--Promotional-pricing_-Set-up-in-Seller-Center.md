Promotional pricing: Set up via the Price and promotion template

Last updated on Jun 30, 2025

Reading time: 4 min

* * *

Overview

Promotional pricing allows you to offer customers great deals on your items. In this guide, you'll learn how to use the [Price and promotion template](https://seller.walmart.com/api/downloadStaticAsset/Price.xlsm) to add or remove Promotional Pricing in Seller Center.

## Set up promo pricing

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center. Select the _Update items_ button, then choose _Update with file_.

### Step 2 – Download & complete the template

In the _Update with file_ dialogue box, select _Show Walmart templates_. Choose the _Price and Promotion template_.

Once downloaded, choose _Create_ from the **Promo Setting Action** column and fill out theSKU, including the promo price and start and end dates. As a reminder, promotional pricing is considered a temporary price reduction and should last no more than 180 days.

### Step 3 – Upload the template

After you’ve completed the template, return to the **Update with file** dialog box to upload the completed file. From the dropdown menu, choose the _Price and promotion_ option _._ Then, you can either drag the file to the drop zone or open a saved file by selecting _browse_ to choose from your file explorer. Updates can appear as quickly as 15 minutes or can take up to four hours.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Do I need to choose a promo type or promo placement?

No. If you don't choose either option, the promotional price will still appear on Walmart.com regardless of whether the promo price is 10 percent less than the [comparison price](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/Comparison-Price-rules).

* * *

Do I need to fill out the selling price or MSRP?

No. These fields are optional, however, if you choose to fill out the MSRP, it’ll be used to calculate the strikethrough price.

* * *

Can I use the Price and promotion template to update my price?

The Price and promotion template can be used to update both prices and promotions. To preserve the Excel file formatting, do not delete columns, use formulas or add extra spaces.

* * *

How long does it take for the update to apply?

Updates can appear as quickly as 15 minutes or can take up to four hours.

If a promotion start time is less than 30 minutes from the current time, it isn't guaranteed that your promotion will go live at the start time. Once you submit the file in Seller Center, you can track its uploading status on the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) dashboard.

The start and end times of your promotions are in Universal Time Zone (UTC) and not in your local time zone. We recommend setting up your promotions at least 24 hours before the promo starts, with a 12:00 a.m. UTC start time and an end date that is less than 180 days after the start date. API users should submit dates in ISO-8601 format with UTC offsets. For a UTC Conversion table, learn more [here](https://www.worldtimebuddy.com/utc-to-est-converter).

* * *

Can I add multiple promotions?

Yes. You can create multiple promotions per item by inputting the same SKU on a different row. If you'd like to run multiple promotions during different time periods in one file, confirm the dates and times don't overlap before submitting the file. Upload multiple SKUs in a single file to avoid potential bandwidth throttling.

* * *

How can I delete a promotion?

You can delete a promotion in two ways:

- Select _Delete_ from the drop-down menu under the **Promo Setting Action** column. Then, enter the _Promo Price Start Date_ and _Promo Price End Date_ that match your existing promotion and leave the rest of the columns blank.
- Select _Replace All_ from the drop-down menu under the **Promo Setting Action** column and leave the rest of the columns blank. Keep in mind that _Replace All_ will delete all your existing promotions for the particular SKU.

Notes

The promo price start date and end date has to exactly match with the date and time of an existing promotion for the promotion deletion to be successful.

* * *

How can I make sure my template uploads will be successful?

If you’re copying and pasting information from another source, don’t change the formatting. To preserve the Excel file formatting, select _Special Paste_, then _Values_. The start and end times of your promotions are in Universal Time Zone (UTC) and not in your local time zone. Finally, save the Excel file by choosing the save icon. If you’d like to rename the file, right-click the saved file and select _Rename_ without changing the file type.

* * *

How do I change my promotion status?

Follow the steps above and make your desired changes. Select _Delete_ when you’d like to delete the specific promotion for your item. Select _Replace all_ when you'd like to either delete all existing promotions or delete and create new promotions at the same time. You’re responsible for keeping track of the promotions you have set.

Notes

If you choose to delete and create new promotions at the same time, you must fill out the promo price, start date and end date.

* * *

Where can I see my active promotions?

You can run a **Promotions Report** or go to the [**Promotions**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/promotions) dashboard in Seller Center to see all active promotions.

* * *

Can I submit a price update for a SKU that has a promotion scheduled?

If the price update happens at a time when no promotion is active, then it’ll function as a normal price update. If there’s an active promotion, then price updates are accepted but will not appear on Walmart.com until the active promotion ends. Once the active promotion ends, the price will be based on the last successful price update until the next promotion starts.

* * *

How long does it take for a promo to publish on Walmart.com?

Promotion updates can take up to four hours to publish. For this reason, you should upload your Price and promotion template in Seller Center at least 24 hours before you want the promo to begin. As a reminder, the start and end times of your promotions are in Universal Time Zone (UTC) and not in your local time zone.

* * *

Can I set up a promotion for individual customers?

No, promotions are set at the item-level. It’s not possible to offer deals or discounts to individuals. Offering deals or discounts to individual customers constitutes marketing and solicitation and is prohibited in the Marketplace Retailer Agreement.

* * *

Can I view my active promotions if I create them via the Price and promotion template?

Yes, your promotions that have been created via the Price and promotion template will also be captured in the [**Promotions**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/promotions) dashboard under the **External Promotion** category.
