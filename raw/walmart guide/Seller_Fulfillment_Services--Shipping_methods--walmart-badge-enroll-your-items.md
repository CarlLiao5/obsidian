Walmart+ badge: Enroll your items

Last updated on Nov 18, 2025

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Shipping%20methods/walmart-badge-enroll-your-items#overview)
- [Enroll your items](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Shipping%20methods/walmart-badge-enroll-your-items#enroll)
- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Shipping%20methods/walmart-badge-enroll-your-items#frequently-asked-questions)


* * *

Overview

Participants in the [Walmart+ badge program](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-badge-program-overview) can enroll and unenroll items at any time. To find out if you're eligible to participate in the program, visit the [**Walmart+ Seller Fulfilled**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/walmart-plus) page in Seller Center. In this guide, you'll learn how to enroll items into the Walmart+ badge program.

## Enroll your items

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to the [**Walmart+ Seller Fulfilled**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/walmart-plus) page in Seller Center, locate the **Enroll items** in Walmart+ section. Your items are organized into three categories:

- **Enrolled:** Items that are currently enrolled in the Walmart+ badge program.
- **Eligible:** You can enroll these items into the Walmart+ badge program. They meet the program requirements.
- **Ineligible:** You can't enroll these items into the Walmart+ badge program. They don't meet the program requirements.

### Step 2 — Enroll items

To enroll items, select the **Eligible** tab. You can select the _Manual enroll_ button to choose which items you want to enroll, or the _Auto enroll_ button to automatically enroll all eligible items. **Auto enroll** only applies to items that are active in your Marketplace catalog at the time of enrollment.

If you enroll manually, you can enroll eligible items individually, or with a spreadsheet:

- **Individually:** Locate the item you want, select the three dots in the **Actions** column and then select _Enroll_.
- **With a spreadsheet:** Select the _Enroll items via spreadsheet_ button and choose _Step 1: Download_. Follow the steps to download a spreadsheet. When you're ready, use the same window to re-upload your completed spreadsheet.

### Step 3 — Receive the badge

As a reminder, your item must ship in three calendar days or less to receive the badge. After successful enrollment, the Walmart+ badge and a **Free Shipping** badge will appear under the item listing.

Notes:

Not all items in your catalog may meet Walmart's criteria, and enrollment doesn't guarantee the Walmart+ badge. Because Walmart continuously monitors seller and item performance, the Walmart+ badge may or may not display on a listing as performance metrics change.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

I enrolled an item in the Walmart+ badge program. Why isn’t the Walmart+ badge displaying?

First, check the [**Walmart+ Seller Fulfilled**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/walmart-plus) page in Seller Center to verify that you're eligible for the program. Then confirm that your item is enrolled. If your item is enrolled and meets all the [Seller Performance Standards](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Seller-performance-standards), it's likely that the shipping speed for your item exceeds the three calendar days or less requirement. The Walmart+ badge should display when the shipping speed requirement is met.
