Item setup methods: Overview

Last updated on Jun 30, 2025

Reading time: 3 min

* * *

Overview

Once you’ve completed the [account setup process](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Onboarding/Complete-onboarding-in-Seller-Center) in Seller Center, you can start to build your product listings on Walmart Marketplace in different ways. This guide gives a high-level overview of each method.

Notes:

If you’d like to use Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) to pick, pack and ship items, make sure to also complete [WFS seller onboarding and setup](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-seller-onboarding-setup).

## How does it work?

### Multiple items

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **Method** | **Description** |
| **Express setup** | The fastest way to list up to 50 items at once that already exist in Walmart's catalog. For more information, review [_Add multiple items: Express setup_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Add-a-new-item:-Search-the-Walmart-catalog) _._ |
| **Create new items** | List new items not sold in Walmart’s catalog using Walmart's Full Setup template. For more information, review [_Add multiple items: Full setup_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup%20methods/Add-items-in-bulk:-full-setup). |
| **Match Walmart’s catalog** | The fastest way to list thousands of items at once that already exist in Walmart's catalog. For more information, review [_Add multiple items: Match Walmart's catalog_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Add-items-in-bulk:-Set-up-items-by-match). |
| **Import items** | If you sell on another external marketplace, or if you have a Supplier One catalog, you can import those items into your Marketplace catalog. For more information, review [_Add multiple items: Import your catalog_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/add-multiple-items-import-your-catalog) or [_Add multiple items: Import your supplier catalog_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup%20methods/add-multiple-items-import-your-supplier-catalog). |
| **Virtual packs** | Create multiple pack sizes of an existing item with a discount for shoppers who buy in bulk. For more information, review [_Add multiple items: Virtual packs_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Variant%20management/Create-a-virtual-pack:-full-setup-template). |
| **Convert from seller-fulfilled to Walmart-fulfilled** | If you've already set up items in your catalog and would like to fulfill them with Walmart, you can switch them over to Walmart-fulfilled. | **WFS multi-box** | Add Walmart-fulfilled items that ship in multiple boxes. For more information, review [_WFS multi-box: Set up items_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-multi-box-items). |

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Do Walmart-fulfilled items have specific item setup requirements?

Yes. For all Walmart-fulfilled items, you're required to provide additional information, such as country of origin, dimensions and [trade item configuration](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-trade-item-configuration). This allows us to store and fulfill items on your behalf. Walmart-fulfilled items that contain [potentially hazardous components or chemicals](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20policies%20&%20standards/wfs-hazardous-materials-overview) will also require additional screening. Our compliance review helps us properly store, handle, transport and/or dispose of potentially hazardous materials.

* * *

How is setting up a Walmart-fulfilled item different from seller-fulfilled?

With seller-fulfilled items, you use your own fulfillment centers to manage, store and ship your products. When you choose Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), we pick, pack and ship orders for you.

During item setup with WFS, you’ll also need to enter more required information, like country of origin, dimensions, [compliance](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup) and [trade item hierarchy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-trade-item-configuration).

* * *

Do items within the Lawn Chemicals and Live Plants categories have item setup restrictions?

Yes. Items in the Lawn Chemicals and Live Plants categories must comply with local, state and federal laws to be sold on Walmart.com. Before setting up these items in your Marketplace catalog, familiarize yourself with [_Prohibited Products Policy: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Prohibited%20products%20&%20brands/Prohibited-products-policy:-overview) _._ If you need to add sales restrictions, contact your account manager.

* * *

How do I update an item’s content?

You may be able to suggest changes to your item’s content after you’ve finished setting up your item. However, because there may be multiple sellers providing content for the same item, you may not always be able to define which content will be displayed on Walmart.com. Walmart will determine the best available content for items unless it is provided by a brand owner or authorized reseller. Items that cannot be updated will appear locked during the updating process. Learn more in [_How to update content: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/How-to-update-content:-Overview).

* * *

Do I need to choose a Solution Provider?

No, you can choose to manage your own Marketplace account if you’d like.
