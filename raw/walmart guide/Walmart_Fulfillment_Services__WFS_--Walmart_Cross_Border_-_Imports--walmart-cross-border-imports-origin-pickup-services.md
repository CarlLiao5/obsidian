Walmart Cross Border: Imports – Origin pickup services

Last updated on Mar 24, 2026

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Walmart%20Cross%20Border%20-%20Imports/walmart-cross-border-imports-origin-pickup-services#overview)
- [Book origin pickup](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Walmart%20Cross%20Border%20-%20Imports/walmart-cross-border-imports-origin-pickup-services#book_origin_pickup)

* * *

Overview

Origin Pickup makes less than container load (LCL) shipping easier for sellers. Your goods are picked up from the supplier, transported to the Yantian Container Freight Station (CFS), and consolidated for ocean shipment. All shipments using this service are routed through this facility for faster, smoother delivery into the network.

### What this means for you

- Book pickup and ocean freight together in Seller Center
- Avoid arranging separate local trucking
- Ability to track pickup appointments and actual pickup milestones all in one place
- Sellers no longer need to pay pickup costs upfront. Now those costs are due upon final delivery of the shipment, giving sellers an additional 30+ days before payment is required.


### Pricing

Pricing is estimated based on the cargo’s weight and volume (measured in cubic meters, “CBM”) and includes origin pickup to the Container Freight Station (CFS). The estimated price assumes cargo is delivered to the origin warehouse before the NRA expiration date. If cargo is delivered outside the valid range, a new NRA will be required.

### Availability

Origin pickup for eligible Yantian LCL shipments in Guangdong Province will be available starting March 24, 2026. The table below shows the supported pickup locations.

| **Greater Shenzhen Area** | **Greater Guangzhou Area** | **Other Guangdong Cities** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Shenzhen<br>Dongguan<br>Huizhou<br>Heyuan<br>Meizhou | Guangzhou <br>Foshan <br>Zhongshan <br>Zhuhai <br>Jiangmen <br>Zhaoqing | Shantou <br>Chaozhou <br>Jieyang <br>Qingyuan <br>Yunfu <br>Shaoguan <br>Zhanjiang <br>Maoming <br>Yangjian |

Factories located within 200 km of the CFS may be prioritized for inbound shipments to help your inventory move faster. Keep in mind that pickup times can vary, and eligibility doesn’t guarantee same-day or expedited service.

### Cargo requirements

LCL shipments have a few important requirements and limitations. Goods such as hazardous materials, regulated goods, and dangerous goods are not permitted, and Seller Center will not allow these items to be added to an LCL shipment. Shipments are limited to a maximum of 20,000 kg and 55 CBM, though FCL may be more cost effective if your cargo fills more than half a container. Make sure to upload your Commercial Invoice (CI) in Seller Center within 24 hours of cargo drop-off at the warehouse. Missing this step could result in delays or additional charges.

## Book origin pickup

Bookings must be made at least 7 calendar days before the cargo drop-off date, to follow LCL booking guidance. See the [Walmart Cross Border: Imports – Less than container load shipments](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-cross-border-imports-less-than-container-load-lcl-shipments#prepare-shipment) guide.

A pickup will be considered _missed_ if cargo:

- isn’t ready for shipment
- the address is incorrect
- required documentation isn’t complete
- the cargo arrives after the NRA expiration

A reattempt may be scheduled, but fees could apply, and vessel cut-off times may be affected.

### Step 1 – Create your international shipping plan

On the **Booking** landing page, under **Enter shipment details**, add your information to begin your shipping plan.

### Step 2 – Select address

You can use the dropdown menu under **Select address** to view and choose from your saved addresses. Then select _Continue_ to move forward with your booking.
