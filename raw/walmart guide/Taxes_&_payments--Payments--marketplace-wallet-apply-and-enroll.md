Marketplace Wallet: Apply and enroll

Last updated on Mar 19, 2025

Reading time: 5 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Payments/marketplace-wallet-apply-and-enroll#overview)
- [Apply and enroll](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Payments/marketplace-wallet-apply-and-enroll#apply)
- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Payments/marketplace-wallet-apply-and-enroll#frequently-asked-questions)

* * *

Overview

The Marketplace Wallet, powered by our bank provider, is a deposit account embedded in Walmart’s secure platform\* that allows you to receive and transfer your payouts in Seller Center. In this guide, you’ll learn how to set up your account with our bank provider account and transfer funds.

Notes:

\*At this time, only sellers registered in the U.S. with an IRS tax ID number can enroll in the Marketplace Wallet. Deposit holding and other banking services are provided to you by JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A, Member FDIC. Walmart, Inc. administers and services the deposit account on behalf of the Bank. Walmart, Inc. is not a bank.

## Apply and enroll

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to the Seller Center [homepage](https://seller.walmart.com/home) and select _Set up payments._ On the **Payment providers** page, choose _Set up_ in the **Marketplace Wallet** tile.

Notes:

This step is only visible while you’re completing the [Marketplace onboarding process](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Onboarding/Complete-onboarding-in-Seller-Center). If you’ve already completed onboarding and want to use the Marketplace Wallet, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

### Step 2 – Provide business details

Once you provide information about your business to our bank provider, your financial controller must also provide additional details. The financial controller will be the only person in your business authorized to transact with your Marketplace Wallet account. They’ll need to submit their own information and government identification. After providing the business details, you must also consent to and agree to the business agreement, which also contains terms and conditions.

### Step 3 – Link your bank account

You can link your external bank account once your Marketplace Wallet application is approved by our bank partner. To link your bank account, navigate to your account settingsin Seller Center, then select [_Marketplace Wallet_](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/financial-settings/wallet-account-settings) under **Financial Settings.** Choose the _Link a bank account_ button.

The name on your linked account must match the name on your Marketplace Wallet account. You'll provide the category of the account, the account type, the routing and the account number, then select _Link bank account_ **.**

Notes:

In some situations, our bank partner may send a microdeposit to verify ownership of your linked bank account.

### Step 4 – Deposit funds

Once verification is complete and you have a positive balance from your sales, you’ll receive payouts to your wallet. Your financial controller can transfer your funds to your linked business account through ACH with no hidden fees.  Navigate to the [**Marketplace Wallet**](https://seller.walmart.com/payments/wallet) page in Seller Center and select _Send money_ to transfer funds. Funds should arrive within two to three business days.

Your deposit account is embedded on the Walmart platform with global security infrastructures and monitoring. Marketplace Wallet funds in the deposit account are insured by the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC)\*.

Notes:

\*The Marketplace Wallet powered by our bank provider is a deposit account insured by the FDIC up to $250,000.00 per depositor at JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A., for each account ownership category.  The availability of FDIC deposit insurance coverage for the deposit account protects against the failure of JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A., and not of Walmart, Inc.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

I’m unable to access the Marketplace Wallet page. What should I do?

Marketplace Wallet is only available if you’re setting up your payment settings for the first time during the onboarding process. If you already receive payments through a third-party payout provider and want to use the Marketplace Wallet, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

How long will it take to approve my application?

Applying for Marketplace Wallet only takes a few minutes. Our bank provider will review your application and determine whether to approve you, based on the business information you provide them. You may receive an email from our bank provider during the process requesting additional documents or info.

* * *

How is Marketplace Wallet different from third-party payout providers?

Marketplace Wallet, powered by our bank provider, is integrated with Walmart’s Marketplace so you receive dedicated support without needing to leave the Seller Center. Deposited funds are FDIC insured\* and has no hidden fees for ACH transfers to a linked bank account.

\*The Marketplace Wallet powered by J.P.Morgan is a deposit account insured by the FDIC up to $250,000.00 per depositor at JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A., for each account ownership category.  The availability of FDIC deposit insurance coverage for the deposit account protects against the failure of JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A., and not of Walmart, Inc.

* * *

Where can I find my Marketplace Wallet transactional reports?

Navigate to the [Marketplace Wallet](https://seller.walmart.com/payments/wallet) section under the **Payments** section in Seller Center to view your transactional reports.

* * *

How can I verify microdeposits?

When you are linking your bank account to the Marketplace Wallet deposit accounts, you may receive microdeposits from our bank provider to the linked account. You'll need to verify those amounts in Seller Center. If the amounts you entered match the amount deposited, the verification is successful. Ensure you’re correctly entering your bank account number and bank routing number correctly.

* * *

What percentage of my payouts would I receive using Marketplace Wallet?

If you have a U.S. bank account, Walmart can provide a net sales payout through an electronic funds transfer directly to your chosen payout provider bank account. In this method, Walmart bears all costs for settling USD via U.S. bank-automated clearing house (ACH) payments. This means you’ll receive 100% of the funds you’re owed when being paid through ACH.

* * *

Can I have multiple bank accounts linked at once?

No. Only one bank account can be linked to Marketplace Wallet at a time. If you need to change your bank account information, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

How do I terminate my Marketplace Wallet account, and who should I contact?

To close your deposit account with our bank provider, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.
