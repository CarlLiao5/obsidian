Ship with Walmart: Buy shipping labels

Last updated on Nov 2, 2025

Reading time: 4 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Ship%20with%20Walmart/buy-shipping-labels-in-seller-center#overview)
- [Buy a shipping label](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Ship%20with%20Walmart/buy-shipping-labels-in-seller-center#buy)
- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Ship%20with%20Walmart/buy-shipping-labels-in-seller-center#frequently-asked-questions)

Overview

If you self-fulfill your Marketplace orders, you can purchase discounted shipping labels in Seller Center by using [**Ship with Walmart**](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Ship%20with%20Walmart/Cross-border%20services/Ship-with-Walmart-Overview) or through [Walmart APIs](https://developer.walmart.com/us-marketplace/reference/tokenapi). In this guide, we’ll show you how to purchase a label in Seller Center.

Notes:

API support is available for USPS, FedEx, Yanwen and SF Express. UPS isn't supported.

## Buy a shipping label

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to the [**Orders**](https://seller.walmart.com/orders/manage-orders?orderGroups=Unshipped) page in Seller Center.

Pro tip

We recommendconfiguring your [default shipping and label settings](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/ship-with-walmart/account-settings) first to apply to all future label printing settings for your orders, although it isn’t required.

Notes:

You’re responsible for any applicable charges resulting from misclassification or reclassification because of incorrect package type, size or weight.

### Step 2 — Purchase the label

Once you’re ready to ship orders, follow the instructions below for ordering labels for individual orders or for several orders at once. However, you can only buy shipping labels from Walmart for seller-fulfilled orders.

**Individual orders:**

- In the **Unshipped** tab select the underlined purchase order (PO) number for the package you want to ship. Be sure to check the **Fulfilled by** column for the item you want to ship, and ensure it says **Seller.**


- Choose _Ship items_ and under **Shipping details**, select _Ship with Walmart to_ enter in your preferred package type and weight.


- Select the _Estimate rate_  button to calculate the estimated shipping label rates and delivery methods. The prices shown are estimates and may vary by the actual weight and size once scanned by the carrier. The final cost of the shipping label will be reflected in your **Payments Statement Summary** as a Walmart Shipping Label Service Charge.


![2510_SWW-Buy-shipping-labels.gif](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt4af48cc4125bc735/6903819bb5c526651c5efdfd/2510_SWW-Buy-shipping-labels.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

**Bulk orders:**

- In the **Unshipped** tab, select the checkboxes next to multiple packages you want to ship and select _Process orders_.


- Complete the required information for each row and select the _Update_  button to retrieve information and pricing to generate your shipping label.


- To ship from the China fulfillment center, change the **Ship from** field for items shipping from China. Provide the required information in Mandarin for **Item description** and the **Ship from address.** Then, choose the item that contains the checkbox to accurately state the contents in the package.

- Finally, select all the rows that you want to proceed buying labels for and choose the **Buy Shipping** button to complete the label purchase.


![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltb687e077a7cc600a/66e463750ce18d79a738c44b/11090_GIF_(1).gif?branch=us_mplearn)

Pro tip

Select the _Highlight missing fields_ button to learn which fields you need to fill out per package to complete the purchase (e.g., **Ship method,** **Add-ons** and **Declared value)**.

Notes:

The first time you use Ship with Walmart to print your shipping label, you’ll need to agree to our Terms of Service and any terms and agreements required by our carriers and partners. You may see an agreement popup window if any of the terms agreed to are updated or changed. Once you’ve read and agreed to all required terms, select the _Accept_ and _Submit_ buttons.

### Step 3 — Print the label

Once your label has been generated, select the _Print label_ button. After you’ve printed your label and are ready to ship, select the _Mark as Shipped_ button in **Order details**. Once you mark the order as shipped, the label can’t be canceled or modified.

Notes:

Access to preferred carrier rates for eligible shipments are subject to this program’s [Terms of Service](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt21c4a68205029aca/67c756f2a00ad9164d087c8c/Ship_With_Walmart_Program_Terms_091622.pdf?branch=us_mplearn).

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What should I keep in mind when shipping from China to the U.S.?

For custom requirements, you’ll need to verify and provide the From Address in Mandarin if you’re shipping from China. Additionally, you must accurately select package content information and verify the description of the package contents in Mandarin. Yanwen and SF Express are the only carriers applicable for shipments from China to the U.S. If your item is already mapped to ship from a China fulfillment center, the system will display the information required to complete the purchase.

Once your shipment is prepared with Ship with Walmart labels on each package, check for pickup coverage on the carrier’s website or contact information listed below. If you’re located outside the pickup coverage area, deliver packages to the carrier’s hub.

- **Yanwen**: Contact the carrier offline or navigate to [Yanwen’s portal](https://portal.yw56.com.cn/user/login) .


- **SF Express**: Call the hotline at (+86) 755 95338 or visit [SF International](https://www.sf-international.com/cn/sc/consign).


* * *

Will I get billed for labels purchased from my account by Walmart?

No, you will get billed through your carrier. You can also review shipping costs prior to shipping using the [Ship with Walmart shipping calculator](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/ship-with-walmart-shipping-calculator).

* * *

When will I be charged for the shipping label?

You’re charged for the label when you mark the order as shipped. If you don’t use the label, you’ll be refunded. Depending on the carrier:

- Refunds may take 14–30 days to appear in your account.


- Any adjustments (credit or debit) to label charges typically take two to three weeks.


To learn more about your Ship with Walmart charges, download the [Ship with Walmart Carrier Reconciliation Report](https://seller.walmart.com/analytics/reports). This report includes estimated and final label charges, insurance costs and carrier-scanned dimensions and weights. Once you’ve purchased your shipping label, it may take a few days for the final charges to appear in the report.

* * *

My item was lost in transit or lost after delivery. What should I do?

There’s no action required from you if the item was shipped within the US using Ship with Walmart labels. Ship with Walmart offers **automatic protections** on orders (up to $100) lost in transit and lost after delivery (except if using Bring your own account). You can track all open or closed claims from the [**Claims**](https://seller.walmart.com/orders/claims) dashboard under **Orders** in Seller Center.

* * *

My Ship From address isn't accurate. How do I update it?

You can update your Ship From address in [**Seller fulfillment**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/seller-fulfillment) settings in Seller Center. If you make an update, check your **Shipping Templates** settings to confirm that the correct fulfillment center details are listed. For more information, visit [_Shipping Templates: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping%20Templates/Shipping-templates:-overview) _._   You can also change your address for individual orders from the [**Orders**](https://seller.walmart.com/orders/manage-orders) page.

* * *

Why am I being asked to accept multiple terms and conditions when I try to create a label?

You may have multiple terms to agree to depending on whether you’ve used Ship with Walmart previously, as well as which carriers and services you’ve chosen.

* * *

Can I use my own carrier rates when creating a label in Seller Center?

Yes, you can [bring your own carrier account](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Ship%20with%20Walmart/ship-with-walmart-bring-your-own-account) and purchase labels if needed.

* * *

Why was my package delivered by a different carrier than the one I chose when purchasing my label?

UPS and FedEx have economy shipping services that contract with USPS for last-mile delivery. UPS Ground Saver and FedEx Ground Economy are carrier services that may use USPS to deliver shipments.

* * *

How can I contact Walmart and carriers for support?

If you have questions, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support. For shipping related questions, you can directly contact Yanwen support team at [ywkfwem@yw56.com.cn](mailto:%20ywkfwem@yw56.com.cn) or call the SF Express hotline at (+86) 755 95338.
