Add multiple items: Import your catalog

Last updated on Oct 29, 2025

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup%20methods/add-multiple-items-import-your-catalog#overview)

- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup%20methods/add-multiple-items-import-your-catalog#frequently-asked-questions)

Overview

If you already sell items on an external marketplace and want to sell them on Walmart.com, you can easily import them into your Marketplace catalog. In this guide, we’ll show you how to use Walmart’s **Import items** tool to import external items and set them up as [**Walmart Fulfilled**](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20basics/walmart-fulfillment-services-wfs-overview) or [**Seller Fulfilled**](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-and-fulfillment-policy).

Notes:

You must have admin level access to Seller Center to use this item setup method.

## Import your catalog

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center and select the _Add items_ button.

### Step 2 — Import items

In the **Import items** section, select _Import items._ On the next page, select _Get Started_ and follow the steps to connect to your external marketplace account. Once you confirm the import, your external items will be imported into your Marketplace catalog and then saved as **Draft** items.

Notes:

The connection and import are managed by Versori, Walmart's approved Solution Partner. Versori only imports content that you own, such as item titles and descriptions. Versori doesn't import images, so you'll need to add images to your imported items in Seller Center after the import is complete.

![2510_Import-Your-Catalog_screenshot.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt5bde9369e62df335/69038dbf12543051fee9bcc6/2510_Catalog-import-tool-for-WFS_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 3 — Review your drafts

When the import is complete, select _View my catalog._ A confirmation window will appear to let you know how many items have been imported. Select _Review items_.

- **Required fields**: Because your external items are now **Draft** items in your Marketplace catalog, you’ll need to review each item and verify that its information is correct. Check the item title and description and make sure that the **Required** sections are complete.
- **Fulfillment method**: When importing your items, the fulfillment option will default to [Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS)](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20basics/walmart-fulfillment-services-wfs-overview) if the item is eligible. If you prefer to fulfill the orders on your own, change the fulfillment method to **Seller Fulfilled**. You can change the fulfillment method at any time.

Pro tip

If you’re not yet ready to fulfill your item through Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), leave the **Walmart** **Fulfilled** option selected. This keeps the item eligible for WFS, and you can still ship the item’s orders on your own. To start using WFS, just send inventory to a Walmart fulfillment center.

Notes:

Changing an imported item’s fulfillment method from **Walmart Fulfilled** to **Seller Fulfilled** removes Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS)–related details such as item dimensions. If you change the fulfillment method, you’ll need to re-enter the WFS information.

### Step 4 — Submit and publish

After submitting your items, you can track their status from the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) in Seller Center. Updates may take up to 24 hours. Any data errors that are detected during the updates will require extra processing time.

Notes:

To ensure safe storage and handling, any Walmart-fulfilled item that contains a hazardous material must go through a compliance review. A compliance review can take up to three business days. You can check an item’s review status on the [**Pending review**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/pending-review) page.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How much does it cost to import my external marketplace catalog? Do I need to sign up for Versori?

There's no cost to import an external marketplace catalog. You only need to:

- Use a Walmart Marketplace account with admin level access to Seller Center.

- Provide the credentials needed to allow Versori to access your external marketplace account.


* * *

Will Walmart have permanent access to my external marketplace data?

Walmart does not have access to your external marketplace account. Versori only has temporary access to your external account while it manages your account connection and data import.

* * *

Can I import my external marketplace catalog as many times as I want?

Currently, you can only import an external marketplace catalog once. If you add items to your external catalog after your initial import, you'll need to manually add them to your Walmart Marketplace catalog. For more information on how to add items to your **Catalog** in Seller Center, visit [Item setup methods: Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/Item-setup-methods:-Overview).

* * *

How do I check for and resolve errors?

To check published items for errors, navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center and select the **Errors** tab. To view items that are on hold, navigate to the [**Pending review**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/pending-review) page and select the _Action Needed_ or _In Review_ links for more information.

* * *

How is setting up a Walmart-fulfilled item different from seller-fulfilled?

With seller-fulfilled items, you use your own fulfillment centers to manage, store and ship your products. When you choose Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), we pick, pack and ship orders for you.

During item setup with WFS, you'll also need to enter more required information, like country of origin, dimensions, [compliance](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup) and [trade item hierarchy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-trade-item-configuration).

* * *

What else do I need to do for Walmart-fulfilled items?

After you publish a Walmart-fulfilled item, you’ll need to create a shipping plan to [send the item’s inventory](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-inbound-orders:-Send-inventory) to Walmart fulfillment centers.
