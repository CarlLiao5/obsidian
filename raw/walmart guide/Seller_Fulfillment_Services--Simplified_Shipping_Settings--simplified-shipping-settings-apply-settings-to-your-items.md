Simplified Shipping Settings: Apply settings to existing items

Last updated on Apr 9, 2026

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

In this guide:

Overview

In this guide, you’ll learn the various ways to apply Simplified Shipping Settings to your SKUs if you want to quickly make the switch from Shipping Templates or if you want to only  configure a few of your SKUs through Simplified Shipping Settings.

Pro tip

Before beginning this process, we recommend setting up your fulfillment center and adding inventory.

Notes:

Simplified Shipping Settings is compatible with free and paid shipping **,** [virtual packs](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup%20methods/Create-a-virtual-pack:-full-setup-template) and most domestic USPS, UPS and FedEx services. However, it doesn’t support Ship to Store items, international or freight shipping or third-party logistics services such as Flexport.

## Apply Simplified Shipping Settings

### Option 1 – Migrate items

If you use [Shipping Templates](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/academy/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/shipping-templates-overview) to configure your shipping settings and want to use Simplified Shipping Settings instead, you can migrate all your eligible SKUs at once. Navigate to the [**Simplified Shipping Settings**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/simplified-shipping/simplified-carriers) page in Seller Center and select the **Manage settings** drop-down menu. Then, choose _Item migration_.

On the next page, set up your preferred carriers and shipping services. You can migrate any SKUs that are set up for expedited delivery with free shipping. To ensure expedited shipping for these SKUs, add all of the expedited shipping carriers that you use.

You can also customize carrier methods by region. For example, you can use expedited carriers in some regions and ground carriers in other regions. Once you select _Migrate my items,_ Walmart will automatically migrate all of your eligible SKUs and settings from Shipping Templates to Simplified Shipping Settings.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt5c39f318bd956199/684b1f08fc7cdb60694d9cb0/2506_simplified-shipping-apply-settings-existing-items.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Option 2 – Apply settings individually

To apply Simplified Shipping Settings to individual SKUs, navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center. You must have the **Shipping template** column present to apply shipping settings. Add this column by selecting it in the **Columns** configuration menu.

Then, locate the item you want to update and select the shipping template name under the **Shipping template** column for that item. Choose _Simplified shipping settings_  then _Update_ to apply your new shipping settings.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt5d7571cb00dabbab/685475e162bf79858b6ba67b/2506_sss_apply_settings_existing.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

## Option 3 – Apply settings in bulk

If you want to apply Simplified Shipping Settings in bulk, navigate to your **Catalog** in Seller Center. Next,select the _Update items_ button and choose _Update with file_. From the **Update with file** dialog box, choose _Show Walmart templates_ and select _Item shipping config_. Complete the template including the SKU IDs in the first column and select the _Simplified Shipping Settings_ option in the second column.

Once you’ve completed the template, return to the **Update with file** dialog box. Select the **Item shipping config** template type in the drop-down menu, then drag-and-drop or browse to your completed **Item shipping config** file to submit it.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Are all my offers eligible for Simplified Shipping Settings?

Most seller-fulfilled items with free shipping are eligible for Simplified Shipping Settings. Simplified Shipping Settings is not compatible with Ship to Store items, international or freight shipping or third-party logistics services such as Flexport.

* * *

Why am I unable to add new items to my Automated Shipping Templates?

Automated Shipping Templates is no longer a shipping settings option. Instead, you can migrate your SKUs to Simplified Shipping Settings through one of the methods listed above. Simplified Shipping Settings offer more accurate and faster promises along with On-Time Delivery protections, as long as you meet our [shipping policy requirements](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-and-fulfillment-policy).

* * *

When I add a new item to my catalog, do I need to map it to Simplified Shipping Settings?

No. As long as the item is supported by Simplified Shipping Settings, newly added items will automatically inherit your Simplified Shipping Settings. Additionally, items that aren’t in a published status that are changed to published status will automatically inherit your Simplified Shipping Settings.

* * *

Why don’t my items show my Simplified Shipping Settings applied in my catalog in Seller Center?

When you apply Simplified Shipping Settings to your items, you can view the status of those changes in your Seller Center [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed). It can take up to four hours for those changes to update in your **Catalog.** However, once your settings have beenprocessed in your **Activity Feed**, those updates will be visible to customers.

* * *

If I apply Simplified Shipping Settings to my items, can I switch back to using Shipping Templates?

Yes. You can revert back to Shipping Templates following the same steps described in this guide. However, instead of selecting Simplified Shipping Settings, you can choose a Shipping Template instead. If you need assistance, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support and request to have your items reverted to your prior manual settings. Allow up to one week for your request to be received and completed.

* * *

Can I use Walmart APIs to migrate items?

Yes, you can use [Walmart APIs](https://developer.walmart.com/us-marketplace/reference/updatefclevelsimplifiedshippingsettings) to migrate items and streamline shipping.
