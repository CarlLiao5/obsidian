Under-performing item removal policy

Last updated on Dec 11, 2025

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/Under-performing-item-removal-policy#frequently-asked-questions)

Overview

At Walmart, we review all Marketplace listings twice a year to make sure customers have a consistent shopping experience on Walmart.com. If we identify a listing that is deemed under-performing, it will be unpublished from Walmart Marketplace. Keep reading to learn more about this policy.

## What is the policy?

Items must be listed and transactable for a predetermined timeframe, based on the nature and seasonality of each category or they could be deemed as under-performing. Exceptions include strategic items or categories and slow-moving items. Other under-performance indicators include but are not limited to:

- No sales for the item during the predetermined timeframe.
- No sales of items within the same variant groups during the predetermined timeframe.

Even though Walmart conducts catalog reviews semi-annually, Walmart reserves the right to unpublish items at any time. If your item is unpublished due to underperformance, it won’t be relisted until there is a substantial improvement to the listing. If the same item is removed multiple times, Walmart reserves the right to permanently remove the item from our catalog.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

My item has been removed due to under-performance. What should I do?

After you’ve reviewed your listing and made improvements, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

How can I view my items that have been removed for under-performance?

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/manage-items) within Seller Center. Select Filters, then **Status Change Reason**. Choose Manually Unpublished and Apply to view your items that have been unpublished by Walmart. You can also download your [Item Report](https://seller.walmart.com/items-and-inventory/reports?returnPATH=%2Fitem%2Flist) to find the **Status Change Reason** column. All items that have been removed due to under-performance will have "Under-performing" listed in the explanatory text.

* * *

My items are seasonal; will they be removed during the off-season?

Items are regularly evaluated to avoid the necessity of factoring in seasonality.
