Overview

Sponsored Search advertising from Walmart Connect gets your brand and products in front of customers who are actively searching and browsing Walmart’s site and app for products like yours. You only pay when customers click your ad. There are three ways to use Sponsored Search advertising: **Sponsored Products**, **Sponsored Brands** and **Sponsored Videos**.

## How does it work?

Sponsored Products ads are served to customers actively searching for products like yours on Walmart's site or app. They appear on prominent, high-traffic spots such as the homepage, browse, search, item and stock up pages.

You can submit bids for Sponsored Products ads. Ads are selected based on relevancy and bids. The most relevant product with the highest bid wins the ad placement.

Sponsored Brands sends your brand logo and products to the top of relevant search results. This premium placement is available to Marketplace brand owners registered with the [Walmart Brand Portal](https://brandportal.walmart.com/?_gl=1*1n874j8*_ga*MzQyMjEwMDg1LjE2NzgyMTU3MzI.*_ga_1LB22TM2MG*MTY3ODkyMzQ4OC4yMC4xLjE2Nzg5MjM0OTUuMC4wLjA.). Sponsored Videos are a premium ad placement designed to improve the visibility of advertised items within search results using high-quality video ad creative.

All three ad placements are sold on a **cost per click** basis, which means you only pay when a customer selects your ad.

Visit the [**Advertising**](https://seller.walmart.com/advertising/home) page in Seller Center to learn more about how Walmart's advertising solutions can help you grow your business.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Which type of Sponsored Search ads are best for me?

All three ad types are useful in driving product visibility and sales. Choose one or all three depending on the goals and needs of your business.

* * *

What are some differences between Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands and Sponsored Videos?

Below are the major differences between the three ways to use Sponsored Search:

- **Sponsored Products:** Help boost product visibility and conversions by showcasing your listings in Walmart search results.

- **Sponsored Brands:** Drive conversion and help drive brand awareness by allowing sellers to customize their ad creative with a logo and custom headline. Sponsored Brands can especially benefit smaller brands, newer brands and brands with new products that may not yet have high organic search rankings in Walmart search results.

- **Sponsored Videos:** Help brands and products stand out in search results with video ads that capture customer attention through motion, sight and sound. These videos can help influence Walmart shoppers’ decisions by showing them product benefits at the consideration point of their shopping journey.


* * *

Can I use all Sponsored Search options at once? Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands and Sponsored Videos?

Yes. If you are eligible, you can use all three.

* * *

How much do Sponsored Products cost?

Sponsored products are CPC (cost per click) ads that are sold on a second-price auction basis. This means you set a maximum bid you are willing to pay when a customer clicks on your ads but only pay what is needed to win the auction. Relevancy + bid price determines the auction winner.

* * *

How do I register my brand on Walmart Marketplace?

If eligible, you can register your brand [here](https://brandportal.walmart.com/?_gl=1*1n874j8*_ga*MzQyMjEwMDg1LjE2NzgyMTU3MzI.*_ga_1LB22TM2MG*MTY3ODkyMzQ4OC4yMC4xLjE2Nzg5MjM0OTUuMC4wLjA.).

* * *

What other advertising solutions can I use on Walmart Marketplace?

Visit the [**Advertising**](https://seller.walmart.com/advertising/home) page in Seller Center to learn more about all of the Walmart advertising solutions available.

* * *

Are all items eligible for Sponsored Search?

Not all items are eligible. Because all condition offers share an item ID, Pre-Owned and Restored items in the following product types are not eligible for Sponsored Search: modems, magazines, auto wipers and VCRs.
