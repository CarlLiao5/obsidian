How to update content: Overview

Last updated on Apr 2, 2026

Reading time: 2 min

Overview

You can help create a great shopping experience on Walmart.com by keeping your item content up to date, such as an item’s product name, site description and images. In this guide, we’ll cover how Walmart’s content process works and the methods you can use to update your content.

Notes:

AI-generated content must be accurate, truthful, rights-compliant, and aligned with Marketplace policies, including our [_Product details policy_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-Detail-Page:-overview).

## How does it work?

Before you get started, it’s important to understand how Walmart’s content process works. Walmart defines content as any information that provides product details about an item, such as product name, site description, key features and images. Content also includes item attributes, which are the values that help customers understand an item’s features and characteristics.

To ensure a high-quality shopping experience, Walmart selects the best content for each item attribute, giving priority to content provided directly by [brand owners and their authorized resellers](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Brand%20Portal/brand-portal-overview). This means that if you don’t have brand owner or authorized reseller privileges, you won’t be able to update some content fields, including blank fields.

If you need more access to an item’s content, you can [register as a brand owner](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Brand%20Portal/Register-your-brand-on-Walmart-Marketplace) or become an authorized reseller.

Notes:

Because brand owners and authorized resellers have priority, there are times when you won’t be able to decide what content is published on Walmart.com. If you’re not able to update your content, follow our [troubleshooting steps](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/troubleshooting-content-errors) for more information.

## Additional guidelines

To help drive sales and increase the chances of Walmart publishing your content, review Walmart’s [content standards and requirements](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Content-standards:-Overview) for each of the product categories and product types you’re selling. Then, make sure that the information you provide for each of your item attributes meets those standards and requirements. You can also use Walmart’s [AI-powered content suggestions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Item%20management/Update-item-content-individually-in-Seller-Center) for your product title, site description and key features.

When you’re ready to update your content, choose the method that works best for you:

| **Method** | **Use case** |
| --- | --- |
| **Single item update method** | Use your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/manage-items) to quickly update a few items. Learn how to [update item content individually](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Item%20management/Update-item-content-individually-in-Seller-Center) _._ |
| **Bulk update method** | Use a spreadsheet to update multiple items at once. Learn how to [update item content in bulk](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Item%20management/Update-item-content-in-bulk-in-Seller-Center) _._ |
| **API integration** | If you’re publishing a large catalog of items and have a technical team to support your operations, consider using APIs. Learn how to [integrate with APIs](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/Integration-methods-API) [.](https://sellerhelp.walmart.com/s/guide?article=000006422&language=en_US) |
| **Approved Solution Provider** | Walmart Marketplace works with several third-party providers that can help you outsource content updates. Learn how to [choose an Approved Solution Provider](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/Choose-a-solution-provider). |
