Troubleshoot product variant errors

Last updated on Aug 20, 2025

Reading time: 1 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Variant%20management/Troubleshoot-product-variant-errors#overview)
- [Troubleshoot product variant errors](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Variant%20management/Troubleshoot-product-variant-errors#errors)

* * *

Overview

When making changes to your variant groups, you may get an error. This guide can help you troubleshoot and correct these errors. If you’re unsure of how to add or remove items from your variant groups and need guidance, check out [_Manage a Variant Group_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Variant%20management/Manage-a-variant-group) _._

## Troubleshoot product variant errors

### 1\. Error: "Each variant group ID can only show up to 1,000 items on site. Please ensure your group has a maximum of 999 items."

#### What should I do?

Remove items from the group, or separate the group into two groups that meet the item limit. Learn how to [manage a variant group](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Variant%20management/Manage-a-variant-group).

### 2\. Error: "Multiple items have the same values for their variant attributes. Please provide unique values or add more product variations to ensure your items are shown on site."

#### What should I do?

Update the attributes. Each item in your catalog must have unique attribute information in order to be sold as a unique item on Walmart.com. You can do either of the following:

1. Make sure that the **Product Variation** values are unique. For example, if two bath towel items have the same **Color** value, such as _Blue_, update one of the values to _Navy blue_.

2. Add more **Product Variation** values to further differentiate the items. For example, if two T-shirt items have the same **Color** value, such as _Red_, add a **Size** value to each item.For example, item A could be _Red/Small_ and item B could be _Red/Medium_.
