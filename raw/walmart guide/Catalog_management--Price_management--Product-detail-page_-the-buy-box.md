Product detail page: The Buy Box

Reading time: 1 min

Overview

On Walmart Marketplace, multiple sellers can offer the same item. These offers are listed on one item page on Walmart.com, but only one offer will win the Buy Box spot. Winning the Buy Box increases the visibility of your offer, improves your chances of attracting more customers and builds trust.

## How does it work?

The Buy Box winner is generally the listing that offers the best value to the customer, based on their geographic location. Walmart analyzes all attributes of your offer when determining the Buy Box winner including your offer’s price, shipping fee, delivery speed, your [seller performance](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Seller-performance-standards) metrics and the customer’s experience. The Buy Box is located above the **Add to cart** button on the item page, where a majority of purchases occur. Below is an example.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blta46bac6207dac4a6/69cffd1c5acfacf53060cbb9/2603_Buy-box-product-detail-page.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

In certain cases where the offer is priced substantially in excess of prices currently or recently offered on Walmart.com or on competing sites, but not at a level requiring automatic unpublishing, we’ll remove the **Add to cart** button, as well as the offer details such as price and shipping from the item page. Customers can still purchase the item via the more seller options transaction flow.  For more information, visit [Pricing rules](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Pricing-rules).

Pro tip

Use our [Buy Box checklist](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt03b9aea6f6536380/buy-box-best-practices.pdf?branch=test2) as a guide to help increase your chances of securing the Buy Box.

## Additional guidelines

The following tools are available to help you learn more about your Buy Box offer:

| Feature | Learn more |
| --- | --- |
| **Pricing insights dashboard** | Use the [**Pricing Insights**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/insights) dashboard to track your overall Buy Box win rate or track it by item. Compare your item’s current price with the price of the Buy Box winner to understand how competitive your pricing is.<br>You can also view historical insights to analyze how you’ve been winning the Buy Box over time. |
| **Buy Box report** | The [Buy Box report](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Reporting/Generate-a-buy-box-report) gives you an overview of your products that have competed for the Buy Box including their SKUs, it shows whether or not you won and more. |

Pro tip

Consider enabling the Walmart [Repricer](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Repricer:-overview) and applying the Buy Box strategy rule to automate competitive pricing across Walmart.com and external marketplaces.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Why isn’t my Add to cart button showing on the item page?

In certain cases, if your offer is not eligible to win the Buy Box, we’ll remove the **Add to cart** button, as well as the offer details such as price and shipping from the item page. Customers can still purchase the item via the more seller options transaction flow. For more information, review our [_Pricing rules_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Pricing-rules).
