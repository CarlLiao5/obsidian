Overview

Walmart Cross Border: Imports offers inbound full and less than container load transportation from select ports of loading in Asiato Walmart fulfillment centers in the United States. This guide outlines the benefits of our import services, eligibility requirements, what you’ll need to enroll, and a high-level overview of the process for booking international inbound shipments.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt503f94f214a2ac6e/66ed8634d579c975514617dc/Screenshot_2024-09-20_at_10.24.00_AM.png?branch=us_mplearn)

## Benefits

Walmart Cross Border: Imports offers several benefits:

- Access to direct import and Walmart’s first-rate supply chain partners and teams.


- Ocean freight shipment for both standard and oversize inventory.


- Easy, end-to-end shipment process includes tracking your estimates, bookings, shipment status, payments and more in Seller Center


- Seamless experience with dedicated support for exceptions handling and inbound prioritization for entire shipment cycle.


- End-to-end support in Mandarin in the local time zone.


- Option to either select Walmart's designated customs broker or use your own broker to manage the customs clearance process.


- Support for 20 ft (dry), 40 ft (dry and high cube), and 45 ft (high cube) shipping containers, and [less than container load](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-cross-border-imports-less-than-container-load-lcl-shipments) shipments.


- Clear rate quote with the NVOCC Negotiated Rate Arrangement (NRA).


Notes:

Non-vessel operating common carrier (NVOCC) services are provided by WMSC Logistics Services, LLC.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt8f66d871ad1ebf7a/67378092b058a0faaca3410d/Mockup_On_boarding_and_Setup_Guide_Final-02.png?branch=us_mplearn)

## Eligibility

To become a Walmart Imports seller, you must meet the following qualifications:

- You’re using Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) to fulfill US customer orders placed on [Walmart.com](https://www.walmart.com/).

- Your Walmart Marketplace account is [in good standing](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller-suspension-and-termination). If your Marketplace sales history is less than 90 days, it may take additional time to review your enrollment request.

- You have a tax profile and billing services settings validated in Seller Center, with either an ACH account or [credit card billing method](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Billing-Methods:-Add-a-credit-card) on file. [Learn more](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/WFS-settings-billing-information#HowItWorks) about how Walmart uses your billing method.


- You’re sourcing or manufacturing your goods in China, Vietnam, or India. You can ship your goods from the Ningbo, Qingdao, Shanghai, Xiamen or Yantian ports in China, the Hai Phong or Ho Chi Minh City ports in Vietnam, and the Nhava Sheva and Tuticorin ports in India.

## Getting started

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt7db7796137835b4a/66a2ad0aee6c0f345c662ab5/Screenshot_2024-07-25_at_3.50.52_PM.png?branch=us_mplearn)

To get started with Walmart Cross Border: Imports, you'll submit your enrollment for review in Seller Center. Review our [How to enroll guide](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-cross-border-imports-enrollment-overview) for more information about the information you’ll provide during this process.

Once you’re enrolled, you can use Seller Center to:

- Estimate the cost of inbound ocean freight shipments on the **Shipments** page.


- Book a shipment on the **Shipments** page 14 calendar days prior to the expected cargo ready date for FCL, and 7 days prior to the expected cargo drop-off date for LCL.


- Print WFS box labels after booking a shipment.


- View inventory shipment statuses on the **Shipments** page.


Pro tip

Before you begin enrollment, please verify that you have a validated tax profile and billing service settings in Seller Center.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt484a98450766b3d8/673780927a33855b0a36ee17/Mockup_On_boarding_and_Setup_Guide_Final-04.png?branch=us_mplearn)

## What to expect

Once your containers are shipped from your selected origin ports and clear US customs, we’ll then transport your inventory to one of our WFS transfer stations:

**Standard**: Inventory is distributed to multiple fulfillment centers through [Inventory Transfer Service (ITS)](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/WFS-Inventory-Transfer-Services). This process of distributing your inventory across our fulfillment network ensures fast, reliable delivery to customers.

**Oversized**: Inventory remains at the destination fulfillment center.

Notes:

Ocean transportation services are provided by WMSC Logistics Services, LLC., a wholly owned subsidiary of Walmart Inc., licensed by the Federal Maritime Commission (FMC) as a Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier (NVOCC). License number FMC OTI No. 032243NF

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Which origin ports are available?

Walmart Imports currently offers ocean freight from the following ports across Asia and will be adding more soon.

- Ningbo, Zhejiang, China (CNNGB)
- Qingdao, Shandong, China (CNQDG)
- Shanghai, Shanghai, China (CNSGH)
- Tianjin, Tianjin, China (CNTJN)

- Xiamen, Fujian, China (CNXMG)
- Yantian, Guangdong, China (CNYTN)
- Nhava Sheva, India (INNSA)
- Tuticorin, India (INTUT)
- Hai Phong, Vietnam (VNHPH)
- Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam (VNSGN)


* * *

Do I need insurance?

You're responsible for having ocean freight insurance to cover damaged or missing inventory.

* * *

Do I need an ACH account?

You’re required to use ACH for US duties. You'll need a US tax ID in order to set up your ACH account.

* * *

What happens to my inventory once it arrives at its destination? Do you mirror inventory across the US for me?

Once your inventory clears customs and arrives at the transfer station, standard-sized inventory will be [distributed across the country](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/WFS-Inventory-Transfer-Services#imports) so it’s closer to customers. Oversize inventory will be fulfilled directly from the destination facility. Walmart will arrange delivery, so you don’t have to.

* * *

Will you submit verified gross mass (VGM) for me?

Yes, we will submit the VGM for you. China Operations will ask you to provide all relevant information to submit the VGM.

* * *

Will you handle exporter customs for me?

No, the exporter of record will need to clear customs in a timely manner to meet the scheduled vessel.

* * *

Do I need to have an import bond?

Yes, you’re required to have a continuous bond registered with US customs.

* * *

How do I track my shipment?

You can view your shipments at any time by visiting the [Shipments](https://seller.walmart.com/wfsLite/sendinventory) page.

* * *

How will I know if my enrollment request has been approved?

It may take several days our team to verify and approve your enrollment request. Your enrollment contact will be notified once your enrollment request is approved. You can also check your enrollment status in Seller Center by navigating to **Settings > Shipping Profile > Walmart Imports**.

* * *

How long does it take for shipments to arrive to the US from Asia?

Under normal market conditions, we estimate it will take 35-60 calendar days from the cargo ready date to when the container arrives to the place of delivery. This estimate depends on origin port, destination, mode of transit and other factors. We provide estimated delivery dates for each shipment during the booking process.

The total average time for shipments to the West Coast is 44 days, to the Midwest is 50 days, and to the East Coast is 55 days.

* * *

How do you handle returns? Will items be returned to Asia? Am I still required to have a US return address?

Returns will be handled as outlined in [_WFS returns overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20basics/wfs-returns-overview), except that return to vendor (RTV) is limited to the US. We cannot export returns.

* * *

Where can I find the codes and compliance info I need?

You can find the codes and compliance in the [WFS Routing and Packaging Guide](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt57da3349569cc081/WFS_Routing_Guide.pdf?branch=us_mplearn).
