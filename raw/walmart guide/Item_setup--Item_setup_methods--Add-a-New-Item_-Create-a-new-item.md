Overview

This guide outlines how to add a new item to your Marketplace **catalog** in Seller Center if it’s not currently offered on Walmart.com. If you want to add multiple new items at once, review [_Add multiple items: Full setup._](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup%20methods/Add-items-in-bulk:-full-setup)

## Add a single item

### **Step 1 — Get started**

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center and select the _Add items_ button. Select the _Create items_  button and choose _Single item_.

Pro tip

Use our AI assistant to help you generate content for your product name, site description and key features. Just select _Help me write_,provide a brief description of your item and add relevant images.  Be sure to check for accuracy as AI can make mistakes. If you’re satisfied with the output, select _Accept_. You can still make edits after selecting Accept.

Notes:

AI-generated content must be accurate, truthful, rights-compliant, and aligned with Marketplace policies, including our [_Product details policy._](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-Detail-Page:-overview)

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltf49ae7d399688168/6840a87279339c78899e03a1/2505_add-item-create-new-item_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 2 — Item details and attributes

Once you complete the **Required to list** section, the system generates recommended categories in the **Categorization** section. Once you select your category, subcategory and product type, you’ll need to select a fulfillment option and complete the rest of the form by providing the relevant attributes.

If you aren't sure which category to choose, review our [_content standards_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Content-standards:-Overview). If you’re unsure what an attribute means or how to complete it, select the info icon to learn more.

Notes:

Make sure your product ID adheres to the [_Product identifier (GTIN, UPC, ISBN or EAN) policy_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Choose-a-product-identifier).

### Step 3 – Submit

Once all the required fields are complete, select _Submit_ at the bottom right of the screen. After submitting your item, you can track its status from the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) in Seller Center. Updates may take up to 24 hours.

Any data errors that are detected during the updates will require extra processing time.

Notes:

You’re responsible for the accuracy of the information provided and ensuring that it complies with all laws, regulations and licensing requirements regarding the sale, shipping, transportation and delivery of these items.

Pro tip

Use the copy feature to quickly create a similar item that may have slightly different features such as the image. Once your initial item is set up, find the item on the **Catalog** page, then select _Copy item_ from the dropdown menu in the **Actions** column. Then update the **Required to list** fields with the attributes that differ for the copied item.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How can I track my item setup?

After submitting your item, you can track its status from the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) in Seller Center. Updates may take up to 24 hours. Any data errors that are detected during the updates will require extra processing time.

* * *

What if there’s already an item that matches my product ID in Walmart’s existing catalog?

If another item uses the same product ID, your item will be merged with the existing Walmart.com item. You can also use **Express setup** to search for and add a new item based on Walmart’s existing catalog. To learn more, check out [_Add multiple items: Express setup_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Add-a-new-item:-Search-the-Walmart-catalog). You’ll still need to create your own SKU and provide other offer information for the item, as these are unique to each seller.

* * *

How is setting up a Walmart-fulfilled item different from seller-fulfilled?

With seller-fulfilled items, you use your own fulfillment centers to manage, store and ship your products. When you choose Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), we pick, pack and ship orders for you.

During item setup with WFS, you’ll also need to enter more required information, like country of origin, dimensions, [compliance](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup) and [trade item hierarchy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-trade-item-configuration).

* * *

Can I remove the bulleted formatting in the Key Features field?

No. The key features you add are automatically formatted in a bulleted list to help customers easily review the benefits of the product. If you need to remove a key feature, you can delete the row.

* * *

I’m still having item setup issues. What should I do?

If you have questions, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.
