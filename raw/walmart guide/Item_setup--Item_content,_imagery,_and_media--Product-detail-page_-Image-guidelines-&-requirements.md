Overview

As a Marketplace seller, you must comply with Walmart Marketplace’s image guidelines and requirements. High quality images help provide accurate content for customers so they can enjoy a positive shopping experience on Walmart.com.

## How does it work?

### Content requirements

Images must not violate the [_Prohibited Products Policy_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Prohibited%20products%20&%20brands/Prohibited-products-policy:-overview) or any other Walmart policies. This includes but is not limited to:

- Content that portrays, glorifies, or promotes hatred, violence or discrimination.

- Content that displays explicit nudity or vulgar language.

- Content that contains obscene, sexually suggestive or pornographic material.

- Content deemed culturally inappropriate or insensitive.


Notes:

AI-generated content must be accurate, truthful, rights-compliant, and aligned with Marketplace policies, including our [_Product details policy_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-Detail-Page:-overview).

### File type requirements

- **File Format:** JPEG, JPG, PNG, BMP (GIF images aren't permitted)

- **Color Format:** RGB

- **File Name:** We recommend that you use the 14-digit GTIN in the file name

- **File Size:** 5MB or less

- **Bit Depth:** 8 bits per pixel

- **Pixel Dimensions:** 2200px x 2200px

- **Minimum Pixel Dimensions for Zoom:** 1500px x 1500px

- **Swatch Pixel Dimensions:** 100px x 100px

- **Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (Square)

- **Background:** Seamless white (255/255/255 RGB)


### Image URL requirements

Walmart offers image hosting so that you don't need your own external image space. If you're using Walmart's hosting service, there are URL limitations. Review the table below for supported and unsupported URL formats.

| --- | --- |

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Are there specific image requirements for each product category?

Yes. Depending on your product, you may have additional requirements to follow. For more information, refer to [_Content Standards: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Content-standards:-Overview).

* * *

What happens if my images don't follow all requirements?

Noncompliance may result in products being unpublished.

* * *

Can I use stock photos?

No, stock photos are not allowed. You must use actual product images that accurately reflect the product being sold on Walmart Marketplace.

* * *

I am participating in the Walmart Pre-Owned Program; do I need to include images in my product listings?

Yes. Product images are required for the following item conditions to help set the customer’s expectations on the items they’ll receive: **Remanufactured**, **Pre-Owned: Like New**, **Pre-Owned: Good**, **Pre-Owned: Fair** and **New with Defects.**

* * *

I submitted an image. Why isn’t it showing on Walmart.com?

Because there may be multiple sellers providing content for the same item, you may not always be able to define which content will be displayed on Walmart.com. Walmart will determine the best available content for items unless it's provided by a brand owner or authorized reseller. Items that can't be updated will appear locked during the updating process. Learn more in [_How to update content: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/How-to-update-content:-Overview).

* * *

I’m not sure if my image URL domain is whitelisted by Walmart. How can I confirm?

If you work with an approved Solution Provider, reach out to them. If not, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support _._

* * *

My URL seems to meet the requirements, but it’s not functioning properly. What should I do?

If you work with an approved Solution Provider, reach out to them. If not, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support _._

* * *

I am having trouble uploading my image. Who can I contact for help?

If you work with an approved [Solution Provider](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/Choose-a-solution-provider), reach out to them. If not, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

My Amazon URLs failed the HTTP HEAD call. What should I do?

If you encounter this problem, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support _._

* * *

What should I do if my image URLs are not on one of the supported ports?

Contact your content service provider and ask them to host your images on a different port.

* * *

Can I add images that include descriptions of my item's condition?

No. Images can’t contain descriptions of the item’s condition (e.g., good, grade A, scratched, refurbished, etc.).
