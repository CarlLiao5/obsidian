Valid tracking number policy

Last updated on Feb 10, 2026

Reading time: 3 min

* * *

In this guide:

* * *

Overview

You’re required to provide valid tracking information to Walmart as soon as you’ve shipped a Marketplace order and marked the order as _Shipped_ in Seller Center or with Walmart APIs. In this guide, you’ll learn more information about our tracking policy.

## What is the policy?

Prior to shipping the item, you’ll need to give your shipping carrier authorization to provide shipment tracking data to Walmart. You must provide valid tracking information to Walmart for at least 99% of your shipments. Per our [Seller Performance Standards](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Seller-performance-standards), if you provide invalid tracking or fail to meet the 99% standard, it may result in selling restrictions, suspension or termination.

## Additional guidelines

Tracking numbers must meet the following requirements:

- Correct formatting.
- No special characters (e.g., ~ \` ! @ # $ % ^ & \* ( ) \_ - + = { \[ } \] \| \ : ; " ' < , > . ? /).

- Has at least one valid carrier scan on record.
- It can't be the same as the purchase order number or sales order number.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How can I ensure I provide a valid tracking number?

Verify the accuracy of the carrier’s name and confirm that the number field contains a tracking ID.

* * *

What should I do if my tracking number is invalid?

If you received an error after adding a tracking number, this means the tracking number doesn't match the selected carrier or the required format. To fix the issue, check for typos and make sure the correct carrier is selected. If you have questions, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

How can I ship an order in multiple boxes?

To ship an order in multiple boxes, select the [split shipments](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Order%20status/Split-customer-orders-in-Seller-Center) option on the **Orders** page in Seller Center.

* * *

When should I update the purchase order (PO) lines?

You should change the status of PO lines to _Shipped_ once the package is in the carrier’s possession. If you relabel a package with a new tracking number, you must inform us of the new tracking number. Double-check the tracking number before you update the order. You must also correctly configure shipping settings for less-than-truckload (LTL) products.

* * *

Can I ship an order with a carrier that’s not a Walmart-approved carrier?

No. Shipping a Walmart order using a non-approved carrier is not recommended and may result in suppression, suspension or termination because the tracking information cannot be validated by Walmart. To avoid non-compliance, ensure that you ship with a Walmart-approved [parcel](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-methods:-parcel-shipping-carriers) or [freight](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-methods:-freight-shipping-carriers) carrier.

* * *

How do I edit a tracking number after I’ve submitted it to Walmart?

After updating the order status to **Shipped**, you can edit the tracking number for up to 24 hours or until the carrier's first touch scan is received in the Walmart system, whichever is earlier. For more information, review [_Update tracking numbers in Seller Center_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Order%20management/Order%20status/Update-tracking-numbers-in-Seller-Center).

* * *

Can I use stamps or prepaid envelopes to ship orders?

No. All orders must have a valid tracking number.
