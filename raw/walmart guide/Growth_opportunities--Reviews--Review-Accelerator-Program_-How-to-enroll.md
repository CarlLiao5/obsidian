Review Accelerator: Post-Purchase Reviews program

Last updated on Mar 2, 2026

Reading time: 3 min

* * *

Overview

The Post-Purchase Reviews program is a [Review Accelerator](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Reviews/review-accelerator-overview) solution that allows you to incentivize customers with a digital reward in exchange for their feedback after they’ve purchased your products. In this guide, you’ll learn how to manage item enrollment.

## Enroll items

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to the [**Review Accelerator**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/review-accelerator) page under the **Growth** section inSeller Center. Select _Enroll items_ and choose the **Post-Purchase Reviews** option to begin. The eligible items list is refreshed by Wednesday of each week. Items are displayed in descending order, with those having a greater chance of conversion appearing at the top.

Notes:

Only one seller can enroll an item at a time. If you can’t find a particular item on your eligible item list, it might already be enrolled by another seller or may fail to qualify due to eligibility criteria.

### Step 2 — Select items and customize target reviews and incentive amounts

On the nextpage, select the checkbox next to individual items to enroll or upload in bulk by choosing the _upload items via spreadsheet_ button. You can edit **Target reviews** by selecting a new target from the drop-down options and update your incentive dollar amount from the **Incentive amount** column. Select _Add # items_ to confirm enrollment.

Notes:

The minimum allowed incentive amount per review is $5. The maximum amount is the lesser of 25% of the item price or $25.

### Step 3 — Confirm and enroll items

Review your selected items, target reviews and incentive amounts on the **Review & Submit** page. Then, select _Submit enrollment_ to complete enrollment.

Participating items are automatically removed from the program once they have received the target number of incentivized reviews. However, customers can continue to submit additional reviews up to 48 hours after items are unenrolled.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What happens to my existing list of enrolled items in Review Accelerator?

Items enrolled in Review Accelerator prior to the name change will remain enrolled, but they will now be under the Post-Purchase Reviews program.

* * *

How can I view my Post-Purchase Reviews charges?

You can view charges from the **Post-Purchase Reviews** dashboard on the [**Review Accelerator**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/review-accelerator) page. Next to the search field, select the _Download_ button to download a report of your Post-Purchase Reviews data. The data includes information about charges, pending charges, settlement dates and credits such as Pro Seller credits.

* * *

I want to remove a review from my listing. What should I do?

Walmart won’t modify or remove a review unless it meets the [guidelines for removal](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Troubleshooting/Request-to-remove-a-review).

* * *

Is each eligible item enrolled into the Review Accelerator program charged a specific fee?

Yes, the fee is based on cost per customer review. You’ll find the cost per review for each item in the item’s **Cost per review** column during the enrollment process.

* * *

What other fees will I be charged for?

You’ll get charged for collected reviews up to the amount you’ve set in the **Target reviews** column. Regardless of the rating, all customer reviews that have passed moderation will be posted on the item page and can be identified with an incentivized review badge. You’re only charged for the targeted number of reviews that pass moderation successfully and get posted on Walmart.com. You won’t be charged for additional reviews.

* * *

Does Walmart verify the authenticity of reviews?

Yes. All incentivized reviews are from verified purchasers, where Walmart has confirmed that a Walmart customer bought the participating item. Also, customers won’t know about the incentive before making a purchase.

* * *

Will each individual variant be enrolled in the Post-Purchase Reviews program?

If at least one of the variants qualifies for the Post-Purchase Reviews program, the base item ID will appear as eligible. Since all variant groups share reviews, enrolling the base variant will enroll all variants into the program. Reviews collected from individual variants will be added to the collective number of reviews and counted toward the program’s eligibility requirements.

* * *

Do I have to be a Seller Center user to enroll items into the program?

No. If you prefer to use APIs, visit the [Walmart Developer Portal](https://developer.walmart.com/us-marketplace/docs/introduction-to-marketplace-apis) for the Post-Purchase Reviews APIs. You must first accept the terms and conditions in Seller Center if you’d like to use the Post-Purchase Reviews APIs.

* * *

How do I remove enrolled items?

From the **Post Purchase Reviews** dashboard, select the checkbox next to the item and choose _Unenroll_. Once unenrolled, customers will no longer receive an incentive when they review the item, but the item will remain visible in your dashboard history.

* * *

How do I view items with completed targeted reviews?

You can view enrolled, completed and unenrolled items from the **Post-Purchase Reviews** dashboard. Filter the **Status** column to display completed items that have received the target number of incentivized reviews. These items will be automatically removed from the program and can’t be re-enrolled.

* * *

Why do I see incentivized reviews on my item when I didn’t enroll the item in the Post-Purchase Reviews program?

Your item may also be sold by Walmart or other sellers who may have enrolled it in the Post-Purchase Reviews program to collect customer feedback. Since reviews are associated with the item rather than a specific seller or offer, incentivized reviews may appear even if you didn’t enroll the item into the Post-Purchase Reviews program.

* * *
