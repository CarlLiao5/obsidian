Delete items in bulk in Seller Center

Last updated on Jan 14, 2026

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

Overview

If multiple items are outdated, consider removing them in bulk to keep your Marketplace catalog up to date. In this guide, you’ll learn how to permanently delete items in bulk using the spreadsheet.

Notes:

You must have [admin level](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Account%20settings/Update-user-information) access in Seller Center to delete an item from your catalog. If you don't have admin level access, reach out to your account administrator for help. Seller Support won’t process bulk delete requests.

## Delete items in bulk

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center. Select _Update items_, then choose _Update items with file._

### Step 2 – Download the spreadsheet

Select _Bulk SKU deletion_ to download the spreadsheet. Once you open the downloaded file, add the SKUs you wish to delete. You can add up to 10,000 SKUs per request.

![2511_Delete-items-in-bulk.gif](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt941b9206b24053a8/690bcff4e2f52207f6f02179/2511_Delete-items-in-bulk.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

You can’t delete Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) items, drafts, items with an **Error** status or items that are still processing.

### Step 3 – Upload a spreadsheet

Once you’ve added the SKUs you wish to delete to the spreadsheet, save the file and re-upload it to the **Update items with file** page in Seller Center. Under **Step 2**, select _Bulk SKU deletion_ from the list, and choose _Browse_ to add the file from your device. The process may take up to 72 hours to complete.

You can track the status of your deletion request from the [**Analytics**](https://seller.walmart.com/analytics/reports) page in Seller Center. Under **Report type**, choose _Delete SKU repor_ t. Reports refresh every 24 hours.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Can I delete items directly from my catalog?

You can delete up to 100 SKUs in bulk [from your **Catalog**](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Item%20management/Delete-an-item-in-Seller-Center), and may submit up to 10 requests per hour .

* * *

Why am I receiving an upload error message?

You may only upload one excel file per hour. Attempting to upload more than one may result in an error.
