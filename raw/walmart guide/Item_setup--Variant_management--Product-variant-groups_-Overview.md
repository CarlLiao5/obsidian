Product variant groups: Overview

Last updated on Jul 1, 2025

Reading time: 1 min

* * *

In this guide:

* * *

Overview

A product variant group is a group of individual items that differ by attributes such as a T-shirt that comes in different sizes and colors. You can create and manage variant groups in [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) or using [Walmart APIs](https://developer.walmart.com/doc/us/mp/us-mp-items/). In this guide, you'll learn how to manage product variant groups in Seller Center.

## How does it work?

| Methods | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Create a new variant group** | You can create a new variant group in one of three ways: using your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) page, uploading a template in Seller Center or via APIs. Visit [_Create a variant group in Seller Center_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Variant%20management/Create-a-variant-group:-Items-dashboard) and [_Create a variant group: Full Setup_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Variant%20management/Create-a-variant-group:-Full-Setup-Template) for details |
| **Manage a variant group** | Once you've created a variant group, you can edit, add or remove items from the group, combine it with another group, separate it into two groups or add new variant attributes to the group. Visit [_Manage a variant group_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Variant%20management/Manage-a-variant-group) to learn more. |
| **Troubleshoot product variant group issues** | When making changes to a variant group, you may need to address an error before the changes can be saved. Visit [_Troubleshoot product variant errors_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Variant%20management/Troubleshoot-product-variant-errors) to learn what different errors mean and how to solve them. |

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Why are product variant groups important for my listings?

Product variants give customers more options to choose from and help with searchability, so it’s important to create and manage them properly.
