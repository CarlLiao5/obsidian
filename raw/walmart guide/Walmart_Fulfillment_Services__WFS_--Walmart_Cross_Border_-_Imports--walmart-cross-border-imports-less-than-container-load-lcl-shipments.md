# What is LCL?

Less than container load (LCL) shipments are an ocean transportation solution for sellers shipping a quantity of cargo smaller than what would fit in a 20’ container. The seller (or their representative Shipper) delivers cargo to an origin consolidation warehouse, also known as a container freight station or CFS. The origin warehouse team measures and weighs the cargo, then loads it into a container with other sellers’ cargo to ship to a Walmart fulfillment center together.

# How it works

WCB’s LCL program currently supports shipping from the following ports in China: Ningbo, Shanghai, Yantian, and Xiamen, and from Nhava Sheva and Tuticorin in India. More ports will be added in the future for all sellers.

Here’s how to ship LCL with Walmart Imports:

## 1 - Enroll in Walmart Cross Border: Imports

Enrollment is the same process whether you will be sending LCL or FCL shipments. Review all the steps in this [Walmart Cross Border: Imports - How to enroll](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-cross-border-imports-enrollment-overview) guide.

## 2 - Walmart's designated customs broker

You may choose to use your own customs broker, but customs broker service fees are included for every LCL shipment when using Walmart’s designated broker. Using Walmart’s designated broker allows you to get updates on customs clearance status right in Seller Center.

## 3 - Estimate price and book a shipment

Most of the process for getting a price estimate and adding items to your inbound order is the same for LCL and FCL shipments. But there are a few differences when shipping LCL vs. FCL.

We suggest first reviewing the general process for FCL shipments in the [Walmart Cross Border: Imports - Estimate pricing and book a shipment](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart-Cross-Border-Imports-Estimate-pricing-and-book-a-shipment) guide if you haven’t already. Then, review what’s different when shipping LCL.

### **LCL-specific details:**

- **Booking timeline**

  - We recommend that you **book at least 7 calendar days before cargo drop-off date.**

- **Estimated price**


  - For LCL shipments your estimate for a shipment is based on the weight or volume of the cargo. Make sure you add the most accurate weight and volume you can when booking your shipment in Seller Center. The actual final cost will be provided when your inventory is weighed and measured at the origin consolidation warehouse, also known as a container freight station (CFS), before shipping.

- **Charge calculation**


  - **Minimum charge:** The minimum charge is 1 cubic meter (cbm).

  - **Charge rate calculation:** Your LCL charge is based on whichever is greater, weight or volume. This is shown as "Weight or measure" on your Negotiated Rate Arrangement (NRA). Remember that your final cost is determined after the origin warehouse confirms your cargo’s weight and volume. If your cargo’s **confirmed** weight or volume differs from the amount provided during booking by more or less than the minimum charge unit, your cargo may be repriced using the other measurement.

- **Communication**


  - You will receive the same emails and notifications as you would with FCL.  Additionally, you will receive for LCL:


    - An email for cargo drop-off appointment coordination.

    - An email **from the origin consolidation warehouse,** if the measurements differ from what you provided during booking, to confirm the cargo weight and volume measurements. You must confirm these weight and volume measurements via email reply before we load your cargo.

## 5 - Prepare your shipment

Learn how to prepare your inventory for shipment in the [Walmart Cross Border: Imports - Prepare an international inbound shipment](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart-Cross-Border-Imports-Prepare-an-international-inbound-shipment) guide.

### **What’s different for LCL:**

- Hazmat, regulated goods and dangerous goods are not currently permitted to ship via LCL. Seller Center will not allow you to add hazmat or regulated items to an LCL shipment.
- The maximum weight and volume for LCL shipments is 20,000 kg and 55 cbm. However, if your inventory is likely to fill more than half the container, FCL may offer a better rate.
- Regardless of whether you are using Walmart’s designated broker or your own broker, you must upload your Commercial Invoice (CI) in Seller Center for any LCL booking within 24 hours of cargo drop-off at the origin consolidation warehouse.

  - We collect the CI on behalf of our destination operator in case they must transport an LCL container on a bonded truck to a bonded warehouse for deconsolidation.
  - To upload, go to the **Shipments** page and select the **International** tab to find your LCL booking. Select the three dots, then select **Documents,** then upload the CI. Not providing this document on time could result in shipment delays and additional costs.

## 6 - Deliver your cargo

FCL shippers deliver containers directly to the port, but the cargo delivery process is different for an LCL shipment.

**Origin Pickup** **:** For Origin Pickup, we now offer service from Yantian, China for your LCL shipments. This allows cargo to be collected directly from your supplier. To learn more, see the Walmart Cross Border: Imports - [Origin Pickup Service guide](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Walmart%20Cross%20Border%20-%20Imports/walmart-cross-border-imports-origin-pickup-services).

### **LCL-specific delivery process:**

1. **Deliver to CFS:** The shipper takes the cargo to the origin consolidation warehouse, also known as a container freight station (CFS).


1. Each CFS has its own drop-off rules. Log in to Marketplace Learn to view the [Walmart Cross Border: Imports - Origin delivery overview guide](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-cross-border-imports-lcl-origin-delivery).

3. **Weigh and measure:** The origin team weighs and measures the cargo.


1. You’ll get an email from the origin agent if there are any differences beyond the minimum charge unit between the weight or volume provided at booking and the measured weight or volume.


5. **Pack and deliver:** The CFS team packs your goods into the container and delivers it to the port.

6. **Transit and delivery:** After the container is at the port, the transit, tracking, destination activity, WFS receiving, and billing are the same as FCL.


1. Estimated delivery time for China - 39 days total


      1. 15 days from booking date to departure from origin

      2. 16-18 days on the water

      3. 7 days from the port of discharge to arrival at a WFS fulfillment center


3. Estimated delivery time for India - 51 to 53 days total

Keep in mind that this estimate depends on origin port, destination, mode of transit and other factors. We provide estimated delivery dates for each shipment during the booking process.

Notes:

Cargo may occasionally arrive at the origin warehouse with potential damages observed by the origin team. If you're given the option to continue with the shipment and choose to do so, your bill of lading will be updated to clarify that Walmart won't be held liable for any damages, or for any claims, losses, or liabilities arising from transporting or handling damaged cargo.

## 7 - Edit or cancel your shipment

After submitting your inbound order, you will receive a confirmed cargo drop-off range based on your requested cargo drop-off date.

- You may edit your involved parties within 24 hours of booking submission.


- You may edit your items to send or cargo details until 1 day before your cargo drop-off date.


If you need to edit or cancel your shipment after the cargo drop-off date, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support. Rather than describing your issue, select _Issue categories_, then select these links: _Walmart Cross Border_ \> _Walmart Imports_. After selecting your specific issue category from the Walmart Imports list, select _Get in touch with us_.

# Reasons to use LCL shipments

- Test a new product. Start with a smaller order to gauge customer interest.


- Ship a small amount of a different size item than you normally ship. For example, you could ship bed frames with FCL but want to include several boxes of bedsheets as an LCL shipment.

- Save money over typical air freight costs.


## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Will the items from a single LCL inbound shipment be split into different containers?

No, the items will not be divided into different containers.

* * *

How does Walmart calculate estimated pricing and confirm an NRA?

The estimated price you see is based on your cargo arriving at the origin warehouse (Container Freight Station – CFS) prior to NRA expiration. You must deliver your cargo within the valid range, or you'll be asked to approve a new NRA valid for the date you deliver.

* * *

How will my delivery be handled if my LCL shipment requires more than one truck to deliver the items to the origin warehouse (Container Freight Station – CFS)?

All trucks delivering a shipment must arrive at the origin warehouse (Container Freight Station – CFS) at the same time. At the time the booking is confirmed, the origin agent will provide you with a CFS order and order number for the shipment. The CFS order contains information about the shipment. Use the order number when making a delivery appointment with the origin warehouse (Container Freight Station – CFS) and let them know how many trucks will be delivering your order.
