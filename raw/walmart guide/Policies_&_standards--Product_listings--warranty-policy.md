Warranty Policy

Last updated on Dec 10, 2025

Reading time: 4 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/warranty-policy#overview)
- [What is the policy?](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/warranty-policy#what)
- [Additional guidelines](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/warranty-policy#additional)
- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/warranty-policy#frequently-asked-questions)

Overview

If you want to offer an item in new condition that isn’t being offered with a standard manufacturer warranty, you’ll need to set the item up as its own listing. In this guide, you’ll learn about warranty requirements for listings.

## What is the policy?

Items that come with a manufacturer’s warranty must either include the manufacturer warranty in the offer or be listed separately with a clear disclosure stating otherwise.

Pro tip

Prior to creating a new warranty version item, search the catalog for existing items to avoid creating a [duplicate listing](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/duplicate-listings-policy).

## Additional guidelines

You’re required to disclose the non-manufacturer warranty details in the product title, description and warranty details. Use the table below for examples of how to do so.

Notes:

Any non-compliance may result in performance actions ranging from unpublishing listings, temporary suspension, return to vendor of Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) inventory at the seller’s expense or termination of your Marketplace account.

| Attribute | Requirement for items sold without manufacturer warranty |
| --- | --- |
| **Title** | The title must include the following disclaimer at the end of the title:  <br>- Sold without manufacturer warranty <br>  <br>E.g., **Nintendo Switch Blue & red Joy-con (Sold without manufacturer warranty)** |
| **Description** | The item’s product details, including a short or long description, must include the following statement:  <br>- This product is sold without manufacturer warranty. |
| **Warranty information** | The item’s warranty information section must include the following statement: <br>- This product is sold without manufacturer warranty. |

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What if my item doesn’t include a valid manufacturer warranty?

If your item doesn’t include a manufacturer warranty, you must [set up your item](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/Item-setup-methods:-Overview) as a no manufacturer warranty item. Search the Walmart catalog for the no manufacturer warranty version. If no results exist in the catalog, complete a full item setup using a [UPC exemption](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Choose-a-product-identifier) if required to avoid GTIN/UPC mismatch. If you still have questions, select the _Help_ button in the Seller Center menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

What do I do if my listing is unpublished?

Most listing issues can be resolved from the [**Unpublished Items**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/unpublished-items) dashboard. If your item is flagged with an error and you’d like to republish the listing, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

How can I avoid creating a warranty version mismatch?

When creating a new warranty version item, search the Walmart catalog for the product ID (UPC, GTIN, or ISBN) and confirm that the recommended item has the correct warranty details. If only a manufacturer warranty version is recommended based on item ID or if a no manufacturer warranty version doesn't exist in the Walmart catalog, complete a [full item setup](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/Item-setup-methods:-Overview) for the no manufacturer warranty version by providing a seller purchased GTIN or requesting a Walmart provided custom GTIN.

* * *

What do I do if I have WFS inventory tied to the manufacturer warranty listing?

If you’ve combined supply among different versions located in Walmart’s WFS facilities, you must discontinue fulfillment on those orders or inbound comingled inventory and unpublish all offers. Download and fill out the [inventory transfer template](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt0cd7ca164fabcc64/WFS_Inventory_Transfer_Request.xlsx?branch=test2) to relabel existing inventory, then select the _Help button_ in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.
