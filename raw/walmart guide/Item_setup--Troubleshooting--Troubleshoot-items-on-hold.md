Troubleshoot items on hold

Last updated on Apr 2, 2026

Reading time: 1 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Troubleshooting/Troubleshoot-items-on-hold#overview)
- [Troubleshoot items pending review](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Troubleshooting/Troubleshoot-items-on-hold#troubleshoot)
- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Troubleshooting/Troubleshoot-items-on-hold#frequently-asked-questions)

* * *

Overview

Walmart may place a hold on the setup and sale of items that don’t meet the [_Prohibited Products Policy_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Prohibited%20products%20&%20brands/Prohibited-products-policy:-overview) _._ In this guide, you’ll learn how to use the [**Pending Review**](https://seller.walmart.com/items-and-inventory/items-on-hold) dashboard in Seller Center to review items that have been put on hold.

## Troubleshoot items pending review

The **Pending Review** dashboard is organized into three tabs, each representing a category of reasons why your item is on hold: WFS Compliance, Trust and Safety, and Potential Duplicates.

### WFS Compliance

Items that are fulfilled by Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) have different requirements than seller-fulfilled. If your WFS item contains a chemical, aerosol or battery, it may be put on hold for a compliance review for up to three business days.

**What should I do?**

Learn more about the review process of hazardous materials [here](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup) _._

### Trust and Safety

#### Restricted

If an item is listed as **Restricted**, Walmart has determined that it doesn’t comply with our standards and can’t be set up on Walmart Marketplace.

**What should I do?**

If you’re eligible to appeal the decision, select the _Appeal_ button and follow the steps to submit your appeal.

If your item has been restricted and you’re unable to appeal or the appeal is denied, learn more about the [_Prohibited Products Policy_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Prohibited%20products%20&%20brands/Prohibited-products-policy:-overview) to help prevent future issues. If you believe the item should not be restricted, select the _Help_ button in the Seller Center menu bar to contact Support.

#### In Review

If an item is listed as **In Review**, Walmart is reviewing the item, or the item is getting a hazardous materials assessment. It will be approved or listed as **Restricted** upon completion.

**What should I do?**

Once approved, the item moves from the **Pending Review** dashboard and completes the item setup process. Check for the item to appear in your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items).

### Potential duplicates

If a Marketplace item is offered by multiple sellers and shares the same product identifier (or product ID), it may be listed here as a potential duplicate.

**What should I do?**

Review your items for [potential duplicates](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/duplicate-listings-policy?locale=en-US) to ensure no items are unpublished.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

I also see a tab for WFS Compliance in my dashboard. What does that mean?

If you use Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), you’ll also see a tab for **WFS Compliance**. Use the WFS tab to monitor items fulfilled through Walmart, and the **Trust And Safety** tab to monitor self-fulfilled items.
