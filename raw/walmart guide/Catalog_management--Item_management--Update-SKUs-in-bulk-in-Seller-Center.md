Update SKU IDs in bulk in Seller Center

Last updated on Mar 16, 2026

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

Overview

When you set up your items on Walmart Marketplace, each item’s product identifier is associated with a single SKU ID. In this guide, you’ll learn how to update the SKU IDs for several items at once using the full item setup spreadsheet.

Notes:

You must select the _Add items_ button to download and complete all the attribute requirements in the template to update your SKUs.

## Update SKU IDs

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center and select the _Add items_ button. Select the _Upload in bulk_ link located under **Add thousands of items using a spreadsheet**. On the next page, navigate to the **New items** section and select the _Bulk spreadsheet_ button.  Select _Seller fulfillment_ as the preferred fulfillment type, then choose _Next_.

Notes:

You can’t change SKUs for items fulfilled through Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS). They’re unique to the items and can't be replaced or reused. This avoids confusion and item mix-ups at fulfillment centers.

### Step 2 — Build spreadsheet

Select _browse product type categories_ to search product types of item SKU IDs you want to update, then choose _Next._ Choose up to 10 product types and select _Next._ Finally, review the summary and select the _Build spreadsheet_ button to download the spreadsheet.

In the template, update your SKU IDs and complete the required fields and item attributes, including the column labeled **SKU Update** in the template’s  **Optional** section. You must choose _Yes_ from the dropdown menu for the SKUs to successfully update.

![2412_7896_Update-skus-bulk-SC_GIF.gif](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltbd83f8c8010fd750/6772f56a2fdcc3afe00beeef/2412_7896_Update-skus-bulk-SC_GIF.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 3 — Upload the completed template

To upload your completed spreadsheet, return to the same window and select the _Upload spreadsheet_ button _._ If the previous window closed, navigate to your Catalog and select  _Add items_. Then, choose the _Upload in bulk_ link located under **Add thousands of items using a spreadsheet**.

Drag and drop your file onto the browser window under **Upload** or select the _browse_ link to locate the file on your computer.  Select _Next_ to upload the spreadsheet. Updates can appear as quickly as 15 minutes or can take up to four hours.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt18933d8683f2fc61/6997434101173b05446a28cb/2602_Update-SKUs-bulk-SC_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How can I check the status of my SKU update?

You can track progress in the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed?feedsFor=uploads).

* * *

Can I update SKUs using API?

Yes. To update a SKU using API, refer to the steps listed under **Maintain an item** in the [API documentation](https://developer.walmart.com/doc/us/mp/us-mp-items/#1249) of the Walmart Developer Portal.
