Multichannel Solutions: Add sales channels

Overview

A **sales channel** is any online marketplace or website where you sell items. For example, you might create a channel for your online storefront, or for other channels like Amazon, eBay, Temu or Shein. With Walmart Multichannel Solutions, we’ll fulfill eligible orders from any eCommerce site.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt896713e327ee90bc/66c3873354be6cf971184740/Multichannel-Onboarding_Infographic.jpg?branch=us_mplearn)

## Add sales channels

Here's how to create a sales channel:

1. Log into Seller Center and go to the [**Multichannel settings**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/multichannel-setup) page.

2. Select **Add channel**.

3. From the dropdown, choose an existing third-party channel or create your own. (Don't forget to give your sales channels a unique name, so you can tell each channel apart.)

4. Choose a ship-from address. This is where we’ll send customer returns and undeliverable shipments.

5. Choose your shipment carrier. Some channels prefer specific carriers, and we'll highlight our recommendation based on the channel. You are welcome to switch to any package you like.

    -Package A includes all carriers: FedEx, UPS, USPS, LaserShip, and OnTrac.

    -Package B includes UPS, LaserShip, and OnTrac. It will exclude Fedex and USPS.

    -Package C includes Fedex, UPS, and USPS. It will exclude LaserShip and OnTrac.

**NOTE: Restricting Carriers will limit coverage area and may affect shipping SLAs.**

6. Add the sales channel. Create as many channels as you need.


![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt27ad9bbde6d89fdd/68a8def3edefc3901f4f02c5/MCS_Choose_Carrier_3.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

We generate a unique sales channel ID to tell each channel apart during fulfillment. You’ll use this sales channel ID when you create customer orders. You can always find sales channel ID on the [**Sales channels**](https://seller.walmart.com/wfs/multi-channel-solutions/sales-channels) page.

#### Transactional billing

When you add or update your sales channels from the multichannel settings page, eligible sellers can also switch from default, invoice-based billing to transactional billing. With this option, you pay per order(s), giving you more flexibility and the ability to lower your overall balance. In some cases, Walmart may switch sellers to this billing type to resolve high balances.

Notes:

Before you add a sales channel, you first need to have a billing method on file for all related fees. Refer to the Walmart Multichannel Solutions [eligibility checklist](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Walmart%20Multichannel%20Solutions/walmart-multichannel-solutions#eligibility) for more details.

Pro tip

You must create a sales channel before placing customer orders.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Are there any eCommerce channels you don’t support?

We can fulfill to any sales channel, as long as it’s a WFS-eligible item within the 50 US states and Puerto Rico.
