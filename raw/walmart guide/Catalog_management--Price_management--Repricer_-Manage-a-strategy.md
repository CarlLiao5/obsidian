Repricer: Manage a strategy

Last updated on Aug 10, 2025

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

Overview

Once you enable the Repricer, you can go back and adjust the settings of your strategy. If you need to make updates to your strategy, follow the steps below.

## Manage a strategy

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to the **Pricing** section in Seller Center and choose [_Automate pricing_](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/automate-pricing) _._ From here, you can manage the items assigned to a strategy.

### Step 2 – Locate the item

Locate the item you’d like to manage the strategy for.

### Step 3 – Manage your strategy

To edit the strategy, select the three dots under the **Action** column and choose _Edit._  You can pause a strategy, delete a strategy, or manage individual items.

- **Edit a strategy:** You can edit the strategy name, amount or configuration. If you’d like, you can check the box marked **Use your last submitted price if there’s no comparable price found** or **Include items on promotion** **.** Then, make your changes and select _Save changes._


Notes:

The maximum percentage you can enter is 99%. For the **Meet or Beat External Price** strategy, you may enter a minimum value of 0% or $0.00 if you want to match prices on other websites.

- **Pause a strategy:** Choose _Pause strategy_. Then, select _Pause strategy_ to confirm. The Repricer will be suspended until you resume activity. If you wish to resume a strategy, simply select the horizontal dots for that item again and choose _Resume strategy._
- **Remove items from a strategy:** To remove SKUs from a strategy, select _Manage items,_ then on the next page, choose _Bulk update_. Download the Repricer template. In the template, select the first cell under the **Repricer** column. From the dropdown menu, choose _No Strategy/Delete_ _from Repricer._ Upload the template onto the field in Seller Center and select _Submit_.
- **Delete a strategy**: To delete a strategy, choose the three dots and select _Delete strategy._

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What happens when I delete a strategy?

If you choose to delete a strategy, all items assigned to that strategy will be removed from the Repricer. The strategy will be deleted 15 minutes after processing the request. Consider pausing the strategy as an alternative if you want to revisit the criteria or item assignments.

* * *

What does the warning sign mean for my active strategies?

This means that one or more items require your attention. Select _Active_ to view the error then choose _Manage strategies_ to address it.

* * *

Will the Repricer change my shipping price?

No. The Repricer only adjusts the item price and will never change your shipping price.

* * *

For the Buy Box strategy rule, what happens if I check the box “Match the external price when it's lower than the Buy Box price after applying your beat by amount.”

Check this box if you’d like the Repricer to adjust enrolled items to match the external price while beating the Buy Box target price by your beat by amount. Checking this box ensures that the Repricer tool can adjust your items to beat the Buy Box target by your beat by amount or match the external price target (whichever is lower).

* * *

Will the Repricer update my promotional price?

The Repricer will only adjust the promotional offer if the “Reprice items on promotion”checkbox is selected and there’s an external price that’s lower than the promotional offer. As a reminder, the Repricer will never adjust the price below the minimum price set by you. If you have an existing strategy enabled for SKUs that are actively running a promotion, you need to edit the existing strategy and select the _Reprice items on promotion_ checkbox to reprice those items.
