Packaging policy

Overview

You’re required to comply with Walmart Marketplace packaging policies as described below. Following these requirements helps build customer trust and helps to provide a consistent experience for Walmart customers.

## What is the policy?

Walmart Marketplace orders or packaging may not reference any other marketplace business or fulfillment provider. Under the [_Seller Performance Standards_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Seller-performance-standards), you may not purchase products from another retailer and have the order shipped directly to a Walmart customer. Noncompliance with these policies and the policies below may result in warnings, selling restrictions, suspension or even termination.

| **Allowed** | **Prohibited** |
| --- | --- |
| - Shipping in a nonbranded box with a neutral color. <br>  <br>- Multi Channel Fulfillment, as long as you ship packages in neutral packaging using unbranded delivery vehicles, which means neither can display any logos, trademarks or branding of the other retailer.<br>- Drop-shipping your orders (for DSVs) using manufacturer boxes or the manufacturer of the item. <br>  <br>- Unbranded packing slips as a convenience to the customer. <br>  <br>- Printing out packing slips through the **Order Management** section in Seller Center. <br>  <br>- Listing your name and address on the box. | - Seller-branded boxes.<br>- Other retailers’ packaging.<br>- Promotional material from your company or any other entity.<br>- Original supplier invoices that display your costs.<br>- Messaging that violates the [Seller Code of Conduct](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Marketplace-Seller-Code-of-Conduct) or other Marketplace policies. |

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Can I include a shipping invoice in my Walmart Marketplace orders?

Yes. However, invoices from other retailers’ packaging are prohibited.

* * *

Can I purchase the items in my Walmart Marketplace order from another retailer, provide that retailer with the customer details and have them ship the order?

No.

* * *

What is the standard for item packaging for refurbished products?

Refurbished packaging should appear like new. Item boxes should not be torn, damaged or scratched.

* * *

Can I use Amazon Multi Channel Fulfillment (MCF)?

Yes. When using Amazon MCF, you must ship the items in neutral packaging and block Amazon Logistics as a carrier to ensure orders aren’t delivered using Amazon-branded vehicles and to comply with Walmart’s [shipping and fulfillment policy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-and-fulfillment-policy).
