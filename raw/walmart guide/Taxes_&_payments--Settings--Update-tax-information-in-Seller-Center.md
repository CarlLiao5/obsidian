Update business information in Seller Center

Last updated on Mar 8, 2026

Reading time: 3 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Settings/Update-tax-information-in-Seller-Center#overview)

- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Settings/Update-tax-information-in-Seller-Center#frequently-asked-questions)

* * *

Overview

In this guide, you’ll learn how to make updates to your tax ID, Employer Identification Number (EIN), legal business name or business address, if needed. You can only make these updates if you have an admin access to Seller Center.

## Update your information

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to the [**Business Information**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/partner-profile/tax-profile) page in Seller Center and review each field for accuracy.

### Step 2 – Update info

To add details or make changes, select the _Edit Business details_ link or the _Edit market details_ link for your market and fill out the required fields, including your legal business name, address and tax ID.

Notes:

You can’t edit your business information if you submitted changes recently and they’re still under review. You can’t proceed with your updates until you provide the required documentation. Failure to provide the correct information may delay your request.

### Step 3 – Submit and review

Some updates require you to provide additional details, including a reason for the change and supporting documentation. Once you’ve provided these details, select the _Submit changes_ button. Walmart reviews all business information changes and may require detailed review in accordance with our verification and compliance procedures.

During the review process, you’ll get a message that we’re still reviewing a previous update. You can’t update your business information until the review is complete. If your submission was approved, the submitted changes are effective immediately and you don’t have to take any further action.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Which tax classification should I choose?

[The tax classification](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Onboarding/Tax-classifications-and-documentation) field is automatically populated based on the information you provided.

* * *

What do I do if my submitted changes are denied?

If Walmart denies your submission, the submitted changes won’t go into effect and you’ll need to select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support. We'll email you when the review process is complete and you can make additional edits in these fields, if necessary.

* * *

How long does the review process take?

The review process may take five to seven business days. The submitted changes are effective immediately after approval and you don’t have to take further action.

* * *

I’m an international seller. How do I update my tax ID and business information?

If you’re an international seller, you can update your tax ID and business information in Seller Center by following the instructions in this guide.
