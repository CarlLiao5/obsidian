Overview

Walmart Marketplace Capital provides merchant cash advances to help eligible businesses expand their Marketplace operations. The amount offered is based on your Marketplace sales and is available to U.S.-based sellers only. In this guide, you’ll learn about the available programs under Walmart Marketplace Capital and how they work.

## How does it work?

You can get merchant cash advances and flexible financing through three Walmart Marketplace Capital programs. Keep reading to learn about their benefits.

| Cash Advance Program | Benefits |
| --- | --- |
| **Capital by Walmart** | - One flat fee with no operational overheads<br>- Repayment deductions based on your future sales<br>- Access to funding from multiple channels via invitation or by request, as available |
| **Capital by Parafin** | - One capital fee<br>- No interest or late fees<br>- Payments based on a fixed percentage of your sales<br>- Repayment periods of up to nine months |
| **Capital for Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS)** | - Ability to sell now, pay later through flexible repayment<br>- Financing tailored to your stocking strategy or inventory needs<br>- Ability to harness to full potential of WFS |

Notes:

Capital by Parafin is funded by a third-party capital provider and isn't serviced nor funded by Walmart.

If your business is eligible to receive a merchant cash advance, you’ll be notified in Seller Center and via email. Eligibility includes, but is not limited to, the following criteria:

- Must have six months of Marketplace sales
- Must have a U.S. Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN)
- Must have a sales volume and performance on Walmart Marketplace
- Must have a business risk profile
- Must adhere to our [_Seller Performance Standards_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Seller-performance-standards)
- Sole proprietors are not eligible to receive offers
- U.S.-based businesses only


You can learn more about these programs [here](https://marketplace.walmart.com/walmart-marketplace-capital/) _._

## Additional guidelines

Your baseline payment amount of purchased receivables is estimated using your historical sales. You can check the repayment amount before you accept the offer. If you accept a Capital by Walmart or Capital for Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) offer, you’ll receive funding via the same payment method you use for payouts from Walmart Marketplace through your payment processor.

We collect repayment automatically from your payouts during each settlement cycle for Capital by Walmart and Capital for Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS). In case of insufficient funds in the settlement account, we may debit your bank account, processors or other platforms where you have future receivables.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Is there any interest or fees?

We charge a competitive flat fee as a percentage of the amount advanced and it doesn’t change during the repayment period. The fee is included in the total repayment amount and is paid as a part of your regular payments.

* * *

How is the offer amount calculated?

Offer amounts are based on such criteria as your Walmart Marketplace sales and performance, country of incorporation and business risk profile.

* * *

Can I apply?

No. There is no option to apply for the advance.

* * *

Whom do I contact if I have additional questions about Walmart Marketplace Capital?

If you have questions or need assistance with any of the available programs under Walmart Marketplace Capital, refer to the following contact information:

**Capital by Walmart:** [MPWorkingCapital@wal-mart.com](mailto:MPWorkingCapital@wal-mart.com)

- **Capital by Parafin:** [walmart-capital@parafin.com](mailto:walmart-capital@parafin.com) or call (855) 340-3149

- **Capital for Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS)** : [MPWorkingCapital@wal-mart.com](mailto:MPWorkingCapital@wal-mart.com)


* * *

Can I choose to use a third-party financing provider instead of Walmart Marketplace Capital?

Yes, Walmart Marketplace Capital enables third-party offers. See Seller Center for more information.

* * *

Can I opt out of data sharing with Parafin?

Yes, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

Can I request a capital offer?

Some eligible sellers will get an option to request a capital offer based on their business needs. However, Walmart doesn’t guarantee an offer as that’s dependent on various seller performance metrics.
