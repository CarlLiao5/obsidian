Update tracking numbers in Seller Center

Reading time: 1 min

Overview

The [**Orders**](https://seller.walmart.com/orders/manage-orders) page in Seller Center displays customer order details and where to add valid tracking information once you ship an order. After marking the order status **Shipped**, you can edit the tracking number for up to 24 hours or until the carrier first touch scan is received in the Walmart system, whichever is earlier. In this guide, you’ll learn you how to update your tracking information.

## Update tracking numbers in Seller Center

### Step 1 — Find the order

Navigate to the [**Orders**](https://seller.walmart.com/orders/manage-orders) page and use your purchase order number (PO#) to locate your order. Select the purchase order number to view your order details.

### Step 2 — Enter new shipping details

Select the _three horizontal dots_ next to the item, then select _Enter new shipping details_. This option is only available for up to 24 hours or until the carrier first touch scan is received in the Walmart system, whichever is earlier.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt9fc92e26cb5df53e/66b12848492a2e3cc82d52ef/11186_Screenshot-1.png?branch=test2)

### Step 3 — Update your information

Enter your carrier and tracking number information. Select _Update shipping_ to save the changes.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt346df3c11a446ad1/66b12847492a2e36c42d52eb/11186_Screenshot-2.png?branch=test2)

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What should I do if my tracking number is invalid?

If you received an error after adding a tracking number, this means the tracking number doesn't match the selected carrier or the required format. To fix the issue, check for typos and make sure the correct carrier is selected. If you have questions, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

What if I don’t have a tracking number for an order?

You must provide valid tracking numbers for at least 99% of your shipments. Providing invalid tracking or failing to meet the 99% standard may result in selling restrictions, suspension or termination. For more information, visit [_Seller Performance Standards_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Seller-performance-standards).

* * *

Which carriers do you accept?

Check out these lists of Walmart-approved [parcel shipping carriers](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-methods:-parcel-shipping-carriers) and [freight shipping carriers](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-methods:-freight-shipping-carriers).
