Overview

When you send inventory to Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), you’ll need to follow our labeling requirements. Each unit of inventory needs an item label, and each shipment needs receiving labels that tell us what’s inside. In this guide, learn about acceptable labels and how to download them from Seller Center.

Pro tip

Download the [WFS Routing and Packaging Guide](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt57da3349569cc081/WFS_Routing_Guide.pdf?branch=us_mplearn) for complete guidance.

## Item barcodes and label guidelines

All items must have barcode labels on the outermost part of the sellable unit. The label helps us identify the item, so we’re storing and shipping the right item to customers.

- Place a readable and scannable label with GTIN (14 digits) or UPC (12 digits) on each unit's packaging. You may choose to buy a UPC or GTIN from [GS1](https://www.gs1us.org/upcs-barcodes-prefixes/how-to-get-a-upc-barcode?utm_source=partner&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=idgtm-walmart-).

  - EAN, vendor SKU number and FNSKU number are **not** acceptable barcodes or product identifiers.

- Do not put the item barcode on the box. It should be on the individual, sellable units only.
- Each variant item must have a unique barcode. Variant items without unique barcodes will not be received and may be returned to you at your expense.
- You must cover up old labels and barcodes. Old manufacturer barcodes may cause confusion at the fulfillment center and lead to delays.
- You may include additional product details on a label as long as the correct barcode is included.
- All labels must be visible and scannable. The fulfillment center may refuse and return at your expense any unit that doesn’t have a scannable label with item data. We may also charge a fee if the item doesn’t contain a scannable barcode.

  - Any damaged labels or barcodes will cause a delay in receiving time.

All items must be clearly and permanently marked with the country of origin. The label must be located where it can be seen while casually handling the item. Where the label goes depends on the item:

- For clothing, a label stating “Made in . . .” qualifies.

- For shoes, the country-of-origin label must physically be on _both_ shoes in the pair. The country of origin needs to be on more than just the website or shoebox.

- For some items, the country-of-origin label may go on the outer packaging.


## Print item labels

Follow these steps to download item labels through Seller Center:

1. Log into Seller Center and go to the [**Inventory**](https://seller.walmart.com/wfsLite/manage-inventory) page.

2. Select the items you need labels for.

3. Choose the _Print GTIN label_ option.

4. Choose the size and number of labels you need, then download.

5. Print labels and apply them to your items.


![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltd90f1ab4f46dce24/687815456f46ee261a0cd41e/Labels_select_items.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Printing tips

All barcodes must be scannable by Walmart equipment. To maintain print quality:

- Use a printer with a resolution of 300 DPI or greater.

- Use the correct label stock for your printer.

- Periodically test your printed barcodes by scanning them.

- Test, clean, and replace your printer heads as needed.


## Print receiving labels

At the fulfillment center, we scan the WFS receiving label to verify the shipment and its contents. It is not a carrier label. Your receiving label must include:

- Return name and address (your information)
- Ship-to address (fulfillment center)
- Shipment ID (generated by WFS)
- Shipment ID barcode
- Box or pallet ID
- GTIN and quantity

Follow these steps to download receiving labels through Seller Center:

1. Log into Seller Center and go to the [**Shipping Plans**](https://seller.walmart.com/wfs/shipments) page.
2. Select _View plan_.
3. For each shipment, download the box or pallet receiving labels.
4. Print and stick the labels to your boxes or pallets.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt7bfa0fed3f507560/682bbc9ea8205f503c73d9d7/view_tasks.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Pro tip

Select _Review item packing_ to download the packing list. This tells you which items go in which boxes. If your receiving labels have unique box IDs, the packing list shows you how each box should be packed.

## Print carrier labels

If you’re using the [Walmart Preferred Carrier Program](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/Walmart-Preferred-Carrier-(WPC)-program), you’ll also need to need to download a carrier label. This is what the carrier will scan during pickup to deliver to the correct address. Follow the same steps as above to download your carrier labels.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

I use FNSKUs to send items to Amazon. Will WFS use something similar?

No, the SKU will remain the same as what you entered during item setup. We do not accept FNSKU labels for receiving. We require scannable GTIN or UPC labels.

* * *

How do I know which items to pack into which box?

Download the packing list to help you remember which items go in which box and how many boxes you have. From the [**Shipping Plans**](https://seller.walmart.com/wfs/shipments) page, select _View plan_. Then look for the _Review item packing_ button. This will show you a list of items in the shipment, the units per box, box IDs and number of boxes. Use this information to correctly pack boxes and match the receiving labels.

* * *

Do you provide templates for labels, packing slips or warning labels?

You can print box and pallet labels in Seller Center by following the directions in this guide. Packing slips are optional, but we do require a bill of lading for freight shipments. We do not provide templates for warning labels, which should be printed or attached to any poly bags.

* * *

Does WFS have a label size requirement or restriction?

No, there is no size restriction. We only require that your labels are clearly visible and include all important item information and warnings.

* * *

Will WFS place Walmart barcodes on my products?

For a small fee, WFS can prepare your inventory, including item labeling. See [Prep Services](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-prep-services) for more detail.
