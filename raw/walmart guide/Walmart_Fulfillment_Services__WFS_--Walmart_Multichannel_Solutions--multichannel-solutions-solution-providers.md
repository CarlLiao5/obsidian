Overview

Learn how to automate your business for Walmart Multichannel Solutions (MCS) by using solution providers to integrate with our APIs. Get started by choosing one that works for you from our current list and contacting them through the methods provided.

### Benefits of using solution providers for MCS

Our integrated solution providers can get you connected faster with MCS. They can also help streamline your business by managing customer orders, giving real-time visibility into inventory and coordinating restocks across all your sales channels including Amazon, eBay, Temu, Shein, your own website and more.

Some providers offer full-service integration, while others only perform specific functions. Each integration is tailored to your specific needs, so reach out to the provider of your choice directly to gather information on costs, features, and capabilities.  You can also use the MCS filter in the [Seller Center app store](https://seller.walmart.com/apps/app-listings) to get visibility into which solution providers are integrated with MCS. You will find this filter under _supported programs_.

Notes:

To streamline automation with MCS, ensure that MCS is set as your default and/or preferred fulfillment option with your solution provider.

### How to get started

Create a Walmart catalog and configure settings in Seller Center by following the steps outlined in [_Multichannel Solutions: Set up items_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Walmart%20Multichannel%20Solutions/multichannel-set-up-items) and [_Manage sales channels_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Walmart%20Multichannel%20Solutions/multichannel-sales-channels). Once you’re set up, reach out to one of the solution providers highlighted in the table to help you connect with MCS.
