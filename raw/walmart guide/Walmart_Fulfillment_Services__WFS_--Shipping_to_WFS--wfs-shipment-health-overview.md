WFS shipment health: Overview

Last updated on Oct 6, 2025

Reading time: 5 min

* * *

In this guide:

Overview

You can view inbound shipment health at the top of the [**Shipping Plans**](https://seller.walmart.com/wfs/shipments) page. The focus areas help you track the quality and accuracy of shipments you’ve sent to Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), in accordance with the [WFS Routing and Packaging Guide](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt57da3349569cc081/WFS_Routing_Guide.pdf?branch=us_mplearn). By addressing repeat issues, you can avoid delays in receiving. In this guide, we’ll explain what the focus areas mean and how to improve.

Notes:

This program is currently in the beta phase and available only to a select group of WFS sellers.

## How does it work?

The focus areas show repeat issues from the past 180 days. These include **only closed or canceled WFS shipments**, so you don’t need to change anything in shipments you’ve already sent. But by looking back at past shipments, you can spot patterns and avoid them next time. The better your shipment health, the more likely we can receive inventory quickly and accurately.

Your shipment health is based on these 3 focus areas:

| Focus area | Description | How to avoid |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Missing tracking number | US domestic shipments sent with your own carrier that need tracking numbers. When you add a tracking number, it lets us know when to expect a shipment. | - Add tracking numbers as soon as you get them from the carrier. Just filter shipments by the _Tracking needed_ status, then select _Add tracking_.<br>  <br>- You can also go to the [WFS overview](https://seller.walmart.com/wfs/wfs-overview) page to find shipments with missing tracking numbers. Look for the _Shipments requiring action_ heading. |
| Abandoned shipments | Shipments that never arrived at Walmart and have no activity. If shipments don’t arrive as planned, it can slow down the WFS network. | - Send shipments as soon as possible.<br>  <br>- Cancel the shipping plan if you’re not sending the shipment. You can filter by date to find old plans.<br>  <br>- If you use your own carrier, be accurate with the estimated ship date in your shipping plan. |
| Unplanned prep | Shipments with items that didn’t meet packing and labeling requirements. We charged an unplanned prep fee to fix items. When items are correctly prepared, we can receive them without any delays. | - Check the [WFS Routing and Packaging Guide](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt57da3349569cc081/WFS_Routing_Guide.pdf?branch=us_mplearn) to make sure you’re meeting our packing and labeling requirements (e.g., scannable barcodes, poly bags).<br>  <br>- Download the [Settlement Report](https://seller.walmart.com/wfs/reports) to view unplanned prep fees.<br>  <br>- Have WFS [prepare items](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-prep-services) for you to save on time, labor and unplanned fees. |

## View focus areas

Here’s how to find and fix focus areas:

1. Log into Seller Center and navigate to the [**Shipping Plans**](https://seller.walmart.com/wfs/shipments) page.
2. Review each group at the top of the page. The percentage shows how many shipments were affected out of all inactive shipments in the past 180 days.
3. Select the button under each group to view recommendations for next time.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Do I need to take any action?

We highly recommend working to improve your shipment health, but it is optional. With just small process changes, your shipments can become more accurate, which means they can be received more efficiently.

* * *

Why can’t I see which shipments are impacted?

The focus areas only include closed or canceled shipments in the past 180 days. The goal isn’t to change past shipments, but to use the information to improve your future shipments.

* * *

How often is shipment health updated?

The data refreshes every day at 3pm PT. We recommend checking your shipment health every 4 weeks for changes. Keep in mind that the focus areas only include closed or canceled shipments. If you’ve just sent a shipment, you won’t see any immediate updates.

* * *

What happens if my shipment health doesn't get better?

The focus areas are there to guide and help you improve shipping processes. You won’t be penalized for poor performance. However, your inventory may not be received as quickly or efficiently because we encounter issues with your shipments.
