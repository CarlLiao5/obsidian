Comparison price rules

Last updated on Mar 25, 2025

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

Overview

The Comparison Price is used as a reference price to determine strikethrough and discounted pricing on [Walmart.com](http://walmart.com/). In this guide, you’ll learn how the Comparison Price is defined.

## How does it work?

The Comparison Price is defined by an item’s **List Price or Was** **Price**.

- **List Price:** This is the manufacturer's suggested retail price (MSRP) for the item. Except for books, [Walmart.com](https://www.walmart.com/) uses List Price as the basis for the selling price if the item was purchased by [Walmart.com](http://walmart.com/) customers at that price or offered by other retailers at or above that price in the past 90 days or 180 days for seasonal items. List Price may not be the prevailing market price for an item.

Pro tip

To ensure Walmart Marketplace has the correct comparison prices for your items, you are encouraged to submit a List Price through one of our [_Item Setup Methods_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/Item-setup-methods:-Overview) or through [_Item Maintenance._](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Item%20management/Update-item-content-in-bulk-in-Seller-Center)

- **Was Price:** Thisis either the 90-day median price paid by customers for the item on [Walmart.com](https://www.walmart.com/)(excluding special promotions like holiday campaigns, limited time deals, rollbacks, and clearance) or the median price offered by Walmart or Marketplace sellers for the item on [Walmart.com](https://www.walmart.com/) for at least 28 out of the last 90 days (excluding special promotions like holiday campaigns, limited time deals, rollbacks, and clearance).

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How often does the Comparison Price get updated?

The Comparison Price is updated daily.

* * *

How long does it take for the List Price to update after I submit a new price?

After you submit a List Price, Walmart will validate the price and update it within the same day.

* * *

Where can I find the Comparison Price in Seller Center?

You can find your item’s Comparison Price by downloading an [_Item Report._](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Reporting/Generate-an-item-report)

* * *

What price will be displayed to the customer if I have an item with two promotions, such as a Flash Deal price and a strikethrough promotional price, set up at the same time?

The lowest promotional price you've set up for the relevant time period will be displayed. This action may also alter the strikethrough that your item may be eligible to receive while the promotion is in effect.

* * *

How long does it take for the Comparison Price to appear in Seller Center?

The Comparison Price will appear in Seller Center two days after a new item has been listed, provided the item has either a sale or a List Price. If neither occurs, the Comparison Price will appear after 28 days.
