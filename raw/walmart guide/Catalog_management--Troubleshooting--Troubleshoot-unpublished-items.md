Troubleshoot unpublished items

Last updated on Nov 24, 2025

Reading time: 3 min

* * *

Overview

If your item has been unpublished from Walmart.com for not meeting Marketplace standards, you can use the [**Unpublished Items**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/unpublished-items) dashboard in Seller Center or generate an [Item Report](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Reporting/Generate-an-item-report) to check which items have been impacted. This guide will help you troubleshoot every unpublished reason you may encounter so you can make an update and get your listing republished.

## Listing issues

All Listing Issues can be resolved from the [**Unpublished Items**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/unpublished-items) dashboard.

### Error Message: Primary image missing

**What should I do?**

Update the item with a new Primary Image. Since there may be multiple sellers providing content for the same item, you may not always be able to define which content will be displayed on Walmart.com. Walmart will determine the best available content for items unless it’s provided by a brand owner or authorized reseller. Items that can't be updated will appear locked during the updating process. If you’re having trouble, contact support in [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home).

### Error Message: Price missing

**What should I do?**

Update your List Price.

### Error Message: Egregious shipping cost

**What should I do?** Update the shipping fee listed in your [Shipping Template](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping%20Templates/Shipping-templates:-manage-settings) to be more competitive with market conditions. Your offer is measured by shipping price relative to item price.When the shipping fee for an unpublished offer falls back within an acceptable range, Walmart will automatically republish the offer, which typically takes up to 48 hours. For more information, review [_Pricing rules_.](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Pricing-rules%22%20HYPERLINK%20%22https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Pricing-rules%22%20HYPERLINK%20%22https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Pricing-rules)

### Error Message: Reasonable price not satisfied

**What should I do?**

If any offers are priced egregiously higher than prices currently or recently offered for the same item, they are flagged by the [Pricing Rule](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Pricing-rules), and Walmart automatically unpublishes the offer in question with the reason code listed as **Reasonable Price Not Satisfied**. If prices change on Walmart.com or monitored competing websites and the item now adheres to pricing rules, Walmart will automatically republish the offer, typically within 48 hours.

You can also proactively [update your price](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/How-to-Update-Price:-Overview). If your offer remains unpublished after you've submitted a more competitive price, continue to adjust the price until your item no longer violates the Pricing Rule.

For any unpublished item, you can also find a **Reference price** on the [**Unpublished Items**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/unpublished-items) dashboard. You can use this reference price to help you update your listing to a more competitive price and get your item republished. Walmart calculates a competitive reference price for each item using price points from competitor websites, historical selling price, Buy Box winner price, average offer price and Walmart e-commerce and store price.

### Error Message: Pricing error

**What should I do?** In certain cases, Walmart may automatically unpublish offers that have been priced erroneously too low, to protect the seller from unintended losses and customer from experiencing cancellations. The reason code listed for such items is **Pricing Error**. In such a case, use the Reference price or other provided price signals as a reasonable benchmark or reference to increase price until it no longer violates the Pricing Rule. When the price is adhering to the pricing rules, Walmart will automatically republish the offer, which typically takes up to 48 hours.

If you intended to set a price that appears to be unintentionally low compared to the usual selling price, you may submit relevant documentation to demonstrate that the price is intentional and complies with Walmart’s Pricing Rules. To do this, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support. Walmart will review such documentation at its sole discretion and may, but is not obligated to, reinstate the offer if it determines that the price does not violate its policies.

For more information, review the [_Pricing rules_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Pricing-rules) _._

### Business policy

All Business Policy issues can be appealed from the [**Unpublished Items**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/unpublished-items) dashboard.

### Error Message: UPC mismatch

**What should I do?**

[Update the item’s product ID](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Item%20management/Update-a-product-ID) and resubmit the updated item data in Seller Center or via Walmart APIs. If you’re having trouble finding the correct product ID, contact the manufacturer or visit the manufacturer’s site. If you believe your item was unpublished incorrectly, you can appeal the decision.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How can I ensure my items stay published in the future?

Be sure to familiarize yourself with all [Walmart Marketplace retailer policies](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Onboarding/Walmart-Marketplace-retailer-policies) to prevent future listing errors.

* * *

How do I know if my item's status changes?

Run an [Item report](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Reporting/Generate-an-item-report) in Seller Center and review the Status Change column.

* * *

What do I do if my items remain unpublished?

If more than 48 hours have passed and you believe your offer remains incorrectly unpublished, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support. Once you’ve confirmed the correct price and provided any necessary documentation, Walmart will review your request and may republish your offer if the price is adhering to the pricing rules. Walmart has pricing policies to protect both sellers and customers, so it's essential to ensure that your prices are accurate and comply with our [pricing rules](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Pricing-rules).
