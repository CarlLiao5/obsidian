Listing Quality: Fix issues

Overview

The **Listing Quality** dashboard provides suggestions to improve your item's individual component scores including Content quality, Price competitiveness, Shipping, Published & in stock and Ratings & Reviews. In this guide, you’ll learn how to review suggestions (if applicable) and update your listings.

Pro tip

Accepting the Listing Quality suggestions helps improve individual component scores, positively impacting your overall **Listing Quality** score.

## Fix Listing Quality issues

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to the [**Listing Quality**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/listing-quality) dashboardand select _Fix issues_ under the **Action** column for the item you want to update.

### Step 2 – Identify areas for improvement

On the next page, the Listing Quality widget displays your item’s overall score and its component-level breakdown. Prioritize addressing component categories with scores between 0–59%.

![2602_Listing-quality_Actions_screenshot.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blta5ba0014f5288f6c/69934ce90f515bba0c1f0175/2602_Listing-quality_Actions_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Pro tip

Items marked **Mid** or **High** under the **Impact** column represent the best opportunities for improvement.

### Step 3 – Update item details

Once you’ve identified areas of improvement, locate the component categoryyou want to fix and use the table below to help update the relevant details. Your score may not be updated immediately. Newly published offers may take up to a week to reflect within the **Listing Quality** dashboard.

| Component | Recommendation |
| --- | --- |
| **Shipping** | Earn a 100% **Shipping** score by offering fast and free shipping (three days or less) through [Simplified Shipping Settings](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Simplified%20Shipping%20Settings/simplified-shipping-settings-overview) or [Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS)](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20basics/walmart-fulfillment-services-wfs-overview) and stay stocked to maintain your score. |
| **Content quality** | Ensure the accuracy and completeness of your [item’s name, description, key features and images](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-Detail-Page:-overview). Some attributes can only be updated by the [brand owner or authorized resellers](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/How-to-update-content:-Overview). |
| **Ratings & Reviews** | Collect more reviews on eligible items through the [Review Accelerator](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/review-accelerator-overview) or [Review Syndication](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Reviews/Marketplace-reviews:-review-syndication) program. |
| **Published and in stock** | Ensure your items [stay stocked](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Success-Hub:-restock-your-seller-fulfilled-inventory) and available for purchase. |
| **Price competitiveness** | Use the [Repricer](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Repricer:-overview) or the [Price Incentives program](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Price%20Incentives/price-incentives-overview) to adjust your price to match or beat competitors for better visibility and sales. |

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What are the benefits of improving my score?

By improving your score, you’ll increase your chances of achieving [Pro Seller](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Items%20and%20inventory/pro-seller-overview) status.

* * *

Where can I find extra support?

Interested in a classroom style learning session? [Register here](https://feedbackally.walmart.com/survey/UlLgYypvvIahLRl/) for a Marketplace bootcamp where you’ll learn how to create high-quality listings and apply marketing strategies to boost visibility, traffic and growth.
