Seller Performance Standards: Overview

Reading time: 3 min

Overview

Walmart conducts regular reviews of all Marketplace orders to ensure performance standards are met. The key metrics evaluated are Cancellation Rate, On-Time Delivery Rate, Valid Tracking Rate, Seller Response Rate, Return Rate, Item Not Received Rate and Negative Feedback Rate. In this guide, you’ll learn more about the performance standards and expectations.

Notes:

If you use Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), most performance metrics will be met for you, with the exception of the Negative Feedback Rate.

## What is the policy?

Seller Performance Standards are evaluated over the last 30 days or 60 days and help you stay informed about your performance. You’re required to meet the established standards shown in the table below. To ensure your account remains in good standing, you should regularly monitor your metrics from the [Performance](https://seller.walmart.com/performance/order-and-fulfillment) dashboard in Seller Center, the [Walmart Seller app](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-seller-app-how-do-i-monitor-my-performance) or through [Seller Performance APIs](https://developer.walmart.com/us-marketplace/docs/seller-performance-api-overview).

If your account fails to meet any of these standards, you must take immediate action to improve your performance. Failure to do so may result in [suppression, suspension or termination](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20policies%20&%20standards/Seller-suspension-and-termination) of your account.

## Additional guidelines

When order and fulfillment issues arise, the [**Performance**](https://seller.walmart.com/performance/order-and-fulfillment) dashboard in Seller Center summarizes current performance standing, the number of impacted customers and a list of drivers contributing to your performance score. Refundsand Cancellations standards also include impacts in total Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) loss. You can view metrics for specified time frames and download reports to see which orders are affected. Factors are categorized by:

- **Seller accountable:** Performance scores are calculated using seller accountable metrics. These are factors that may affect the customer’s experience and are in the seller’s control. (e.g., late hand-over to carrier, out-of-stock, incorrect item).
- **Not accountable:** Performance factors that may be affecting the customer's experience but are out of the seller's control (e.g., weather delay, customer requested).

It’s your responsibility to review account performance and proactively improve performance metrics that don’t meet standards. If your performance falls short of any of these standards, we’ll notify you by email and notification within Seller Center, along with providing recommendations to help you address the issue. Learn more about the types of [performance notifications](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Performance-alarms) you may receive. You cannot unsubscribe from these communications. They’re crucial for keeping you informed about actions that may affect your account status, including potential suspensions.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Why is it important that I meet the Seller Performance Standards?

Meeting all eight key metrics of the Seller Performance Standards offers many benefits that can enhance your overall seller performance. Additionally, when you meet our standards and additional performance criteria, you can qualify for [Pro Seller status](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Items%20and%20inventory/pro-seller-overview).

* * *

What if my performance is affected by circumstances outside of my control?

We understand that certain types of issues or events may impact your performance data. Walmart proactively monitors severe weather, natural disasters, fraud holds and related data.

* * *

Can I appeal a suspension or suppression?

If your account is suspended or suppressed and it's appealable, then you must demonstrate a commitment to improving your performance. You can submit an [appeal with a business plan of action](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Troubleshooting/Appeal-an-account-suspension) for your selling privileges to be reinstated.

* * *

Can I appeal a termination?

No. Appeals of termination aren’t accepted and your product listings will be deactivated accordingly.

* * *

What are Simplified Shipping Settings metrics?

If you use [Simplified Shipping Settings](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Simplified%20Shipping%20Settings/simplified-shipping-settings-overview) to ship your items, you can protect your On-Time Delivery performance metrics to help keep you in good standing with Walmart's Seller Performance Standards when there are disruptions with your shipping carrier.

When you meet the Simplified Shipping Settings criteria, your On-Time Delivery performance metric won't be impacted by carrier delays out of your control.

* * *

Will I be penalized for selecting the “No response needed” option instead of replying to a customer message?

No. Choosing _No response needed_ doesn’t impact your Seller Response Rate.
