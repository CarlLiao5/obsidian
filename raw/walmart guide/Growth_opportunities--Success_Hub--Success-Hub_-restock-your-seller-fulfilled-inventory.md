Success Hub: Restock your seller-fulfilled inventory

Last updated on Nov 24, 2025

Reading time: 1 min

* * *

Overview

Success Hub makes suggestions to replenish seller-fulfilled inventory for out-of-stock items and high-demand SKUs that are forecasted to run out of stock up to 8 weeks ahead. In this guide, you'll learn how to restock your inventory so that you can secure future sales and Gross Merchandise Value (GMV).

## Restock your seller-fulfilled inventory

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to **Success Hub** by selecting [**Growth**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/success-hub) in Seller Center to access your current inventory suggestions, then choose the _Add inventory_ button.

Notes:

The suggestions will clear as you add inventory to items to meet the suggested value. This newly added inventory is added to the current units available to sell.

### Step 2 – Add inventory

Select _Add inventory_ from the **Actions** column, add the desired number of units and select _Add inventory_.

Notes:

To download a report of your inventory suggestions, select the _Download_ button.

![2511_Success-Hub-Restock-Your-Inventory.gif](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt8d78f19a398b3148/6924ff0e060471082beb85d7/2511_Success-Hub-Restock-Your-Inventory.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

Pro tip

On the Success Hub page under _Top items to optimize,_ select an item cardto view inventory insights and suggestions. In the **Add units to fulfillment centers** field, enter the number of items you wish to restock then choose _Add inventory_.

![2511_Success-Hub-Restock-Your-Inventory_gif-2.gif](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt951b183f6a65ec6f/6924ff212781846f1d55b0ee/2511_Success-Hub-Restock-Your-Inventory_gif-2.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How can I update my inventory?

You can [update inventory](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/How-to-update-seller-fulfilled-inventory:-overview) in [Seller Center](http://seller.walmart.com/) through Walmart APIs or with your preferred Solution Provider.

* * *

Do I have to accept all Success Hub suggestions?

No. All insights are suggestive in nature and you're free to set your own price. There is no business expectation or requirement that you accept any Success Hub suggestions. As a seller, you have no obligation and make no promises to purchase any minimum amount of merchandise to list or sell on the Walmart Marketplace other than what you deem adequate.

Success Hub suggestions are based on publicly available pricing information, past purchasing history and representations about quantities to be purchased are not binding on the Walmart Marketplace and Walmart shall not be liable for any act or expenditure (including but not limited to expenditures for equipment, labor, materials, packaging or capital expenditures) by the seller in reliance on them.
