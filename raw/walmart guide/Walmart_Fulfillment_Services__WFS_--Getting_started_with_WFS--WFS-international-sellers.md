Overview

These are specific questions we hear from sellers who ship inventory from outside the US. However, all guidelines in the [WFS Routing and Packaging Guide](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt57da3349569cc081/WFS_Routing_Guide.pdf?branch=us_mplearn), such as packing and labeling, also apply to international sellers.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What do I need to do as an international shipper using WFS?

WFS accepts imports which meet the following requirements.

- You are responsible for clearing US Customs. Sellers using [Walmart Cross Border - Imports](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-cross-border-imports-overview) should follow the program guidelines if using Walmart's designated broker for customs clearance.
- You must meet [WFS product requirements](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Select-items-for-WFS).
- Products must be properly prepared and packed under the [WFS Routing and Packaging Guide](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt57da3349569cc081/WFS_Routing_Guide.pdf?branch=us_mplearn).
- You must have a US address for returns.

* * *

Does WFS manage international shipping or customs clearance for sellers?

Sellers may choose to use [Walmart Cross Border - Imports](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-cross-border-imports-overview) to transport their goods from Asia to WFS fulfillment centers in the US, which includes the option to use Walmart's designated broker to assist with customs clearance. Otherwise, we do not. Please work with a freight forwarding company to ship inventory to the US. You are also responsible for clearing US Customs before items arrive at a WFS facility.

* * *

Who’s responsible for paying duties?

You are responsible for duties and import taxes. Walmart is not the importer of record.

* * *

Does Walmart act as the ultimate consignee?

No, Walmart does not. If you’re a US entity with an EIN, you should act as the ultimate consignee. If you don’t have an EIN, you’ll need to name another business.

* * *

Do Walmart preferred carriers handle international pickup from the port?

No, our preferred carriers can only pick up from a US warehouse. Work with a freight forwarder or your own carrier to [schedule a delivery](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/wfs-inbound-orders-schedule-delivery-appointments) from the port to your assigned WFS facility.

* * *

Is a US return address required?

Per compliance regulations, you’re required to have a US location where returns can be sent to if needed.

* * *

Can I use EAN on labels?

No, you must use GTIN or UPC for labels.

* * *

How do I submit a claim for shortages or missing inventory?

We require 3 forms of shipment confirmation, including a detailed list of items included in the shipment. These documents must be in English or include an English translation. Go to [_Open a Dispute Claim_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Troubleshooting/Troubleshoot-WFS-receiving-issues:-open-a-dispute-claim) for details.

* * *

Can I use WFS for Walmart Marketplace Canada or Mexico?

[WFS Canada](https://sellerhelp.walmart.ca/s/guide?article=000009934) and Mexico are available to select sellers. You can apply to join the waitlist, and the team will reach out to you. Please note that inventory must be shipped to the fulfillment centers in Canada and can’t be shared between US and Canadian facilities. Product requirements, [fees](https://sellerhelp.walmart.ca/s/guide?article=000009935), [inventory preparation](https://sellerhelp.walmart.ca/s/guide?article=000009936) and more will also differ.
