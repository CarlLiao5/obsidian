Repricer: Overview

Last updated on Feb 23, 2026

Reading time: 4 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Repricer:-overview#overview)

* * *

Overview

**The Repricer** lets you enable automatic price adjustments for items in your catalog. You can easily create custom strategies, assign eligible items, monitor performance and qualify for Referral Fee discounts. In the guide, you’ll learn about the different pricing strategies you can apply to your SKUs.

Notes:

You must first accept the Automate Pricing Terms & Conditions which authorizes Walmart to adjust your item prices on your behalf.

## How does it work?

The [**Automate pricing**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/automate-pricing?tab=repricer) page is where you can create Repricer strategies for your items based on the rules you set. Once you’ve created and assigned a rule, it’ll automatically be applied to that item. For example, you could create a rule to meet or beat the winning Buy Box price on Walmart.com or match external price strategies.

Below you’ll find the different price comparison options you can automate. You can assign strategies to as many items as you want.

Pro tip

Prioritize assigning strategies to items that generate high site traffic and those with competitive offers on Walmart.com and external websites.

You can choose from the following **competitive price options** when creating a strategy.

| Pricing strategy | Offer |
| --- | --- |
| **Match Competitive price** | This strategy matches the Competitive price when it’s lower than your price after your pricing adjustment is applied. |
| **Adjust against Competitive price** | This strategy applies your pricing adjustment to whichever is lower between the Buy Box price and Competitive price. |
| **Don't compete with Competitive price** | This strategy applies your adjustment amount only to the Buy Box price. Your Competitive price isn’t considered. |
| **Compare with Competitive price only** | This strategy applies  your adjustment amount only to the Competitive price. Your Buy Box price isn’t considered. |

Notes:

The Repricer will only adjust the item price and doesn’t change your shipping price. Allow up to four hours for prices to be adjusted after uploading an item and selecting a strategy.

## Additional guidelines

You can follow the steps [in this guide](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Repricer:-Create-a-strategy) to create your first strategy.

You can review the strategy preview to simulate how your pricing logic would apply to sample data. It shows your **Current price**, **Buy Box price**, **Competitive price** and your **Price with your strategy** after applying the selected adjustment. Items must have a minimum and maximum price entered to be assigned to a strategy.

You can also review an item's price history through the **Current Price** column. You may pause or delete a strategy at any time through the **Action** column.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What happens if another Marketplace seller uses the Repricer to adjust pricing for the same item as mine?

The Repricer will auto-adjust the price for each offer based on each seller’s individual strategy settings.

* * *

What happens if the competing offer is out of stock or is no longer available?

Select the **Use your last submitted price if there's no comparable price found** checkbox under the **Configure your strategy** section when creating or editing a strategy, then the Repricer will adjust to your last submitted price and will continue to stay there until a new competing offer becomes available. Once one is available, automated pricing will resume.

* * *

Can I assign items that are already part of another strategy?

No. Items already assigned to a different strategy must first be removed before being added to a new one. Navigate to the **Items in Other Strategies** section to identify duplicates.

* * *

How can I fix items that show a warning symbol under the Repricer status column?

Select _Active_ to learn which assigned items require attention then choose **_Manage items_** to review and resolve issues such as minimum allowed price reached, no current price match found or ineligible for promotion.
