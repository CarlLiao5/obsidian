Shipping methods: Expedited delivery program

Last updated on Feb 3, 2026

Reading time: 4 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-methods:-expedited-delivery-programs#overview)
- [How does it work?](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-methods:-expedited-delivery-programs#how)
- [Additional guidelines](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-methods:-expedited-delivery-programs#additional)

Overview

Walmart’s expedited delivery program lets you offer OneDay or TwoDay delivery using your own shipping methods, logistics and carriers. You'll maintain full control over fulfillment with the benefit of faster delivery promises. In this guide, you’ll learn more about these seller-managed expedited delivery options and how to participate.

## How does it work?

This seller-managed fulfillment option allows you to manage your fulfillment network by shipping expedited items from your warehouse or with a fulfillment provider. Walmart prioritizes these listings by enhancing their search visibility, boosting their Buy Box advantage, displaying the fast-shipping badge and giving them a higher preference during Marketplace sales events.

Enrollment is automatic once you meet eligibility requirements. To qualify, you must:

- Have an active Seller Center account. Inactive, suspended or terminated accounts aren't eligible.


- Meet the minimum performance standards. Check the [**Performance**](https://seller.walmart.com/performance/order-and-fulfillment) dashboardin Seller Center to confirm your performanceover the last 30 days meets these requirements:


  - On-time delivery rate: >90%

  - Valid tracking rate: >95%

  - Cancellation rate: <1.5%


- Be a Walmart Marketplace seller for more than 90 days or have fulfilled a minimum of 20 orders that meet the minimum performance standards within the last 30 days.


Notes:

Confirm your enrollment by navigating to the [**Shipping info**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/shipping-info) page in your **Shipping Profile**. Check the status message for OneDay and TwoDay shipping under **Premium Program Participation**. This message also includes details in the event you don’t qualify.

## Additional guidelines

Once you have access to the program, the next step is to configure your shipping settings using one of these available methods:

| **Shipping setting** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| Simplified Shipping Settings | Calculates delivery times using your [updated fulfillment settings](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Simplified%20Shipping%20Settings/simplified-shipping-settings-set-up-your-settings) closing the gap between your promised and actual delivery times. Walmart recommends selecting the faster shipping service as your shipping priority. |
| Manual Shipping Templates | Assigns OneDay or TwoDay shipping at the SKU or template level using [manual Shipping Templates](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Shipping%20Templates/Shipping-templates:-add-settings-manually). |

Consider these suggested fulfillment settings for an optimal configuration that maintains the fast-shipping badge.

| Setting | Suggested configuration |
| --- | --- |
| Lag time | Set expedited offers to 0. |
| Inventory | Ensure inventory levels are maintained across all fulfillment centers for all SKUs offering expedited shipping options. |
| Operating schedule | Consider expanding your [fulfillment center operating schedule](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Fulfillment%20settings/Fulfillment-Center-operating-schedule) by implementing a 5pm local cut-off time. This adjustment would allow more customers across Walmart.com to see and benefit from the TwoDay delivery promise. |
| Weekend delivery | Enable Saturday or Sunday delivery, provided your carriers can guarantee weekend delivery. |
| Carrier services | When possible, use premium carrier services such as FedEx Express to ensure reliable TwoDay delivery. Ground carrier services can also meet TwoDay delivery promises when the order originates close to the fulfillment center. |
| Shipping labels | Receive a 25% discount on Ship with Walmart labels. |

Notes:

You’re solely responsible for verifying if the products listed under a specific program can be delivered on time. It’s your responsibility to determine if there are any constraints such as weight, distance or other factors that may prevent timely shipping as part of that program and to list them accordingly.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Can I use a third-party shipping provider for expedited delivery?

If you can’t fulfill your items within one or two business days or you don’t meet other requirements of the seller-managed option, you can use third-party shipping providers, [Flexport](https://marketplace.walmart.com/solution-providers/flexport/) or [Shipbob](https://partners.shipbob.com/directory/walmart/).

* * *

How will I know if I’m approved for expedited delivery if I use a third-party provider?

When you choose to fulfill using Flexport or Shipbob, there’s no review necessary. You'll be automatically approved for the OneDay and TwoDay delivery program after completing their registration steps.

* * *

Can I opt out of the program later?

With the seller-managed option, you can disable participation for all regions or opt out of the program entirely by deleting the OneDay or TwoDay shipping settings rules you’ve set up in your Shipping Template.

If you sign up for the program with Flexport, you’ll be able to adjust your fast shipping preferences directly from the Flexport dashboard.   If you sign up for Shipbob, you’ll have the ability to integrate Shipbob functionality with Seller Support to ensure smooth third-party logistics.

* * *

I used a third-party option (Flexport or Shipbob) but would like to switch over to the other. However, the fulfillment option is grayed out and I can’t use it. Why?

Due to legal reasons, Shipbob and Flexport can’t be used interchangeably on Seller Center.

* * *

Will expedited shipping help with site visibility?

Expedited delivery offers boost Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) by an average of more than 20% compared to non-expedited offers.

* * *

How are expedited deliveries calculated?

You’re required to deliver on or before the Estimated Delivery Date (EDD) for at least 90% of OneDay and TwoDay delivery orders in a 30-day period.  OneDay and TwoDay delivery will show an EDD that’s one, two or three business days after the order date. Assuming that there isn’t lag time, orders received Monday through Friday before your fulfillment center cutoff time will show an EDD one or two business days after the order date.

Orders you receive after your fulfillment center cutoff time on a business day, or on a weekend or holiday, will show an EDD that’s two or three business days after the order date. Your carrier should deliver the package to the buyer on or before the EDD.  If the delivery carrier attempts but fails to deliver a package, it counts as being delivered for the purposes of this requirement.

* * *

Can I charge customers a shipping fee for OneDay or TwoDay delivery?

You can charge a shipping fee or choose to offer free shipping when using OneDay delivery; however, you can’t charge customers a shipping fee when using TwoDay delivery.

* * *

What happens if my shipping performance falls below the required standards?

If your account does not meet the required shipping performance metrics, our automated system will temporarily remove you from the program and notify you by email. Once your performance improves and meets the eligibility criteria again, you’ll be automatically re-enrolled after seven days.
