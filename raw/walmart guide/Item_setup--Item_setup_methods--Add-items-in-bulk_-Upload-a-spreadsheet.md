Add multiple items: Upload a spreadsheet

Overview

In this guide, you’ll learn how to upload a bulk listing report or custom spreadsheet from any external marketplace to build your Walmart catalog.

## Upload a spreadsheet

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center and select the _Add items_ button. Select the _Upload in bulk_ link located under **Add thousands of items using a spreadsheet**.

Notes:

You must complete [onboarding in Seller Center](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Onboarding/Complete-onboarding-in-Seller-Center) before you can add items in bulk. Additionally, make sure your product IDs entered on the spreadsheet adhere to the [_Product identifier (GTIN, UPC, ISBN, or EAN) policy_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Choose-a-product-identifier) _._

### Step 2 – Upload a spreadsheet

On the **Add Items** page, you can drag and drop your file onto the browser window under **Upload**, or select the _browse_ link to locate the file on your computer.

Notes:

You can only upload one file at a time. Spreadsheets can be in any Excel format but must not exceed 5 MB.

![2502_Add-items-bulk-upload-spreadsheet_screenshot.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blteee4b210727869b8/67a4f5107de04e6d8df5abac/2502_Add-items-bulk-upload-spreadsheet_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 3 — Track the status

After submitting your template, you can track its status from the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) in Seller Center. Updates may take up to 24 hours. Any data errors that are detected during the updates will require extra processing time.

Notes:

By uploading a file, you represent and warrant that you're the owner of or have a license or other agreement that allows you to use this content, including any copyrights, trademarks, trade dress, design patents and/or any other rights and that you have the legal right to provide this report and its contents to Walmart.

You also acknowledge and instruct Walmart to work on your behalf to convert your file to Walmart’s item specification format through programmatic, manual, or other means. If you’re not the brand owner, Walmart’s content algorithms may prioritize the brand owner’s content over your item content in our catalog.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Why are some of my items not uploading?

After submitting your items, you can track their status from the [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) in Seller Center. Updates may take up to 24 hours. Any data errors that are detected during the updates will require extra processing time.

* * *

Why can’t I find my item when I search Walmart.com?

For your item to appear on Walmart search, your offer must be in **Published** status and have available inventory.

* * *

How do I update the item’s content?

You may be able to suggest changes to your item’s content after you’ve finished setting up your item. However, because there may be multiple sellers providing content for the same item, you may not always be able to define which content will be displayed on Walmart.com. Walmart will determine the best available content for items unless it is provided by a brand owner or authorized reseller.

Items that can't be updated will appear locked during the updating process. For additional information, visit [_How to update content: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/How-to-update-content:-Overview).

* * *

How is setting up a Walmart-fulfilled item different from setting up a seller-fulfilled item?

With seller-fulfilled items, you use your own fulfillment centers to manage, store and ship your products. When you choose Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), we pick, pack and ship orders for you.

During item setup with WFS, you’ll also need to enter more required information, like country of origin, dimensions, [compliance](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20item%20setup/WFS-hazmat-item-setup) and  [trade item hierarchy](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS-trade-item-configuration).
