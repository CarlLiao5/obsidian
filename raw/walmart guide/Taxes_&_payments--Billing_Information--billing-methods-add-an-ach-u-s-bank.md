Billing methods: Add ACH (U.S. bank)

Last updated on Dec 18, 2024

Reading time: 6 min

* * *

In this guide:

Overview

The billing method on Seller Center allows you to automatically pay outstanding fees — including fulfillment fees, storage fees and customer refunds — at the end of your weekly and biweekly Marketplace settlement cycle using the billing method of your choice. In this guide, you’ll learn how to add or update your billing method details within Seller Center.

## Add ACH (U.S. bank)

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to the [**Billing Services**](https://seller.walmart.com/finance-and-tax/billingServices) page in Seller Center.

### Step 2 – Add a billing method

To add and manage billing methods, select _Add billing method._ Then, select the _ACH bank account_ option and select _Continue_. Fill out bank account information and select _Submit_.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt32a3efb523e0d65c/675cb4a6a37ce11e08f16ceb/2412_12212_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

If you have a U.S. bank account, you can choose either ACH or credit card. If you don't have a U.S. bank account, you can only use a credit card.

### Step 3 – Verify your account

You should receive a microdeposit, or small deposit of $0.01-$0.99 from Paypal, PaypalMTCU or PaypalBTCU, to verify your account. When you receive the microdeposits in your account, log into Seller Center and confirm the amount by selecting _Verify account_. You’ll need to enter the amount of the microdeposit as it appears in your bank transactions.

Notes:

Account verification usually takes up to three business days after entering microdeposits information on Seller Center. After verification is complete, you can set the account as your default billing method. The microdeposits will be withdrawn from your account typically within three business days.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Why are you requesting my billing information?

We need your billing information to set up automated billing through our platform. After a one-time setup, the platform can help you save time and effort when paying for additional value-added services like Walmart Fulfillment Services, Walmart Media Connect services, Search Engine Marketing and customer refund and chargebacks.

* * *

Which billing methods do you accept?

Our platform is designed to accept bank accounts from all U.S. banks running on the Automated Clearing House (ACH) network.

Make sure you add bank accounts that have both debit and credit capabilities for a successful verification.

Notes

Select sellers will also have an option to provide a credit card as a billing method.

* * *

How does billing work?

Billing works in the following ways:

- Walmart Fulfillment Services fees, such as fulfillment and storage fees, customer refunds, and other fees are deducted from your sales-generated revenue before a final settlement is paid out to you.


- If the sales revenue doesn’t cover the fees and customer refunds, we'll use our platform to help you pay the excess fee amount through the billing method you provide.


- We won’t charge any billing processing fees.


* * *

How frequently am I billed?

Your billing cycle will match your current settlement cycle. For example, if your settlement cycle is every two weeks, then your billing cycle will also be every two weeks.

* * *

Is the billing method the same as the payment method?

The billing method is different from the payment method. The payment method is for receiving payments from Walmart Marketplace via payment service providers. Billing method is solely for paying seller services fees and outstanding dues to Walmart Marketplace.

* * *

Can I pay for my invoice with more than one billing method?

Our platform is set to charge only one billing method, which will be the default method that you assign. You can change your default option at any time. If you continue to experience issues, please contact your financial service provider or our support team for assistance.

* * *

What happens if I change my settlement cycle?

If your'e on a biweekly settlement cycle and change to weekly, or vice versa, your billing cycle will automatically change to match for your convenience.

* * *

How can I check my reflected charges?

Billing charges will be reflected in the Statement Summary section of Seller Center. Additionally, the total billed amount will be shown against the Amount charged. Each value-add service should also provide reporting of the charges.

* * *

Why do I need to verify my bank account?

We'll need to take a few more steps to safely verify your bank account using microdeposits.

* * *

How do I identify microdeposits on my bank account statement?

You’ll receive two small deposits of $0.01-$0.99 from Paypal, PAYPALMTCU, PaypalBTCU, PAYPALMTBU, WFMSBRNT01 and 2205717726. Once they’ve reached your account, sign into Seller Center and confirm the amounts by selecting _Verify my account_. The amount will be withdrawn from your account typically within three business days.

You’ll also be asked to reverify your account if the microdeposit amounts don’t match and if you’ve exceeded the maximum limit trying with incorrect amounts.

* * *

What should I do if I haven't received microdeposits or validation has failed?

This can happen if you've placed restrictions on your bank account or your bank account doesn’t accept ACH deposits or credits.

A successful verification involves crediting two microdeposits into your bank account and debiting them.

If your bank confirms the transaction was blocked, you can do the following:

- **Bank confirms blocked transaction due to restriction:** Request the restriction be removed or an exception is made. Return to Seller Center and try to verify your account again


- **Provide the following ACH IDs if asked:** PAYPALMTCU, PaypalBTCU, PAYPALMTBU, WFMSBRNT01 and 2205717726


- **Bank doesn’t accept ACH deposits:** Start the process again from _Use different bank account_


* * *

I’ve entered the microdeposits; why do I still see a Verify my account option against my bank account information in the Financial Settings page?

The deposit verification usually takes two to three business days. If you’ve entered the microdeposits, no further action is needed from you. Once verification is complete, you can set the account as your default billing method.

* * *

What should I do if my billing method is rejected?

If your billing method is rejected and you're using a valid bank account, check that the routing and account numbers are correct. If you continue to experience issues, please contact your bank or our support team for assistance.

Notes

Avoid requesting micro deposits multiple times. Only do so in case there are errors with the information entered.

* * *

What should I do if my bill payment fails?

If your bill payment fails, check your bank account to make sure that there are sufficient funds and no active holds. If you continue to experience issues, contact your bank or our support team for assistance.
