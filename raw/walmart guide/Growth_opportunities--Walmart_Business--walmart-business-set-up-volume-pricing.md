Walmart Business: Set up volume-based pricing

Last updated on Aug 26, 2025

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

Overview

Once enrolled in Walmart Business, you can set up your volume-based pricing to benefit customers who buy products in larger quantities. In this guide, you’ll learn how to set up and manage your volume pricing for Walmart business.

Notes:

Participation in the Walmart Business program is by invitation-only and not available to all Marketplace sellers. If you’re interested in selling on Walmart Business, you can apply [here](https://share.hsforms.com/1orll4wTlQFeWF7WNmBCpKg3lz2f) and your request will be reviewed on a first-come, first-served basis. [Check your enrollment status](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/exclusive-programs) before submitting a request to enroll.

## Set up volume-based pricing

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center and select _Add to strategy_ under the **Business strategy** column. Or you can begin on the **Automate pricing** page.

### Step 2 – Create a strategy

There are two ways to create a pricing strategy based on the volume of your inventory:

- **Create a strategy through your catalog**: Navigate to your **Catalog** and locate the item you wish to create a strategy for. Under the **Business strategy** column, select _Add to strategy_ then **Create a strategy.** Select _Business strateg_ y and enter the quantities and discount amounts. Once you name your strategy, choose _Create strategy._


- **Create a strategy through pricing:** Navigate to the **Automate pricing** page in Seller Center, select _Create strategy_ and choose _Business strategy_. Enter the quantities and discount amounts. Once you name your strategy, choose _Create strategy._


### Step 3 – Assign items in bulk

Navigate to the **Automate pricing** page and select _Upload items_. Choose Assign items to business strategy, then download the **B2B Volume pricing spreadsheet**. Fill out the template and enter in the **SKU,** then select a **Strategy name** from the dropdown menu. Once complete, navigate back to the _Upload items_ page in Seller Center to drag and drop your items for upload.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Which of my items will be displayed on Walmart.com?

Eligible items will be automatically displayed on [Business.Walmart.com](https://business.walmart.com/) without any action needed from you. Most items are eligible, with some exclusions based on product types or item performance. For more information, view this [guide](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Product%20listings/Start-selling-on-Walmart-Business). If your Walmart.com price is lower than your business price, your lower price will be displayed on Business.Walmart.com.

* * *

How do I know which items qualify for the Walmart Business program?

Most items qualify. However, some are excluded based on product types or item performance. The Business.Walmart.com price is only available to customers shopping on Business.Walmart.com and the Walmart Business app.

To view eligible items, [download a report](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Product%20listings/Start-selling-on-Walmart-Business).

* * *

Am I required to offer a business price?

No. If you prefer to not offer a price on Business.Walmart.com that is equal or lower than your Walmart.com price, you can opt out by following the steps [in this guide](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Product%20listings/Start-selling-on-Walmart-Business).

* * *

How do I remove items in bulk from an existing pricing strategy?

To remove items in bulk, navigate to the **Automate pricing** page and select _Upload items._ Choose **Assign items to business strategy** to download the spreadsheet. Provide the SKU and under the **Strategy name** field, select _Delete/remove from strategy_ from the drop-down menu. Drag and drop the template onto the **Upload with file** window.

* * *

When assigning items in bulk, do I need to fill out all of the attributes on the template?

No, not all attributes are required. You must fill out the SKU and strategy name. You may update or remove the remaining attributes based on your needs.

* * *

Where can I find the items available on my Walmart Business site?

You can also review the steps [in this guide](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Walmart%20Business/walmart-business-reporting) to download the B2B item or order report.

* * *

Where can I review the progress of my updates?

You can review all business strategy updates on your [**Activity feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed?feedsFor=uploads).

* * *

Why am I receiving a pricing error?

Walmart may automatically pause offers that are too low to protect you from unintended loss. This may be shown as a **Pricing error**,which can be remedied by adjusting the discount tier structure within your business strategy or altering your single item price within the business strategy to keep it from going too low as quantities increase.

Once you adhere to the [pricing rules](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Pricing-rules), the offer will be available again, which could take up to 48 hours.
