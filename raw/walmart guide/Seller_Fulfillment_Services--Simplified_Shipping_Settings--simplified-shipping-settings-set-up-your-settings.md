Simplified Shipping Settings: Manage carrier settings

Last updated on Apr 9, 2026

Reading time: 4 min

* * *

Overview

In this guide, you'll learn how to adjust and customize your carrier settings if you choose to use Simplified Shipping Settingsto fulfill your Marketplace orders. You can add multiple carrier methods and assign your preferred carriers and shipping speeds to different regions after you complete onboarding.

Notes:

Simplified Shipping Settings is compatible with free and paid shipping **,** [virtual packs](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup%20methods/Create-a-virtual-pack:-full-setup-template) and most domestic USPS, UPS and FedEx services. However, it doesn’t support Ship to Store items, international or freight shipping or third-party logistics services such as Flexport.

## Manage carrier settings

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to the **Shipping Profile** section on the Account settings page in Seller Center and select [_Simplified Shipping Settings_](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/simplified-shipping/simplified-carriers). Select _Set up shipping_ to begin.

### Step 2 – Choose your carrier and shipping priority

- **Carrier**: Choose the carrier and carrier service that you use most frequently. By default, the contiguous 48 U.S. states are selected. If you want to edit the settings of a carrier, select the three dots to the right of the carrier and select _Edit_. Choose _Edit regions_ to modify your carrier coverage to include states such as Hawaii or Alaska.



If you use more than one carrier service, choose _Add new carrier_ to add others.  Select the carrier, service and priority then select the _Create Carrier_ button. Select the checkbox to automatically apply your Simplified Shipping Settings to all eligible published items.

Notes:

To make edits to your carrier entry, select the three dots to the right of the **Carrier and Service** you wish to update, then select _Edit._ Once complete, choose _Update carrier_ to save your changes. If you wish to remove a carrier option, select _Remove_ instead then _Yes_ to confirm your changes.

- **Shipping priority:** Select _Shipping priority_ in Manage settings. Walmart recommends selecting the faster shipping service as your shipping priority. If you're setting up more than one carrier service, make sure to put your selected carriers in the order you’d like Walmart to prioritize them.

Pro tip

The accuracy of your fulfillment center settings is important to ensure that Walmart can select the best location to ship your orders from and to provide delivery promises to your customers based on that location. Review your seller-fulfilled [fulfillment center settings](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Fulfillment%20settings/Fulfillment-methods:-overview) to ensure that your fulfillment center details are accurate.

### Step 3 – Customize your settings (optional)

After completing your initial set up, you can customize your settings for the following:

- **Carrier settings** **:** Customize carrier service settings for each of your fulfillment centers if needed by selecting the _Edit_ button next to the fulfillment center. If any of your fulfillment centers aren’t listed, select the _Add Fulfillment Center_ button. Visit [here](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Fulfillment%20settings/add-a-seller-managed-fulfillment-center-in-seller-center) for more information on adding fulfillment centers.
- **Shipping rate options:** You can choose to create a **Custom shipping rate** with paid shipping, offer **Free shipping** or a combination of both to customize by state or region.

  - **Custom shipping rate:** Select this option if you'd like to charge customers for [shipping](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Simplified%20Shipping%20Settings/simplified-shipping-settings-manage-item-group-settings).
  - **Free shipping:** Choose this option if you're offering free shipping. If you choose this option, it's mandatory to have at least one carrier with free shipping to all 48 contiguous states.

- **Item group settings:** Create customized shipping settings for groups of items that require special shipping needs (e.g., price, brand, product type or weight). For example, you can set up preferences such as only shipping via ground for items of a certain value. You can also configure a base rate or an additional rate by weight or item count through paid shipping using [item group settings](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Simplified%20Shipping%20Settings/simplified-shipping-settings-manage-item-group-settings).



Fill out all fields, review the information then select _Next_. Ensure that all information has been provided then choose _Save to draft_.

- **Ship methods**: Ensure your top selling items are shipped using premium shipping methods to improve customer experience and sales by offering faster promises. You can then configure remaining assortments for ground shipping methods. This table lists the available shipping methods and carriers.


| Ground ship methods | Premium ship methods |
| --- | --- |
| FedEx Home Delivery | USPS Priority |
| FedEx Ground Economy | UPS Next Day Air |
| UPS Ground | USPS Priority Mail Express |
| UPS SurePost | FedEx Express |
| USPS Ground | UPS 2nd Day Air |
|  | FedEx Overnight |

Notes:

If you wish to not offer free shipping for any assortment, you must [create an item group](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Simplified%20Shipping%20Settings/simplified-shipping-settings-manage-item-group-settings?locale=en-US) to apply that setting to all items within.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What do I do after I set up my settings?

You must allocate inventory to each of your fulfillment centers. For more information on adding and managing inventory, visit [_How to update seller-fulfilled inventory: Overview_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Getting%20ready%20to%20sell/How-to-update-seller-fulfilled-inventory:-overview).

* * *

If I don’t use the one-click option to convert all of my items to Simplified Shipping Settings, how can I convert items later?

If you didn’t choose to automatically convert all your items to Simplified Shipping Settings during the initial setup process, you’ll need to apply these settings to your items either  [individually or in bulk](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/simplified-shipping-settings-apply-settings-to-your-items) before your settings are effective.

* * *

When I add a new SKU to my catalog, do I need to map it to Simplified Shipping Settings?

No. As long as the item is supported by Simplified Shipping Settings, newly added SKUs will automatically inherit your settings. Additionally, SKUs that aren’t in a published status that are changed to published status will automatically inherit your settings.

* * *

Are all my offers eligible for Simplified Shipping Settings?

Most seller-fulfilled items with free shipping are eligible for Simplified Shipping Settings.  Simplified Shipping Settings is not compatible with Ship to Store items, international or freight shipping, or third-party logistics services such as Flexport.

* * *

Can I change my carrier after an order is placed?

We recommend using your chosen carrier and carrier service listed in your settings. If you choose to switch the carrier and carrier service after an order is placed and the order is late, you’ll be responsible for the late delivery.

* * *

Can I fulfill from another warehouse?

You should use the same warehouse (zip code) as listed in your template to fulfill the order. If you fulfill the order from another warehouse and the order is late, you’ll be responsible for the late delivery.

Notes

Make sure to set up your current warehouse address and zip code in your template to ensure you’re fulfilling the orders from the right warehouse. Using your virtual node is not recommended if you aren’t shipping your orders from that location.

* * *

Can I use more than one carrier service for the entire U.S.?

You must have one carrier service that covers the contiguous U.S. You can offer additional carrier services and customize the regional coverage of any additional carrier services offered.

* * *

Why can't I add shipping fees to my Simplified Shipping Settings?

Simplified Shipping Settings is only available if you offer free shipping. If you don’t offer free shipping, you’ll need to set up your shipping configuration using [Shipping Templates](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping%20Templates/Shipping-templates:-overview).

* * *

Why do I get an error stating shipping fee has increased too much?

Shipping prices are evaluated against reasonable market rates. If your shipping fee increases beyond a set threshold compared to your previously configured rate, the change won't be allowed in order to comply with Walmart's [Pricing rules](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Price%20management/Pricing-rules).
