Overview

When you use a Walmart preferred carrier, we’ll help you coordinate pickup and delivery of your Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) shipment. In this guide, learn how to print labels, confirm the pickup date and get ready for the carrier.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt311a697778dc4a34/6877df3eee343026caaae082/WFS_Preferred-carrier-journey_Infographic.jpg?branch=us_mplearn)

## Pickup for parcel

You can drop off packages to the nearest FedEx or UPS store at your convenience. Or to arrange a pickup, you can schedule online:

- [FedEx on-call pickup](https://www.fedex.com/en-us/shipping/schedule-manage-pickups.html)
- [UPS schedule a pickup](https://wwwapps.ups.com/pickup/schedule?loc=en_US)

Make sure to print [carrier and receiving labels](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WPC-prepare-pickup#labels) before the carrier takes your packages.

* * *

## Pickup for LTL and FTL

For full truckload (FTL) shipments, we'll schedule the pickup for you using your preferred pickup date. We’ll **email you a confirmed date** based on the carrier, your preferences and operating hours.

If the pickup date has passed, but we haven’t heard from your contact person, we may consider the shipment invalid and cancel it. For FTL shipments, you may be charged a no-show fee if the carrier attempted to pick up, but the pickup was unsuccessful.

For less than truckload (LTL), we'll schedule the pickup for you if you're assigned AAA Cooper, Arkansas Best Freight, Central, Estes Express, FedEx, Saia, TForce or XPO.\*

Notes:

\*If you're assigned a different carrier, please [contact the carrier](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WPC-prepare-pickup#carriercontacts) directly to schedule a pickup.

## What if I need to reschedule the pickup?

For some LTL shipments, you can reschedule directly in Seller Center if your carrier is AAA Cooper, Arkansas Best Freight, Central, Estes Express, FedEx, Saia, TForce or XPO. Here's how:

1. Go to the [**Shipping Plans**](https://seller.walmart.com/wfsLite/sendinventory) page and find the plan.
2. Select _View plan_.
3. In the **Carrier pickup** section, select _Reschedule_.
4. Choose a new date and confirm. This may affect your shipment price.


Once a new pickup is confirmed, the previous pickup and bill of lading will be canceled. Please reprint your bill of lading with an updated load ID. For FTL and all other LTL carriers, please [contact the carrier](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WPC-prepare-pickup#carriercontacts) to reschedule. We also recommend contacting the carrier to make sure they canceled the original pickup.

* * *

## What if I don’t get a pickup confirmation?

If your LTL shipment is assigned to AAA Cooper, Arkansas Best Freight, Central, Estes Express, FedEx, Saia, TForce or XPO, we may have difficulty scheduling a pickup. This may happen due to technical reasons or if the carrier doesn’t have availability close to your preferred pickup date. You’ll need to [contact the carrier](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WPC-prepare-pickup#carriercontacts) directly to reschedule.

To schedule a pickup with a preferred carrier:

1. Use the following list to contact your assigned carrier.
2. Tell the carrier that this is part of a Walmart Fulfillment Services shipment, then provide the load ID. You’ll find this number on your [bill of lading](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WPC-prepare-pickup#billoflading).
3. Provide the pickup ID. You can find this by searching for the shipping plan, selecting _View plan_ and looking under **Carrier pickup** in the plan.

## Print carrier and receiving labels

Once you have a confirmed pickup date, you’re ready to stage and load the shipment. Make sure to follow the labeling and packing requirements in the .

Depending on the type of shipment, you’ll need to print these labels and stick them to each master case or pallet.

- **Carrier label:** Used by the carrier to deliver to the correct address.
- **Receiving label:** Used by the fulfillment center to verify the shipment and its contents. Each label has a shipment ID and box/pallet ID.

Here’s how to print labels:

1. Go to the page and find your plan.

2. Select _View plan_.

3. For each shipment, download the carrier and receiving labels. For the carrier label, enter the confirmed pickup date.

4. Print and stick the labels to your cases or pallets.

If you need to print item labels, go to for help.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt7bfa0fed3f507560/682bbc9ea8205f503c73d9d7/view_tasks.png?branch=us_mplearn)

## Print bill of lading

For freight shipments, you’ll also need a bill of lading. This is a list of goods that identifies the shipper, carrier and consignee. Give the bill of lading to the carrier when they get to your warehouse. When they deliver to the fulfillment center, a Walmart associate will sign the bill of lading.

For LTL, the bill of lading will be ready to download in a few minutes. For FTL, it’ll be ready within 24 hours of pickup. We’ll email the bill of lading to you once it’s ready. You can also print it from Seller Center:

1. Go to the [**Shipping Plans**](https://seller.walmart.com/wfsLite/sendinventory) page and find your plan.
2. Select _View plan_.
3. For each shipment, download the bill of lading.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Do I need to schedule a delivery appointment with the fulfillment center?

No, we’ll take care of that when you use a preferred carrier.

* * *

Do I need to use Scheduler 2.0?

No, [Scheduler 2.0](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/wfs-inbound-orders-schedule-delivery-appointments) is used by carriers to schedule delivery appointments at a fulfillment center. All preferred carriers already have access, so you don’t need to manage this.

* * *

Where do I find the load and pickup IDs?

The load ID is in your bill of lading. The pickup ID is listed in your pickup confirmation email. You can also find the pickup ID by searching for the shipping plan in Seller Center, then selecting _View plan_. The pickup ID will be under the **Carrier pickup** section.

* * *

What happens if the carrier doesn’t show up on the confirmed pickup date?

The carrier may be delayed due to local weather, mechanical issues or other unforeseen problems. Please [contact the carrier](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WPC-prepare-pickup#carriercontacts) directly to check. If the carrier still hasn’t arrived, contact Support or email Walmart Transportation at transinbdecom@walmart.com. Make sure to add your shipment ID, load ID and pickup ID, found on the bill of lading and shipping plan.

For FTL shipments, you may be charged a no-show fee if the carrier attempted to pick up, but the pickup was unsuccessful.

* * *

Why hasn’t a preferred carrier been assigned to my FTL shipment?

You’ll be assigned a carrier within 24 hours of pickup. If a carrier hasn’t been confirmed before pickup, please contact Support or email Walmart Transportation at transinbdecom@walmart.com.
