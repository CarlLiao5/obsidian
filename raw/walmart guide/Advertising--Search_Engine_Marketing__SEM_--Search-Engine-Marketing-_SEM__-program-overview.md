Search Engine Marketing (SEM): Program overview

Overview

Search Engine Marketing (SEM) is a paid advertising tool that enables you to create Google Shopping ads for your Walmart products. These ads help increase the visibility of your products for shoppers searching on Google. In this guide, you’ll learn more about the SEM program and the guidelines and requirements to participate.

Notes:

If you're participating in this program, you’re responsible for being compliant with all [policies, rules and guidelines](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/Search-Engine-Marketing-(SEM):-program-policy) that apply to all advertising placements. Failure to adhere may result in account suspension or termination from the Marketplace program.

## How does it work?

Once you [set up and launch your campaign,](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Promotions/Search-Engine-Marketing-(SEM):-campaign-setup) your Google Shopping ads will appear in the ads section of [Google.com](http://google.com/) search results during a relevant search for products like yours. These ads leverage:

- Proprietary AI algorithms that automatically set and adjust bids for your ads based on data.


- Walmart's brandand scale to reach a broader customer set.


- Cost-per-click (CPC) modelwhere you only pay when potential customers click on your ads.


![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltaa9ed0f0aa30ec4c/65fd9bcd173c18000ae45847/11195_SEM_Program_overview.png)

When customers click on your ads, they’re directed to your offer on Walmart.com where they can complete their purchase. Your offer is shown whether or not you’re winning the Buy Box.

Pro tip

Shopping ads are powered by product data. Accurate Walmart product attributes such as title, price, image and description are crucial for ad eligibility and adherence to [Google's requirements](https://support.google.com/merchants/answer/7052112?hl=en).

Build a cohesive onsite and offsite marketing strategy by integrating SEM with your existing Walmart Connect strategy. Search Engine Marketing can be leveraged for your items including high-performing products, new product launches, seasonal promotions and low-traffic products.

Notes:

If you don’t meet the minimum statement balance requirements you may be required to add a billing method before starting a campaign.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How do I set up a Search Engine Marketing (SEM) campaign?

Create new SEM campaigns by selecting the + _Create new campaign_ button on the [**Search Engine Marketing**](https://seller.walmart.com/advertising/search-engine-marketing) page in Seller Center. You can learn more about how to [set up SEM campaigns](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Promotions/Search-Engine-Marketing-(SEM):-campaign-setup).

* * *

How is Search Engine Marketing (SEM) different from Sponsored Search Ads?

SEM ads appear to external shoppers searching on Google and are shown regardless of whether or not the offer is winning the Buy Box on Walmart.com.

Sponsored Search ads are shown to customers already shopping on Walmart.com and are only shown if the offer is winning the Buy Box.

* * *

How is Search Engine Marketing (SEM) different from Search Engine Optimization (SEO)?

SEM are paid ads, annotated with Sponsored or Ads, that appear at the top of search engine results pages (SERPs) and are prominently placed providing high visibility.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) are unpaid listings that help to increase visibility and traffic to your products. These listings typically appear below the paid ads and are ranked based on relevance and quality of the listing page.

* * *

How can I improve my campaign performance?

Email [MP-SEM-Assist@walmart.com](mailto:MP-SEM-Assist@walmart.com) to schedule a session with our dedicated team and learn more about campaign management and optimization.

* * *

How do I pay for campaigns?

You’re charged based on a Cost-per-click (CPC) model. This means you only incur costs when potential customers click on your ads, with no additional fees.

Campaign charges are billed through the normal settlement process. If you have a payment method on file, we’ll bill that payment method if your campaign charges exceed your account balance.

* * *

How does attribution work?

We use a proprietary 14-day lookback model based on customer clicks. If you have further questions, email [MP-SEM-Assist@walmart.com](mailto:MP-SEM-Assist@walmart.com) to schedule a session.

* * *

Can I add all items to Search Engine Marketing (SEM) campaigns?

Not all items can be added. Pre-Owned and Restored items in the following product types are not eligible for SEM campaigns: modems, magazines, auto wipers and VCRs.  Items also need to align with SEM policies found [here](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/Search-Engine-Marketing-(SEM):-program-policy).
