Walmart Business: Set up pricing

Overview

After you’ve applied and enrolled onto the Walmart Business platform, your next step is to set up custom pricing that caters to business to business (B2B) customers who often buy products in larger quantities. In this guide, you’ll learn how to set up and manage your Walmart Business pricing.

Notes:

Participation in the Walmart Business program is by invitation-only and not available to all Marketplace sellers. If you’re interested in selling on Walmart Business, [apply](https://share.hsforms.com/1orll4wTlQFeWF7WNmBCpKg3lz2f) and we’ll review your request on a first-come, first-served basis. [Check your enrollment status](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/exclusive-programs) before submitting a request to enroll.

## Set up Business pricing

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center.

### Step 2 – Set your price

By default, the items you sell on Walmart.com will also be available on Business.Walmart.com for the same price. You can update your item’s price for the Walmart Business program individually or in bulk. If your Walmart.com price is lower than your Business.Walmart.com price, Walmart Business will display the lower price.

- **Update prices individually**: Locate the item you wish to update and select the value under the **Business price** column. Add the new price and choose _Update._

![2512_WM-business_set-up-pricing_gif-1.gif](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltde54921b904be9d6/695d4bb84ef91122c5ab91cd/2512_WM-business_set-up-pricing_gif-1.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

- **Update prices in bulk**: Select the _Update Items_ button at the top of the page and choose _Update with file_ from the dropdown menu. On the next page, select the _Update price and set promo price_ link and save the template to your computer. Then, open the template and make your updates.



If you’re only submitting basic price updates, you only need to fill out the **SKU** and **Business Price** information. You can remove the rest of the attributes or update them based on your needs.



To upload the template, navigate back to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items), select the _Update items_ button and choose _Update with file_. Then use the file browser window to upload the template. When you’re ready, select _Submit_.



You can track the template’s status from the [**Activity feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed?feedsFor=uploads) in Seller Center.

![2511_Update-seller-fulfilled-inventory-bulk-seller-center_gif-2.gif](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blteb91ef92889a6f38/6930b21120c57c5b2ed6b78f/2511_Update-seller-fulfilled-inventory-bulk-seller-center_gif-2.gif?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 3 – Review your published prices

Eligible items will be automatically displayed on Business.Walmart.com without any action needed from you. Updates can appear in as quickly as 15 minutes or can take up to four hours.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How do I know which items qualify for the Walmart Business program?

Most items qualify. However, some are excluded based on product types or item performance. The Business.Walmart.com price is only available to customers shopping on Business.Walmart.com and the Walmart Business app.

To view eligible items, [download a report](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/walmart-business-reporting).
