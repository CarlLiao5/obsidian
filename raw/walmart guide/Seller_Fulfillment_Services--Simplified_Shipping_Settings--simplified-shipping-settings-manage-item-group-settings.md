Simplified Shipping Settings: Manage item group settings

Last updated on Apr 9, 2026

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

In this guide:

Overview

You can use Simplified Shipping Settings to set up shipping for an item group based on product type, brand, and item price and weight. For example, you can create a rule to always use FedEx Overnight to ship laptops priced over $1,000. In this guide, you’ll learn how to manage item group settings.

Pro tip

Creating an item group is optional. Before you get started, make sure to [apply Simplified Shipping Settings to existing items](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/simplified-shipping-settings-apply-settings-to-your-items).

Notes:

Simplified Shipping Settings is compatible with free and paid shipping **,** [virtual packs](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup%20methods/Create-a-virtual-pack:-full-setup-template) and most domestic USPS, UPS and FedEx services. However, it doesn’t support Ship to Store items, international or freight shipping or third-party logistics services such as Flexport.

## Manage item group settings

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to the [**Simplified Shipping Settings**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/simplified-shipping/simplified-carriers) page in Seller Center and select the _Item groups settings_ tab. Then select the _Edit draft_ button.

### Step 2 – Create item group

Select the _Add new_ button. You’ll name the item group, then provide the condition settings. For example, you can add conditions that specify the product type, brand, price and weight of the items in your item group. When you’re ready, select _Next_.

On the next page, choose your preferred carrier and carrier service, such as FedEx and FedEx 2Day. Then select the _Assign fulfillment center_ link and choose your fulfillment centers. For example, you can choose to apply the FedEx and FedEx 2Day settings only to fulfillment centers in specific states.

Select _Next_ to review your information and make any final edits. Select _Save to draft_ to save the new item group and its settings as a draft, and to return to the **Manage item groups settings** page. When you’re ready to publish the new item group, select _Publish_.

![2506_simplified-shipping-add-new-item-group_screenshot.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltf41c969cab444fde/6843314fa2e0cbbaadc3054d/2506_simplified-shipping-add-new-item-group_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

You can publish edits to an item group only once every 24 hours. We recommend waiting until you can publish a group of edits together. Edits will take effect within an hour after they’re published.

### Step 3 – Update item groups

To update and manage existing item groups, navigate back to the [**Simplified Shipping Settings**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/simplified-shipping/simplified-carriers) page and select the [**Item groups settings**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/simplified-shipping/simplified-carriers) tab. Then select the _Edit_ _draft_ button. You can manage your item groups using any of the methods below.

- **Remove:** To delete an existing item group, navigate to the **Item groups** section and select the _Remove_ link. Choose an item group and select the _Remove_ button.


- **Edit:** To edit an existing item group, navigate to the **Item groups** section and select the _Edit_ button. Choose an item group and select _Continue_. Then, follow the steps to edit the item group settings.


- **Rearrange priority:** You can choose the priority of your shipping settings. The shipping settings with the highest priority (at the top of the list) are applied first. To manage the priority of your item groups, navigate to the **Rearrange priority** section and select the _Edit_ button. Then, hold and drag an item group to move it to the position you want.

- **Paid Shipping:** Select _Custom Shipping Rate_ option to charge customers for standard shipping and handling or a shipping fee per item or weight and region. Once you've reviewed the total fee including shipping and handling along with the shipping fee per item or weight, select _Continue_. Ensure that all information has been provided then choose _Save settings_. The total includes the standard shipping and handling fee as well as shipping fee per item.


## Frequently asked questions

* * *

How can I download a report of my item group settings?

Navigate to the [**Simplified Shipping Settings**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/shipping-profile/simplified-shipping/simplified-carriers) page in Seller Center and select the **Item group settings** tab. Select the _View item groups report_ link andselect the _Generate report_ button. You can download your report from your [**Activity feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed).

* * *

How often can I make changes to my item groups?

You can edit item groups whenever you want, but you can only publish edits once every 24 hours. Edits will take effect within an hour after they're published. We recommend that you edit your groups, save the edits as a draft and then publish all the edits together.

* * *

What happens when an item matches the settings in multiple item groups?

Walmart applies item group settings in order of priority, starting with the item group that's first on the **Rearrange priority** list. For example, if an item matches the settings in Item Group A and Item Group B, and Item Group B is first on the **Rearrange priority** list, Walmart applies the settings from Item Group B first. After that, item group settings are applied in descending order.

* * *

How many item groups can I create?

You can create up to 20 item groups.
