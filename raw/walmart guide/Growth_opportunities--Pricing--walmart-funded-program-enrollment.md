Walmart-funded incentives: Program enrollment

Last updated on Feb 12, 2026

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

* * *

Overview

Your participation in the [Walmart-funded incentives program](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Promotions/price-incentives-overview) is by default. In this guide, you’ll learn how to manage your program enrollment.

Notes:

Walmart-funded incentives are provided at Walmart’s discretion. Walmart doesn’t guarantee that you’ll receive incentives for enrolled items.

## Manage program enrollment

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to the [**Incentives**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/incentives) page in Seller Center under the **Pricing** section. Choose the [**Walmart-funded** **incentives**](https://seller.walmart.com/pricing/incentives?tab=walmart_funded) tab and select the _Manage Walmart-funded incentives_ _link._

![2602_Pricing-incentives_Walmart-funded-program-enrollment_screenshot.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt59d5f85635437fdd/698cb6fa61c2041d320da700/2602_Pricing-incentives_Walmart-funded-program-enrollment_screenshot.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 2 – Turn the program on or off

Next, select the _toggle button_ to turn the program on or off _._ When you turn off the Walmart-funded incentives program, all items will be removed (opted-out) from receiving Walmart-funded incentives. When you turn on this incentive program, all your items are automatically opted-in.

Pro tip

If you want to actively participate in the program, but you don’t want to include every item in your catalog, you can manage enrollment for each of your items individually. For more information, visit [_Walmart-funded incentives: Manage item enrollment_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Growth%20opportunities/Price%20Incentives/walmart-funded-incentives-manage-item-enrollment) _._

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Do Walmart-funded incentives expire?

Walmart-funded incentives can expire at Walmart’s discretion and the item price will revert to your last submitted price.

* * *

What happens if items are unenrolled from Walmart-funded incentives?

When items are unenrolled, they’re removed from any current and future Walmart-funded incentives until they are opted-in again.

* * *

How does a Referral Fee adjustment affect my payment?

Although the price displayed and charged to the customer is lower than the price provided by you, your payment isn’t affected. You’ll receive the same net amount as if the item were sold at the price submitted due to Walmart-funded incentives.

* * *

What happens if I set a promotion for an item?

The item price under the program is calculated from the current offer, including any promotions.

* * *

How can I tell if a customer order contains an item receiving Walmart-funded incentives?

Customer orders will display a Price Incentive within a pop-up window indicating which incentive program.

* * *

How do I ensure that MAPs (Minimum Advertised Prices) aren’t violated?

You should exclude MAP items using the bulk template process. You can add additional SKUs to the list to opt-out as needed.
