Success Hub: Get more reviews with incentives

Last updated on Sep 9, 2025

Reading time: 1 min

* * *

Overview

[**Success Hub**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/success-hub) offers suggestions for eligible items in your catalog that have the potential togenerate more reviews through incentives. In this guide, you’ll learn how to enroll eligible items, customize target reviews and change incentive amounts.

## Enroll items

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to **Success Hub** by selecting [**Growth**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/success-hub) in Seller Center. In the **Get more reviews with incentives** section, choose the _Enroll items_ button.

### Step 2 – Customize target reviews and incentive amounts

On the next page, select the checkboxes beside the row for each item you want to enroll. Select a target review quantity from the drop-down menu in the **Reviews targeted** column, then modify the customer incentive in the **Customer i** **ncentive per review** column.

Notes:

The minimum incentive amount is $5 and the maximum is $25 or 25% of the item, whichever is lower.

### Step 3 – Enroll items

Once you’ve determined your target reviews and the customer incentive amount, select _Enroll x items_.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Will I be charged if customers don’t complete a review?

No. You will only be charged when a customer completes a review and it’s published. The amount due will be deducted from your account balance at the end of your billing cycle.

* * *

What happens after my item has received the target number of reviews?

Your item will be automatically unenrolled from the program once it has received the target number of incentivized reviews. Customers can continue to submit reviews up to 48 hours after the item is unenrolled. You’ll only be charged for the target number of reviews per participating item.

* * *

How do I remove enrolled items?

You can remove enrolled items from the [**Review Accelerator**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/review-accelerator?programSelected=incentivized_reviews) page in Seller Center. Select the checkboxes for the items to remove and Choose _Unenroll_.

* * *

Can multiple sellers enroll the same item in the incentives program?

No. Only one seller at a time can enroll an item for incentives.

* * *

I want to remove a review from my listing. What should I do?

Walmart won’t modify or remove a review unless it meets the guidelines for removal. Learn more about requesting a review removal [here](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Listing%20optimization/Troubleshooting/Request-to-remove-a-review).
