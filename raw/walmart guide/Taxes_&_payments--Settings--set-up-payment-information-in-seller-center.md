Set up payout information in Seller Center

Last updated on Mar 19, 2025

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

* * *

Overview

If you have an administrator Seller Center account, you can set up your [Marketplace Wallet](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/marketplace-wallet-apply-and-enroll) or you can use a third-party payout processor to receive your payout funds. This guide shows you how to set up your payout information in Seller Center.

## Set up payout information

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to the [**Payment Info**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/financial-settings/payment-info) page in Seller Center.

Notes:

Only users with an admin Seller Center account can see financial settings in Seller Center.

### Step 2 – Set up payout info

Select the _Set up_ button next to the payout processor you want to set up.

Notes:

If you want to use the Marketplace Wallet, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

### Step 3 –  Get a verification code & verify

When you set up payout information in Seller Center for the first time, you’re required to enter a verification code that you’ll receive from Walmart via text message. The code will be sent to the primary business contact listed in your [**Manage Contacts**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/partner-profile/manage-contacts) settings in Seller Center. Input the verification code you received and select _Continue._

Notes:

If you need to update your primary business contact, you must select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Are all payout processors available to every seller?

Availability varies by [location](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Payments/Payment-processing). If you’re in the U.S. and interested in the Marketplace Wallet, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

Why do I see a $1.50 withdrawal fee while withdrawing funds from my Payoneer account?

If you signed up for a Payoneer account through Payoneer directly instead of through the link in Walmart Seller Center, you may incur a $1.50 fee upon the withdrawal of funds via your Payoneer MyAccount. Contact [Payoneer](https://payoneer.custhelp.com/) directly to learn how you can avoid or be refunded this Payoneer fee, as they arise from Payoneer’s policies, not Walmart’s.

* * *

How do I change my payout information?

To make changes to your payout information, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

How is the Marketplace Wallet different from third-party payout processors?

Marketplace Wallet powered by our bank provider, is integrated with Walmart’s Marketplace so you receive dedicated support without needing to leave the Seller Center. Deposited funds are FDIC insured\* and has no hidden fees for ACH transfers to a linked bank account.

\*The Marketplace Wallet powered by J.P.Morgan is a deposit account insured by the FDIC up to $250,000.00 per depositor at JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A., for each account ownership category.  The availability of FDIC deposit insurance coverage for the deposit account protects against the failure of JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A., and not of Walmart, Inc.

* * *

I already receive payouts through a third-party payout processor. How can I change my payout method to the Marketplace Wallet?

You can terminate your relationship with your existing third-party payout processor and create a Marketplace Wallet, but you can’t hold both accounts at the same time. If you're interested to learn more, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.

* * *

I am using the Marketplace Wallet. Can I have multiple bank accounts linked at once?

No. Only one bank account can be linked to the Marketplace Wallet at one time. If you need to change your bank account information, select the _Help_ button in the [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) menu bar to contact Support.
