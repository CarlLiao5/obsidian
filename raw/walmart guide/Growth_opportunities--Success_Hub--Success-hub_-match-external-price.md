Overview

Success Hub offers suggestions on ways to make your catalog more competitive by meeting or beating publicly available offers on other marketplaces. In this guide, you’ll learn how to use price suggestions to help stay ahead of the competition.

## Match external price

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to your current external price suggestions in **Success Hub** by selecting [**Growth**](https://seller.walmart.com/growth/success-hub) in Seller Center. Under the **Optimize your catalog with bulk actions** section, choose the _Match prices_ button. To view the external listing, select the three dots on the right side of the page and choose _View competitor listing._

### Step 2 – Select a suggestion

When you are ready to choose which suggestions you would like to implement, select the checkbox to the left of the item row. You can also select _Show selected only_ to display all selected items. Then select _Match External Price on (x) items_.

You can review offers for items eligible for a reduced Referral Fee incentive by selecting the dollar sign under the **Reduced Referral Fee incentives** column. To accept the lower price, select _Accept price._ You can download a report of your external price match suggestions by selecting _Download_.

Notes:

You’ll receive the Reduced Referral Fee incentive if you reduce the price of an item to match or be lower than the competitive price.

Pro tip

On the Success Hub page under _Top items to optimize,_ select an item cardto view price insights and suggestions. Review the pricing insights provided. Select an item card with suggested offers to update your price then choose _Match offer gap_ to apply the change.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What do I do if my price or product is incorrectly matched?

If you notice the price or product is incorrectly matched, you can select _Report an incorrect match_ under the three dots on the right side of the **Match external prices** page and submit the correct URL for the listing.

* * *

Do I have to accept all Success Hub suggestions?

No. All insights are suggestive in nature and you are free to set your own price. There is no business expectation or requirement that you accept any Success Hub suggestions. As a seller, you have no obligation and make no promises to purchase any minimum amount of merchandise to list or sell on the Walmart Marketplace other than what you deem adequate.

Success Hub suggestions are based on publicly available pricing information, past purchasing history and representations about quantities to be purchased are not binding on the Walmart Marketplace and Walmart shall not be liable for any act or expenditure (including but not limited to expenditures for equipment, labor, materials, packaging or capital expenditures) by the seller in reliance on them.
