Overview

As a Marketplace seller, there are a few ways you can fulfill your orders: you can fulfill the orders yourself through **Seller Fulfillment Services**, leverage **Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS)** or get support from two of our third-party Solution Providers, **Deliverr**(also known as Flexport) and **Shipbob.** Keep reading to learn more about each fulfillment method.

## How does it work?

### Seller Fulfillment Services (SFS)

If you prefer to use your own fulfillment center to manage, store and ship your products, opt for Seller Fulfillment Services. You’ll be responsible for shipping your items on time from your facility. To set up a seller-managed fulfillment center, follow the steps in [this guide.](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Fulfillment%20settings/add-a-seller-managed-fulfillment-center-in-seller-center)

### Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS)

[Walmart Fulfillment Services](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/walmart-fulfillment-services-wfs-overview) offers end-to-end fulfillment services for Walmart Marketplace sellers. We’ll store your inventory at a Walmart fulfillment center and when a customer places an order on Walmart.com, we’ll pick, pack and ship the item to the customer on your behalf. WFS also handles all customer support and returns for these orders.

Participating items are displayed with 2-Day Shipping and Fulfilled by Walmart tags for increased product visibility on Walmart.com, which may help improve conversion. If you’ve been approved to sell on Walmart Marketplace, you already have access to WFS. As soon as you add your [WFS details](https://seller.walmart.com/walmart-fulfillment) in Seller Center, you can get started.

### Third-party solution provider

- **Deliverr (Flexport):** Deliverr, also known as Flexport, is responsible for storing and shipping items for you and participating SKUs will automatically be eligible for [Walmart TwoDay Delivery](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-methods:-expedited-delivery-programs). Reach out to [Deliverr](https://www.flexport.com/products/walmart/) directly to get started. Once you’ve created an account with Deliverr, you can set up the third-party managed fulfillment center in Seller Center.

Notes:

If you want to fulfill items with Deliverr using Standard or Value shipping, contact Deliverr and ask them to upload a Shipping Overrides file.

- **ShipBob**: ShipBob is a global logistics platform that fulfills your orders and assists with shipping items while ensuring eligible items are integrated for [Walmart TwoDay Delivery](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Shipping%20methods/Shipping-methods:-expedited-delivery-programs). For more information, reach out to [Shipbob](https://marketplace.walmart.com/solutions-providers/shipbob/) directly to get started. Once you’ve created an account with Shipbob, you can set up the third-party managed fulfillment center in Seller Center.

Notes:

Deliverr and Shipbob can’t be used interchangeably. If you have used Shipbob or Deliverr in the past as a Fulfillment Partner, you can’t use the other.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Which fulfillment method should I choose?

That depends entirely upon your business needs. Choose the fulfillment method that makes the most sense for your situation.

* * *

Can I use more than one fulfillment method at a time?

No, only one fulfillment partner is allowed ( [Deliverr](https://www.flexport.com/products/walmart/) or [Shipbob](https://marketplace.walmart.com/solutions-providers/shipbob/)) for your entire catalog. They can't be used interchangeably.

* * *

How much does each fulfillment method cost?

- WFS’s cost structure includes a fixed monthly storage fee and a fulfillment price that's based on the item weight alone (excluding add-on fees for apparel, hazardous materials, items with low retail prices, and oversize items).

- Reach out to [Deliverr](https://www.flexport.com/products/walmart/) or [Shipbob](https://marketplace.walmart.com/solutions-providers/shipbob/) directly for pricing details.
