Update fulfillment lag time in Seller Center

Last updated on Dec 22, 2025

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

In this guide:

- [Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Fulfillment%20settings/Update-fulfillment-lag-time-in-Seller-Center#overview)
- [Update lag time](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Fulfillment%20settings/Update-fulfillment-lag-time-in-Seller-Center#update)
- [Frequently asked questions](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Seller%20Fulfillment%20Services/Fulfillment%20settings/Update-fulfillment-lag-time-in-Seller-Center#frequently-asked-questions)

* * *

Overview

Fulfillment lag time is the number of days it takes you to prepare an item for shipment. Walmart Marketplace requires you to ship orders within two operational days after receiving an order unless you’ve submitted a [lag time exemption request](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Shipping%20&%20fulfillment/Fulfillment%20settings/Submit-a-lag-time-exemption-request)  and received approval from Walmart. If you’ve submitted a lag time exemption request and it was approved, you need to apply your lag time to the applicable SKUs. In this guide, you'll learn how to update your lag time in Seller Center.

Notes:

Only add lag time where necessary. Adding lag time to SKUs can limit the benefits of the OneDay and TwoDay Delivery programs.

## Update lag time

### Step 1 — Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center and select the _Update items_ button and choose _Update with file_ from the dropdown menu.

### Step 2 — Download and complete the template

Select the _Change fulfillment lag time_ link and save the template to your computer. Then open the template and update the SKUs and corresponding lag times.

![2412_9060_screenshot.png](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt6b40f56198ab3108/69309c6e4870f30229370987/2511_update-fulfillment-lag-time-in-seller-center.png?branch=us_mplearn)

### Step 3 — Upload the template

Navigate back to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) and select the _Update items_ button and choose _Update with file_. Then use the file browser window to upload the template. When you’re ready, select _Submit_.

Updates can appear in as quickly as 15 minutes or can take up to four hours to reflect in the **Fulfillment lag time** column and on Walmart.com.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt260407213ecee8df/64fb26f0103154000dae63c6/LagTime_2.png?branch=test2)

Notes:

Lag time is discretionary and granted on a case-by-case basis. Walmart reserves the right to change your lag time exception in its sole discretion.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Do I have to use the template to update lag time?

No. You can also update lag time from the [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) page in Seller Center. Select the _Columns_ button, select the **Fulfillment lag time** checkbox and then select _Apply._ Back on the **Catalog** page, locate the **Fulfillment lag time** column and select the number that is displayed.

All items have a default lag time allowance of zero or one.  Any lag time greater than one must be approved through a lag time exemption request.  Choose your lag time then select _Update_. Updates can appear as quickly as 15 minutes or can take up to four hours to reflect in the Fulfillment lag time column and on Walmart.com.

* * *

How can I track the status of my file submission?

You can track the status of your submission on the [**Activity feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed).

* * *

Is there a limit to how many items I can upload at once?

Yes. You can upload up to 20,000 items in one template. If you attempt to upload a template with more than 20,000 rows, you'll receive an error.

* * *

Why am I receiving an error in the template?

You could receive an error if you change the file type or if you put a value greater than one in the Fulfillment lag time column.
