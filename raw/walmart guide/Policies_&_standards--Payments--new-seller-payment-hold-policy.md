New Seller Payment Hold Policy

Last updated on Dec 9, 2025

Reading time: 1 min

* * *

Overview

If you’re a new Marketplace seller, you’ll experience a [payment hold](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Payments/Payment-activities) after you begin selling to build sales history and credibility with customers. Keep reading to learn about this policy and how it works.

## What is the policy?

All Marketplace sellers are subjected to a payment hold, also known as a rolling delay of payments. A rolling delay means that a portion of your payments will be delayed, or held back by Walmart.

This rolling delay starts the day of your first shipped order and continues for a certain period, such as 30 days, before you receive the full payment. The timeframe of the rolling delay of payment varies depending on your country of incorporation. Use the table below as a reference.

| Country of incorporation | Rolling delay |
| --- | --- |
| **United States** | Up to 14 days and will end once 90 days have passed since your first order has been shipped, and you've received $7,500 in payments. |
| **Non-U.S.** | Up to 21 days and will end once 90 days have passed since your first order has been shipped, and you've received $7,500 in payments. |

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

What are these payment activities on my payment statement?

Learn more about payment activities [here](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Payments/Payment-activities).

* * *

I’m having trouble understanding some of the terminology listed on my payment statement. What should I do?

Review this [guide](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Taxes%20&%20payments/Payments/Payment-statements-and-transactions) for the definitions of each term listed on your payment statement.

* * *

I have been selling at Walmart for more than 90 days, why am I still experiencing a payment hold?

You need to also receive $7,500 in your total payments from Walmart Marketplace for the payment hold to be lifted.

* * *

Will I receive payment in the first 90 days of selling on Walmart Marketplace?

Yes. You could receive payments as soon as 30 days after your first sale. The hold is a rolling delay in payments and until 90 days have passed since your first order was shipped and you’ve received $7,500 in payments, the payment hold will be lifted.
