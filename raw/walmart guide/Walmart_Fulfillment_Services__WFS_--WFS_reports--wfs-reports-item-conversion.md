WFS Reports: Item conversion

Last updated on Oct 29, 2025

Reading time: 2 min

* * *

In this guide:

[Overview](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20reports/wfs-reports-item-conversion#overview)

[Access this report](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20reports/wfs-reports-item-conversion#accessthisreport)

[Dive into details](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20reports/wfs-reports-item-conversion#diveintodetails)

[Understand report metrics](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20reports/wfs-reports-item-conversion#understand)

* * *

Overview

Not sure which items to bring to WFS? The Item conversion report identifies which seller-fulfilled items would perform best if you converted them to Walmart-fulfilled. It’s available to any seller who already has at least one Walmart-fulfilled item in their catalog.

### Access this report

- Log in to Seller Center

- Select **WFS>Reports** from the left navigation pane

- Under Growth, select **Download** for the **Item conversion** report

- Set your desired time period

- Select **Download** and open the report

### Dive into details

#### How to use this report effectively

- We rank recommendations from highest expected sales to lowest.
- The report shows a recommended quantity to inbound based on each item’s expected sales and Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) potential for the next 30 days.
- The report is refreshed twice a month with the latest recommendations.

#### Important insights

- We suggest acting quickly on high-priority recommendations to help make the most of your GMV penetration.  The expected demand is time-sensitive.
- If you have seller-fulfilled items with the potential for increased sales if converted to Walmart-fulfilled, select **Fulfill with WFS** to review and convert these items.

### Understand report metrics

| **Column name** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| Report Run Date | Date when the report was generated |
| GTIN | 14-digit Global Trade Item Number, including check digit, that's unique to the item. If it's less than 14 digits, add zeros at the beginning |
| Item Name | Title of the item |
| Item ID | Unique identifier assigned by Walmart |
| Vendor SKU | Unique identifier created by the seller |
| Past 30 days Seller AUR | Sales of an item divided by the number of units sold in the past 30 days. |
| Past 30 days sales (weekly average) | Average weekly GMV of an item over the past 30 days. |
| Suggested units | Number of units we suggest you send to WFS based on predicted sales for 30 days after they're converted and available to sell. |
| Walmart-fulfilled GMV (weekly average) | What we predict you'll earn each week for the next 30 days once the item is converted, sent to WFS and made available to sell. |
| Estimated sales lift (weekly average) | The sales lift we predict you'll see each week after this item is converted, sent to WFS and made available to sell. |
| Total estimated sales lift | Percentage of GMV increase we estimate you'll see if you convert the item to WFS and send the suggested units. It considers several factors including past sales, seasonality, listing quality, pricing and more. Only items with greater than 1% lift are included. |
| Item conversion priority | 'High' is a dynamic recommendation based on past sales, product information, listing quality, price and external demand signals. 'Standard' is calculated from past sales, two-day shipping and Buy Box wins. |
