Overview

Before you ship your products to customers, you’ll need to set up your shipping settings and define rules for each region you deliver to. **Simplified Shipping Settings** is one option that you can use to ship your items and avoid late deliveries due to carrier issues. In this guide, you’ll learn how Simplified Shipping Settings works.

Notes:

Simplified Shipping Settings is compatible with free and paid shipping **,** [virtual packs](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup%20methods/Create-a-virtual-pack:-full-setup-template) and most domestic USPS, UPS and FedEx services. However, it doesn’t support Ship to Store items, international or freight shipping or third-party logistics services such as Flexport.

## How does it work?

When your fulfillment settings are updated to include your most-frequently used carriers, shipping methods and fulfillment locations, Simplified Shipping Settings uses real-time carrier transit data to help you close the gap between your actual and promised delivery times.

With Simplified Shipping Settings you can:

| Feature | Get started |
| --- | --- |
| **Manage your preferred carrier and shipping settings** | Customize your carrier services for each fulfillment center as needed. Assign your preferred carriers and shipping speeds to different regions. To get started, visit [_Simplified Shipping Settings: Manage carrier settings_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/simplified-shipping-settings-set-up-your-settings). |
| **Apply settings to your eligible items** | Apply your settings to all your eligible published items, including new items you add to your catalog. To get started, visit [_Simplified Shipping Settings: Apply settings to existing items_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/simplified-shipping-settings-apply-settings-to-your-items). |
| **Manage shipping rules at the item level** | Create custom rules for items that may have specific shipping requirements (e.g., electronics, luxury items, specific brands, etc.). To get started, visit [_Simplified Shipping Settings: Manage item group settings_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/simplified-shipping-settings-manage-item-group-settings). |

## Additional guidelines

Simplified Shipping Settings also helps protect your On-Time Delivery (OTD) performance metrics to help keep you in good standing with [Walmart's Seller Performance Standards](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Seller-performance-standards) when there are disruptions with your shipping carrier.

If you meet the following Simplified Shipping Settings criteria, your On-Time Delivery performance metric will not be impacted by carrier delays out of your control:

| Criteria | Performance metric |
| --- | --- |
| **On-Time Shipping** | 100% of your orders were shipped on or before the Estimated Ship Date (ESD). |
| **Carrier method accuracy** | 100% of your orders were shipped using your selected carrier method. |
| **Ship from location accuracy** | 100% of your orders were shipped from the recommended fulfillment center location. |

You can track your performance in real time via the [**Performance**](https://seller.walmart.com/performance/order-and-fulfillment) dashboard in Seller Center.

Pro tip

Your preferred shipping and carrier settings directly impact your performance. Visit your order details for recommendations on how to improve your metrics. If you're using the [Orders API](https://developer.walmart.com/doc/us/mp/us-mp-orders/) to process orders, check your API settings to make sure your ship from location and carrier methods are accurate.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Can I use more than one carrier service for the entire U.S.?

You must have one carrier service that covers the contiguous U.S. You can offer additional carrier services and customize the regional coverage of any additional carrier services offered.

* * *

Are all my offers eligible for Simplified Shipping Settings?

Most seller-fulfilled items with free shipping are eligible for Simplified Shipping Settings. Simplified Shipping Settings is not compatible with Ship to Store items, international or freight shipping or third-party logistics services such as Flexport.
