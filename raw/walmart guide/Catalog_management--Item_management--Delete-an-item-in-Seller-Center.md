Delete an item in Seller Center

Last updated on Jan 14, 2026

Reading time: 1 min

* * *

Overview

If your item is outdated, consider removing it to keep your Marketplace catalog organized and up to date. In this guide, we’ll show you how to permanently delete your items in Seller Center.

Notes:

You must have [admin level](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Getting%20started/Account%20settings/Update-user-information) access in Seller Center to delete an item from your catalog. If you don’t have admin access, reach out to your account administrator for help. You can’t delete an item through API. Seller Support won’t process bulk delete requests.

## Delete an item

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to your [**Catalog**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center and select the checkbox next to each item you want to remove.

### Step 2 – Delete items

Once you’ve selected your items, choose the three horizontal dots and select _Delete items_.

Pro tip

You can delete up to 100 SKUs in bulk from your catalog and submit up to 10 requests per hour.

Notes:

You can’t delete Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) items, drafts, items with an **Error** status or items that are still processing.

### Step 3 – Confirm deletion

You’ll be asked to confirm that your items will be unpublished and permanently deleted. Once you confirm, an alert will appear with the option to copy the Feed ID for your deleted items. You can copy the ID or navigate to your [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) to view a record of the deletion.

Your selected items will be deleted within 48 hours. It may take up to 72 hours before your items are removed from your Catalog.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Where can I find my deleted items?

Navigate to your [**Activity Feed**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/activity-feed) and find your items in the list. You can also search for them by Feed ID, or check the **SKU or file name** and **Feed type** columns for files marked as _DELETE\_ITEM_ and _Delete item_.

* * *

How do I upload my completed Inventory template?

For details on the Inventory template, review [_Update seller-fulfilled inventory in bulk in Seller Center_](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Catalog%20management/Seller-fulfilled%20inventory%20management/Update-seller-fulfilled-inventory-in-bulk-in-Seller-Center) _._

* * *

How can I delete more than 100 SKUs?

You can delete up to 10,000 items using [a spreadsheet](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/delete-items-in-bulk-in-seller-center).
