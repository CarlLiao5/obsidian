WFS shipping plans: Send domestic inventory

Last updated on Feb 11, 2026

Reading time: 6 min

Overview

When you use Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS), you’ll need to send inventory to fulfillment centers so we can pick, pack and ship orders for you. In this guide, learn how to create a shipping plan, pack shipments and track progress.

## Create a shipping plan

A shipping plan tells us what items you’re sending, how you’re sending them and when we can expect them to arrive. You must create a shipping plan _before_ you send your items to a WFS fulfillment center. If not, your inventory will be delayed or rejected during check-in.

### Step 1 – Get started

Navigate to [**Shipping Plans**](https://seller.walmart.com/wfs/shipments) in Seller Center and select _Create plan_. If applicable, choose whether you’re shipping from a US domestic address or international through [Walmart Cross Border - Imports](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20programs%20&%20services/walmart-cross-border-imports-overview).

### Step 2 – Add items

Search for items in your catalog, or upload a spreadsheet with multiple items at once. Then enter the number of units you're sending and how they're packed. This information must be accurate so we can calculate which fulfillment centers your inventory should be delivered to. This lets us strategically place inventory close to customers so that we can offer fast delivery speeds.

Pro tip

For single SKU items, you have [the option](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/wfs-box-contents-experience) to save units, weight and dimensions as a packing template. You'll be able to reuse the template in future shipments.

### Step 3 – Choose distribution

Your plan may be split into multiple shipments to help with next-day and weekend fulfillment. If so, you’ll see an option to [distribute inventory yourself](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20policies%20&%20standards/wfs-shipping-plans-distribute-inventory-yourself) or use [Inventory Transfer Service.](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-Inventory-Transfer-Services)

### Step 4 – Select shipping

Choose the carrier and shipping type, then enter master case or pallet details. Use a [Walmart preferred carrier](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/Walmart-Preferred-Carrier-(WPC)-program) to get discounted rates, reliable pickup and automatic tracking. If you [use your own carrier](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-inbound-orders-Choose-a-shipment-method?locale=en-US), you’ll need to arrange pickup and delivery yourself. If you have multiple shipments, make sure to complete this step in each tab.

You can also download receiving labels for master cases. This is helpful if you want to start packing and see which items go in which boxes.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt6daccd4fcdec33e3/698e0f162b1b82926c4803d6/shipping_step_packing_list.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

If you change earlier details or edit a draft, it may reset the shipment. You may then need to download new receiving labels.

### Step 5 – Add contact details

Add a pickup contact and operating hours. This step is optional if you’re using your own carrier or sending parcel.

### Step 6 – Review shipping plan

Confirm the details for each shipment, then select _Create plan_.

* * *

## Save a draft

Not ready to commit to the shipping plan? You can save it at any point by selecting _Save as draft_ and coming back later.

Go to the **Drafts** tab on the Shipping Plans page, then select _Edit draft_ to continue where you left off. You’ll still need to complete all steps and submit the plan before shipping inventory. If you no longer need a draft, you can delete it by selecting the trash icon.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt66a65ccf24577fde/68b07ab941e63b21d85c2af9/Send_domestic_edit_draft.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Notes:

Ship-to locations, estimated prices and shipment IDs will reset by the date specified in your shipping plan. If you’ve already printed receiving labels, make sure the information is still up to date and reprint them if needed. Otherwise, you may be shipping to facilities that can’t accept your inventory.

## Prepare shipments

On the [**Shipping Plans**](https://seller.walmart.com/wfs/shipments) page, select _View plan_ to manage all tasks for the shipment. This may include:

- [Downloading receiving labels](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-inbound-orders-Labels) for master cases and pallets
- Downloading bill of lading
- Downloading carrier labels if you use your own carrier
- [Viewing or rescheduling pickup](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WPC-prepare-pickup) for Walmart preferred carriers

Also make sure your shipments and items follow our [labeling and packing guidelines](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/WFS%20basics/WFS-routing-guide-overview).

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt7bfa0fed3f507560/682bbc9ea8205f503c73d9d7/view_tasks.png?branch=us_mplearn)

Pro tip

Select _Review item packing_ to download the packing list. This tells you which items go in which boxes. If you receiving labels have unique box IDs, the packing list shows you how each box should be packed.

## Track shipment status

Track carrier milestones and receiving progress from the [**Shipping Plans**](https://seller.walmart.com/wfs/shipments) page. Here are some of the statuses you can expect:

- **Processing:** Shipment is submitted. Labels and other information will be available soon.
- **Action required:** Shipment is missing carrier and packing details. Select _Set up_ to finish the shipment.
- **Carrier pending:** We're assigning a Walmart preferred carrier to the shipment.
- **Tracking needed:** Add the carrier and tracking number to get delivery updates.
- **Awaiting transit:** Shipment is at your warehouse and ready for transportation.
- **In transit:** Shipment is on its way to a Walmart facility.
- **Out for delivery:** Shipment is arriving at a Walmart facility soon.
- **Arrived:** Shipment has been delivered to a Walmart facility, but receiving hasn’t started yet.
- **Transfer in progress:** Shipment arrived at a transfer station. We’re distributing inventory to other locations to unlock next-day and weekend delivery. Track items to view transfer progress.
- **Receiving:** Items are being inspected and stored. Once items are received, it can take 2 business days for them to become available to sell.
- **Issues:** Receiving is delayed due to an issue with the shipment.
- **Completed:** Receiving is done for the shipment.
- **Closed:** Shipment is closed and no longer eligible for disputes.
- **Canceled:** Shipment you canceled or that we canceled due to no activity.

Once a shipment’s in _Awaiting transit_, you can check the tracking history and estimated delivery. Just select _Track_ for a quick view of the shipment’s progress.

![](https://azure-na-images.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/bltf5b0330f53082658/68754978ef20eff8ecebfd39/Send_domestic_track_carrier.png?branch=us_mplearn)

If you’re using your own carrier, make sure to add the tracking numbers once you get them. This helps us know when to expect the shipment, but also gives you real-time visibility in Seller Center. To add tracking numbers, filter the status by _Tracking needed_ and select _Add tracking_.

## Track item receiving

After a shipment has arrived, switch to the _Track items_ tab for a quick view of fill rates. For more details, you can select _View progress_.

## Cancel a shipping plan

You can cancel a shipping plan before it’s picked up by a carrier or arrives at the fulfillment center. If any of the shipments have an _In transit_ status or beyond, you won’t be able to cancel. To optimize your assortment across the WFS network, you can only cancel a shipping plan and not individual shipments.

To cancel a shipping plan, search for the plan or shipment ID on the Shipping Plans page. Then select _View plan_ and look for the _Cancel plan_ option in the top corner.

Notes:

If you choose a Walmart preferred carrier, you may have a limited window to void charges and edit the shipment: 24 hours for small parcel and 1 hour for freight. This helps ensure we're locking in the best carrier rate at the time you set up your shipment.

## Frequently asked questions

* * *

Can I change items or quantities?

After you create the shipping plan, you won’t be able to change items or number of units. If you’re not sure, you can always save the plan as a draft and come back to it.

* * *

How soon can I ship inventory after creating a shipping plan?

If there are no errors in your shipping plan, you can ship immediately. Alternatively, you can create a shipping plan ahead of time for a future shipment.

* * *

Can I choose the fulfillment center I ship to?

No, you must send inventory to your assigned facility. We’ll pick compatible WFS facilities based on your ship-from address. If any of your items within a shipment has special handling requirements, such as hazardous materials, we may assign a different facility that can safely store them.

* * *

How will inventory be distributed across multiple fulfillment centers?

We may ask you to split inventory into multiple shipments and distribute it to fulfillment centers across the country. We choose the final destinations based on the item’s level on hand, on order and geography-based demand. You can have us distribute for you through [Inventory Transfer Service](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Shipping%20to%20WFS/WFS-Inventory-Transfer-Services) or arrange each shipment on your own.

* * *

When is peak? When should I send holiday inventory to WFS?

Peak is October 1 to December 31. Leading up to the holiday season, we’ll recommend the ideal date to send inventory by. Make sure your [contact details](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Walmart%20Fulfillment%20Services%20(WFS)/Getting%20started%20with%20WFS/WFS-settings-contact-information) in Seller Center are accurate.

* * *

Are there any storage limits at the fulfillment centers?

There are currently no minimums or limitations on the amount of inventory you can send to a fulfillment center.
